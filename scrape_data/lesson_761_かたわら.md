## About

About かたわら

English

Japanese

傍かたわら, seen primarily in hiragana, is a noun in Japanese that means 'bystander', in regard to something that is alongside or in the same place as the main focus. As a grammatical structure, it will be translated as 'while (A)', 'in addition to (A)', or 'besides (A)'.

かたわら may come after nouns followed by の, or after the dictionary form of verbs.

彼かれは飲食店いんしょくてんを経営けいえいしているかたわら、鳶職人とびしょくにんとしても働はたらいている。

Besides running a restaurant, he also works as a steeplejack.

グレッググラフィンは大学だいがくの教授きょうじゅをしているかたわら、歌手かしゅとして活動かつどうしている。

In addition to being a college professor, Greg Graffin is also active as a singer.

私わたしの妹いもうとは本業ほんぎょうのかたわら、英会話えいかいわ教室きょうしつで英語えいごを教おしえている。

My sister teaches English at an English conversation class in addition to her day job.

妻つまは子育こそだてのかたわら、近所きんじょの子供達こどもたちにそろばんを教おしえている。

My wife teaches abacus to kids in the neighborhood in addition to raising our children.

Something that can be remembered from the 'bystander' meaning of this word is that かたわら will always come after the primary action or state of (A), while (B) is considered to just be there incidentally to (A), or as being done secondarily to it. This usually takes the form of something like a side job or a hobby.

Caution

Unlike ながら, かたわら does not necessarily indicate two actions that are happening at the exact same time, just actions that are happening independently of each other with one being the focus, and the other happening to a lesser extent within the same subject's life, day to day activities, etc.

Moreover, besides holding the position of a Japanese professor at the university, he is also active as a manga artist.

ブルースは、昼ひるは財閥ざいばつを経営けいえいする傍かたわら、夜よるはバーマンとして危険きけん地域ちいきを見回みまわっている。

During the daytime, Bruce manages his family corporation, while at night he patrols dangerous areas as BARMAN.

いくつかの国くにでは、女性じょせいは仕事しごとの傍かたわら、苦労くろうをいとわず子供こどもを育そだてている。立派りっぱなことだと思おもう。

In some countries, women work in addition to raising their children, not minding the hardships. I think that is quite praiseworthy.

指導医しどういとして病院びょういんに務つとめる傍かたわら、コンサルタントと専門医せんもんいとして企業きぎょうでも業務ぎょうむを行おこなっています。

In addition to working as the preceptor at the hospital, she also engages in business as a consultant and a medical specialist.

歌手かしゅとして活動かつどうする傍かたわら女優じょゆう業ぎょうも立派りっぱにこなす彼女かのじょは、一体いったい一年いちねんでいくら稼かせいでいるのだろう…。

In relation to her, who in addition to getting along as a singer, is also doing well as an excellent actress, I wonder how much she earns in a year.

娘むすめは美容びようの専門せんもん学校がっこうに通かよう傍かたわらアルバイトを通とおして自分じぶんで学費がくひを賄まかなっているので感心かんしんしている。

In addition to my daughter going to a beauty school, she is also paying her own tuition fees through part-time work, so I'm thankful.

僕ぼくが務つとめる会社かいしゃは釣つりに関かんする製品せいひんを製造せいぞうする傍かたわら、環境かんきょう保全ほぜんのために木きを植うえる活動かつどうもしています。

In addition to manufacturing fishing-related products, the company I work for also engages in tree-planting activities for environmental preservation.

僕ぼくの父ちちは製薬せいやく会社がいしゃで新薬しんやくの開発かいはつをする傍かたわら僕ぼくたち三人さんにん兄弟きょうだいの子育こそだてにも奮闘ふんとうしています。

My father, who in addition to developing novel drugs at a drug manufacturing company, is also putting strenuous efforts into raising us three siblings.

私わたしの将来しょうらいの夢ゆめは主婦しゅふとして子育こそだてに全まっとうする傍かたわら、自分じぶんの芸術げいじゅつの才能さいのうを生いかして少すこしお金かねを稼かせぐ事ことです。

As for my future dream, in addition to carrying out the raising of children as a housewife, I'd also like to make the most of my performance abilities and earn a bit of money.

大学だいがくに通かよう傍かたわら自身じしんの壮絶そうぜつな幼少ようしょう期きに関かんする自叙じじょ伝でんを執筆しっぴつするなんて、彼かれには相当そうとう体力たいりょくがあるのだろう。

In addition to going to university, something like writing an autobiography about the prime of their youth, that guy has a considerable amount of stamina.

## About

About につれて

English

Japanese

につれて, or に連つれて when using kanji, is an expression that may be translated as 'as (A), then (B)', or 'in proportion to (A), (B)'. It is formed through the use of the case marking particle に, the る - Verb 連つれる 'to take along', and the conjunction particle て. Literally, the translation is similar to 'being taken along with (A), (B)', or 'in tow with (A), (B)'.

につれて will be used after the plain, non-past form of verbs, or nouns.

毎日まいにち運転うんてんをするにつれて、どんどん運転うんてんが嫌きらいになってきた。

As I drove everyday, I came to dislike driving more and more.

夏なつになるにつれて、日ひが昇のぼるのが早はやくなってきた。

Along with it becoming summer, the time the sun comes up is getting earlier.

犬いぬの成長せいちょうにつれて、食欲しょくよくが変かわる。

As the dog grows, how much he eats changes (in proportion).

時代じだいの変化へんかにつれて、食文化しょくぶんかも変かわる。

As the times change, food culture also changes.

As 連つれる is a transitive verb, (A) につれて (B) implies that (A) is causing (B) to happen simultaneously. This does not have to be something that (A) is purposely doing (although it may be), just a 'domino' type effect, where (A) is considered the instigator.

冬ふゆになるにつれて、太陽たいようが沈しずむ時間じかんが早はやくなる。

Along with it becoming winter, the time the sun sets gets earlier. (Natural, as winter directly affects the time the sun sets)

冬ふゆになるにつれて、犬いぬが成長せいちょうしていく。

In tow with it becoming winter, the dog grows more. (Unnatural, as there is no relationship)

## Examples

Examples

00:04

Sentence
Translation

日ひがたつに連つれて、怪我けがが悪化あっかしてきた。

As the days go by, my injury gets worse and worse.

こちらの木きが大おおきくなるに連つれて、影かげが広ひろがります。

As these trees grow tall, their shade spreads.

子供こどもは、成長せいちょうするに連つれて、性格せいかくが変かわる。

Along with their growth, the personality of children changes.

進すすむに連つれて道みちがどんどん狭せまくなるから心配しんぱいになってきた。

As I continued on, the road grew increasingly more narrow, so I started to feel anxious.

経済けいざいの発展はってんに連つれて一般いっぱん市民しみんの日常にちじょう生活せいかつは良よくなっていく。

As the economy develops, the daily life of citizens improves.

冬ふゆの間あいだは、日ひが沈しずむに連つれてどんどん温度おんどが下さがっていく。

During winter, the temperature rapidly decreases as the sun goes down.

子供こどもが大おおきくなるに連つれて、一緒いっしょに出でかける機会きかいが少すくなくなってきた。

As your children grow older, you have fewer and fewer chances to go out and do things with them.

商品しょうひんが売うれるに連つれて、品質ひんしつが落おちることもあるようです。

As sales increase, the quality of the product could sometimes get worse.

大人おとなになるに連つれて、いい人ひとも悪わるい人ひともいるということが分わかってきた。

As I grow older, I am beginning to realize that there are good and bad people.

仕事しごとが忙いそがしくなるに連つれて、睡眠すいみん時間じかんが少すくなくなっています。

As I get busier with my job, I have less and less time for sleep.

結婚けっこん式しきを挙あげる日ひが近ちかづくに連つれて、色々いろいろと不安ふあんになってきた。

(Along) with our wedding day drawing nearer, I am starting to feel anxious about various things.

パソコンを使つかう機会きかいが増ふえるに連つれて、だんだん漢字かんじが書かけなくなっている。

As the opportunity to use computers increases, people gradually lose their ability to write kanji.

## About

About どうやら

English

Japanese

どうやら can be translated as 'apparently', or 'it seems like', and is often grouped together with other phrases of speculation such as ようだ, そうだ, and らしい. Due to this, どうやら simply strengthens the uncertainty of things that the speaker is already unsure about. This is in a similar fashion to phrases like 'in some way it seems like (A)'.

Like many adverbs that express amount or speculation, どうやら will appear at the beginning of sentences and modify the meaning of the entire phrase.

鈴木すずきくんはいるか？居いないのかな？どうやら今日きょうも休やすんでいるみたいだね。

Is Suzuki-kun present? Maybe he's not here? It seems like he's taking today off as well.

どうやら私わたしは彼女かのじょには必要ひつようがないようだ。

It seems like I am not needed by her.

どうやら彼かれは結婚けっこんしているらしいよ。

I heard that he is apparently married.

Fun-fact

While classified as a standalone adverb, どうやら is originally a combination of どう 'how' and やら, a 助詞じょし (particle) that is used for denoting uncertainty about singular things or lists. With this in mind, it is similar to the way that 'or whatever', or 'or some such' may be added to a sentence in English in order to emphasize that (A) is not certain.

お隣となりの鈴木すずきさんがどうやら来月らいげつからイギリスに行いくらしいですよ。

Our neighbor, Suzuki-san, is apparently going to England next month, or something.

Sentence
Translation

あの二人ふたり、どうやら結婚けっこんしてる感かんじだよ。

It seems like those two are married.

あの動画どうが、どうやら削除さくじょされたみたいだ。

It looks like that video was deleted.

どうやら今年ことしの紅葉こうようは遅おくれているらしい。

It looks like fall colors are late this year.

どうやら「心当こころあたりがない」というのは本当ほんとうらしい。

Apparently it is true that he 'has no clue.'

彼かれはどうやらお小遣こづかいをもらったことがないようです。

It seems like he has never received an allowance.

どうやら、俺おれは電気でんきをつけっぱなしにしたらしい。すみません。

It looks like I left the light on. I'm sorry.

彼女かのじょはどうやら本気ほんきでコンクールに出品しゅっぴんしたいらしい。

It looks like she is serious in wanting to enter an exhibit in the contest.

どうやら祭日さいじつはあのお店みせやってないみたいだよ。

Apparently that store isn't open on national holidays.

あの施設しせつの完成かんせいは、どうやら再来年さらいねんになるらしいです。

It looks like that facility will be finished the year after next.

ＮＨＫエヌエイチケー集金人しゅうきんにん：「どうやら、私わたしはこの辺あたりでは歓迎かんげいされないようだ...」

NHK Collector: 'Apparently, I am not welcome here.'

結婚式けっこんしきの客きゃく１いち：「あれは誰だれ？」
結婚式けっこんしきの客きゃく２に：「どうやら花嫁はなよめの兄あにみたいだな。」

Wedding Guest 1: 'Who is that?'
Wedding Guest 2: 'Apparently, it is the bride's elder brother.'

知しってた？ どうやら 田中たなかさんは彼氏かれしと別わかれて、今いまはフリーらしいよ。

Did you know? Apparently, Tanaka broke up with her boyfriend. She is free now!

## About

About 始末だ

English

Japanese

始末しまつ, a noun meaning ‘settlement’, or ‘end result’ is commonly used in order to indicate a negative sequence of events that has been passed through, causing an equally negative result to come about at the end. It will be translated as ‘to wind up as (A)’, ‘to end up as (A)’, or ‘to come about that (A)’.

This expression will be used primarily after verbs in their dictionary form.

あんだけダブルチェックをするように言いったのに、この始末しまつだ。

I told him so many times to double check, but this is the result.

みんなで祭まつりに行いくつもりだったのに、みんながドタキャンするから、結局けっきょく一人ひとりで行いく始末しまつだ。

We were supposed to go to the festival together, but everyone canceled suddenly, so I ended up going alone.

その客きゃくは店中みせじゅうをぐちゃぐちゃにしたあげく、何なにも買かわないで帰かえる始末しまつだ。

After messing up the whole store, they ended up leaving without buying anything.

Due to 始末しまつ being used to highlight negative outcomes that are the result of going through a specific event, it will often appear in sentences that also include words like とうとう ‘to come to a head’, 挙あげ句く ‘after a long process’, or 結局けっきょく ‘in the end’. It may seem redundant to include these phrases, but as 始末しまつ has the more literal translation of ‘to culminate in’ (including everything from beginning to end), it is more emphatic, rather than redundant.

## Examples

Examples

00:09

Sentence
Translation

武田たけださんは小ちいさな声こえで職場しょくばでハラスメントを受うけたことを伝つたえてきて、しまいに大おおきな声こえで泣なき出だす始末しまつだ。

Takeda-san quietly told me that she was being harassed at work, and wound up crying out loud.

あいつは、自分じぶんが起おこしたミスなのに、謝あやまるどころか私わたしのせいにする始末しまつだ。

Even though it was a mistake that he had made, far from apologizing, he ended up blaming me!

せっかく友達ともだちと海うみへ行いく計画けいかくを立たてたのに、田中たなかは大事だいじな用ようがあって行いけないとか、武田たけだもシフトが入はいってたとかで、結局けっきょく私わたし一ひと人りだけで行いく始末しまつだ。

Even though I went out of my way to plan this trip to the beach with friends, Tanaka said that he had something important to do and couldn't go, while Takeda said that he had a shift. So I ended up going all by myself.

武田たけだ：「山田やまださん、顔色かおいろが悪わるいですけど・・・」
山田やまだ：「夜よる遅おそくまでゲームをしていたので、おかげでこの始末しまつだ。」

Takeda: 'Yamada-san, you don't look so good...'
Yamada : 'I was playing video games until late at night, and this is the result.'

田中たなかには二度にどと仕事しごとは頼たのまないぞ。納期のうきは守まもらないし仕上しあがりも悪わるかったのに、言いい訳わけばかりならべて。最後さいごには追加ついかの料金りょうきんを払はらえと言いい出だす始末しまつだ。

I'll never ask Tanaka to work for me again. He can't meet deadlines, his workmanship is poor, and he only gives excuse after excuse. In the end he told me to pay an additional fee.

プロジェクトを期限内きげんないに終おえることができなくて、上司じょうしの逆鱗げきりんに触ふれてしまう始末しまつだ。

I couldn't finish the project on time, and I wound up infuriating my boss.

重おもい買かい物袋ものぶくろが破やぶれて中身なかみが地面じめんに落おちたところ：「下したから抱かかえなかったら、この始末しまつだ。」

After a heavy shopping bag has just ripped, and the contents fell out: 'This is what comes about when you don't hold it from the bottom.'

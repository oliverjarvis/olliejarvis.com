## About

About にとどまらず

English

Japanese

にとどまらず is a formal structure that pairs the case marking particle に, the う-Verb 止とどまる 'to remain', or 'to be limited to', and the classical auxiliary verb ず 'not'. にとどまらず is generally translated as 'not limited to (A)', or 'not stopping at (A)', and follows words indicating an extent, before going on to explain how something spreads beyond (A).

にとどまらず will be seen following either nouns, or verbs in their dictionary form.

彼かれは会社かいしゃの経費けいひを管理かんりするにとどまらず、社員しゃいんの給料きゅうりょうも管理かんりしている。

He not only manages the company's expenses, but also the salaries of its employees.

先生せんせいは授業じゅぎょうを行おこなうにとどまらず、部活ぶかつの指導しどうなどもしなくてはならない。

Teachers must not only teach classes, but also supervise club activities.

寿司すしは日本にほんにとどまらず、世界中せかいじゅうで人気にんきがある。

Sushi is popular not only in Japan but all over the world.

この漫画まんがは子供こどもにとどまらず、大人おとなの間あいだでも話題わだいになっている。

This manga is not limited to children, but has become a topic of conversation among adults as well.

This expression may be used to highlight either positive or negative effects.

Caution

にとどまらず will only be used when (B) is something that is expanding outward from the pre-existing boundaries of (A). This means that (B) will not be something that isn't directly tied to (A) somehow.

このコンサートのチケットにとどまらず、限定げんていグッズなども高たかかった。

Not only were tickets to this concert expensive, but also limited edition merchandise. (Unnatural Japanese)

It is unbelievable that yesterday's nightmare was not only a dream, but would actually happen in reality…

Boxstation7 is not only popular in Japan, but also abroad.

By studying Japanese, one does not only acquire linguistic ability but also a Japanese way of thinking.

Company website: 'Our company is not only selling products but also designing them.'

Doctor Hazama's field of research is not limited to genetics, but it also extends to microbiology.

Limited not only to within Korea, K-pop has become popular worldwide as of late.

飼かい猫ねこの癌がんが足あしにとどまらず全身ぜんしんに転移てんいしている事ことを受うけ、急いそいで治療ちりょうを受うけさせる事ことにした。

Accepting that my pet cat's cancer is not limited to her leg, but has moved to her whole body, I quickly decided to make her undertake treatment.

私わたしが運営うんえいしているフィットネスの利用者りようしゃは若わかい男女だんじょにとどまらず、高齢者こうれいしゃや障害者しょうがいしゃにまで及およびます。

Among my fitness operation’s consumers, not limited to young men and women, they also extend to the elderly and handicapped.

Not stopping at her good looks, that idol also has things like high singing skills and communication ability.

大雨おおあめによる被害ひがいは洪水こうずいだけにとどまらず、山岳さんがく地方ちほうでは多おおくの場所ばしょで地滑じすべりが発生はっせいした。

With damages not only limited to flooding due to the heavy rain, there were also many areas in the mountain region where landslides occured.

This NGO not only works to eliminate absolute poverty, but also addresses the reduction of relative poverty.

国内こくないで一位いちいを争あらそうほどの人気にんきを誇ほこるテーマパークは、休日きゅうじつにとどまらず平日へいじつも混雑こんざつしているそうです。

Among the national theme parks that boast popularity to the degree of fighting for the number one spot, I heard that they are crowded not only on the weekends, but also weekdays.

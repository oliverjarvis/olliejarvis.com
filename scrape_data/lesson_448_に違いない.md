## About

About に違いない

English

Japanese

When the case marking particle に is combined with the noun 違ちがい 'difference', and the い-Adjective ない, an expression similar to 'there is no doubt that (A)', or 'I'm sure that (A)' is created.

に違ちがいない may be used with any verbs, い-Adjectives, な-Adjectives or nouns in their plain form.

彼かれらは、先生せんせいがいなくなったらふざけるに違ちがいない。

There is no doubt that they will muck around once the teacher has gone.

あなたが美味おいしいというなら、美味おいしいに違ちがいない。

If you say that it is good, I have no doubt that it is.

タナカ君くんは全然ぜんぜん料理りょうりを食たべてない。この料理りょうりが嫌きらいに違ちがいない。

Tanaka-kun is not eating his food at all. There is no doubt that he does not like it.

今いまのピンポンは近所きんじょの子供こどものいたずらに違ちがいない。

There's no doubt that the doorbell I just heard must have been a prank by the neighbor's kid.

Fun Fact

As に is marking the preceding word in this expression as an existing location (or state), 違ちがいない is stating that no 'variance' exists within that state. This means that the literal translation of に違ちがいない is close to 'there is no discrepancy in (A)'.

あの人ひとはカメラマンに囲かこまれている、有名人ゆうめいじんに違ちがいない。

That person is surrounded by cameramen. There is no discrepancy in that person being famous. (No reason to disbelieve)

Caution

It should be noted that 違ちがいない is a combination of a noun and an い-Adjective, and not the negative form of the う - Verb 違ちがう, which would be 違ちがわない. 違ちがいない translates as 'there is no difference', while 違ちがわない would translate as 'it is not different'.

## Examples

Examples

00:03

Sentence
Translation

あの顔かおは日本人にほんじんに違ちがいない。

That face... there is no doubt that that person is Japanese.

彼女かのじょは病気びょうきに違ちがいない。

There is no doubt that she is sick.

こんなに遅おそいなんて…、何なにかあったに違ちがいない。

Being so late... there is no doubt that something happened.

あの言いい方かただと、何なにか隠かくしているに違ちがいない。

From the way he speaks, there is no doubt that he is hiding something.

大雪おおゆきが降ふって大変たいへんだったに違ちがいない。

I am sure that it was tough after that heavy snowfall.

あんな転ころび方かたをして、痛いたいに違ちがいない。

Falling in that way, there is no doubt that it hurts.

電話でんわの声こえからいって、２０にじゅう代だいに違ちがいない。

Judging from the voice on the phone, I am sure they were in their twenties.

最近さいきん全然ぜんぜん休やすみがないから、彼かれは疲つかれているに違ちがいない。

He hasn't rested at all recently, so I am sure he is tired.

あの格好かっこうからいって、あの人ひとはお金かね持もちに違ちがいない。

Judging from appearances, I am sure that person is loaded.

あの体からだつきからいって、相当そうとう筋きんトレをしているに違ちがいない。

Judging from his body build, there is no doubt that he is working out a lot.

何回なんかい呼よび鈴りんを押おしても出でてこない。きっと寝ねているに違ちがいない。

No matter how many times I ring the bell, they won't come out. I am sure they are sleeping.

現場げんばで彼かれの髪かみの毛けが見みつかったので、彼かれは犯人はんにんに違ちがいない。

His hair was found at the scene. There is no doubt that he is the offender.

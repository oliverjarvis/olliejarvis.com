## About

About あまり～ない

あまり is a very flexible word in Japanese, that is used in several different grammar points. When paired with the ない form of a word, it translates to 'not very (A)', or 'hardly (A)'. To pair あまり (or the casual form, あんまり) with another word, we just need to place it at the beginning of a phrase, or directly before the ない statement.

今いまの時間じかんだったらあまり並ならばないと思うよ。

At this time, I think we would not have to stand in line for very long.

私わたしには犬いぬがいるからあまり寂さびしくない。

I have a dog, so I don't feel lonely very much.

最近さいきんはどこもあまり平和へいわではない。

Recently, nowhere has been very peaceful.

そこの肉屋にくやの肉にくはあまりいい肉にくではない。

Meat from that butcher over there is hardly good.

Fun Fact
Although あまり～ない is regularly translated as 'not very' the literal translation is closer to 'not excessively (A)'. This is due to 余あまり (the kanji which this word comes from), meaning 'excessive', or 'to be in excess'.

あまり has this meaning of 'excessive' in all of its grammar points, and the nuance will only change based on whether the sentence itself is positive (does not use ない), or negative (uses ない).

## Examples

Examples

Sentence
Translation

このケーキはあまり美味おいしくない。

This cake isn't very delicious.

あまり肉にくを食たべない。

I don't eat very much meat.

あまり大変たいへんなことではないから、心配しんぱいいらないよ。

It is not really a big deal, so you don't have to worry. (isn't that)

寿司すしをあまり食たべたいと思おもわない。

I don't really think I want to eat sushi.

この漢字かんじの意味いみがあまり分わかりません。

I don't really understand the meaning of this kanji.

彼かれと時間じかんを過すごすのはあまり好すきではない。

I don't enjoy spending time with him very much.

彼女かのじょは私わたしの気持きもちをあまり理解りかいしていないと思おもう。

I think that she doesn't really understand how I feel.

最近さいきんは彼かれの事ことをあまり考かんがえていない。

I haven't been thinking about him very much recently.

あまりいい車くるまではないけど、走はしれるからいいんじゃない？

This car is hardly great, but it runs so that's good enough, right? (not that great)

あまり好すきじゃない食たべ物ものもたまに食たべなくてはいけない。

Sometimes you also have to eat food that you don't really enjoy.

説明せつめいしてくれたけど、あまり意味いみが分わからない。

You provided an explanation, but I don't really understand.

先生せんせいの授業じゅぎょうがとても分わかりやすかったけど、テストの点数てんすうがあまり良よくなかった。

The teacher's class was really easy to understand, but my test scores were not very good.

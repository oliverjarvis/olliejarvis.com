## About

About が気になる

English

Japanese

One of the many collocations in Japanese based on 気き 'attention', or 'focus', is 気きになる. This is just a combination of the noun 気き, に, and the う-Verb なる 'to become'. Also, 気になる will come after (A) が, where (A) marks the thing that is drawing the 'attention' or 'focus' of the speaker.

The most common translations for this structure are 'to be interested in (A)', or 'to be concerned about (A)'. This grammar pattern will appear before nouns, and noun-like phrases.

家いえ自体じたいは良いいと思おもうんですけど、やっぱり値段ねだんが気きになります。なんでこんなに安やすいんですか？

I think the house itself is nice, however I am concerned about the price. Why is it so cheap?

私わたしは高橋たかはしさんが気きになる。

I am interested in Takahashi-san.

やっぱりお前まえがさっき言いったことが気きになる。

I am still concerned about what you said earlier.

私わたしは好すきな人ひとの考かんがえていることが気きになる。

I am interested in what the person I like is thinking about.

Caution

Unlike 気きにする, which implies more that the speaker in intentionally putting their 'focus' on (A) due to it being difficult to ignore, the use of なる just highlights that (A) is 'becoming' something that is being focused on naturally, due to either the speakers concern or interest in the result.

私わたしが気きにすることは彼かれが良いい人ひとかどうかだけです。

All I care about is whether he is a good person or not.

私わたしが気きになることは彼かれが良いい人ひとかどうかだけです。

The only thing that I am concerned about is whether he is a good person or not.

I am interested in that movie. I only know the summary of it though.

I am interested in people who wear this perfume. (concerned with)

I am even more interested in the box than the cookies. It is so cute!

新しん商品しょうひんの売行うれゆきが気きになる。

I am concerned with the demand of the new product.

あの人ひとがいつも食たべているおやつが気きになる。

I am concerned about the snacks that that person is always eating.

大好だいすきな選手せんしゅがいつも噛かんでいるガムのメーカーが気きになる。

I am interested in who makes the gum that my favorite athlete always chews.

この足跡あしあとが気きになる。もしかしたら熊くまの足跡あしあとかもしれません。

I am concerned about these tracks. It is possible that they are bear tracks.

友達ともだちの日本語にほんごのアクセントが気きになる。出身しゅっしんはどこなんだろう…

I am interested in my friend's Japanese accent. I wonder where he is from.

最近さいきん発売はつばいされた珍めずらしい魚さかなの缶詰かんづめが気きになっているんです。

I am interested in a canned fish that is rare and was recently put on sale.

あの人ひとの言いい方かたがいちいちきになる。上うえから目線めせんだからだと思おもう。

I am concerned about that person's way of speaking. I think that it is because they are talking from a superior's standpoint.

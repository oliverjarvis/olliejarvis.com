## About

About それに

The conjunction それに is regularly considered to be one word in Japanese, but is more correctly just a combination of the pronoun それ, and the case marking particle に.

The thing that それ is 'substituting' for is the entire clause/sentence that came before it, while に highlights that extra information is about to be added. In this way, it translates to 'and to that', or 'and on that', where 'that' is the previous information. Often, it is translated simply as 'moreover', 'in addition', or 'and'.

それに彼かれは家事かじもしないんでしょう？

In addition, he doesn't do house chores either, right?

リサの会社かいしゃには残業ざんぎょうがない。それに給料きゅうりょうもいい。

Lisa's company doesn't have overtime work. What's more, her salary is good too.

Because それに is 'adding' information with に, the (B) information itself must be something that would logically happen 'in addition to (A)', or 'as a result of (A)'. Therefore, それに will highlight something that has either all positive, or all negative connotations. Never both.

昨日きのう、道みちで五ご千円せんえんを拾ひろって、それに車くるまが抽選ちゅうせんで当あたった！

Yesterday, I picked up 5 thousand yen on the street, and in addition to that I won a car in the lottery. (Natural Japanese, as both events are positive)

最近さいきんは雨あめがいっぱい降ふるし、それに風かぜも強つよいし、外そとに出でれなくて寂さびしい。

Recently, it has been raining a lot, and in addition to that the wind has been strong. I'm sad that I can't go outside. (Natural Japanese, as both events are negative)

今朝けさはマッサージに行いって、それにスピード違反いはんの切符きっぷも切きられた。

I went to get a massage this morning, and moreover I also got a speeding ticket. (Unnatural Japanese, as one event is positive, while the other is negative)

Exercise is fun. In addition, it's good for you.

I work late into the night. In addition, I am up early.

This Japanese-style hotel's food is delicious. Moreover, the surrounding scenery is beautiful.

日本にほんは住すみやすい。それに病院代びょういんだいが安やすい。

It is easy to live in Japan. Moreover medical expenses are reasonable.

山やまの上うえからの景色けしきが綺麗きれい。それに空気くうきも綺麗きれい。

The scenery from atop the mountain is beautiful. In addition the air is clear.

このレストランのデザートはアップルパイ。それにアイスクリームが付ついてる。

This restaurant's dessert is apple pie. In addition, ice cream is included.

Even if you buy a tablet, you wouldn't really use it right? Besides you would do nothing but watch Youtube, right?

あの夫婦ふうふには子供こどもが六ろく人にん居いて、それに犬いぬを四匹よんひきも飼かっています。

That couple has six children, in addition to also having four dogs.

嫁よめさんが車くるまが必要ひつようと言いっているけど、置おく場所ばしょがない。それに電車でんしゃで通かようから要いらないと思おもう。

My wife keeps saying that we need a car, but we don't have anywhere to put it. Moreover we take the train, so I don't think we need one.

犬いぬを飼かいたいが、庭にわがない。それに家いえが狭せまいから可哀想かわいそうだと思おもう。

I want to get a dog, but I don't have a yard. In addition my house is small so I don't think that it would be fair to the dog.

誕生日たんじょうびにギターをもらったけど弾ひき方かたが分わからない。それに習ならいたいと思おもわない。

I got a guitar for my birthday, but I don't know how to play. Moreover I don't want to learn.

コーラを４よん杯はい頼たのんだら、一杯いっぱい無料むりょうです。それにフライドポテトが付ついています。

If you order four colas, you get one free. In addition you get some french fries.

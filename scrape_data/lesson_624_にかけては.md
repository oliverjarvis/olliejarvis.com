## About

About にかけては

English

Japanese

When に is combined with the て-form of the る-Verb 掛かける 'to hang' or 'to suspend' and は, a common phrase meaning 'when it comes to (A), (B)', or 'regarding (A), (B)' is created. (A) にかけては (B) is used to express that within a certain field or domain, (B) is the best, or has a distinct advantage.

This expression will be seen following a noun expressing the 'field' that is being discussed.

大工だいくの技術ぎじゅつにかけては彼かれが一番いちばんだ。

When it comes to carpentry skills, he is the best.

私わたしはゲームは下手へただけど、スポーツにかけては誰だれにも負まけない。

I am bad at video games, but when it comes to sports, I won't lose to anyone.

あのスマホは機能きのうにかけては、他ほかのスマホとは比くらべ物ものにはならないほどいい。

There are no other smartphones that can compare to this one when it comes to features.

Caution

にかけては will always express a positive within (B). Due to this, it will be unnatural to use when highlighting a negative aspect or point.

先輩せんぱいの歌唱力かしょうりょくにかけては、社内しゃないで一番いちばん下手へただ。

My senpai is the worst in the company when it comes to singing. (Unnatural Japanese)

Caution

Be careful not to confuse this grammar point with the にかけて that will appear primarily after nouns that indicate some sort of time-frame within which something occurs.

朝あさから夕方ゆうがたにかけて肉にくをゆっくりと煮込にこんだ。

I slow-cooked the meat from morning to night.

When it comes to playing RPG games, I won't lose to anyone.

格闘かくとうにかけてはリュ◯の右みぎに出でる者ものはいない。

When it comes to hand to hand fighting, there is no one superior to Ry*.

算数さんすうにかけてはあの子こがうちのクラスでトップだと思おもう。

When it comes to math, I think that child is the top in my class.

香水こうすいにかけては彼女かのじょが一番いちばん詳くわしいと思おもう。

When it comes to perfume, I think she is the most well versed.

クーラーの掃除そうじにかけてはあの会社かいしゃが一番いちばんだと思おもいます。

When it comes to cleaning air conditioners, that company is number one, I think.

あのアイドルはダンスでは大たいしたことはないけど、歌うたうことにかけては一流いちりゅうだ。

That idol's dancing skills are nothing special, but when it comes to singing — she is first class.

稽古けいこの量りょうにかけては負まけていませんよ。実力じつりょくはまだまだですが。

When it comes to the amount of practice, I am not falling behind. But I am still a long way from competency.

春子はるこは運転うんてんの腕前うでまえ（スキル）にかけては僕ぼくよりずっと上手じょうずだ。

Regarding Haruko's driving skills, they are far superior to mine.

金魚きんぎょにかけては僕ぼくの叔父おじさんより詳くわしい人ひとに会あったことがない。

When it comes to goldfish, I have never met someone more well versed than my uncle.

君きみは空想くうそうにかけては超ちょう一流いちりゅうだよ。もっと現実げんじつを見みて欲ほしいものだ。

When it comes to daydreaming, you are first rate. I want you to take a look at reality more.

クリーニングの技術ぎじゅつにかけては君きみが我わが社しゃで一番いちばんだと思おもっている。

When it comes to (dry) cleaning techniques, I am thinking that you are the best in our company.

原稿げんこうを書かく速はやさにかけては、あの作家さっかさんにかなう者ものはいないと思おもう。

When it comes to speed of writing a manuscript, I don't think there is anyone who can surpass that author.

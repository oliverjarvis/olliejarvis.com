## About

About てごらん

English

Japanese

てご覧らん is an honorific language expression in Japanese that is used in a similar way to なさい. It is usually translated as 'please try to (A)', where (A) is the verb that ご覧らん is connected to, through the use of the conjunction particle て.

この本ほんを読よんでごらん。この本ほんを読よんだら日本にほんについて色々いろいろと学まなべるよ。

Try reading this book. If you read it, you will learn various things about Japan.

外そとを見みてごらん。虹にじが出でているよ。

Look outside. There is a rainbow.

However, despite being translated as 'please try to (A)', the literal meaning of 覧らん is 'to look', or 'to peruse' (to look at one's own leisure, without needing to rush). Due to this, てごらん can be thought of as being quite similar to てみる 'to try', but with the added emphasis of 'try as you like, when it suits you'.

Caution

As ご覧らん is honorific language, it cannot be used to refer to your own actions, or things that you will 'try'. It will only ever be used to make suggestions to other people about what they should try.

これ食たべてごらん。あなただったら絶対ぜったいに好すきだと思おもうよ。

Try eating this. If it's you, I think you'll definitely like it.

Caution

Sometimes ごらん and なさい will be paired together. This slightly increases the strength of the suggestion, and may be perceived as a very light command. Because of this, ごらんなさい is primarily used by superiors/elders, when requesting that a subordinate/younger person try something.

見みてごらんなさい。こんな酷ひどいことをネットに書かかれたんだよ、酷ひどくない？

Look at this. Someone wrote something this cruel on the internet. Don't you think this is awful?

その景色けしきを見みてごらん。

(Try to) take a look at that scenery.

ニュースを見みてごらん。あなたが映うつっているよ。

(Try to) take a look at the news. You are on TV.

あの蝶々ちょうちょうを捕つかまえてごらんなさい。

Try to catch that butterfly.

手てを貸かしてごらん。手袋てぶくろをはめてあげるから。

(Try to) give me your hands. I will put your gloves on.

もう一度いちどやってごらん。きっとうまくできるよ。

Try it once more. I know you can do it.

怒おこらないから…正直しょうじきに言いってごらんなさい。

I won't get angry... Try telling the truth.

まだ学まなんでいない文法ぶんぽうを勉強べんきょうしてごらん。

Try studying grammar you haven't learned yet.

耳みみを澄すましてごらん。小鳥ことりの歌声うたごえが聞きこえるよ。

Try listening carefully. You'll hear the birds singing.

もっと練習れんしゅうしてごらん。絶対ぜったいできるようになるから。

Try practicing more. You will definitely be able to do it.

触さわってごらん。こんな手触てざわり初はじめてだよ。

Try touching it. This is the first time I have ever felt something like this.

まずは辞書じしょで調しらべてごらん。それでも分わからなかったら質問しつもんしてね。

First, try looking it up in the dictionary. If you still don't understand, ask me.

もう一ひとつの方法ほうほうを試ためしてごらん。うまくいくかもしれないよ。

Try to do it another way. It just might work.

## About

About に至るまで

English

Japanese

至いたる is an う-Verb which can be roughly translated as 'to attain (A)', or 'to reach the climax of (A)'. This verb is seen in several different grammar structures that all basically highlight that some extreme or unexpected extent is being reached. When paired with に and まで, に至いたるまで is often interpreted as 'everything from (A) to (B)'.

このライトノベルは子供こどもから大人おとなに至いたるまで、幅広はばひろい層そうで人気にんきがある。

This light novel is popular with a wide range of audiences, from children to adults.

明日あしたは大阪おおさかから福岡ふくおかに至いたるまで、一日中いちにちじゅう雨あめが降ふるようです。

Apparently it is going to be raining all day tomorrow, from Osaka to Fukuoka.

祖父そふは成人せいじんしてから退職たいしょくに至いたるまで、ずっと同おなじ会社かいしゃで働はたらいてきた。

My grandfather has worked for the same company, from when he became an adult until retirement.

Comparative statements such as から or より frequently come directly after (A), while に至いたるまで will come directly after (B). This marks (B) as the 'extreme' extent. Both (A) and (B) will either be nouns or noun phrases.

The 'to reach a climax' meaning of 至いたる itself can be used as an indicator that grammar points that use this verb all indicate some kind of exceptionally wide range between (A) and (B). Due to this, 至いたるまで can be thought of as simply a strong emphasis of まで by itself, which also marks an extent.

Nowadays, CPUs can be found in various kinds of devices, from computers to toothbrushes, etc.

All citizens are equal under the law, from the prime minister to an ordinary person.

航海こうかいの初はじめから事故じこそのものに至いたるまでの難破船なんぱせんの航海日誌こうかいにっしが見みつかった。

A shipwreck's logbook covering everything from the beginning of the voyage to the accident itself has been found.

In Asia, chopsticks have been used to eat for a long time, from long ago to the present.

When it comes to cars, Takumi Fujihara is a person that knows everything, from rotors to operating a steering wheel.

石川県いしかわけんにある「法師ほうし」旅館りょかんは７１８ななひゃくじゅうはち年ねんから今いまに至いたるまで１３００せんさんびゃく年ねん以上いじょう商あきないし続つづけている。

The Japanese-style inn called 'Houshi' that is located in Ishikawa prefecture has been operating continuously for over 1300 years from 718 right up until now.

この会社かいしゃはスマートフォンの販売はんばいから大学だいがくの経営けいえいに至いたるまで、たくさんのビジネスを運営うんえいしている。

This company is running numerous businesses, from selling smartphones to owning a university.

手足てあしから髪かみの毛け一本いっぽんに至いたるまで身体中しんたいじゅうのすべてが震ふるえていた。そのとき、それくらい怖こわかった。

My whole body was shaking, from my hands and feet to the individual strands of my hair. That's just how scared I was then.

​‎バブル景気けいき崩壊ほうかいにあって、小企業しょうきぎょうから大企業だいきぎょうに至いたるまでいろいろな会社かいしゃが倒産とうさんを余儀よぎなくされている。

Due to the bursting of the bubble economy, various companies were forced into bankruptcy, from small ones to major ones.

この自動車じどうしゃ修理工場しゅうりこうじょうでは、修理しゅうりはもちろん、洗車せんしゃからチューニングに至いたるまで、あなたの車くるまを安全あんぜんにお取とり扱あつかいします。

This auto repair shop will not only make repairs, but will take good care of your car, doing everything from cleaning to tuning it.

国税庁こくぜいちょうに調しらべられるとき、銀行口座ぎんこうこうざのお金かねの額がくから１０じゅう年ねん前まえの金曜きんようの朝あさにお弁当べんとうにいくらで買かったかに至いたるまで何なんでもチェックされる。

When you are investigated by a national tax agency everything is checked, from money in your bank account to how much you spent on a bentou, Friday morning 10 years ago.

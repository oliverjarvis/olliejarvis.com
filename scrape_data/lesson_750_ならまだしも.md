## About

About ならまだしも

English

Japanese

ならまだしも is a combination of なら 'if', まだ 'still', and the adverbial particle しも, which has the primary purpose of strengthening the word that comes before it. When used, this structure will often be translated as '(A) is fine, but not (B)', or '(A) is fine, but it's different when it's (B)'.

ならまだしも is a phrase that usually separates the first and second half of sentences, so will be attached to the end of the (A) phrase, marking the statement that is acceptable.

暑あついだけならまだしも、湿度しつども高たかいからどこにも行いきたくない。

If it was hot, that would be fine, but because it is also humid, I don't want to go.

二に、三日さんにちならまだしも、二週間にしゅうかんなんて待まてませんよ。

If it was 2 or three days, we could wait, but 2 weeks? We can't wait that long.

一回いっかいだけならまだしも、毎日まいにち同おなじことで怒おこられて何なんにも思おもわないの。

If it was only once, okay, but don't you feel any sort of way being told the same thing everyday?

Due to the way that しも alters まだ, a more literal translation of this grammar point can be thought of as 'if it's (A), then of course there's still that, but as for (B)'.

Fun Fact

しも is a classical particle that is not frequently used in modern times outside of set phrases or expressions like ならまだしも, 今いましも 'right this moment', and だれしも 'absolutely anybody'. In all cases, it merely strengthens the word directly before it.

今いましも家いえを出でようとしていたところだった。

I was just about to leave the house.

誰だれしも長年ながねん使つかってきた釣つり竿ざおには愛着あいちゃくがある。

Everyone has an attachment to a fishing rod they have used for many years.

I am sorry, if it is just going out then it is ok, but marriage is out of the question. (is not okay)

すごく恥はずかしい！お父とうさん、ビーチでならまだしも、半裸はんらで外そとに出でるのはやめてほしい！

This is so embarrassing! Papa, if it is a beach, it is ok, but going half naked outside? I want you to stop! (it is not okay)

「ねぇ、歩あるいて行いこうよ。」
「自転車じてんしゃならまだしも、そんな遠とおくまで歩あるいて行いくのは無理むりだよ。」

'C'mon, let's go on foot!'
'If it was on a bicycle, then it would be ok, but it is impossible to go that far on foot!' (is not okay)

隣人りんじんがうちの駐車ちゅうしゃ区域くいきにたまに車くるまを止とめるならまだしも、いつも止とめているから、いい加減かげん腹はらが立たつよ。

If my neighbor uses my parking spot from time to time then it's ok, but because he is always parking there, I get pissed. (it is not okay)

ツイート：「自分じぶんが感染かんせんするだけならまだしも祖父母そふぼや親おやなどが病気びょうきになってしまうのがとても恐おそろしいです。」

Tweet: 'If it is just me becoming infected, then it is ok, but my grandparents or parents becoming ill is really scary.' (is not okay)

ただ寒さむいだけならまだしもこんなに雪ゆきが降ふっているなら外そとでのピクニックは中止ちゅうしか延期えんきにするべきだ。

It's not so bad if it's just cold, but if there's this much snow falling, you ought to cancel or postpone your picnic outside.

一度いちどや二度にどならまだしも三日間みっかかん続つづけて遅刻ちこくをしてきた社員しゃいんには何なにかしらの罰ばつを与あたえよう…。

It's different if it's one or two times, but for an employee that has continued to come in late for three days in a row, let's give them some kind of punishment.

待まち合あわせに１０じゅっ分ぷん遅おくれてくるくらいならまだしも、２に時間じかんも遅刻ちこくして謝あやまらないなんてどうかしているよ！

It is not so bad if it is coming around 10 minutes late for a meeting time, but something like being 2 hours late and not apologizing means they are not right in the head!

ただ風かぜが強つよいだけならまだしも、台風たいふう並なみに強つよいこの風かぜの中なかで飛行機ひこうきが飛とぶことはないでしょう。

It's fine if it is merely just a strong wind, but not when a plane is flying through wind that's strength is in line with that of a typhoon, right? (it is not okay)

３さん人にんや４よ人にんならまだしも、１０じゅう人にんも友達ともだちを家いえに呼よんでパーティーをするなんて、隣人りんじんから文句もんくを言いわれるに違ちがいない。

If it is 3 or 4 people then it is ok, but something like calling 10 or so friends over to your house for a party, there's no mistake that the neighbors will complain. (it is not okay)

カロリーが高たかいだけならまだしも、材料ざいりょうに沢山たくさんの添加物てんかぶつまで入はいっているのならそれを食たべるのは遠慮えんりょしておくよ。

It's not so bad if it is just high calorie, but if it's something where there are even lots of additives in the ingredients, I will hold back from eating it.

友達ともだちに出荷しゅっか遅延ちえんについて苦情くじょうを言いう：「１いっ週間しゅうかんとか２に週間しゅうかんならまだしも、出荷しゅっかまで３ヶ月さんかげつも待まつなんて全まったく受うけ入いれられない！」

Complaining to a friend about a shipment delay: 'If it was one or two weeks, that would be fine, but waiting something like 3 months until the shipment? That is completely unacceptable!' (not okay)

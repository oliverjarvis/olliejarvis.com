## About

About ならともかく

English

Japanese

When なら, the hypothetical form of the auxiliary verb だ is paired with the adverb ともかく 'to set aside', it will often be translated as 'if it is (A), sure, but not in the case of (B)', or more simply 'putting (A) aside, (B)'. This structure usually just indicates that if it were (A), then the result or situation would be different, but because it is not (A), the result or situation is not different (it is as it currently is).

なら is often classified as and functions in the same way as an adverbial particle, meaning that it will primarily be appended to the plain form of nouns, verbs, or adjectives. However, ならともかく itself is used mainly after nouns.

俺おれの妹いもうとならともかく、俺おれはそんなことしねぇ〜よ。

If it's my little sister, sure, but I would never do such a thing.

丈夫じょうぶで何回なんかいも使つかえるものならともかく、なんでわざわざ高たかくて使つかい捨すてみたいなもんを買かうんですか。

If it is tough and can be used multiple times, sure, but why would you go out of your way to buy something that is expensive and basically disposable.

新人しんじんならともかく、君きみみたいなベテランがわからないようじゃ困こまったもんだよ。

I understand if a newbie didn't know, but it is a problem if a long time worker like you didn't know.

For more details about ともかく, please see the fun fact on the はともかく lesson page!

Sentence
Translation

生魚なまざかなならともかく、納豆なっとうは食たべられません。

If it's raw fish, sure, but I can't eat natto.

夜よるならともかく、夏なつは日中にっちゅうは外そとに出でられない。

If it's night, sure, but you can't go outside during the day in summer.

赤あかん坊ぼうならともかく、子供こどもには叱しかる事ことも必要ひつようだ。

If it's a baby, sure, but it's necessary to scold kids.

社長しゃちょうならともかく、平社員ひらしゃいんに決定権けっていけんはありません。

If it's the president, sure, but a rank-and-file staff member has no right to make decisions.

イケメンならともかく、そうでない人ひとはナンパで成功せいこうしないだろう。

If it's a hottie, sure, but a person that isn't would surely not succeed at picking someone up.

得意とくいな科目かもくならともかく、そうでないならテスト勉強べんきょうが必要ひつようだ。

If it's a subject I'm good at, sure, but if it's not, test study will be necessary.

試合しあいに勝かったのならともかく、負まけたのだから反省はんせいしなさい。

If you won the contest, sure, but since you lost, learn from it.

アメリカならともかく、日本にほんでは露出度ろしゅつどの高たかい服ふくは着きられない。

If it was America, sure, but in Japan, you can't wear something with that much exposure.

男性だんせいの方ほうが女性じょせいよりも偉えらい？昭和しょうわならともかく、今いまは令和れいわだ。

Men are higher status than women? If it was Showa, sure, but it's Reiwa now.

日本にほんならともかく、フランスで自分じぶんの荷物にもつから目めを離はなしたら荷物にもつがなくなるだろう。

If it was Japan, sure, but if you take your eyes off your luggage in France, it will probably disappear.

お金持かねもちならともかく、そうでない人ひとは飛行機ひこうきのビジネスクラスには乗のれない。

If you're a rich person, sure, but for people that aren't, you can't ride in business class on a plane.

ただ思おもうだけならともかく、差別的さべつてきな発言はつげんをするのは許ゆるされません。

If it's nothing more than a thought, sure, but it is unforgivable if you blurt out a discriminatory statement.

## About

About はおろか

English

Japanese

は疎おろか is an expression that works similarly to はもちろん 'of course (A), but (B)' in Japanese. 疎おろか itself is a な-Adjective that means 'negligent', or 'alienation'. However, this use of 疎おろか is not seen much in modern Japanese, and the word will mainly be used in set expressions where it doesn't behave exactly as a な-Adjective usually would. Despite this, the meaning of 'alienation', or 'completely removed from' is kept in はおろか, as it simply means 'completely putting aside (A), (B)'.

Common translations include 'let alone (A), (B)', and 'it's needless to say that (A), when (B)'. (B) and (A) will usually be things that fall into the same category, where (B) is considered the far more obvious of the two. Due to this obviousness, (B) will often be immediately followed by the adverbial particle さえ. This shows that (B) is the noteworthy minimum extent.

彼かれは掃除そうじはおろか、料理りょうりさえできない。

He can't even cook, let alone clean.

私わたしはスーパーはおろか、コンビニさえもないど田舎いなかに住すんでいる。

I live in a very rural area where there isn't a convenience store, let alone a supermarket.

父ちちはパソコンはおろか、スマホさえも使つかうことができない。

My father can't use a smartphone, let alone a computer.

Alternatively, さえ may be exchanged with other adverbial particles that fill a similar purpose such a も, すら, or まで. In most cases, は疎おろか sentences tend to have a fairly negative nuance.

「ルームメイトはオムライスは疎おろか、おにぎりさえ作つくれない。」

'My roommate cannot even make onigiri, let alone omurice.'

掃除機そうじきをかけることは疎おろか、彼かれはゴミ拾ひろいさえしない。

Let alone turning on the vacuum cleaner, he won’t even pick up the trash.

パスタは疎おろかお米こめさえ食たべるのをやめていたら痩やせました。

Let alone pasta, when I stopped eating even rice, I lost weight.

貴重品きちょうひんは疎おろか、身みにつけていた服ふくまで奪うばわれてしまった。

Not to mention my valuables, even the clothes off my back were stolen.

今月こんげつの生活せいかつは疎おろか、今日きょうでさえ生いきていけるか分わかりません。

Much less living this month, I am not really even sure if I can live today.

フランス語ごは疎おろか英語えいごも話はなせないのにフランスへ行いこうとしています。

Let alone French, despite not even knowing English, I am planning on going to France.

「最近さいきん、痔じが痛いたみ出だしたなぁ。座すわってるのは疎おろか、歩あるくのも痛いたいぞ。」

'Recently my hemorrhoids have been so painful. It even hurts to walk, much less sit.'

小説しょうせつ： 「敵てきは疎おろか味方みかたからも恐おそれられ、『バーサーカー』と言いわれるようになった。」

Novel: Feared not only by enemies but also by her allies, she came to be called 'berserker'.

彼かれは太ふとりすぎて、座すわっているときは疎おろか寝ねている時ときでさえ呼吸こきゅうが辛つらそうだ。

He is so overweight that, let alone when he is sitting, he struggles to breathe even when he is sleeping.

今いまや都会とかいの夜空よぞらには天あまの川がわは疎おろか、星ほしすら見みえなくなってしまった。

Nowadays even the stars have stopped being visible in the night sky of the city, much less the Milky Way.

彼かれはアクションアドベンチャーの有名ゆうめいな本ほんをシリーズで執筆しっぴつしたが、実じつは祖国そこくは疎おろか、故郷こきょうすら出でたことがないという。

It is said that although he wrote a famous action-adventure series, he has never left his hometown, let alone motherland.

日本語にほんごの文字もじと中国語ちゅうごくごの文字もじの区別くべつは疎おろか、ロシア語ごの文字もじとの区別くべつさえつかない人ひとも少すくなくない。

There are quite a few people who cannot tell the difference between Japanese characters and Russian characters, let alone Japanese Characters and Chinese characters.

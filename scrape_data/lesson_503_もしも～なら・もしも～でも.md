## About

About もしも～なら・もしも～でも

English

Japanese

もしも is an adverb in Japanese that strengthens the base meaning of もし 'a bit'. Therefore, the literal meaning of もしも is 'even a bit'. Despite this, as もし and もしも are almost exclusively paired with sentences that use some sort of assumption, or 'hypothesis' based expression, もしも will often be translated as 'supposing that'.

もしも will be used at the beginning of sentences that include almost any form of hypothesis expression. For example, ば, と, なら, or ても.

もしも、今年ことし中じゅうに引ひっ越こすのならば、僕ぼくが手伝てつだってやるよ。

Supposing that you move within this year, I will help you.

もしも仕事しごとで怪我けがをすると、現場げんばが止とまるので気きをつけてください。

Supposing that you get hurt at work, the site will be shut down, so please be careful.

もしも明日あした休やすみなら、一緒いっしょに博物館はくぶつかんへいかない？

Assuming you have a day off tomorrow, do you want to go to the museum with me?

もしも彼かれがいたとしても、今日きょう中じゅうには終おわらなかっただろう。

Supposing he were even here, don't you think we would not have been able to complete it by today?

Although もしも is considered a stand-alone adverb, it is just a combination of もし, and the adverbial particle も.

Caution

もし and もしも are most often used to express some type of result. This means that (B) will happen if (A) happens, or if (A) is true. However, たとえ is contrastive, and therefore asks 'what the result' of a specific situation may be.

もし、また宿題しゅくだいを忘わすれたら親おやに電話でんわをします。

Assuming you forget your homework again, I will call your parents. (Calling their parents will be the result of forgetting their homework)

たとえ、テストが難むずかしくても最後さいごまで諦あきらめないで頑張がんばってください。

If, for example, the test is hard, don't give up, and do your best until the very end. (If the test is difficult, doing your best is not a result, just one of many choices that could be made)

Sentence
Translation

もしもドラえもんがいたなら、助たすけてもらえるのに。

If Doraemon were here, he would help us.

もしも僕ぼくがドラえもんでも助たすけられない。

Even if I were Doraemon I would be unable to help.

もしも戦たたかうなら囲碁いごがいい。

If we are going to battle, I say we settle it with a game of Go.

もしも可能かのうなら、僕ぼくも仲間なかまに入はいりたい。

If possible, I want to join you.

もしもこれが現実げんじつでも、受うけ入いれられると思おもいます。

Even if this is a hard fact, I think that I can accept it.

もしも子供こどもがいなくても、僕ぼくはたぶん幸しあわせだよ。

I would probably be happy even if I didn't have any children.

もしも私わたしが君きみの立場たちばでも、同おなじことをしたでしょう。

If I was in your place, I would have done the same thing.

もしも明日あした雨あめなら、遠足えんそくは延期えんきになります。

If it rains tomorrow, the trip will be postponed.

もしもこの計画けいかくが実現じつげんしたなら、この街まちはもっと住すみやすくなるでしょう。

If the plan becomes a reality, this town will become much easier to live in.

もしもこの店みせがなくなっても、一生いっしょう忘わすれません。

Even if something happens to this store, I will never forget it, as long as I live.

セレナ：「もしも願ねがいが叶かなうなら、もう一度いちどだけ君きみに会あいたい。」

Serena:'If my dream comes true, I want to see you just one more time.'

シリクス：「もしも生うまれ変かわるなら、その時ときも僕ぼくと結婚けっこんしてくれる？」

Sirix: 'If you were reborn, would you marry me again?'

## About

About とは比べものにならない

English

Japanese

In order to emphasize that something is far beyond what could possibly be compared to (A), the emphatic phrase とは比くらべものにならない will be used.

This is a combination of the case-marking particle と, the adverbial particle は, the neologism 比くらべもの 'a comparable thing', the case-marking particle に, and the negated form of the う-Verb なる 'to become', ならない. This may seem like a lot, but it is just expressing that '(A) will not be a comparable thing'.

This structure will be used before nouns, where that noun is the thing that cannot be compared. This is usually due to quality, quantity, or some other feature that is easily measurable.

Ａエー社しゃの作つくる部品ぶひんはいいが、Ｂビー社しゃの作つくる部品ぶひんとは比くらべ物ものにならない。

Parts made by Company A are good, but they are not comparable to the parts made by Company B.

お母かあさんのスパゲティーとは比くらべものにならないが、ここのシェフが作つくるやつはなかなか美味おいしい。

The spaghetti prepared by the chef here is quite good, but it is nothing compared to the spaghetti my mother makes.

岐阜ぎふは寒さむいが、北海道ほっかいどうとは比くらべものにはならない。

Gifu is cold, but it is nothing compared to Hokkaido.

As this grammar pattern is emphatic, it will indicate that the difference between (A) and (B) is quite large, without specifically expressing the amount. Additionally, like in the final example, we can sometimes see the use of the adverbial particle は a second time before ならない. This just adds further emphasis or highlights contrast.

Fun Fact

As a reminder, a neologism is just a set word or phrase which has come to be used a certain way due to habits or customs. 比くらべもの itself is just a mix of the conjunctive form of the る-Verb 比くらべる 'to compare', and the noun もの 'thing'.

Examples

Sentence
Translation

私わたしの絵画かいがなど須すノ宮みやさんのとは比くらべものにならない。

My paintings are nothing compared to Sunomiya's.

日本にほんの地震じしんの多おおさはオーストラリアとは比くらべものにならない。

The amount of Japan’s earthquakes is beyond comparison to Australia.

光ひかりの速はやさでも、この宇宙うちゅうの大おおきさとは比くらべものにならない。

Even the speed of light is nothing when compared to the size of the universe.

この寿司すしは悪わるくはないが良質りょうしつの寿司すしとは比くらべものにならない。

This sushi is not bad, but it is nothing compared to good quality sushi.

北海道ほっかいどうは寒さむいといえどもシベリアとは比くらべものにならないと思おもう。

Even though Hokkaido is cold, I think it is nothing when compared to Siberia.

私わたしもコスメが好すきですが、コスメマニアの橋本はしもとさんとは比くらべものになりません。

I like cosmetics, but it’s nothing compared to Hashimoto-san, the cosmetic freak.

スマホのパフォーマンスは当時とうじとは比くらべものにならないほど上あがってます。

The performance of smartphones has improved to a degree that is beyond comparison to back then.

確たしかにお金かねに余裕よゆうはある方ほうだが、富豪ふごうたちとは比くらべものにならない。

While it is true that I am on the having-excess-money side of things, it’s nothing compared to the wealthy.

スーパー火山かざんの噴火ふんかの被害ひがいは他たの火山かざんとは比くらべものにならないとされます。

It is considered that the damage from a supervolcano eruption is beyond comparison to other volcanoes.

今日きょうは少すこし贅沢ぜいたくをしましたが、浪費癖ろうひぐせのある祖母そぼとは比くらべものになりません。

I was a bit extravagant today, but it’s nothing compared to my grandma who has wasteful habits.

上手じょうずにピアノを弾ひくことができたが、プロの演奏えんそうを聴きいてプロとは比くらべものにならないと思おもい知しった。

I can play the piano well, but after hearing a pro play, I realized that I am nothing compared to them.

お父とうさん：「もちろんこのウイスキーも美味おいしいけど、やっぱりあの高級こうきゅうなやつとは比くらべものにならないな〜。」

Father: ‘Of course, this whiskey is tasty, but yeah, it’s nothing compared to that high-class one.’

自分じぶんで作つくるご飯はんもいいけど、誰だれかに作つくってもらうご飯はんとは比くらべものになりません。

Making your own food is good, but it doesn’t compare to the food that you have someone make for you.

## About

About 確かに

English

Japanese

確たしかに is the adverbial use of the な-Adjective 確たしか, meaning 'to be certain', or 'to be assured'. It may be used in the same way as 'certainly' or 'surely' in English, especially when the speaker wants to highlight (A) is definitely the case.

確たしかに彼かれはイケメンですが、マナーが悪わるいです。

He is certainly handsome, but he has bad manners.

確たしかにさっきのは言いい過すぎだった。ごめん。

What I said earlier was certainly out of line. I'm sorry.

Caution

When translated as 'certainly', 確たしかに is only used agreeing, and cannot be used for giving permission like 'certainly' in English.

確たしかに結構けっこう難むずかしいね。ちょっと甘あまく見みてたわ。

This is certainly difficult. I underestimated it. (Natural Japanese)

確たしかに！宿題しゅくだいが終おわってるならゲームをしてもいいよ。

Certainly! You may play video games if you finish your homework. (Unnatural Japanese)

Caution

When the に is removed, 確たしか is often used in a similar way to 'if I remember correctly', 'if I'm not mistaken', or 'now that you mention it'.

あの人ひとは確たしかたくやくんのおじさんだったような気きがする。

If I recall correctly, I feel like that person was Takuya-kun's uncle.

そう言いえば、前まえいくら借かりたっけ？確たしか３さん千円ぜんえんだったよね？

By the way, how much money did I borrow the other day? If I remember correctly, it was three-thousand yen, correct?

確たしかに彼かれは信頼しんらいできます。

He certainly is reliable.

確たしかにその服ふくはあなたに似合にあう。

Those clothes certainly look good on you.

みんな焦あせっているようだ。確たしかに期限きげんが迫せまっている。

It appears that everyone is in a hurry. The deadline is certainly approaching.

学校がっこうの規則きそく上じょう、確たしかに生徒せいとは先生せんせいに従したがうべきだ。

From the standpoint of the school's rules, students should certainly obey their teacher.

彼かれのうちは音楽おんがく一家いっからしいが、確たしかに楽器がっきがうまい。

I heard he is from a musical household, but he is certainly skilled with an instrument.

移籍金いせききんはかなりの額がくだったが、確たしかに活躍かつやくしている。

The fee for transferring (to another team) was a considerable amount, but (he) is certainly actively participating.

脂あぶらは控ひかえるよう言いわれています。確たしかにカロリーが高たかいですからね。

It is said that you should avoid fat. Since it is certainly high in calories.

彼かれには愛嬌あいきょうがあって、確たしかに可愛かわいがられている。

He has charm, surely he is loved.

驚おどろきすぎてキョトンとしてしまったが、確たしかに感激かんげきしました。

It was such a surprise that he blanked, but surely he was impressed.

みんな「目めが乾かわく」と言いっていましたが、確たしかに乾燥かんそうしていますね。

Everyone said, 'your eyes will get dry,' but they certainly are, aren't they.

この箱はこは重おもたい。何なにが入はいっているかは分わからないが、確たしかにぎっしり詰つまっている。

This box is heavy. I don't know what is in it, but it certainly is tightly packed.

仕事しごとは山積やまづみだが、確たしかに休息きゅうそくは重要じゅうようだ。

I have heaps of work, but relaxation certainly is important.

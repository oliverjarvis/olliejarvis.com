## About

About のももっともだ

English

Japanese

のももっともだ is a combination of の, も, the な-Adjective もっとも, meaning 'reasonable', or 'plausible', and the auxiliary verb だ. In many cases, the interpretation will be 'it may well be (A)', 'it's only natural that (A)', or 'there's no wonder that (A)'. However, 尤もっとも itself is simply an adjective that affirms that the speaker understands the thought process behind (A), and that it is logical to them, regardless of whether or not they actually agree with it themselves. Due to this, 'it may well be (A)' in English is actually a very close translation of the feeling behind the expression.

のももっともだ will appear with verbs, い-Adjectives, な-Adjectives, and nouns. In the case of nouns, the の will be dropped, as the noun itself will become the expression being marked as the focus.

そんなことを彼女かのじょに言いったのか？彼女かのじょが怒おこるのももっともだ。

You said something like that to your girlfriend? She has every right to be angry.

彼かれはいつも夜よる遅おそくまで残業ざんぎょうをしているから、いつも眠ねむいのももっともだ。

He always works late at night, so it's no wonder he's always sleepy.

彼かれは高校生こうこうせいの頃ころから毎日まいにち日本語にほんごを勉強べんきょうをしてきた。上手じょうずなのももっともだ。

He has studied Japanese every day since high school. It is no wonder he is so good at it.

親おやの反対はんたいももっともだが、俺おれはもう俺おれのやり方かたでやると決きめたから親おやの意見いけんに合あわすつもりはない。

My parents have a good reason to object, but I've already decided to do things my way, so I'm not going to conform to my parents' views.

Due to のももっともだ being used when the speaker is expressing that they understand the underlying reasons behind something, phrases used for explanation such as から or ので will often be seen in the same sentence.

## Examples

Examples

00:05

Sentence
Translation

状況じょうきょうからして、彼かれらがそう考かんがえたのも尤もっともだ。

Given the situation, no wonder they thought so.

あの本ほんがいつも貸かし出だし中ちゅうなのも尤もっともだ。大だいベストセラーだから。

There is good reason that that book is always unavailable (for rent). It is a huge best seller.

ＳＮＳエスエヌエスの投稿とうこう回数かいすうを減へらしたのも尤もっともだ。彼女かのじょは忙いそがしすぎる。

It is only natural that number of SNS messages she sends has decreased. She is much too busy.

会社かいしゃの規約きやくを改正かいせいするのも尤もっともだ。あの規約きやくは古ふるすぎる。

No wonder the company is revising their code of conduct. Those rules are too old.

解説かいせつ者しゃが言いっているのも尤もっともだ。かなり的確てきかくな意見いけんだと思おもいます。

There is good reason that the commentator is saying (those things). It is quite an appropriate viewpoint.

二人ふたりの意見いけんが食くい違ちがうのも尤もっともだ。性格せいかくが違ちがいすぎる。

No wonder their opinions clash. Those two have very different personalities.

彼かれが自信じしん過剰かじょうになるのも尤もっともだ。実力じつりょくがずば抜ぬけている。

It is only natural that he is presumptuous. He has outstanding ability.

彼女かのじょの怒いかりは尤もっともだ。オレがお気きに入いりの花瓶かびんを壊こわしちゃったから。

There is good reason for her anger. It is because I broke her favorite vase.

彼かれが海水浴かいすいよくが嫌きらいなのも尤もっともだ。昔むかし海うみで溺おぼれかけたことがあるから。

There is good reason for him to hate swimming in the ocean. He nearly drowned some time ago.

彼女かのじょが活躍かつやくするのも尤もっともだ。才能さいのうがあり、しかも誰だれよりも努力どりょくをしている。

No wonder she is flourishing. She is a genius. What's more she puts forth more effort than anyone else.

うちの学校がっこうの生徒せいとの学力がくりょくが下さがったのも尤もっともだ。環境かんきょうが悪わるすぎる。

No wonder my school's students' literary ability has decreased. The surroundings are absolutely terrible.

彼かれの作つくった料理りょうりが美味おいしいのも尤もっともだ。十歳じゅっさいから一人ひとりで料理りょうりをしてきたやつだけのことはある。

It is only natural that the dish he made is tasty. This can be expected from a guy who has been cooking by himself since he was 10.

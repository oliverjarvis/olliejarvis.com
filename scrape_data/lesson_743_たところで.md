## About

About たところで

English

Japanese

～たところで is a grammar construction that combines the simple-past form of a verb, the noun ところ 'place', and the case-marking particle で.

As with most other grammar patterns that ところ is seen with, the focus is on the situation of the verb occurring. ～たところで is often translated as 'even if (A), not (B)', or 'even if (A), it won't be (B)'.

This 'even if' nuance is created by the fact that ～たところで sentences will never have something in the past tense in the second half of the sentence, despite using the past tense before ところ. This is similar to the English 'even if (A) were to happen, (B) would still not occur'.

今更いまさら勉強べんきょうを始はじめたところで、合格ごうかくはできないだろう。

Even if I were to start studying now, I would not be able to pass.

もう電車でんしゃは発車はっしゃしてしまったから、急いそいだところで間まに合あわないよ。

The train has already left, so even if you hurry, you won't make it.

少すこし走はしったところで、すぐには痩やせないよ。

Just merely by running, you won't lose weight that quickly.

As can be seen by the examples above, ～たところで will convey things that are counter to what the speaker either wants to, or wishes would happen. Due to this, the (B) half of the sentence will almost always be something unfavorable.

Fun Fact

Due to ～たところで always marking some kind of negative result in (B), (A) will often be preceded by words such as 今いまさら, いくら, or どんなに. These usually just emphasize the frustration that the speaker feels about the inevitable result.

Even if I confessed my love, she wouldn't respond.

そんなにお金かねを稼かせいだところで使つかい切きれないでしょうから、募金ぼきんしてみてはどうですか？

Even if you made that much money, since you surely wouldn't be able to use it all, why don't you try donating them?

すみませんが、今いま注文ちゅうもんをキャンセルしたところで、お金かねは返金へんきんできませんよ。

I am sorry, even if you cancel the order now, we cannot refund your money.

ちょっとやそっと妻つまに優やさしくしたところで妻つまが機嫌きげんを直なおしてくれるとは思おもわない。

Just merely by going out of your way for your wife, I don't think that she'll cheer up.

五分ごふんの運動うんどうを三日間みっかかん続つづけたところで直すぐに成果せいかが見みられるとは限かぎりません。

Even if you continued to exercise for 5 minutes daily for three days, it is not certain that any positive results would immediately be visible.

今更いまさら焦あせって勉強べんきょうを始はじめたところであなたが志望しぼうする大学だいがくに受うかる見込みこみはないだろう。

Even if you started studying in a rush at this late point, you probably have no chance of being accepted by the university that you're hoping for, right?

冷房れいぼうの設定せってい温度おんどを一度いちど上あげたところで節約せつやくできる電力でんりょくはあまり多おおくないが、そのような小ちいさな積つみ重かさねが大事だいじなのである。

Even if you raise the temperature setting of the air-con one degree, you won't be able to economize much on electricity output, but even small accumulations like that are important.

真犯人しんはんにんを追及ついきゅうしたところで遺族いぞくの悲かなしみが消きえることはありませんが、刑事けいじとして何なんとしてでも真犯人しんはんにんを見みつける所存しょぞんであります。

It is not like the bereaved family's sadness will disappear even if the true culprit is sought out, but as a detective, I have the intention of doing whatever it takes to find them.

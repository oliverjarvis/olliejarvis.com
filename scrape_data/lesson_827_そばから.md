## About

About そばから

English

Japanese

そばから is a combination of the noun 側そば 'side', and the case marking particle から 'from'. It illustrates something that happens immediately upon (A), and can be translated as 'as soon as (A), (B)', or 'right after (A), (B)'.

(B) is usually something that is considered annoying, unfavorable, or somehow nullifies (A) by the speaker, but there are exceptions. If the more literal translation of 'from right beside (A), (B)' helps to remember the nuance of (B) happening straight away, then that may be a helpful way to memorize this construction.

そばから will follow verbs in either their dictionary or past-tense forms.

彼女かのじょは約束やくそくをするそばからすぐ約束やくそくを破やぶる。

She breaks her promises as soon as she makes them.

彼かれは注意ちゅういするそばから失敗しっぱいをするから、私わたしのいうことは聞きいていないんだろう。

I guess he doesn't listen to me because he makes mistakes as soon as I warn him.

パソコンを直なおしたそばからまた壊こわれてしまうから困こまっている。

I'm having trouble with my computer because it breaks down just as soon as I fix it.

早はやく読よみすぎると読よんだそばから忘わすれるから、もっとゆっくり読よむ様ようにしている。

I try to read more slowly, because if I read too quickly, I forget as soon as I read it.

Regardless of whether the dictionary or the past-tense form of a verb is used, as そばから means 'right after (A)', the meaning will not change at all.

Caution

そばから will almost always imply that (B) is something that always happens upon (A), rather than being a once-off situation.

車くるまの洗車せんしゃを終おえるそばから、雨あめが降ふってきた。

It started to rain as soon as I was about to finish washing my car.

My bike breaks as soon as I repair it.

あなたって人ひとは、「やるな」と言いったそばからやるんだから。

People like you are just… As soon as I told you not to do it, you still do it…

教おそわったそばから同おなじ間違まちがいをおかした。

I made the same mistake as soon as I learned of it.

ケーキが熱あついから触さわらないように言ったそばから こいつは～。

As soon as I told him not to touch the cake because it was hot, this guy...

新人しんじん育成いくせいがつらい。彼かれらは言いったそばからどんどん忘わすれてしまう。

It's hard to train new employees. As soon as I tell them something, they immediately forget it.

夫おっとはダイエットをすると言いったそばからポテトチップスを食たべ始はじめたが、それはいつもの事ことである。

My husband started eating chips as soon as he said that he will start a diet, but that's the same as always.

バイト代だいが入はいったそばから全すべて使つかってしまい、後あとになってその事ことを後悔こうかいし始はじめた。

As soon as I received the wages for my part time job, I used the whole lot and began to regret it afterwards.

「喧嘩けんかをやめなさい」と息子むすこたちを叱しかったそばから喧嘩けんかをするので、諦あきらめる事ことにしました。

Since my sons will start arguing as soon as I scold them with 'stop fighting', I decided to give up.

聞きいたそばから忘わすれてしまうなら、忘わすれないようにメモを取とるようにしたらどうですか？

If you forget something as soon as you hear it, how about making sure to take a note in order not to forget it?

模試もしが終おわったそばから次つぎの模試もしの対策たいさくを始はじめるなんて、あなたは学生がくせいの鑑かがみですね！

Something like making preparations for the next practice exam as soon as you've finished one, you're really the specimen of a student!

認知症にんちしょうを患わずらっている叔母おばはご飯はんを食たべたそばから忘わすれてしまうので、一日いちにちに何回なんかいもご飯はんを要求ようきゅうしてきます。

Since my auntie, who suffers from dementia, forgets as soon as she has eaten, she comes requesting food many times a day.

メリーさん：「夢ゆめを覚おぼえている？」
ゆめじさん：「夢ゆめなんて起おきたそばから忘わすれてしまうものだけど、夜や中ちゅうに起おきてまた眠ねむると思おもい出だす。」

Merry: 'Do you remember your dreams?'
Yumeji: 'Dreams are something I forget as soon as I wake up, but if I wake up in the middle of the night and go back to sleep then I remember.'

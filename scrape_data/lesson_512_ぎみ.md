## About

About ぎみ

English

Japanese

Coming from the noun 気味きみ meaning 'sensation', ぎみ is a suffix that is used to convey weak signs of (A). ぎみ may be used with nouns, or the conjunctive form of verbs. Often, ぎみ is translated as 'looking (A)', 'a touch of (A)', 'feeling a little (A)', or 'slightly (A)'.

最近さいきん太ふとり気味ぎみだから、ダイエットしなきゃ。

I feel slightly fat, so I should go on a diet.

今週こんしゅうは色々いろいろと忙いそがしかったから疲つかれ気味ぎみだ。

Because I have been busy this week with various things, I am feeling a little tired.

今朝けさは風邪かぜ気味ぎみだったので、学校がっこうを休やすんだ。

Because I felt a little sick this morning, I took a day off of school.

ぎみ refers to the senses of the person who is experiencing them, and therefore may be used to refer to feelings about other people/things, or feelings about oneself.

現場げんばの人ひとがほとんどバテ気味ぎみだったので、作業さぎょうを中断ちゅうだんした。

Most of the people on the site seemed a little worn out, so we stopped work.

電車でんしゃが遅おくれ気味ぎみだから、待まち合あわせ時間じかんまで間まに合あわないかも。

Because the train seems a bit late, I might not be able to make it by our agreed time.

風邪かぜ気味ぎみなのかな？身体からだがだるい。

I feel like I have a cold, my body feels heavy.

As ぎみ expresses 'weak signs of (A)', it is often used to soften a statement, so that the speaker does not appear overconfident.

タカシ君くん疲つかれ気味ぎみじゃない？ちゃんと休憩きゅうけい取とった？

Takashi-kun, you look a bit tired, did you take a proper break?

Caution

Although it comes from 気味きみ, ぎみ will always be said with a ぎ, and not a き. This is a sound change used to distinguish the noun and the suffix.

Sentence
Translation

彼女かのじょは風邪かぜ気味ぎみだと言いった。

She said she was feeling a little sick.

最近さいきん、彼女かのじょは太ふとり気味ぎみらしい。

I heard that lately, she is a bit plump.

貧血ひんけつ気味ぎみなので、採血さいけつはやめておきましょう。

Since you are looking somewhat anemic, let's not draw any blood.

暗くらいニュースばかりで食傷しょくしょう気味ぎみだ。

I feel sick listening to nothing but negative news.

台風たいふうのせいで天気てんきが荒あれ気味ぎみだ。

The weather is a bit stormy due to the typhoon.

暑あつすぎて夏なつバテ気味ぎみだ。

It's so hot that I am feeling a bit of heat fatigue.

あれからずっと経済けいざいが停滞ていたい気味ぎみだ。

The economy has been feeling a bit sluggish ever since (then).

株価かぶかが下降かこう気味ぎみなのを心配しんぱいしている。

I am concerned about the stock prices looking like they will go down.

最近さいきん疲つかれ気味ぎみだから、少すこし休やすんだ方ほうがいい。

You should rest for a bit, because recently you are looking a little tired.

プロジェクトが遅おくれ気味ぎみだから手伝てつだってもらえない？

We are a bit late with the project, so could you give us a hand?

私わたしは発表はっぴょう前まえに緊張きんちょう気味ぎみで倒たおれてしまった。

I was feeling a little nervous before my presentation and fainted.

調子ちょうしは上あがり気味ぎみだから、次つぎの試合しあいはきっと大丈夫だいじょうぶだと思おもいます。

I think that you should be fine for the next game, because you are looking better.

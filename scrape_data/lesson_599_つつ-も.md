## About

About つつ(も)

English

Japanese

In addition to the base meaning of つつ 'while doing (A)' when used by itself, the addition of も simply adds the nuance of 'even'. This is often translated as 'even while doing (A), (B)', or 'although doing (A), (B)'. (B) will almost always be something that is somehow contradictory to (A).

Like つつ, つつも will appear after the ます-stem of a verb that is the original ongoing action.

一人ひとりで洞窟どうくつに入はいるのは危あぶないと知しりつつも、入はいってしまった。

Despite knowing that going in a cave alone is dangerous, I went in.

初はじめてのライブで緊張きんちょうしつつもものすごく楽たのしめました。

Despite being very nervous at my first concert, I was able to enjoy it.

Caution

Like つつ, つつも will usually include a non-physical action in (A), such as reading, listening, looking, thinking, etc. (B) may be either physical or non-physical. Also, in the same way as つつ is similar to ながら, つつも is similar to ながらも.

彼かれは足あしを骨折こっせつしていながらも、大会たいかいに出場しゅつじょうしようとしたところ、監督かんとくに止とめられた。

Despite having a broken leg, he tried to enter the tournament, but was stopped by the coach. (Natural Japanese)

彼かれは足あしを骨折こっせつしていつつも、大会たいかいに出場しゅつじょうしようとしたところ、監督かんとくに止とめられた。

Even while having a broken leg, he tried to enter the tournament, but was stopped by the coach. (Physical action, unnatural Japanese)

なんかこの話はなしは怪あやしいなと思おもいながらも、彼かれのことを信しんじてみた。

Despite thinking, 'this sounds fishy,' I believed him. (Natural Japanese)

なんかこの話はなしは怪あやしいなと思おもいつつも、彼かれのことを信しんじてみた。

Even though I thought that the story was fishy, I believed him. (Non-physical, natural Japanese)

Despite the similarities, it will be important to remember that つつ only appears with verbs, while ながら can be used after almost any type of word.

斜ななめになりつつもなんとか持もちこたえたジェンガ。

Despite becoming slanted, the Jenga tower somehow held out.

上うえを狙ねらいつつも、まるで狙ねらっていないかのような素振すぶりをしています。

Despite aiming up, he is swinging like he isn't even aiming.

南極点なんきょくてん遠征えんせいはよく備そなえつつも、失敗しっぱいした。

Despite (everyone) preparing well, the south pole expedition failed.

車庫しゃこのシャッターを開あけつつも、まだ行いこうか行いくまいか迷まよっていた。

Despite opening the garage door, I was still debating whether or not to go.

船ふねは、沈しずみそうになりつつも、なんとか港みなとまで帰かえって来きた。

Even though the ship was on the verge of sinking, it somehow made it to the harbor.

主人公しゅじんこうはこの地下ちか牢ろうに入はいるのは危あぶないと思おもいつつも、また入はいった。

Despite thinking that entering the dungeon was dangerous, the protagonist still went inside.

臭においが気きになりつつも、汚よごれを放置ほうちしたら大変たいへんなことになった。

Despite being bothered by the smell, I left the dirty thing as it was and it got worse.

弱点じゃくてんを知しりつつほったらかしにしたからこんな悲惨ひさんなことになったんだ。

It became this sort of disaster because we/they neglected it despite knowing its weaknesses.

実力じつりょく不足ぶそくを実感じっかんしつつも、未いまだにプロを諦あきらめられません。

Despite feeling that I lack the ability, I will still not give up being a professional.

彼かれの言いうことは怪あやしいと思おもいつつもとりあえず従したがうことにした。

Even while I think what he says is suspicious, for the time being I have decided to do as he says. (I am thinking)

現代人げんだいじんは朝あさご飯はんを食たべるのは重要じゅうようだと知しりつつも、そうしようとすることを拒否きょひしている。

Modern people, despite knowing that eating breakfast is important, refuse to do so.

危あぶないことだと知しりつつも、科学者かがくしゃは知識ちしきのために、命いのちを掛かけつつ実験じっけんする場合ばあいもある。

Despite knowing that it is dangerous, there are cases when scientists, for the sake of knowledge, carry out experiments while putting their lives on the line.

## About

About 一方だ

English

Japanese

一方いっぽう is a noun that is often seen after verbs, in order to convey the meaning of 'more and more (A)', 'continuing to (A)', or 'getting (A)er and (A)er'.

一方いっぽう will be paired with the auxiliary verb だ (at the end of sentences), or で, だ's conjunctive form when it will be followed by some sort of explanation.

アメリカとロシアの関係かんけいは悪化あっかする一方いっぽうだ。

The relationship between the US and Russia keeps on getting worse.

仕事しごとの量りょうが増ふえる一方いっぽうで、休やすみが取とれない。

Because the amount of work keeps on increasing, I can't take a day off.

As the literal meaning of 一方いっぽう is 'single direction', it may be used with things that are increasing, decreasing, intensifying, or weakening, so long as the shift is all in a single direction.

Caution

一方いっぽう may only be used with verbs that express some kind of change, as 一方いっぽう itself does not express change, just a continuing direction.

高齢化こうれいかが進すすんで、人口じんこうが減へる一方いっぽうだ。

Because of the aging society, the population continues to decline. (Natural)

子供こどもが怪我けがをして泣なく一方いっぽうだ。

The child cries in one direction because he got injured. (Unnatural)

## Examples

Examples

00:03

Sentence
Translation

災害さいがいは増ふえる一方いっぽうです。

More and more disasters happen.

会社かいしゃの財政ざいせいは厳きびしくなる一方いっぽうだ。

The financial situation of the company continues to get more and more strict.

うちの町まちの人口じんこうは減へる一方いっぽうだ。

The population of my town keeps on decreasing.

このままだと借金しゃっきんが増ふえる一方いっぽうだ。

If I keep going on like this, my debts will continue to increase.

残念ざんねんながらこんな事件じけんが増ふえる一方いっぽうだ。

Unfortunately, there are more and more cases like these.

こんなに頑張がんばっているのに、評価ひょうかは下さがる一方いっぽうだ。

Although we are doing our best, the value (of our company) continues to drop.

出費しゅっぴがかさむ一方いっぽうで、全然ぜんぜんお金かねが貯たまらない。

I can't save any money, expenses keep on piling up.

上昇じょうしょうし続つづける物価ぶっかのせいで生活せいかつは苦くるしくなる一方いっぽうだ。

Due to the continuous growth of prices, life keeps on getting harder and harder.

皆みんなが何なにもしようとしないから、地球ちきゅう温暖化おんだんかが悪わるくなる一方いっぽうだ。

Because no one is doing anything about it, global warming is continuing to get worse.

景気けいきは悪わるくなる一方いっぽうだから、ボーナスは出でないかもしれないですよ。

We might not get a bonus because the market keeps getting worse and worse.

彼かれの怒いかりは募つのる一方いっぽうだから、少すこしそっとしておいた方ほうがいいですね。

I think you should leave him alone because he is getting more and more angry.

親おやの病気びょうきは悪化あっかする一方いっぽうだから、回復かいふくする見み込こみはないのかもしれない。

My parent's disease continues to get worse, there might not be any hope of recovery.

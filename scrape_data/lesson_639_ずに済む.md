## About

About ずに済む

English

Japanese

ずに済すむ is a grammar construction used in Japanese to indicate that one can 'get away without doing (A)', or 'it's no problem without (A)'. This structure is just a mix of ず, a literary auxiliary verb that attaches to verbs in the same way as ない (and has the same meaning), に, and 済すむ, a う-Verb meaning 'to come to pass', or 'to end up'. Literally, it can be thought of as meaning 'it will work out without (A)'.

As mentioned, ず connects to verbs in the same way as ない, so this structure will be seen after the verbs that they are negating.

今いま宿題しゅくだいをやっておけば後あとでやらずに済すむから、今いまのうちにやっておこう。

If I do my homework now, I can get by without doing it later, so I shall do it right now.

このＶＩＰブイアイピーパスを使つかえば待またずに済すみます。

If you have this VIP pass, you can get by without waiting.

As the primary element in this grammar point that controls the meaning is 済すむ, other negations such as ないですむ, or なしですむ may also be seen. 無なしで functions as a suffix, so may be used directly after nouns, unlike ないで and ずに.

今月こんげつ契約けいやくすると初期しょき費用ひようを支払しはらわないで済すむので、今月こんげつ中ちゅうに契約けいやくすることをお勧すすめします。

We recommend that you sign the contract within this month so that you can get by without paying the initial cost.

今年ことしの冬ふゆは去年きょねんより暖あたたかかったので、ヒーターなしで済すみました。

Since winter this year was warmer than last year, I got by without using the heater.

In most cases, (A) will be something that is thought of as unpleasant or bothersome, therefore highlighting the speaker's relief that it can be avoided.

## Examples

Examples

00:04

Sentence
Translation

洗あらい物ものをせずに済すむ。

To get away with not doing the dishes.

事故じこが起おこったけど、怪我けがをせずに済すんだ。

An accident occurred, but I got away without getting injured.

犯人はんにんは警察けいさつから、捕つかまらずに済すんだ。

The criminal got away without being arrested by the police.

基礎きそからトレーニングを積つんでいたから、潰つぶれずに済すんだ。

Because I built my training up from the foundation, I got by without being crushed.

ちゃんとペース配分はいぶんしたから、体力たいりょくを消耗しょうもうせずに済すんだ。

Because I properly paced myself, I got by without exhausting my physical strength.

運転士うんてんしの巧たくみな技術ぎじゅつのおかげで脱線だっせんせずに済すんだ。

Thanks to the clever skill of the conductor, they got by without derailing.

芸能人げいのうじんがツイートしてくれたから、宣伝費せんでんひを使つかわずに済すんだ。

Because an entertainer tweeted for me, I got by without having to use advertising expenses.

知人ちじんの紹介しょうかいで入社にゅうしゃできたので、テストは受うけずに済すんだ。

Since I was able to join the company through the introduction of a friend, I got by without taking a test.

長女ちょうじょの的確てきかくな意見いけんのおかげで、中途半端ちゅうとはんぱな決定けっていをせずに済すみました。

Thanks to the accurate opinion of the oldest daughter, we got by without settling a half-baked decision.

あの通訳つうやくさんのおかげで、海外かいがいでの仕事しごとで失敗しっぱいせずに済すみました。

Thanks to that interpreter, I got by without failing overseas.

着々ちゃくちゃくと計画けいかく通どおり進すすんだので、口くちを出ださずに済すんだ。

Since everything progressed steadily according to the plan, we got by without having to say anything.

君きみが予定よていを合あわせてくれたら、みんなのスケジュールを変更へんこうせずに済すむんだけど...

If you would match up your plans with everyone else's, then everyone can get by without having to change their schedule.

## About

About それとも

English

Japanese

それとも is a conjunction in Japanese that may be translated as 'or', or 'otherwise'. It is used to highlight alternative options or choices between (A) and (B). それとも is a combination of それ, and the particles と, and も. This grammar structure will only be used when the options are limited to either (A), or (B).

それとも will appear either after a comma, or at the beginning of new sentences.

今日きょうは文法ぶんぽうの勉強べんきょうをしますか。それとも漢字かんじの勉強べんきょうをしますか。

Do you want to study grammar today? Or rather, do you want to study Kanji?

来週らいしゅうはどこに行いきたい？動物園どうぶつえんに行いく？それとも家いえでゆっくりする？

Where do you want to go next week? Do you want to go to the zoo? Or rather do you want to relax at home?

今日きょうは海うみに行いこうか、それとも川かわに行いこうか、迷まような。

I don't know whether to go to the ocean or the river today.

In many cases, (A) and (B) will be opposites of each other. However, this is not a requirement for using それとも.

## Examples

Examples

00:04

Sentence
Translation

クレジットカードで払はらう？それとも現金げんきんで？

Are you paying with a credit card? Or rather, in cash?

苦情くじょうを言いうべきか、それとも我慢がまんすべきか。

Should we complain? Or rather, should we persevere?

今夜こんやはカラオケに行いく？それとも映画館えいがかん？

Are you going to karaoke tonight? Or rather, to the cinema?

あなたの大学だいがくは国立こくりつ？それとも私立しりつ？

Is your university a national one, or rather a private one?

生いきるべきか、それとも死しぬべきか。それが問題もんだいだ。

To live, or to die. That is the question.

紺こん？それとも黒くろ？どちらのスーツがいいと思おもいますか？

Dark blue, or rather black? Which suit do you think is good?

競馬けいばそれとも競輪けいりん？今日きょうはどちらに行いくんですか？

Horse racing, or rather bicycle racing? Which are you going to today?

白しろ胡椒こしょう？それとも黒くろ胡椒こしょう？どちらがいいですか？

White pepper? Or rather, black pepper? Which is good?

継続けいぞくすべきか、それとも心こころを鬼おににして打うち切きるべきか…

Should I continue? Or rather, should I harden my heart and abort.

自分じぶんで洗あらおうか、それともクリーニングに出だそうか迷まようところです。

I am hesitant. Shall I wash it myself? Or rather, shall I send it off to the dry cleaners?

敬語けいごを使つかうべきか、それともタメ口ぐちで親したしさをアピールすべきか。

Should I use keigo? Or rather, should I appeal to their intimate side and speak frankly?

少すこし風邪かぜ気味ぎみだ。稽古けいこをすべきか、それとも休やすむべきか、微妙びみょうだ。

I am feeling a little sick. It is questionable whether I should practice, or (rather) should I take it easy.

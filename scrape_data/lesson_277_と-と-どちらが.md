## About

About と～と、どちらが

と～と、どちらが is an expression in Japanese that is made from the following parts:

と - The particle (x2) in its meaning of 'and'.

どちら - The polite speech form of 誰だれ 'who', and a slightly newer form of どっち that basically means 'which'.

が - The case marking particle.

This structure is used when the speaker is asking a question about whether someone will choose (A) or (B). It is only used when there are no more than two possible choices.

ピザとラーメンとどちらが好すきですか。

Between pizza and ramen, which do you prefer?

漢字かんじを書かくことと漢字かんじを読よむこととどちらが得意とくいですか。

Between writing and reading kanji, which are you more confident with?

遊園地ゆうえんちに行いくのと動物園どうぶつえんに行いくのとどちらがいいですか。

Between going to the amusement park, and going to the zoo, which is better?

As can be seen above, verbs may be used as part of this structure if they are nominalized with こと, or の. Additionally, the polite form does not specifically need to be used, and どっち may be used instead.

辛からい物ものと甘あまい物ものとどっちが好すき？

Between something spicy and something sweet, which do you like more?

The second と itself is quite often omitted, as どちら (or どっち) implies that both (A) and (B) are part of the same structure.

私わたしと彼女かのじょどっちが好すき？

Between me and her, who do you like?

Fun Fact

As どちら is the polite speech form of 誰だれ, it is also frequently used as a polite way to ask questions about unknown people.

すみません、どちら様さまですか？

I'm sorry, who are you?

Caution

どちらのほう (or どっちのほう) may also be used, but this does not really change the overall nuance. It merely puts slightly more emphasis on the 'direction' of the choice. This is similar to expressions like 'which one are you leaning towards?' in English.

ワインとビールとどちらの方ほうが飲のみたいですか？

Between wine and beer, which would you want to drink more?

Between hiragana and katakana, which one is more difficult to remember?

Between Everest and K2, which one is taller?

電車でんしゃで行いくのと車くるまで行いくのとどちらが早はやいんですか。

Between going by train and going by car, which one is faster?

テニスとバスケとどちらが楽たのしいと思おもう？

Between tennis and basketball, which do you think is more enjoyable?

マンガを読よむことと、新聞しんぶんを読よむこと、どちらが好すきですか。

Between reading manga and reading the newspaper, which do you like?

猫ねこと犬いぬとどちらが可愛かわいいと聞きかれたら、犬いぬの方ほうが可愛かわいいと思おもう。

If I was asked which is cuter, between dogs or cats, I think I would say dogs are (cuter).

二階にかい建だてのマンションと普通ふつうの家いえとどちらに住すんでみたい？

Which one would you rather live in, between a two-story apartment and a house?

苦にがいコーヒーと甘あまいコーヒーとどちらが好このみですか。

Between bitter coffee and sweet coffee, which one do you prefer?

犯人はんにんは逃にげるのと、警察けいさつに行いくのとどちらを選えらぶでしょう。

I wonder which (one) the criminal will choose, between running and going to the police.

スーパーで売うっている林檎りんごとコンビニで売うっている林檎りんごとどちらが安やすい？

Between apples sold at the store and apples sold at a convenience store, which one is cheaper?

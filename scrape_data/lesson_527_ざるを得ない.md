## About

About ざるを得ない

English

Japanese

ざる is the 連体形れんたいけい (attributive form) of the old-fashioned auxiliary verb ざり. ざり carries the same meaning as the modern ない, in negating what comes before it.

When joined with a combination of the る-Verb 得える 'to gain', or 'to acquire', and the auxiliary verb ない, it becomes a double-negative structure meaning that one 'cannot help doing (A)', or 'there is no choice but to (A)'. More literally, it means that one 'can't not (A)'.

For the most part, this structure will conjugate with other verbs in exactly the same way as ない.

山やまの頂上ちょうじょうまで登のぼりたかったが、天候てんこうが悪わるくなって来きたから諦あきらめざるを得えない。

I wanted to hike to the peak of the mountain, but the weather was getting worse so we had no choice but to give up.

先輩せんぱいに指示しじされたので従したがわざるを得えません。

Because my senpai told me to do it, I had no choice but to obey.

Caution

As ざり is an old-fashioned structure, it conjugates a little bit differently with する than the regular ない.

フランスの大学だいがくへ行いくため、フランス語ごを勉強べんきょうしざるを得えない。

In order to go to a college in France, I have no choice but to study French. (Unnatural Japanese)

フランスの大学だいがくへ行いくため、フランス語ごを勉強べんきょうせざるを得えない。

In order to go to a college in France, I have no choice but to study French. (Natural Japanese)

Notice here that instead of しない changing to しざる, せざる will be used instead.

Sentence
Translation

サプライズのプレゼントに、驚おどろかざるを得えなかった。

I couldn't help but be surprised at the surprise present.

思おもい掛がけない出会であいに、感動かんどうせざるを得えなかった。

I couldn't help but be moved at the chance meeting.

田中たなかさんはとても忙いそがしかったのでゴルフの誘さそいを断ことわらざるを得えなかった。

Mr. Tanaka was so busy he had no choice but to turn down an invitation to play golf.

古ふるい家屋かおくは倒壊とうかいする恐おそれがあるから、壊こわさざるを得えません。

The old building is at risk of collapsing, so I have no choice but to tear it down.

今いままで使つかった顕微鏡けんびきょうが壊こわれたから、新あたらしいのを買かわざるを得えない。

The microscope I have been using until now broke, so I have no choice but to buy a new one.

環境かんきょう保護ほごのために、工場こうじょうの排気量はいきりょうを規制きせいせざるを得えない。

In order to protect the environment, we have no choice but to regulate the amount of emission from the factory.

地元じもとのチームではないが、甥おいっ子こが入団にゅうだんしたから応援おうえんせざるを得えない。

The team isn't from my home town, but my nephew joined the team so I can't help but cheer for them.

今度こんどの上司じょうしは外部がいぶからの出向しゅっこうだから、気きを遣つかわざるを得えない。

This superior is being transferred from outside of the company, so I have no choice but to be mindful of his needs.

話はなし合あいが終おわらない。会議かいぎ室しつを借かりる時間じかんを延長えんちょうせざるを得えない。

The discussion is not coming to an end. We have no choice but to extend the amount of time allotted to use this meeting room.

前まえの選手せんしゅが序盤じょばんからかなりのスピードで走はしっているから、今いまはリードを許ゆるさざるを得えない。

The last athlete had quite a bit of speed from the outset, so now I have no choice but to give up the lead.

学会がっかいへの出席しゅっせきは必須ひっすだから、元々もともと予定よていしていた旅行りょこうを諦あきらめざるを得えない。

Because attendance at the academic meeting is compulsory, I have no choice but to give up on the trip that I had originally planned.

電車でんしゃのダイヤの改正かいせいがあり、出勤しゅっきん時間じかんを変更へんこうせざるを得えない状況じょうきょうです。

The train timetables were revised. Now I am in a situation where I have no choice but to change my commute time.

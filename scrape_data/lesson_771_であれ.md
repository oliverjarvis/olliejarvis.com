## About

About であれ

English

Japanese

Functioning as an extension of である, the formal copula that is a combination of the conjunctive form of the auxiliary verb だ, and the う-Verb ある, であれ or であろうと will be used in order to express strong will or conviction.

あれ is simply the imperative form of ある, while あろうと is the positive volitional form combined with the case-marking particle と. Both structures are usually translated as 'even if (A), (B)', or 'no matter if (A), (B)'. Despite this, the more literal meaning of each is closer to 'so it's (A), (B) still won't be affected'. This just emphasizes the fact that (A) is of no relation to the result, like saying 'so what if it is' in English.

Both であれ and であろうと will primarily attach to nouns in sentences that express some kind of question or assumption through words like どんな or たとえ.

子供こどもであれ、法律ほうりつは守まもらなくてはいけない。

Even if you are a child, you still need to obey the law.

理由りゆうが何なんであれ、家族かぞくや友達ともだちを裏切うらぎるのはよくないと思おもう。

No matter the reason, I don't think it is right to betray your family and friends.

いくらあなたが年上としうえであろうと、ここでは一応いちおう私わたしが先輩せんぱいなので私わたしの言いうことに従したがってください。

No matter how much older you are, I am your superior here so please do as I say.

上司じょうしからのメール：「インフルエンザであろうと、会社かいしゃには来きてください。」

Email from one's boss: 'Even if you have the flu, please come to work.'

As both であれ and であろうと use structures that indicate strong will, these grammar patterns will frequently be used in sentences where the speaker is expressing their opinions or resolutions.

## Examples

Examples

00:04

Sentence
Translation

誰だれであれ全すべての人々ひとびとは同様どうように死しぬ。

No matter who you are, all people die similarly.

小説しょうせつ：「どのような状況じょうきょうであれ、逃にげることは許ゆるされない。」

Novel: 'No matter the circumstances, running away is unforgivable.'

何なんであれ、両親りょうしんにとっては我わが子こはいつも愛あいする我わが子こ。

No matter what, from a parent's point of view, their child is always their beloved child.

たとえ小ちいさなものであれ検察官けんさつかんは賄賂わいろを受うけ取とるわけにはいかない。

Even if it is just a small thing, the public prosecutor must not accept a bribe.

たとえ氷こおりのように純粋じゅんすいであれ、世界せかいの悪あくからは免まぬがれられないだろう。

Even if one is as pure as ice, it seems that one cannot avoid the evil of the world.

大人おとなであれ、時ときにお菓子かしでお腹なかを一杯いっぱいにしたい時ときもあるものだ。

Even if you're an adult, it's just a fact that you sometimes want to completely stuff yourself with snacks.

友人ゆうじんの家いえであれ、訪問時ほうもんじは靴くつを脱ぬぐことが、基本的きほんてきな礼儀れいぎです。

Even if it is a friend's house, taking shoes off when visiting are basic manners.

犯人はんにんが未み成年せいねんであれ、そのような凶悪きょうあくな犯罪はんざいを犯おかしたのなら罰ばっするべきです。

No matter whether the perpetrator is underaged, if they committed such a brutal crime, they must be punished.

どんな理由りゆうであれ、大切たいせつな人ひとを裏切うらぎるような行為こういをしたのなら許ゆるされないと思おもうべきだ。

No matter what kind of reason there is, if you've been conducting yourself in a way that betrays those important to you, you ought to assume that you won't be forgiven.

何歳なんさいであれ褒ほめられると嬉うれしいのは変かわらない。だからどんどん僕ぼくの事ことを褒ほめて欲ほしい。

No matter what age, that I will be happy upon getting complimented will not change. Because of that, I want you to compliment me more and more.

どんなに真まっ当とうに生いきている人ひとであれ、人ひとに言いえない秘密ひみつが一ひとつや二ふたつはあるでしょう。

Even if they are a person that lives respectably, they still have one or two secrets that they can't tell anyone, right?

たとえ十年じゅうねん以上いじょうの付つき合あいであれ、自分じぶん以外いがいの人ひとには常つねに敬意けいいを持もって接せっするべきである。

Even if you were dating for over 10 years, for instance, you must always treat someone other than yourself with respect.

## About

About 上

English

Japanese

Attaching to nouns, 上じょう is a suffix that highlights when something is being presented 'from the standpoint of (A)'. The 'standpoint' that 上じょう will indicate is usually a specific field or area of concern. The (B) part of the sentence will then go on to describe what exactly is being considered from that standpoint.

法律ほうりつ上じょう、伊勢海老いせえびを許可きょかなく釣つり上あげて持もって帰かえることが禁止きんしされている。

From a legal standpoint, it is prohibited to fish for lobsters and bring them home without a permit.

規則きそく上じょう、生徒せいとは髪かみを染そめてはいけないのに、髪かみを染そめている生徒せいとが複数ふくすういます。

From the standpoint of the rules, it is prohibited for the students to dye their hair, but there are a few students that have dyed hair.

上じょう is fairly formal, and can be interpreted similarly to に関かんして 'in connection to', in that it is just expressing that (B) is being discussed in relation to (A).

As 上じょう will be used with matters of concern, it is very frequently seen with words that express broad fields, such as work '仕事しごと', 'education' 教育きょういく, 'history' 歴史れきし, 'health' 健康けんこう, 'experience' 経験けいけん, 'the law' 法律ほうりつ, and other similar things.

Caution

Despite expressing standpoints, 上じょう cannot be used to indicate an individual person's point of view. Because of this, it cannot be used as a suffix for names of people or organizations that something is being considered from.

鈴木すずき上じょう、会社かいしゃＡエーが会社かいしゃＢビーに買収ばいしゅうされる事ことはいい事ことだと思おもっている。

From the standpoint of Suzuki, he thinks that company A being bought by company B is a good thing. (Unnatural Japanese)

予算よさん上じょう、その依頼いらいは断ことわるしかない。

From a budget standpoint, I have no choice but to deny that request.

あのビルは構造こうぞう上じょう地震じしんに弱よわい。

That building, from a construction standpoint, is weak to earthquakes.

規則きそく上じょう、このアパートでペットを飼かうのは禁止きんしです。

From the standpoint of the rules, this apartment prohibits pets.

それは飽あくまで計算けいさん上じょうの話はなしで、実際じっさいにはそう簡単かんたんにはいきません。

That is a discussion that will only ever be viewed from the standpoint of forecasting. In practice, it wouldn't go that easily.

立場たちば上じょうああいう発言はつげんをしたが、個人的こじんてきには争あらそう気持きもちは全まったくない。

From that (situation's) standpoint, I made that sort of remark, but personally I'm not in the mood to quarrel at all.

一応いちおうお伝つたえしておきますが、龍りゅうは想像そうぞう上じょうの生いき物ものです。

I'll go ahead and say this: dragons are only creatures from the standpoint of your imagination.

面倒臭めんどうくさいとは思おもいますが、形式けいしき上じょうこの書類しょるいに全部ぜんぶ記入きにゅうしてください。

I think it is bothersome, but for formalities' sake, please fill out all these documents. (From a formality standpoint)

正式せいしきな野球やきゅうのルールとは違ちがうが、便宜べんぎ上じょう盗塁とうるいなしでお願ねがいします。

It is different from the official rules of baseball, but from the standpoint of convenience, no stealing bases please.

会社かいしゃの規律きりつ上じょう、５ご時半じはんまでは仕事しごとをしなければいけない。

From the standpoint of the workplace's rules, you must work until 5:30.

空そら飛とぶ車くるまはもはや空想くうそう上じょうの乗のり物ものではない。

Flying cars are no longer vehicles of fantasy. (From a fantasy standpoint)

法律ほうりつ上じょう、学校がっこうは個人こじん情報じょうほうを漏もらしてはいけない。

From a legal standpoint, schools must not leak personal information.

採点さいてん上じょうあの順位じゅんいだったが、観客かんきゃくの記憶きおくに残のこる演技えんぎだった。

That rank was from a scoring standpoint, but it was a performance that stayed in the audience's memory.

## About

About と考えられる

English

Japanese

In order to express an objective opinion in Japanese, the expression と考かんがえられる will often be used. This is a combination of と, and the passive-indicating auxiliary verb られる. Common translations for this structure include 'can be considered as (A)', 'is thought to be (A)', or 'it is conceivable that (A)'.

As と考かんがえられる begins with と, this structure will frequently appear at the end of entire phrases, converting them into a quote.

あの事故じこは煽あおり運転うんてんのせいだと考かんがえられる。

Tailgating is thought to be the cause of this accident.

日本語にほんごは文字種もじしゅが多おおいことから、第二だいに言語げんごとして習ならうのが難むずかしいと考かんがえられる。

It is thought that Japanese is difficult to learn as a second language due to having several different character types.

When wanting to express a subjective opinion, と思おもわれる will be used instead.

私わたしには彼かれがこの事件じけんの犯人はんにんだと思おもわれる。

It seems to me that he is the one responsible for this incident.

私わたしにはあなたが悪わるいと思おもわれる。

It seems to me that you are in the wrong.

Caution

Compared to と考かんがえられている, と考かんがえられる focuses more on judgements, while the former is primarily used to illustrate widely accepted opinions.

Fun-fact

The primary difference between a subjective and objective opinion is that an objective opinion is primarily based on observable facts about the topic (unbiased and focuses on the object), while a subjective opinion is based more on emotions, feelings, or preferences (may be biased, regardless of what is actually true about the topic).

## Examples

Examples

Sentence
Translation

衆議院しゅうぎいん、いよいよ解散かいさんすると考かんがえられる。

It is thought that the lower house of representatives will finally dissolve.

あの子この親おやは育児いくじ放棄ほうきしていると考かんがえられる。

It is thought that that child's parents are neglecting the child.

あの政策せいさくなら、一定いっていの支持しじが得えられると考かんがえられる。

If it is that policy, it is conceivable that we would receive standardized support.

安易あんいに決定けっていしたと考かんがえられるが、もしかしたら作戦さくせんかもしれません。

It is thought that the decision made was to be easy-going, but it might just be a tactic.

そのプロジェクト、一旦いったん白紙はくしに戻もどすということも考かんがえられませんか？

Is it conceivable that you would think of wiping the slate clean on that project? (won't you?)

この形かたちからすると、何なにかの入いれ物ものだと考かんがえられますが、謎なぞですね。

Judging from the shape, one can think that you put something in this, but it is a mystery.

あの部屋へやは薄暗うすぐらいと考かんがえられる。懐中かいちゅう電灯でんとうを持もって行いこう。

That room can be considered dim. I will (let's) bring a flashlight.

光ひかりの性質せいしつは、実じつは粒子りゅうしと波動はどうの両方りょうほうだと考かんがえられる。

The nature of light is actually thought to be both a particle and a wave.

中国ちゅうごくは経済けいざい成長せいちょうが続つづいていけば新あらたな超ちょう大国たいこくとなると考かんがえられる。

China may well be considered the new superpower if it maintains its current economic growth.

すぐに新あたらしい物ものを欲ほしがるのは、子供こどもを甘あまやかして何なにでも買かってあげていたことが原因げんいんだと考かんがえられる。

It is thought that children immediately desiring new things is caused by parents spoiling them and buying them whatever they want.

動物どうぶつ性せい脂肪しぼうを摂とることは癌がんなどの病気びょうきになる一因いちいん（だ）と考かんがえられるが、果はたしてそうでしょうか？

The consumption of animal fats is thought to be one of the causes of cancer and other conditions. But is it really so?

糖分とうぶんが豊富ほうふな食品しょくひんをあまりに摂とることは２に型がた糖尿とうにょう病びょうなどの病気びょうきになる一因いちいん（だ）と考かんがえられる。

The excessive intake of products rich in sugar is thought to be one of the causes of getting Type 2 diabetes and similar conditions.

## About

About ともなく・ともなしに

English

Japanese

ともなく and ともなしに are structures used to indicate that (A) was being done absentmindedly when (B) happened. It will be translated as 'unconsciously (A) when (B)', or 'without paying attention to (A), (B)'.

This pattern is a combination of the case-marking particle と, the adverbial particle も, and the conjunctive form of the い-Adjective ない. It will be seen after the dictionary form of verbs, and can be thought of as literally meaning something similar to 'even while not intending to (B), while (A), (B)'.

昨日きのうテレビを見みるともなく見みていたら、急きゅうにお母かあさんが出でてきたから驚おどろいた。

When I was absentmindedly watching TV yesterday, I was surprised when my mother suddenly appeared.

話はなしを聞きくともなく、先生せんせいと黒板こくばんの方向ほうこうをぼーっと見みていた。

I absentmindedly listened while spacing out in the direction of my teacher and the blackboard.

ともなしに is similar, but pairs the noun なし 'without' with に, instead of using the conjugative form of ない.

飲のみ会かいで上司じょうしの話はなしを聞きくともなしに聞きいていたら、「お前まえはどう思おもう」と聞きかれたから適当てきとうに返事へんじした。

When I was at a drinking party and was absentmindedly listening to my boss, he asked what I thought, so I gave him a random response.

暇ひまだったから近所きんじょを歩あるくともなしに歩あるいていたら、高校こうこうの同級生どうきゅうせいにばったりあった。

When I was mindlessly walking in my neighborhood because I had nothing to do, I met my classmate from high school.

ともなく and ともなしに will often be seen in sentences that use たら. This puts emphasis on the specific time that something happened while performing another action absentmindedly. Despite translating this way, the action that precedes ともなく itself will be intentional, but the thing that comes about as a result of performing that action will not be what the doer was aiming to achieve.

## Examples

Examples

Sentence
Translation

この曲きょくは行いきつけのバーでいつも流ながれているため、覚おぼえるともなく覚おぼえてしまった。

Since this song is always playing at my favorite bar, I unconsciously memorized it.

隣人りんじんの窓まどを見みるとも無なく見みると、驚おどろいたことに裸はだかの人ひとがいた。

I looked at my neighbor's window without paying attention, and, to my surprise, there was a naked person.

勝者しょうしゃの発表はっぴょうを聞きくとも無なく聞きいていたら、なんと私わたしの名前なまえが挙あがっていた。

I listened absentmindedly to the announcements of the winners, when, to my disbelief, my name came up.

ノベル：「彼かれは夜遅よるおそくテレビを見みるとも無なく見みているうちにいつのまにか寝入ねいってしまったようだ。」

Novel: 'It seems that he fell asleep while he was mindlessly watching TV late at night.'

昨日きのう、考かんがえるとも無なく考かんがえているところに、スズメバチにひどく刺さされて「痛いたっ！」思おもわず叫さけんでしまった。

Yesterday, when I was thinking about nothing in particular, I was badly stung by a wasp, and without thinking shouted: 'Ouch!' (mindlessly)

見みるともなくテレビを見みていると、ビールの広告こうこくに友達ともだちが出演しゅつえんしていて驚おどろきました。

While watching TV without paying too much attention, I was surprised to see a friend appear in a beer commercial.

「何なにも無ない」とはどういう事ことなのか、考かんがえるともなく考かんがえてしまう事ことがたまにあります。

There are occasionally times where I unconsciously contemplate what 'there being nothing' means.

嫌いやな事ことがあると、その事ことを考かんがえるともなく考かんがえてしまうので、そういう時ときは早はやく寝ねるようにしている。

If there is something that I dislike, since I'll absentmindedly think about it, I do my best to go to sleep quickly at those times.

カフェでぼんやりしていると気きづけば隣となりに座すわっている主婦しゅふたちの会話かいわを聞きくともなく聞きいていました。

When lazing around in a cafe, upon reflection, I was listening to the conversation of the couple sitting next to me without paying attention.

山田やまださんと晩ばん御飯ごはんを食たべるとも無なく食たべていたら、急きゅうに彼かれが告白こくはくしてきて驚おどろいた。

When I was absentmindedly eating dinner with Yamada-san, he suddenly confessed (his feelings for me) and it really surprised me.

ライトノベル、あまくささん：「家いえに帰かえるとも無なく帰かえっていたら、突然とつぜん美少女びしょうじょが空そらから落おちてきてびっくりした。」

Light Novel, Amakusa: 'When I was absentmindedly on my way back home, a beautiful girl fell from the sky and startled me.'

学生がくせいの頃ころは親友しんゆうと何なにを話はなすともなく話はなす時間じかんが好すきだったが、今いまはそんなに暇ひまな時間じかんが無ない。

When I was a student, I loved the times when I would mindlessly talk about something with my best friend, but now I don't have that much free time.

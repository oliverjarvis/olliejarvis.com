## About

About のはXの方だ

English

Japanese

のは～の方ほうだ is an expression used to show that (A) (usually despite expectations) is (B). It can be translated as 'the one that (A), is (B)'. This phrase is made up of the following parts:

のは - Used for nominalization (creating a noun phrase). は highlights contrast between two (or more) elements.

の方ほう - A combination of の, the case marking particle, and 方ほう, a noun meaning 'side', or 'direction'.

だ (or です) - The auxiliary verb used for 'determining' something.

The literal meaning of this grammar point is something similar to 'that which is (A), is on the side of (B)'. By saying that something is on the 'side' of (B), it comes across more gently than expressly stating that it 'is (B)'.

To use this construction, attach のは to the end of a phrase, and then follow it with a noun + の方ほうだ. Note that のは will become なのは if the final word before のは is a noun.

言いい出だしたのは俺おれの方ほうだから、俺おれがなんとかしておくよ。

I am the one that said it, so I will do something about it.

そのコップを壊こわしたのはタケルの方ほうだ！俺おれは関係かんけいないよ！

The one that broke the cup is Takeru! I have nothing to do with it!

Fun Fact

This phrase is often used apologetically, to express that someone feels that they are the individual who is (more) responsible (for causing a certain outcome).

謝あやまらなければいけないのは私わたしの方ほうですよ。あなたは何なにも悪わるくないです。

The one that needs to apologize is me. You are not in the wrong at all.

## Examples

Sentence
Translation

危険きけんなのはオレの方ほうだよ。

I am the one who is dangerous.

主おもな賞しょうを受賞じゅしょうするのはあちゃんの方ほうだといいな。

I hope that the one who will win the main prize will be A-chan.
(A-chan is the one who...)

ドアを開あけっぱなしにしたのはキちゃんの方ほうだと思おもう。

I think that Ki-chan was the one who left the doors open (not me).

あのときサカモトくんを誘さそいたかったのは、あたしの方ほうだ。

I am the one who wanted to invite Sakamoto-kun at that time.

わたしが怖こわいのは、犬いぬではなく飼かい主ぬしの方ほうだ。

The owner is the one who I am afraid of, not the dog.

お前まえに言いいつけたのはオレの方ほうだから、彼女かのじょはほっておいて。

It was I who told on you, not her, so leave her alone.

フランシス：「お金かねを持もっているのはアイツの方ほうだ。でも権威けんいを持もっているのはオレさまの方ほうだ。」

Francis: 'It is he who has money. However, I am the one who has power.'

北海道ほっかいどうと沖縄おきなわとでは暖あたたかい気候きこうなのは後者こうしゃの方ほうだ。

Out of Hokkaido and Okinawa, it is the latter that has a warm climate.

きみをいろいろ助たすけてあげたのに、裏切うらぎったのはきみの方ほうだ。信しんじられない。

Even though I did so much for you, you are the one who betrayed me. I can't believe it.

ギャング1：「赤あかいズボンを履はいているのが奴やつなのか？」
ギャング2：「いいえ、私達わたしたちが追おっているのはタバコを吸すってる奴やつの方ほうだろう。」

Gangster1: 'Is it the guy wearing red pants?'
Gangster2: 'No, we are probably searching for the guy who is smoking.'

でもお母かあさん、晩ばんご飯はんのまえにお菓子かしを食たべたのは兄貴あにきの方ほうだよ。

But mom, big bro is the one who ate sweets before dinner (not me)!

「あたしがわるいの。ごめんなさい！」
「君きみが悪わるいんじゃないよ。謝あやまるべきなのはぼくの方ほうだ。」

'It is my fault! I am so sorry!'
'You are not at fault. I am the one who should apologize.'

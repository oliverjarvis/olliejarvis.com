## About

About 込む ①

English

Japanese

In Japanese, the う - Verb 込こむ 'to jam into' is frequently used as a suffix to convey several different meanings. The primary meanings are as follows:

(A) 込こむ - To enter completely into doing (A), or the state of being in (A).

(A) 込こむ - To enter a state of doing (A) that will not be considered complete until 'success'.

(A) 込こむ - To enter a state of doing (A) that cannot be escaped from.

In this lesson, we will exclusively focus on the first meaning, which is usually translated as 'to put into (A)', or 'to go into (A)'. This form will be attached to the conjunctive form of other verbs.

ピンを壁かべに押おし込こむ。

To push a pin into a wall.

薬くすりを飲のみ込こむのが苦手にがてです。

I am not the best at drinking down medicine. (I am not the best at swallowing medicine)

電車でんしゃのドアが閉しまる前まえに、電車でんしゃに走はしり込こんだ。

Before the train door closed, I ran in.

サウナから出でて、水風呂みずぶろに飛とび込こむように入はいった。

I came out of the sauna and went into the water bath as if I was leaping into it.

Despite the multiple meanings, 込こむ can almost always be thought of as 'jamming (B) into (A)'. This means that whatever the (A) action is, will be something that is not easily entered, but also not easily escaped from.

教師きょうし：「教科書きょうかしょに答こたえを書かき込こんでください。」

Teacher: 'Please fill in your textbooks with answers.' (put into)

トムはジェロニモと叫さけびながら、プールに飛とび込こんだ。

Tom jumped into the pool while screaming 'Geronimo!' (put into)

Ａエー：「どうして窓まどが閉しめてあるの？」
Ｂビー：「冷つめたい風かぜが入はいりこまないように。」

A: 'Why is the window closed?'
B: 'So that cold air won't get in.' (put into)

バッキンガム宮殿きゅうでんの警護官けいごかんについて：「笑わらいを飲のみ込こみながら、移動いどうせずに立たったままだった。」

About a Buckingham Palace guard: 'He kept standing, unmoving, while holding in a smile.' (swallowing・put into)

友達ともだちとの話はなし：「彼女かのじょは泥棒どろぼうの顎あごに見事みごとに拳こぶしを打うち込こんだそうだ。」

Chat between friends: 'I heard that she fantastically drove her fist into the thief's face.' (put into)

友達ともだちとの話はなし、Ａエー：「お金かねって、何なにするの？」
Ｂビー：「銀行ぎんこうに払はらい込こもうと思おもう。」

Chat between friends, A: 'What are you gonna do with the money?'
B: 'I plan to deposit it into the bank.' (put into)

ライトノベル、勇者ゆうしゃたちの会話かいわ：「一人ひとりで魔王まおうの城しろに乗のり込こむおつもりですか？おやめ下ください！」

Light novel, chat between heroes: 'Do you plan to march into the demon king's castle alone? Please, stop!' (put into)

行列ぎょうれつで：「（列れつに）わり込こむなんて、図々ずうずうしいですよ！」

In the queue: 'To cut in (line)! How impudent!' (put into)

ライトノベル、ナレーター：「コタカが突然とつぜん、部室ぶしつに走はしり込こんで来きて、彼女かのじょの隣となりに席せきを取とった。」

Light novel, the narrator: 'Kotaka has suddenly run into the club room, and taken a seat next to her.' (put into)

ライトノベル、クラスメートとの話：「ドラゴンをクラスに持もち込こむのはルール違反いはんじゃないか？」

Light novel, chat between classmates : 'Taking a dragon into class, isn't that breaking the rules?' (put into)

友達ともだちとの話はなし：「布団ふとんで寝ねていると、猫ねこが布団ふとんの中なかに潜もぐり込こんできた。」

Chat between friends: 'When I was sleeping, my cat slipped into the futon. ' (put into)

ノベル：「心地ここちよい風かぜが窓まどから吹ふき込こんできた。」

Novel: 'A pleasant breeze has started blowing in from the window.' (put into)

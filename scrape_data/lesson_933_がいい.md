## About

About がいい

がいい is a combination of the case-marking particle が, and the い-Adjective いい. It is used in a way that almost sounds like an order from someone in a higher position, to someone in a lower position. Standard translations will include 'you shall (A)', or 'you should (A)'. However, the more literal translation is 'to (A) would be good'.

(A) will be the dictionary form of a verb. がいい will then directly follow (A).

そんなに私わたしを疑うたがうなら、自分じぶんで試ためしてみるがいい。

If you are going to doubt me that much, you shall try it yourself.

今日きょうは私わたしの奢おごりだ。好すきなだけ食たべるがいい。

It is my treat today. You shall eat as much as you please.

どうだ、檻おりの外そとは気持きもちいいだろ。さあ、行いきたいとこまで飛とぶがいい！

How is that? It's nice outside the cage, isn't it? Go, you shall fly wherever you wish!

In many cases, the person using がいい is in a very high position, or perceives themselves to be in a very high position such as a king, emperor, wise wizard, or something similar. Due to this, がいい is rarely used in real life, and sounds very magnanimous, or even pompous.

Additionally, as いい is being used in a pompous way, the more literary 良よい will frequently be used in its place.

Fun Fact

Verb + がいい may be thought of as a far stronger equivalent of た方ほうがいい 'you should (A)', except that it will only ever be used by those that consider their own suggestion as the only correct course of action.

今日きょうも暑あついから水みず飲のんだほうがいいよ。

It is hot today so you should probably drink water.

Sentence
Translation

そこの君きみ、喉のどが渇かわいてそうだな。ちょうど俺おれの飲のみかけの水みずがあるからこれでも飲のむがいい。

Hey you there, you seem thirsty. I just happened to have a bottle of water, so you should drink some of it.

私わたしに着ついてくるがいい。

You should come with me.

王おうである私わたしに跪ひざまずくがよい。

It would be good if you kneel before me, the king.

お前まえは俺様おれさまに仕つかえるがいい。

You should be my servant.

今日きょうから私わたしの言いいなりになるがいい。

You should obey what I say from today.

罪人ざいにんのお前まえは檻おりの中なかに一生いっしょういるがいい。

It would be good if you, who is a criminal, spent your life on the inside of a cage.

分わからない事ことがあれば私わたしに聞きくがよい。

If there are things that you do not understand, asking me would be good.

お金かねがないのなら、私わたしにお金かねを借かりるがいい。

If you have no money, you should borrow money from me.

一人ひとりが心細こころぼそければ、僕ぼくかアイツを呼よぶがいい。

If you are lonely by yourself, it would be good to call myself or him.

仕事しごとに自信じしんが無ないのなら、私わたしに相談そうだんするがよい。

If you have no confidence in your work, it would be good to discuss it with me.

何なにか悩なやみ事ごとを抱かかえているのなら、私わたしに話はなすがいい。

If you are burdened with troubles, it would be good to talk with me.

僕ぼくが君きみを甘あまやかすから、君きみは好すきなことをするがよい。

Because I will spoil you, you should do what you want.

ストレスが溜たまっているのなら、マッサージで癒いやされるがよい。

If you're accumulating stress, you should get relief with a massage.

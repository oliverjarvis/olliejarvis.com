## About

About まくる

English

Japanese

まくる is a verb that is attached to the conjunctive form of other verbs in order to show that (A) is being done over and over, a lot, or with a great amount of vigor. It comes from the verb う-Verb 捲まくる 'to roll', or 'to turn', so means 'to do (A) around and again'. Common translations include 'to (A) like crazy', 'to do nothing but (A)', and 'to constantly do (A)'.

As mentioned, this structure is attached to the conjunctive form of other verbs.

昨日きのうは釣具屋つりぐやでルアーを買かいまくったから、来週らいしゅうまでご飯はんとふりかけで我慢がまんしなきゃいけない。

I bought a bunch of lures at the tackle shop yesterday, so I have to put up with eating rice and furikake until next week.

久ひさしぶりに実家じっかに帰かえって、母ははの料理りょうりを食たべまくってるから少すこし太ふとってきた。

I have started to gain some weight because I've been eating so much of my mom's food, ever since I came to visit my family after a long time.

昨日きのうはカラオケで歌うたいまくったから、声こえが全然ぜんぜんでない。

I sang like crazy at the karaoke last night, so I lost my voice.

This structure may be used both in positive or negative contexts, and it is quite common for the nuance of まくる to indicate that (A) is being done recklessly, or with abandon. This implies that the speaker isn't thinking about the consequences of doing (A) repeatedly, if any may exist.

Caution

The (A) verb must be something that is volitional, meaning that the speaker must have control over it and its repetition.

しゃっくりが出でまくりすぎて苦くるしい。

I am having a hard time breathing because I am hiccuping like crazy. (Unnatural Japanese)

Hayase and Naoto danced like crazy.

'I've been eating chocolate like crazy lately, so I've gained some weight.'

締しめ切きりが近ちかいから、原稿げんこうを書かきまくった。

Because the deadline is close, I was writing the draft like crazy.

時間じかんさえあれば走はしりまくっている彼かれの足あしはたくましいです。

He, who runs like crazy so long as he has time, has strong legs.

ストレス発散はっさんでメイク道具どうぐを買かいまくったせいで金欠きんけつになった。

Due to buying makeup goods one after the other to relieve stress, I have no money.

私わたしはシャイで、それなのに人ひとと会あうと逆ぎゃくに喋しゃべりまくってしまいます。

I am shy, although when I meet people, I unfortunately talk like crazy.

いくら心配しんぱいだからって、電話でんわをかけまくるのはどうかと思おもうよ。

Regardless of how worried you are, it’s not right to just call constantly.

「まさはるさんは新車しんしゃ購入こうにゅうしたから毎日まいにち乗のりまくっているらしいよ。」

'I heard that Masaharu-san bought a new car and has been driving it all the time, every single day.'

「よしこって喋しゃべるのが大好だいすきだ。ベラベラしゃべりまくるから彼女かのじょには我慢がまんできない。」

'Yoshiko loves to talk. She talks nonstop, like crazy so I can't stand her.'

応援中おうえんちゅうに叫さけびまくったせいで、喉のどが痛いたくなってしまいました。

Due to screaming like crazy while cheering, my throat got sore.

９０きゅうじゅう年代ねんだいにはあのアニメが大だいヒットした。私わたしはファンになって以来いらい、アニメグッズを買かいまくっている。

In the 90s, that anime was a big hit. I have been a fan ever since and I've been buying anime goods like crazy.

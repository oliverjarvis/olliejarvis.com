## About

About を問わず

English

Japanese

を問とわず is a pattern which makes use of the special-class う-Verb 問とう 'to inquire' and the literary auxiliary verb ず, which connects with verbs in the same way, and carries the same meaning as ない. While the literal meaning of this grammar structure is 'without questioning (A), (B)', it is used more as a formal way to express 'regardless of (A), (B)', 'whether (A) or not, (B)', or 'with or without (A)', (B)'.

This structure is seen almost exclusively after nouns, but can also follow structures like か, or かどうか that are modifying adjectives. を will mark the 'question' which is 'not being asked'.

宗教しゅうきょうや国籍こくせきを問とわず、このイベントには誰だれでも参加さんかできます。

Everyone is welcome to attend this event, regardless of religion or nationality.

パソコンの上級者じょうきゅうしゃか初心者しょしんしゃかを問とわず、初はじめてこのサービスをお使つかいになられる方かたには初心者しょしんしゃコースから始はじめてもらいます。

Whether you are an advanced computer user or a beginner, we ask first-time users of this service to start with the beginner course.

このマンションを買かうかどうかを問とわず、この物件ぶっけんを押おさえたい場合ばあいは手付金てつけきんを払はらってください。

If you want to hold on to this property, whether you buy this apartment or not, you must pay a deposit.

Occasionally, は will replace を at the beginning of 問わず. This puts further emphasis on contrast, and slightly strengthens the phrase overall.

どのブランドかは問とわず、とにかく新あたらしい靴くつが欲ほしいです。

I just want a new pair of shoes, regardless of which brand.

Caution

Although を問とわず may be thought of as similar to 'regardless', there are actually situations where the nuance is closer to 'in any case for (A), (B)'. This is particularly true when (A) is being expressed as a scope. Let's compare を問とわず to に関かかわらず to see the difference.

彼かれは昼夜ちゅうやを問とわず、ずっと働はたらいている。

Whether it is day or night, he is always working.

彼かれは昼夜ちゅうやに関かかわらず、ずっと働はたらいている。

Regardless of day or night, he is always working.

## Examples

Examples

Sentence
Translation

どんな役やくかは問とわず、主役しゅやくであればね。

Regardless of the role, if it is a leading role (it doesn't matter). (no matter)

私わたしは彼女かのじょと結婚けっこんしたい。手段しゅだんを問とわない。

I want to marry her! No matter what. (regardless・whether or not)

買かう買かわないを問とわず、商品しょうひんを試ためしに来きてください。

(Regardless of) whether you will buy it or not, please come and try the product.

好すき嫌きらいを問とわず探さがせば、仕事しごとは見みつかります。

Regardless of your likes and dislikes, if you look, you will find a job.

上手うまい下手へたを問とわず、習字しゅうじの練習れんしゅうは続つづけてください。

Whether you are good at it or bad at it, please continue to practice your penmanship (calligraphy). (regardless of)

資格しかくを問とわず募集ぼしゅうしたら、変へんな人ひとばかり集あつまってしまう。

If you recruit regardless of qualifications, you'll end up with only odd people. (with or without)

速はやい遅おそいを問とわず、みなさんにマラソンに参加さんかしていただきたいです。

Whether you are fast or slow, we would like everyone to participate in the marathon. (regardless of)

契約けいやくするしないは問とわず、とりあえず説明せつめいは聞きいてもらいたい。

Whether or not you sign a contract, for the time being, I would like you to listen to my explanation. (regardless)

学歴がくれきや経験けいけんを問とわず、やる気きのある応募おうぼ者しゃを雇やとう！

We are hiring motivated applicants with or without experience or academic background! (regardless of)

新あたらしいか古ふるいかを問とわず、全すべての車くるまは保険ほけんが法律ほうりつで義務付ぎむづけられている。

(Regardless of) whether it is new or old, all cars are obligated by law to have insurance.

裕福ゆうふくな家庭かていであるか否いなかを問とわず、日本にっぽんでは初等しょとう・中等ちゅうとう教育きょういくは無料むりょうだ！

(Regardless of) whether the family is wealthy or not, primary and secondary education is free in Japan!

このアプリケーションは、管理かんり者しゃ権限けんげんの有無うむを問とわず誰だれでも使つかうことができる。

This application can be used by anyone, (regardless of) whether they have administrator's rights or not.

## About

About まま(に)

English

Japanese

まま, coming from the kanji 儘まま, which means 'as it is', is a noun in Japanese that is used for describing the 'unchanged state' of something. This expression may be translated as 'while remaining (A)', 'as (A) is', or 'left in the state of (A)'. It can be used after any word in its attributive form. However, when used with verbs, it has a tendency to be used with the た (past) form most frequently.

うぁ〜、マジかよ。電気でんきとガスを点つけたままだった。

Whaaat. Are you serious? I left my gas and electricity on.

部屋へやを汚きたないままにしていたらゴキブリが沢山たくさん出でてきた。

So many cockroaches appeared because I left my room dirty.

いつまでも元気げんきなままでいてね。

Please always stay healthy, okay?

今いまのままだと、一生いっしょう結婚けっこんできないと思おもうよ。

If you are the way you are now, I don't think you'll ever be able to marry.

まま often expresses that something should be left alone, or undisturbed. This is generally seen with verbs in the ない form.

許可きょかを取とらないまま駅えきで撮影さつえいをしていたら、警察けいさつが来きた。

While I was filming at the station without getting a permit, the police were called.

夕食ゆうしょくを食たべないまま寝ねたら、お腹なかが空すいて３さん時じぐらいに起おきてしまった。

Because I slept without eating dinner, I woke up around 3 am from hunger.

Caution

While まま may be followed by the case marking particle に, it is very often omitted. When it appears, however, it will place extra emphasis on the next verb in the sentence.

エンジンを点つけたままにしておいてください。

Please keep your engine on.

扇風機せんぷうきを点つけたままにして家いえを出でる。

I leave my house with my fan turned on. (Often)

Fun Fact

まま may sometimes be pronounced as まんま. However, this variation is primarily only used in casual speech, or writing that is being made to sound like casual speech.

これどこに片付かたづければいいかわからないからこのまんまでいい？

I don’t know where to put this so is it okay if I just leave it here?

The student couldn't answer and stayed quiet.

If you stay on this course, you will be able to see the library.

I like you, just as you are.

音楽おんがくをつけたまま寝ねるのが好すきだよ。

I like to sleep with music left turned on.

およそ千人せんにんが立たったままで講演こうえんを聞きいた。

About 1000 people listened to the lecture while standing (the whole time).

昨日きのう、ヒーターをつけたまま出でかけたんだ。もう少すこしで、自分じぶんの家いえを燃もやすところだった。

While I went out yesterday, I left the heater on. I nearly burned my own house to the ground.

車くるまに乗のったままハンバーガーが買かえるのは便利べんりですね。

It's very convenient to be able to buy hamburgers while remaining seated in a car.

久ひさしぶりに故郷こきょうに帰かえって、すごく驚おどろいた。十年じゅうねん前まえ、外国がいこくへ移住いじゅうしたその日ひのままだった。

After a long time, I had returned to my hometown and was extremely surprised. It was as it had been the day I emigrated, ten years ago.

監督かんとく：「このチームでは、今いまのままだと優勝ゆうしょうできないから、あることを変更へんこうすることに決きめた。」

Coach: 'Since the team can't win the championship staying the way it is, I decided to change things up.'

In Poland, it's rude to shake hands while remaining seated.

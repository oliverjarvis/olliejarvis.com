## About

About べく

English

Japanese

As the conjunctive form of the classical auxiliary verb べし, べく will be attached to the dictionary form of verbs in order to convey the purpose of an action. This will be translated as 'for the purpose of (A), (B)', 'in order to (A), (B)', or 'to ensure that (A), (B)'.

べく itself will only attach to verbs that can be done intentionally, with some purpose in mind. The (B) part of べく sentences will also indicate an intentional action that will be performed in order to reach the goal of (A).

地域ちいきの高齢者こうれいしゃの安全あんぜんを守まもるべく、交番こうばんが設置せっちされました。

In order to ensure the safety of the elderly in the community, a police box has been set.

息子むすこは医者いしゃになるべく、ここ３年間さんねんかんほぼ寝ねずに毎日まいにち勉強べんきょうをしている。

In order to become a doctor, he has been studying these past 3 years without much sleep.

When combining する with べく, due to べく being a classical auxiliary verb, it will attach to the classical stem of する, す. However, in modern Japanese it is fairly common to see both すべく and するべく, with both forms being considered correct.

ここでの交通こうつう事故じこを防止ぼうしすべく、ガードレールなどの設備せつびを設置せっちすることになった。

In order to prevent car accidents here, things such as guardrails are to be installed.

次つぎの大会たいかいで優勝ゆうしょうするべく、彼かれらは毎日まいにち朝あさから夜よるまで練習れんしゅうをするようにしている。

They try to practice every day from morning to night in order to win the next competition.

べく itself is a very formal structure in modern Japanese, so will rarely be used in standard speech.

Fun Fact

Although べし and all of its conjugations have slightly different translations based on the grammar points that they appear in, the underlying meaning can be stemmed from the originating kanji 可べし, meaning 'to be permissible'. The focus with this kanji and all forms of べし are just that the word it is attached to is being marked as a strong goal. This means that rather than 'in order to (A)', べく is closer to 'to attain (A)', in that it adds further emphasis.

In order to become a medical intern, he studied at a medical college for five years.

コーポレートサイト：「クライアントのニーズに応こたえる可べく、我わが社しゃは世界せかいで初はじめて、ＡＩエーアイカスタマーサポートを生うみ出だしました。」

Corporate site: 'In order to meet our customers' needs, our company has developed the world's first AI customer support.'

大学だいがくウェブサイト：「ＩＺＵいず科学技術大学かがくぎじゅつだいがくでは、予定通よていどおり秋期しゅうき講座こうざを開講かいこうす可べく、鋭意えいい準備じゅんびを進すすめてまいりました。」

University website: 'We, at the IZU university of science and technology, have been making preparations in order to launch lectures for the Fall Term as planned.'

伝記でんき：「ホテル業界ぎょうかいでの有力者ゆうりょくしゃとしての地位ちいを固かためる可べく、海外かいがいでもホテルを購入こうにゅうしようとしたが、拒否きょひされてしまった。」

Biography: 'In order to cement his influential position in the hotel world, he also attempted to buy hotels abroad, but he was refused.'

記事きじ：「ＢｉｇＳｏｆｔビッグソフトで培つちかった経験けいけんを最大限さいだいげんに生いかす可べく、 会社かいしゃを立たち上あげて独立どくりつすることに決きめました。」

Article: 'In order to make the best possible use of the experience she gained at BigSoft, she decided to become independent by launching her own company.'

動物嫌どうぶつぎらいを克服こくふくすべく、最近さいきんは動物園どうぶつえんや牧場ぼくじょうに足あしを運はこぶようにしています。

In order to overcome my dislike of animals, I have recently been making sure to lug myself off to places like the zoo and farms.

次つぎの試合しあいで勝かつべく、僕ぼくたちのチームは毎日まいにち何時間なんじかんも一生懸命いっしょうけんめいに練習れんしゅうをしている。

To ensure that we win at the next match, our team is practicing with all of our might for umpteen hours daily.

上野うえの公園こうえんでお花見はなみの場所ばしょを確保かくほするべく、私達わたしたちは朝あさ五時ごじに起おきて場所取ばしょとりをしました。

In order to secure a spot for cherry blossom viewing in Ueno park, we woke up at 5am to stake out a place.

優秀ゆうしゅうな人材じんざいを確保かくほするべく、我わが社しゃの人事じんじ部ぶは各かく分野ぶんやの学生がくせいと社会人しゃかいじんに目めを光ひからせている。

In order to secure an excellent candidate, our company's HR department keeps a watchful eye on students and workers within each and every field.

妻つまの負担ふたんを軽減けいげんして幸しあわせを優先ゆうせんすべく、通常つうじょうより倍ばいほどの期間きかんに渡わたり育休いくきゅうを申請しんせいする事ことにしました。

To ensure the prioritization of my wife's happiness, and reduction of her load, I decided to apply for around twice the childcare leave time, compared to the usual amount.

試験しけんで自分じぶんの実力じつりょくを出だすべく、お腹なかが空すいたり眠ねむくなったりしないように試験しけんの一週間いっしゅうかん前まえからコンディションを整ととのえる予定よていだ。

In order to bring out my true potential in the examination, I plan to make sure not to do things like get hungry or become tired from one week before the test, so that I can sort out my condition.

社長しゃちょうの演説えんぜつ：「当社とうしゃはＣＯシーオー２ツー排出量はいしゅつりょうの低減ていげん義務ぎむを果はたす可べく、工場こうじょうにＣＯシーオー２ツー専用せんようフィルタの取とり付つけを決定けっていいたしました。」

Company president speech: 'Our company has decided to install specialized CO2 filters in our factories to ensure that we meet CO2 emission reduction obligations.'

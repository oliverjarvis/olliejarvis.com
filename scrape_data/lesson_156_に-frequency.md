## About

About に (Frequency)

When the case marking particle に is used, sometimes it has the nuance of 'frequency'. This means that it can be utilized to describe how often something happens/is done. In this grammatical construction, the 'time frame' is followed by に, then followed by the number of times that you would like to say that something happens.

In this way, に keeps its regular nuance of being a 'goal', or 'destination', within which something else takes place.

私わたしは一いっヶ月かげつに一回いっかい友達ともだちと遊園地ゆうえんちに行いく。

I go to the amusement park with my friend every month. (One time per month)

彼かれは一週間いっしゅうかんに２に回かいしか歯はを磨みがかないから、口くちが臭くさい。

Because he only brushes his teeth twice every week, his mouth stinks.

Caution

This use of に should not be confused with で in relation to time. When で is used for 'time', it is expressing the amount of time that is required to do something, or how much time something is expected to take. It is not used for frequency.

２に年間ねんかんで５０ごじゅう万円まんえんも貯ためた。

In two years, I saved up ￥500,000. (Natural Japanese, as this expresses the amount of time that something took)

Due to this, you can think of に as marking a portion (or portions) of time inside of a larger space of time, within which something happens. However, で marks a whole space of time, within which something can be/is completed.

一年いちねんに三回さんかいぐらい海外旅行かいがいりょこうに行いく。

I take trips abroad about three times in a year. (frequency)

一日いちにちに六時間ろくじかんは寝ねる必要ひつようがある。

It is necessary to sleep for six hours every day. (frequency)

一時間いちじかんに十本じゅっぽんの電車でんしゃがある。

There are ten trains every hour. (frequency)

公務員こうむいんは一年いちねんに一回いっかいボーナスをもらう。

Public servants get a bonus every year. (Once each year) (Frequency)

彼かれはタバコを１いち日にちに４０よんじゅっ本ぽんぐらい吸すう。

He smokes about 40 cigarettes every day. (40 each day)

私わたしは子供こどもたちに１いっ週間しゅうかんに５００ごひゃく円えんのお小遣こづかいをあげる。

I give my kids 500 yen every week for their allowance. (500 yen per week)

給料きゅうりょうは月つきに２０にじゅう万まん円えんです。

I make 200,000 yen every month. (200,000 yen per month)

彼女かのじょは１いち日にちに１いち時間じかん日本語にほんごを勉強べんきょうしている。

She studies Japanese one hour every day. (One hour each day)

僕ぼくはよく運動うんどうするから、１いち日にちに３さん回かいプロテインを飲のむ必要ひつようがある。

Because I do a lot of exercise, I need to drink protein (shakes) three times every day.
(three times each day) (frequency)

この駅えきには１いち時間じかんに２に回かい電車でんしゃが来きます。

At this station, two trains come every hour. (Two trains each hour) (frequency)

うちの犬いぬは１いち日にちに２に回かい餌えさをあげなくてはいけない。

I have to feed my dog two times every day. (Two times each day) (frequency)

週しゅうに二回にかいは病院びょういんに来きてください。

Please come to the hospital twice every week. (Two times each week) (frequency)

風邪かぜを引ひきたくないから、１いち日にちに林檎りんごを１いっ個こ食たべて、水みずを２にリットル飲のむ。

I don't want to catch a cold, so I eat an apple and drink two liters of water every day. (Each day)

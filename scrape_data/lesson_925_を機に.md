## About

About を機に

English

Japanese

When used as a noun, 機き ‘opportunity’ indicates a time that is being used as suitable chance to do something. It is slightly formal and will usually be translated as ‘on the occasion of (A), (B)’. However, due to this grammar point being a construction of the adverbial particle を, 機き, and the case-marking particle に, it is indicating that the speaker is actively making use of the opportunity of (A)’. Due to this, it can be understood as ‘taking advantage of (A), (B)’, or ‘taking advantage of the time of (A), (B)’ on many occasions.

を機きに will directly follow nouns marking (A), before going on to explain how that (A) was utilized as an opportunity.

妻つまの妊娠にんしんを機きにタバコを止やめることにした。

Taking advantage of the time of my wife’s pregnancy, I have decided to quit smoking.

車検しゃけんを機きに、新あたらしい車くるまを買かうことにした。

On the occasion of my car inspection, I have decided to buy a new car. (Due to being told that the current car is probably beyond repair)

彼氏かれしと別わかれたのを機きに、新あたらしいバッグを買かった。

Making the most of the breakup with my boyfriend, I bought a new bag. (In order to feel new and fresh)

７年間ななねんかん使つかっていた携帯けいたいがとうとう壊こわれた。これを機きに機種きしゅ変更へんこうすることにした。

My cell phone, which I had been using for 7 years, finally broke. I decided to take this opportunity to change the model.

を機きに tends to be used most frequently when (B) is an action that the speaker is choosing to take primarily with (A) as the enabling factor, or being a good excuse.

## Examples

Examples

00:11

Sentence
Translation

小説しょうせつ：「３さんヶ月かげつ目めにやっと病気びょうきが完治かんちし、これを機きに、入院にゅういん後ご初はじめて退院たいいんすることができた。」

Novel: 'In the third month, she was finally completely cured of her illness, and this opportunity allowed her to leave the hospital for the first time since hospitalization.'

伝記でんき：「大学だいがく卒業そつぎょうを機きに大阪おおさかへ移うつり、プログラミングの職しょくを得えることができました。」

Biography: 'On the opportunity of graduation, I moved to Osaka and was able to land a programming job.'

演説えんぜつ：「今回こんかいの訪問ほうもんを機きに、より多おおくのアメリカ人じんが日本にほんで働はたらくことに興味きょうみを持もつことを願ねがっています。」

Speech: 'We hope that this visit will provide an opportunity for more Americans to be interested in working in Japan.'

小説しょうせつ：「結婚けっこんを機きにたまひこは東京とうきょうを離はなれ、千葉ちばの町まちでゆずきとふたりの新しん生活せいかつを始はじめじめた。」

Novel: 'On the opportunity of getting married, Tamahiko left Tokyo and started a new life with Yuzuki in the town of Chiba.'

伝記でんき：「ひろしは５６ごじゅうろく歳さいの時とき故郷こきょうへ戻もどったのを機きに、両親りょうしんの墓参はかまいりに行いった。」

Biography: 'When Hiroshi returned to his hometown at the age of 56, he took the opportunity to visit his parent's graves.'

この橋はしはアニメのエピソードに登場とうじょうしたことで有名ゆうめいになった。これを機きに、近隣きんりんからだけでなく全国ぜんこくから、大勢おおぜいの人ひとが訪おとずれるようになった。

This bridge became famous when it appeared in an episode of an anime. This opportunity led to a large number of visitors, not only from surrounding areas, but from all over the country.

## About

About ば〜ほど

English

Japanese

ば～ほど is a combination of the conjunction particle ば, and the noun ほど. It is used in a similar way to 'the more ~ the more' in English. Literally, it just highlights that 'if it is (A), then the limit of (A) will be controlled only by its extent'.

To use ば～ほど, ば will be paired with the hypothetical form of any verb/い-Adjective (or noun/な-Adjective with なら on the end), before the same word will be repeated in its attributive form. After this, a (B) part of the sentence will highlight what the expected result of (A) is.

いい肉にくは噛かめば噛かむほど味あじが出でる。

The more you chew good meat, the more flavorful it gets.

公園こうえんは広ひろければ広ひろいほどいい。

The bigger the park is, the better it is.

ビルは丈夫じょうぶならば丈夫じょうぶなほど安心あんしんできる。

The stronger a building is, the safer you feel.

プロならばプロほど速はやく泳およげる。

The more of a pro you are, the faster you can swim.

As である (the formal version of だ) may replace な with な-Adjectives (or なら with nouns), sometimes である will be used instead of な/なら, in regard to both nouns and な-Adjectives.

アプリが便利べんりであれば便利べんりであるほどユーザーが増ふえる。

The more useful an app is, the more users will increase.

職人しょくにんであれば職人しょくにんであるほど凄すごいものが作つくれる。

The more of a craftsman a person is, the more likely that they will be able to make something amazing.

Caution

When this expression is used with する verbs, the noun that する is linked to in the ば part of the sentence does not need to be repeated in the ほど part.

漢字かんじの勉強べんきょうをすればするほど色いろんな本ほんが読よめるようになる。

The more you study kanji, the more various books you will be able to read.

掃除そうじをすればするほど、部屋へやが綺麗きれいになっていく。

The more you clean, the cleaner your room will get.

Fun Fact

Sometimes, the ば 'hypothetical' part of the sentence will be omitted altogether, leaving ほど to perform a similar function by itself.

身体からだを鍛きたえるほど、身体からだが強つよくなる。

The more you workout, the stronger your body gets.

## Examples

Examples

Sentence
Translation

この豆まめを食たべれば食たべるほど痩やせるらしい。

I hear that the more of these beans you eat, the thinner you will become.

早はやければ早はやい程ほどいい。

The sooner the better.

綺麗きれいならば綺麗きれいなほど見み続つづけたくなる。

The more beautiful it is, the more I want to continue looking at it.

勝かてば勝かつほど、またやりたくなる。

The more you win, the more you want to play again.

勉強べんきょうすればする程ほど、日本語にほんごが上手うまくなる。

The more you study, the better you will be at Japanese.

聞きけば聞きくほど怪あやしい話はなしだ。

The more I hear that story, the more suspicious it becomes.

会あえば会あう程ほど、あの人ひとのことが好すきになっていく。

The more we meet, the more I grow fond of that person.

書かけば書かくほど書かきやすくなるペンだな。

The more I write with this pen, the easier it becomes to write with.

鍛きたえれば鍛きたえる程ほど、筋肉きんにくがつくからやめられない。

The more I workout, the more muscle I gain, so I can't stop.

スーパーは近ちかければ近ちかい程ほど買かい物ものに行いきやすい。

The closer the supermarket, the easier it is to go shopping.

進すすめば進すすむほど、引ひき返かえすのが難むずかしくなる。

The further you go, the harder it will become to turn back.

言いえば言いう程ほど、あの人ひととの関係かんけいは悪わるくなる。

The more I say, the worse my relationship with that person becomes.

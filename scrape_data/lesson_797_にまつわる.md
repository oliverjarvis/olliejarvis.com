## About

About にまつわる

English

Japanese

Making use of the う-Verb まつわる 'to coil around', にまつわる is a formal grammar pattern that follows nouns and expresses that something broadly discusses the topic of (A). It is usually translated as 'surrounding (A)', 'associated with (A)', or 'connected to (A)'.

In most cases, まつわる will be both preceded and followed by a noun, with the preceding noun being the topic, and the following noun being the thing that is dealing with that topic.

僕ぼくが小ちいさい頃ころは、おじいちゃんにこの地域ちいきにまつわる怖こわい話はなしをよくしてもらってた。

When I was little, my grandfather used to tell me scary stories connected to this area.

この監督かんとくの映画えいがは戦争せんそうにまつわる映画えいがが多おおい。

Many of this director's films are related to war.

彼かれはこの神社じんじゃにまつわる神話しんわには詳くわしい。

He is familiar with the myths connected to this shrine.

まつわる itself is primarily seen when (B) is some sort of information or explanation related to (A). Because of this, 話はなし 'tale', 物語ものがたり 'story', 伝説でんせつ 'legend', 神話しんわ 'myth', and similar words will often appear as (B).

Fun Fact

纏まつわる shares the same kanji as 纏まとまる, another common verb that means 'to come together', or 'to consolidate'. Although the kanji itself is rarely used outside of books, it may help to remember the nuance being conveyed in まつわる of 'coming together'. This 'coming together around (A)', or simply 'surrounding (A)' translates the feeling of the structure quite accurately.

There are more legends connected to this shrine than one can count.

This is a story surrounding "A Certain Magical Index".

ブログ記事きじ：「猫ねこに纏まつわる英語えいごの慣用表現かんようひょうげん６選ろくせん。」

Blog post title: 'A selection of 6 English idioms related to cats.'

楊よう貴妃きひには美貌びぼうに纏まつわる漢詩かんしが数多かずおおく残のこされている。

There is quite a bit of remaining poetry relating to Yang Guifei's beauty.

アクア女神めがみに纏まつわる神話しんわはこの町まちに代々だいだい伝つたえられている。

A myth surrounding the goddess Aqua has been passed down in this town for generations.

黒猫くろねこに纏まつわる夢ゆめは不吉ふきつとされているが、その噂うわさは本当ほんとうなのだろうか？

Dreams relating to black cats are considered unlucky, but I wonder if that rumor is really true.

ブラジル人じんが経営けいえいするこのカフェはブラジルに纏まつわる雑貨ざっかで装飾そうしょくされています。

This cafe operated by a Brazilian is decorated with various goods associated with Brazil.

ツアーガイド：「今回こんかいは、皆様みなさまに姫路城ひめじじょうに纏まつわる多おおくの逸話いつわについてご紹介しょうかいします。」

Tour guide: 'Now, I would like to tell you about numerous anecdotes associated with Himeji Castle.'

熱あつい指導しどうをする事ことで人気にんきな田中たなか先生せんせいに纏まつわる意外いがいなエピソードを聞ききました。

I heard an unexpected anecdote relating to Tanaka-sensei, who is popular for his passionate guidance.

学校がっこうに纏まつわる心霊しんれい現象げんしょうの話はなしを聞きいてからというもの、放課後ほうかごの学校がっこうは気味きみが悪わるく感かんじる。

After hearing about something like the apparition of a spirit associated with school, I have had an uneasy feeling about after class activities.

大学だいがく時代じだいに生物せいぶつを専攻せんこうしていた母ははは、ウイルスや免疫めんえきに纏まつわる豆知識まめちしきを多おおく持もっています。

My mother, who majored in biology during her university days, always has lots of snippets of information related to viruses and immunology.

ある映画えいが監督かんとくの巨匠きょしょうが映画えいが業界ぎょうかいに纏まつわる黒くろい実態じったいについて暴露ばくろした本ほんが飛とぶように売うれています。

A certain head film director's book that discloses the dark truth surrounding the entertainment industry is flying off the shelves.

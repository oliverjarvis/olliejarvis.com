## About

About ～ない～はない

～ない～はない is an example of a double negative in Japanese, and is a grammar construction that requires careful attention in order not to incorrectly interpret. Both the い-Adjective (形容詞けいようし), and auxiliary verb forms of ない may be used in this construction, depending on what type of word is being negated. Let's take a look at a few examples.

彼かれにはピアノで弾ひけない曲きょくはない。

There is no song that he can't play on the piano.

私わたしは美味おいしくない食たべ物ものは食たべない。

I don't eat food that does not taste good.

彼かれは安やすくない靴くつは買かわない。

He doesn't buy shoes that aren't cheap.

Literally, this grammar pattern is very similar to 'there isn't an (A) that doesn't (B)' in English.

Caution

This term is not to to be confused with なくはない, a more advanced grammar pattern which means 'It's not that it isn't (A)'.

私わたしに書かけない漢字かんじはない。

There is no kanji that I cannot write.

私わたしは漢字かんじを書かけなくはないけど...

It's not that I can't write kanji, but…

本当ほんとうに書かけない漢字かんじはないの？

Is there really not a single kanji that you cannot write?

夢ゆめを見みない人ひとはいないだろう。

There is not a single person who doesn't dream, right?

紙かみを使つかわない会社かいしゃは一ひとつもありません。

There is not a single company that does not use paper.

あそこのカフェで美味おいしくないコーヒーは絶対ぜったいない。

There is no coffee at that cafe that is not delicious.

勉強べんきょうしていない文法ぶんぽうはなかったから、テストには合格ごうかくしました。

There was not a single grammar point that I did not study, so I passed the test.

ボスの家いえにはテレビを置おいていない部屋へやはない。

There is not a single room in my boss' house that does not have a TV.

この大学だいがくで日本語にほんごを勉強べんきょうしていない人ひとはいません。

There is not a person who does not study Japanese at this university.

私わたしの家族かぞくの中なかで海外かいがいに行いったことのない人ひとはいない。

There is not a single person in my family who has not traveled abroad.

私わたしの友達ともだちの中なかでピザが好すきじゃない人ひとは一人ひとりもいない。

There is not a single person in my group of friends that doesn't like pizza.

映画えいがが面白おもしろくて、笑わらわない人ひとは一人ひとりもいなかった。

The movie was so funny that there was not a single person who did not laugh.

お金かねを持もっていない人ひとは買かい物ものに行いかない方ほうがいい。

People who do not have money, should not go shopping.

父親ちちおや：「新あたらしいクラスはどう？友達ともだちを作つくったの？」
子供こども：「好すきじゃないクラスメイトはいないけど、まだ、できてないよ。」

Father: 'How is the new class? Did you make any friends?'
Child: 'There are no classmates I don't like but I haven't made any new friends.'

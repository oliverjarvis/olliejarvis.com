## About

About 込む ②

English

Japanese

In Japanese, the う - Verb 込こむ 'to jam into' is frequently used as a suffix to convey several different meanings. The primary meanings are as follows:

(A) 込こむ - To enter completely into doing (A), or the state of being in (A).

(A) 込こむ - To enter a state of doing (A) that will not be considered complete until 'success'.

(A) 込こむ - To enter a state of doing (A) that cannot be escaped from.

In this lesson, we will exclusively focus on the second and third meanings, which are usually translated as 'to remain in (A)', 'to thoroughly do (A)', 'to do (A) deeply', or 'to do (A) intensely'. This form will be attached to the conjunctive form of other verbs.

警察けいさつが道みちの真まん中なかで座すわり込こんでいる人ひとに話はなしかけた。

The police went to talk to the person that remained seated in the middle of the road.

もうこんな時間じかんか、話はなし込こんじゃったな。

It's already this time, we got lost in talking, didn't we?

そんなに考かんがえ込こまなくてもいいよ。

You don't have to think so deeply about this.

今いまは取とり込こみ中ちゅうなので手てが空あいたらかけなおします。

I am held up working right now, so I will call you back once my hands are free.

Despite the multiple meanings, 込こむ can almost always be thought of as 'jamming (B) into (A)'. This means that whatever the (A) action is, will be something that is not easily entered, but also not easily escaped from.

みんないっせいに黙だまり込こんでいた。

At once, everyone sunk into an intense silence and remained silent.

ノベル：「れつこは気分きぶんが悪わるくなり、座すわり込こんだ。」

Novel: 'Retsuko felt weak and sat down.' (deeply・thoroughly)

疲つかれていたので、シャワーを浴あびずに眠ねむり込こんだ。

I was tired, so I fell deeply asleep without taking a shower. (intensely・thoroughly)

友達ともだちとの話はなし：「何なにを長々ながなが話はなし込こんでたの？」

Chat between friends: 'What were you so intently talking about for so long?' (deeply・thoroughly)

エッセイ：「熱心ねっしんにひとつの事ことに打うち込こむことが必要ひつようだと思おもっている。」

Essay: 'I think that devoting oneself deeply to one thing is important.'

小ちいさな花はなの写真しゃしんを撮とるためにしゃがみ込こむ。

To squat down deeply (with their face between their legs) in order to take a picture of a small flower.

友達ともだちとの話はなし：「テストの前まえに単語たんごを覚おぼえ込こむつもりだよ。」

Chat between friends: 'I plan to thoroughly memorize the vocabulary before the exam.'

会社かいしゃ、上司じょうし：「この資料しりょうは、明日あしたまでによく読よみ込こんでおいて。」
部下ぶか：「はい。」

The company, boss: 'Thoroughly read those documents by tomorrow.'
Subordinate: 'Understood.'

社長しゃちょう：「一日中いちにちじゅう考かんがえ込こんだあとで、会社かいしゃ社長しゃちょうを退任たいにんすることに決きめました。」

Company president: 'After having thought deeply about it all day, I have decided to step down from the position of company president.' (intensely・thoroughly)

明日あしたから旅行りょこうに行いくから、学校がっこうの課題かだいをやり込こんでおくつもりだ。

I am going on a trip tomorrow, so I plan to thoroughly do my school homework.

論文ろんぶん：「しかし、サンタクロースがいると信しんじ込こむ子供こどももいる。」

Essay/article: 'However, there are also children that firmly believe that Santa Claus exists.' (deeply・thoroughly)

ブログの投稿とうこう：「パスワードを思おもい出だせず、しばらく考かんがえ込こんだ。」

Blog post: 'Not being able to recall the password, I plunged deep into thought for a while.' (remained thinking・brood)

## About

About たなり・なり

English

Japanese

When following the た, past-tense form of a verb, the conjunction particle なり 'shape' indicates something existing in an ongoing state, similar to まま. This may be translated as 'while (A)', or 'remaining (A)'.

Due to ～たなり sentences usually describing something happening while in the state of (A), it will often be followed directly by a comma, or by the conjunctive form of the auxiliary verb だ, で. After this, the (B) phrase will then describe what is happening during (A).

～たなり will primarily be used to express an undesirable state continuing despite (A) having happened, although this is not a strict rule.

弟おとうとは彼女かのじょに振ふられて部屋へやに入はいったなり、全然ぜんぜん部屋へやから出でてこなくなってしまった。

Ever since my brother locked himself in his room after his girlfriend broke up with him, he hasn't come out of his room at all.

父ちちが釣つりにいったなりまだ帰かえってこない、大丈夫だいじょうぶかな...

My father hasn't come back home since he went out fishing. I wonder if he is okay…

兄あには上京じょうきょうしたなり、帰かえってこない。

My older brother hasn't come back, ever since he moved to Tokyo.

As ～たなり indicates that (A) is something that is continuing to stay in the same state, it will often have the nuance of 'ever since (A)' in situations where a significant amount of time has passed.

Caution

This なり derives from the noun 形なり 'shape' in its hiragana form, and is different to the なり that is used as the classic copula.

## Examples

Examples

Sentence
Translation

彼かれは腰こしかけたなりじっと考かんがえていた。

While remaining seated, he has been pondering intently.

町まちを離はなれたなり消息しょうそくは途絶とだえた。

We haven't heard from her ever since she left the city.

３年さんねん前まえに涙なみだながらに家出いえでしたなり、帰かえってこない。

She ran away three years ago in tears, and hasn't come back (ever) since.

レース後ご、草くさの上うえに横よこになったなり、泣なき続つづけた。

After the race, he cried while lying down on the grass.

宿泊客しゅくはくきゃくの一人ひとりがドアを開あけたなりにしてあります。

One of the hotel guests left the doors open.

彼女かのじょは写真しゃしんを見みつめたなり、まばたきせずに目めを見開みひらいていた。

She kept staring at the picture intently, without blinking, with her eyes wide open.

ちょっと叱しかっただけで息子むすこは自室じしつに入はいったなり、一向いっこうに出でてこなくなってしまった。

After scolding my son just a little bit, ever since entering his room, he will not come out at all.

学校がっこうに出でかけたなり夜よるになっても帰かえってこない娘むすめを家族かぞく総出そうでで探さがしています。

After not returning home even late at night ever since heading to school, the whole family has been searching together for my daughter.

核心かくしんに迫せまる質問しつもんを投なげかけると、夫おっとは下したを向むいたなり黙だまり込こんでしまいました。

After throwing out a question aimed right at the heart of the issue, while looking down, my husband was completely silent.

子猫こねこは背せの高たかい木きの枝えだから落おちたなりピクリとも動うごかなくなってしまいました。

Ever since falling from the branch of a high tree, my kitten started to not even move an inch.

これから一人ひとり暮ぐらしを始はじめる息子むすこが家いえから出でたなり、妻つまは声こえを出だして泣なき始はじめました。

While our son was leaving home to start living independently from now on, my wife called out and began crying.

部活ぶかつから帰かえってきた弟おとうとが玄関げんかんに倒たおれ込こんだなり寝ねてしまった姿すがたを見みて、弟おとうとが相当そうとう疲つかれている事ことがわかりました。

Seeing the figure of my little brother remain asleep in the entrance to our house ever since having fallen over after returning home from club activities, I realized that he was considerably worn-out.

## About

About ずくめ

English

Japanese

Originally stemming from the suffix use of 尽づく 'completely', or 'using solely', and the addition of め, ずくめ came to be preferentially written as hiragana, with ず replacing づ. This structure may be translated as 'completely (A)', 'all in (A)', or 'nothing but (A)'. Generally, it is attached directly to nouns, and expresses that (A) is to such an extent that nothing apart from (A) can be seen, felt, or noticed.

In cases where ずくめ will directly modify the following noun, の will come between ずくめ and that noun.

今月こんげつも仕事しごとずくめになりそうだ。

It seems like I'm going to be doing nothing but work again this month.

最近さいきんは嫌いやなことずくめで全然ぜんぜん楽たのしくない。

I'm not having the best time right now, because nothing but bad things are happening.

彼かれは黒くろずくめの男おとこたちに誘拐ゆうかいされた。

He was kidnapped by men covered in black.

警察けいさつへの電話でんわ：「もしもし、家いえの前まえに黒くろずくめの車くるまが何台なんだいも止とまっているんで今いまから言いう住所じゅうしょまできてもらってもいいですか。」

Calling the cops: Hello, there are several jet black cars parked in front of my house, so could you please come to the address I'm about to tell you?

Although づくめ may occasionally be seen in older literature, it is mostly considered incorrect kana usage these days.

While ずくめ may be used in either positive or negative contexts, it is mainly seen in set expressions, so is best used with combinations that you have already heard or read.

Fun Fact

尽づくし is another older structure that is used to express things that are separate, but are grouped together. This is usually translated as 'all sorts of (A)', or 'all kinds of (A)'. It is the conjunctive form of the う-Verb 尽つくす 'to be exhaustive' with a voiced pronunciation of the つ.

国くにづくしの偉えらい人ひとが集あつまった。

Great people from all sorts of countries gathered.

新聞しんぶんの見出みだし：「６月ろくがつ猛暑もうしょ記録きろくずくめ」

Newspaper headline: 'June - A Heatwave full of Temperature Records'

規制きせいは必要ひつようであるが規則きそくずくめで窒息ちっそくしそうな社会しゃかいは強圧的きょうあつてきではないだろうか。

Regulations are needed, but isn't a society that is about to suffocate with nothing but restrictions overbearing ?

２０２０にせんにじゅう年ねんは異例いれいずくめの大変たいへんな年としでしたが何なんとか乗のり切きりました。

2020 was a very challenging year full of irregularities, but we somehow got through it.

ツイッターの投稿とうこう：「朝あさから晩ばんまで、ごちそうずくめで結婚けっこんを祝いわう。」

Twitter post: 'We are celebrating the marriage with nothing but delicacies from morning till night.'

黒くろずくめのスーツに身みを包つつんだ男おとこはベンチに座すわり、逆さかさに持もった新聞しんぶんを読よむふりをしていた。

A man dressed in a completely black suit was sitting on a bench and pretending to read a newspaper that was upside down.

今日きょうは妹いもうとが生うまれたし大学だいがくに受うかったし朝あさからいいことずくめで、万々歳ばんばんざいだ。

Today calls for celebration, because nothing but good things have been happening since the morning, like my younger sister being born, and my acceptance to university.

子供こども：「野菜やさいずくめのこの料理りょうりなんて美味おいしくなさそうで食たべたくないよ！」

Child: ‘This food is nothing but vegetables and doesn’t seem tasty so I don’t wanna eat it!’

独ひとり言ごと：「最近さいきんは良いい事ことずくめだから、そろそろ悪わるい事ことが起おこりそう…。」

Mumblings: ‘It’s been nothing but good things recently, so it seems like something bad is about to happen…’

新記録しんきろくずくめの大会たいかいを会場かいじょうで観戦かんせんする事ことが出来できて、とても嬉うれしいです。

Since I was able to spectate an event that had a real streak of new records, I am really thrilled!

映画えいがのナレーター：「彼女かのじょの幸しあわせずくめの毎日まいにちはここで終おわりを告つげました…。」

Movie narrator: ‘It announced to her that her days of nothing but happiness were over.’

父ちちは働はたらきずくめの今いまの生活せいかつに不満ふまんは無ないと言いっていたが、絶対ぜったいに少すこしはあるはずだ。

Although dad was saying that he has no grievances with his current lifestyle of nothing but work, he absolutely has at least some.’

同僚どうりょうの高橋たかはしさんは全身ぜんしんブランドずくめですが、一体いったいそんなお金かねはどこから湧わいているのでしょう…。

My colleague Takahashi-san is covered from head to toe in branded clothes without exception, but where the heck does the money sprout up from for that…’

会社員かいしゃいん：「残業ざんぎょうずくめの毎日まいにちなんて、もう沢山たくさんだ！こんな会社かいしゃさっさと辞やめないと、絶対ぜったいに精神せいしんが病やんでしまう…！」

Company employee: ‘Something like having nothing but overtime work every day, I am at my wits end! If I don’t quit this company straight away my mental health is going to deteriorate...!’

## About

About 当たり

English

Japanese

When attached to the end of numbers or counters, the noun 当あたり 'to hit' will be used as a suffix. In these cases, the meaning is usually translated as 'per (A)', or 'each (A)'. Let's take a look at some examples.

一個いっこ当あたり五ご円えんで作つくることができます。

We are able to make this for 5 yen each.

一食いっしょく当あたりのカロリーは５００ごひゃっｋＣａｌキロカロリーです。

It has 500 calories per serving.

Fun Fact

While the literal meaning of 当あたり is 'to meet' (as in 'to come into physical contact with'), as a suffix, 当あたり has a nuance that is similar to に対たいして 'to be in relation to'. This similarity can be seen in the following:

(A) 当あたり (B) - Regarding (A), (B) equates to it.

(A) に対たいして (B) - Regarding (A), (B) equates to it.

この車くるまは１台いちだい当あたり１いっトンあります。

This car equates to 1 ton per vehicle.

一人ひとり当あたり５０００ごせん円えんで入場にゅうじょうできます。

The price for entry per person equates to 5000 yen.

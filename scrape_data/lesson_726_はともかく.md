## About

About はともかく

English

Japanese

はともかく is an expression which makes use of the adverbial-particle は, and the adverb 兎とも角かく meaning 'to set aside', or 'at any rate'. This structure will be used when the speaker wants to change the topic from (A) to (B), or mention (B) as being something additional on top of (A).

はともかく is primarily seen following nouns in their dictionary forms. In many cases, this will be when (A) is something that the speaker thinks is not worth considering, or not worth making an issue out of when (B) is more important.

この料理りょうりの見みた目めはともかく、味あじはとてもいい。

Apart from the appearance of this dish, it is very delicious.

家賃やちんが高たかいか安やすいかはともかく、東京とうきょう駅えきの近ちかくに住すみたい。

Setting aside whether the rent is expensive or cheap, I want to live near Tokyo station.

給料きゅうりょうはともかく、やりがいのある仕事しごとがしたい。

Setting aside the pay, I want a worthwhile job.

Fun-fact

The adverb 兎とも角かく is thought to have come from the Buddhist expression 兎角亀毛とかくきもう, meaning 'horns on rabbits, fur on turtles', referring to things that do not exist. Although this particular meaning is not widely accepted as being strongly related to the modern meaning of 兎とも角かく, some relation can be drawn between it and English phrases such as '(A) is neither here nor there' in reference to something that is non-existent, or of little importance.

美人びじんか美人びじんじゃないかはともかく、優やさしくて料理りょうりが上手じょうずな人ひとと結婚けっこんをしたい。

I want to marry someone who is kind and a good cook, whether she is beautiful or not is neither here nor there.

冗談じょうだんは兎とも角かく、そろそろビジネスの話はなしだ。

All jokes aside, let's get down to business.

家屋かおくは兎とも角かくとしてもだ、せめてアルバムだけでも持もって逃にげたい。

Setting aside the house, I at least want to take the album and run.

カタカナは兎とも角かく、平仮名ひらがなは読よめないと困こまります。

Setting aside katakana, you will be in trouble if you can't read hiragana.

楽器がっきは兎とも角かく、せめて歌うただけでも歌うたいましょうよ。

Setting aside instruments, let's at least sing a song.

関東かんとうは兎とも角かく、関西かんさいでだけでもいいから売うれたい。

Apart from Kanto, even if it is just Kansai that's fine, I just want to sell it.

看病かんびょうは兎とも角かく、買かい物ものだけでもしてもらえると助たすかります。

Putting aside the caregiving, if you could at least do the shopping (for me), it would be a big help.

行事ぎょうじは兎とも角かく、授業じゅぎょうにはちゃんと出席しゅっせきしてください。

Apart from the event, please also properly attend class.

このスマホの形かたちは兎とも角かく、性能せいのうは一線いっせん級きゅうだと思おもってる。

Apart from the shape of this smartphone, I think its performance is top-notch.

この実験じっけん、方法ほうほうは兎とも角かくとして、結果けっかは予想よそう外がいに素晴すばらしい。

This experiment, setting methods aside, has unexpectedly amazing results.

怒おこった妻つま：「どうしてこんな夜よる遅おそくに帰かえってきたの？それは兎とも角かく、あなたの服ふく、どこなの？」

An angry wife: 'Why are you coming home so late? Apart from that, your clothes, where are they?'

外部がいぶの人間にんげんは兎とも角かく、内部ないぶの人間にんげんにはちゃんと機密きみつを守まもってもらいます。

Apart from the outsiders, the insiders will properly protect the secret.

日本語にほんご能力のうりょく試験しけんに合格ごうかくしたかは兎とも角かく、過去かこ半年はんとし間かんにたくさんのことを学まなんだ。

Setting aside whether I passed the JLPT or not, I have learned a lot over the last six months.

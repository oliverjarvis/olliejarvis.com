## About

About そういえば

English

Japanese

そういえば is a hypothetical structure in Japanese consisting of the adverb そう 'like that', and the ば conjunctive form of the う-Verb 言いう 'to say'. Common translations include 'speaking of which', 'by the way', and 'come to think of it'. These translations are actually very close to the literal translation of 'if saying that' in that they all refer back to the previous statement as being a trigger for bringing up a topic that the speaker wants to discuss.

そういえば will primarily appear at the beginning of sentences, before the speaker will then bring up the topic that they were reminded of.

そういえば、この前まえ借かりた本ほん読よみ終おわったよ！めちゃくちゃ面白おもしろかった！

By the way, I finished reading the book I borrowed from you the other day! It was super interesting!

そういえば、大家おおやさんに家賃やちん振ふり込こんでくれた？

Speaking of which, did you transfer the rent to the landlord for me?

そういえば、さっき駅えきでお母かあさんの友達ともだちに会あったよ。よろしくだって。

Speaking of which, I met your friend at the station. She told me to tell you she said hi.

Caution

Due to そういえば refering to something that has already been brought up over the course of a conversation, it will not be used to move on to completely unrelated topics in the same way that phrases like 'by the way' are used in English.

中田なかたさん： 今度こんどバッティングセンターに行いこ！
高橋たかはしさん：そういえば、藤田ふじたさんと伊藤いとうくんが付つき合あい始はじめたって聞きいた？

Nakata-san: Let's go to the batting cage next time!
Takahashi-san: Speaking of which, did you hear that Fujita-san and Ito-kun has started dating? (Unnatural Japanese)

Fun-fact

そういえば is very similar in the way it is used to といえば, with そう simply replacing the direct quotation of と. Many textbooks will list both as a single grammar point, despite そういえば itself basically being a set phrase.

Come to think of it, it is Mother's Day today.

そういえば、昨夜さくやは何時なんじに家いえに帰かえったの？

Speaking of which, what time did you get home last night?

そういえば、着替きがえ持もってきてくれた？

Speaking of which, did you bring the change of clothes for me?

そういえば、昨日きのうの卒業式そつぎょうしきはどうだった？

Speaking of which, how was the graduation ceremony yesterday?

そういえば、客席きゃくせきに君きみのお父とうさんとお母かあさんがいたよ。

Speaking of which, your father and mother were in the guest seating!

そういえば、お芝居しばいの稽古けいこは順調じゅんちょう？

Speaking of which, is the play rehearsal going well?

そういえば、蛍光灯けいこうとうが切きれたから買かわないといけません。

Speaking of which, the fluorescent light went out, so I have to buy one.

そういえば、最近さいきん全然ぜんぜん親孝行おやこうこうしてないな...

Speaking of which, recently I haven't been treating my parents with filial piety at all...

そういえば、今度こんどコーラスやるんだけど、一緒いっしょにやりませんか？

Speaking of which, I will be singing in a chorus, won't you join me?

そういえば、今度こんどのイベントで機関車きかんしゃに乗のれるみたいですよ。

Speaking of which, it looks like you can ride a locomotive at the upcoming event!

そういえば、クリーニングに出だしていたのを忘わすれてた。取とりに行いかないと。

Speaking of which, I forgot that I had sent stuff to the cleaners. I have to go get it.

そういえば、今度こんど金魚きんぎょ屋やさんに行いくんだけど、買かってきてあげようか？

Speaking of which, I am going to the goldfish shop. Shall I buy you one?

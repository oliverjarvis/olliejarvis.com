## About

About やや

やや is an adverb in Japanese that is similar to 僅わずか in that it means 'a little bit'. However, the key nuance with やや is that the what is being described has the tendency of (A), or is expressing (A) to a sufficient degree that it cannot be denied. It may also be translated as 'marginally', or 'slightly'.

Like many other adverbs of time or amount, やや will often appear at the beginning of sentences.

明日あしたは友達ともだちのお見舞みまいに行いかないといけないので、やや遅おくれます。

Tomorrow, I have to go visit my friend at the hospital, so I will be a little bit late.

この家具かぐは、やや傾かたむいているので直なおさないといけません。

This furniture is slightly slanted, so I need to fix it.

明日あしたは今日きょうよりもやや暑あつくなる見込みこみです。

Tomorrow is expected to be a little bit hotter than today.

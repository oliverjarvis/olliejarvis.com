## About

About べき

English

Japanese

The auxiliary verb べき, being the attributive form of べし, is a structure in Japanese that is often paired with verbs to express that something 'ought to be done'. It implies some sort of moral obligation, or that (A) is the only reasonable course of action. This comes from the kanji 可べき, which carries the meaning of being something 'permissible' (indicating that any other action would not be acceptable).

べき will be paired with the plain (non-past) form of a verb, before being followed by either だ, or another noun.

そういう事ことは本人ほんにんに言いうべきだ。

You ought to say things like that to the person themselves.

お客きゃくさんの意見いけんはちゃんと聞きくべきだ。

You ought to properly listen to customers' opinions.

ハマサキさんは尊敬そんけいするべき人ひとだ。

Hamasaki-san is someone you ought to look up to.

あなたに教おしえるべき事ことはこれで全部ぜんぶです。

With this, I have now taught you all of the things that I ought to.

べき is also sometimes translated as 'must do (A)', or 'should do (A)' but it is important to remember that it will always have the nuance of being the only 'permissible' choice, and is therefore very strong.

Caution

It is important to note that the verb appearing with べき cannot be in past tense, and you will need to use だった or でした after べき, if you would like to say that something 'ought to have been done' in the past.

やばいやばい、車くるまが止とまりそう！高速こうそくに乗のる前まえにガソリンを入いれておくべきだった。

This is not good… ahhh not good, the car is about to stop! I should have filled the car up with gasoline before I got on the highway.

大変たいへん申もうし訳わけございません。こちらがもっと早はやく気きづくべきでした。

We are very sorry. We should have noticed this earlier.

Fun Fact

In modern Japanese, there are only 18 auxiliary verbs, of which べき is not one. べし (the standard form) is considered to be a classical auxiliary verb. Due to this, べき (and べし) are often referred to as classical auxiliary verbs.

Fun Fact

The combination of する and べき is a rare grammatical exception in Japanese, in which two forms are both considered equally correct. Due to this, either するべき, or すべき may be used.

結婚けっこんする前まえに相手あいてと将来しょうらいの話はなしをするべきだ。

Before marrying, you should talk about the future with your partner.

家族かぞくは何なにがあっても大切たいせつにすべきだ。

You should prioritize family no matter what happens.

You should pay taxes.

There are too many things I should think about, so my head hurts.

Saying things that you should say is a duty of a parent.

誰だれも見みていなくても、法律ほうりつは守まもる可べきだ。

Even when no one is looking, you should obey the law.

明日あしたの会議かいぎは重大じゅうだいな話はなしがあるので、出席しゅっせきをする可べきだ。

Because we will talk about something important at tomorrow's meeting, everyone should attend.

君きみが責任せきにんを取とる可べきだ。リーダーなんだから。

You should take responsibility since you are the leader.

この茶碗ちゃわんを買かう可べきだ。こんな掘ほり出だし物ものはあまりないよ。

You should buy this bowl. It is not often that you come across such a bargain.

日本語にほんごを声こえに出だして読よむ可べきだ。その方ほうが上達じょうたつするから。

You should read Japanese aloud, because you will get better that way.

失敗しっぱいしたら早はやめに報告ほうこくす可べきだ。隠かくすと余計よけいに大変たいへんなことになる。

If you make a mistake, you should report it as soon as possible. If you try to hide it, it will only get worse.

痩やせたい人ひとは運動うんどうをす可べきだと思おもう。

I think people who want to slim down should exercise.

This book has a lot of information that people who learn Japanese should know.

さっきあの人ひとに言いわれたことを妻つまに言いう可べきかどうか悩なやむ。

I'm wondering if I should tell my wife what that person said to me.

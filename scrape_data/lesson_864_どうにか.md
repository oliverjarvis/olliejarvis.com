## About

About どうにか

English

Japanese

どうにか is an adverb which is used when something happens 'somehow or other'. It is often used when the speaker has some reason to believe a desire or goal is unlikely to be achieved, yet somehow in the end the desired outcome still occurs.

どうにか is often translated as 'somehow or other' or 'one way or the other'.

条件じょうけんに不安ふあんはあったみたいだけど、どうにか説得せっとくすることができた。

It appears that there were concerns about the conditions, but I was somehow able to persuade them.

どうにか明日あしたまでに解決かいけつしなくてはならない。

I have to solve this problem one way or another by tomorrow.

In some contexts どうにか is also translated as 'barely', emphasizing that effort was put into achieving a result. The result may not be exactly as originally desired, but it has still 'somehow' been managed.

そんなどうにか聞きこえる声こえで話はなされたら何なに言いってるかわからないよ。

If you talk to me with a voice that I can barely hear, I won’t understand what you are trying to say.

どうにか is often paired with the transitive verb する or the intransitive verb なる. どうにかなる means ‘to somehow turn out alright (on its own)’, and implies that things will work out without some specific intervention. どうにかする means ‘to somehow manage to do’, and implies that the method for resolving the situation is uncertain but that by some effort it is possible to solve.

そんな落おち込こむなって、時間じかんが経たてばどうにかなるよ。

Don’t be that upset, things will work out over time.

お母かあさんのこと聞きいたぞ。こっちはこっちでどうにかするから、今いまは家族かぞくとの時間じかんを大切たいせつにしろ。

I heard about your mother. We will work things out on our end, so prioritize spending time with your family right now.

Sentence
Translation

今日きょうの午後ごごまでにどうにか帰かえれたらいいんだけど・・・。

It’d be nice if I could somehow make it back by this afternoon...

どうにかして敵てきの攻撃こうげきを回避かいひしなければ！ このままでは私わたしたちの方ほうが先さきに滅ほろびてしまう！

We need to somehow avoid the enemy’s attack! At this rate we’ll be destroyed first!

どうにか明日あしたまでに終おわらせないと。

I have to somehow get this finished by tomorrow.

どうにかステージの後方こうほうまで声こえを届とどけてくれないと困こまるよ。

It’ll be an issue if you can’t somehow project your voice to the back of the stage.

雨あめがどうにか止やんでほしいと、心こころから願ねがっている。

From the bottom of my heart, I wish that it would somehow stop raining.

どうにか電車でんしゃさえ動うごいてくれたら、実家じっかに帰かえることができるのに。

If only the trains would somehow run, then I’d be able to return home.

全すべてが崩くずれ去さる寸前すんぜんだったが、彼かれの一言ひとことがどうにか皆みなの気持きもちをつなぎとめた。

Everything was on the precipice of ruin, but with a word from him, somehow everyone managed to pull it together.

初対面しょたいめんの人ひとには緊張きんちょうしがちなのだが、どうにか笑顔えがおだけは忘わすれないようにしている。

I tend to get nervous when I meet people for the first time, but somehow I always try to not forget to smile.

途中とちゅうで誰だれかに笑わらわれてもかまわない。私わたしはどうにかこの夢ゆめを形かたちにしたいんだ。

I don’t mind if I am laughed at along the way. I want to somehow give shape to this dream.

住民じゅうみんからの反対はんたいが強つよいという理由りゆうで、計画けいかくの見直みなおしを迫せまられたが、どうにか妥協案だきょうあんを取とりまとめた。

Due to strong opposition from residents, we were forced to change the plan. However, somehow we pulled together a compromise.

理屈りくつでは説明せつめいしきれないほどの困難こんなんに直面ちょくめんしながらも、私わたしはどうにか立たち止どまらずに歩あるき続つづけた。

Even while confronting difficulties which were beyond logical explanation, I somehow continued walking without stopping.

先方せんぽうにプレゼンをするまでにどうにか時間稼じかんかせぎをしてほしい。

I want you to somehow buy us some time before we do the presentation for the client.

どうにか私わたしの作品さくひんを見みていただくことはできませんか？

Would it somehow be possible for you to take a look at my work?

どうにか付つき合あいたての私わたしたちのように、関係かんけいを戻もどせないかな？

I wonder if we could somehow restore our relationship to how it was when we first started dating?

彼女かのじょの心こころはすでに離はなれていたが、私わたしはどうにかしてその理由りゆうを知しろうと、言葉ことばを選えらびながら話はなしかけた。

Her heart had already left me, but endeavoring to somehow know the reason, I carefully chose my words while addressing her.

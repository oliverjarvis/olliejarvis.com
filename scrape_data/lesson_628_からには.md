## About

About からには

English

Japanese

からには is an expression in Japanese that is used to indicate when something is either obvious or determined upon based on the previous information. This may translate as 'as long as (A), (B)', 'since (A), (B)', or 'given that (A), (B)'. It is a combination of から, に, and は, and literally means something like 'from within (A), (B)'.

からには may follow any word in their standard forms, but occasionally also follows nouns or な-Adjectives that end with である.

沖縄おきなわに来きたからには、一番いちばん綺麗きれいなビーチへ行いきたい。

Since I am in Okinawa, I want to go to the prettiest beach.

高たかいスマホを買かったからには、様々さまざまな機能きのうを使つかいこなさなければならない。

Given that I bought an expensive smartphone, I need to make the most of its various features.

有名ゆうめいになったからには、その影響力えいきょうりょくを良いい方向ほうこうに使つかいたい。

Given that I have become famous, I want to use that influence in a positive course.

兄弟きょうだいとして生うまれたからには、お互たがいに助たすけ合あうつもりだ。

Since we were born as siblings, we intend to help each other out.

からには focuses mainly on the determination of the speaker that (A) is something that is the 'base' state (emphasized with には), so going from that point (emphasized with から) is either unavoidable, or the most logical. The second part of the sentence will then make some statement about that determination. This may either be a personal opinion about the situation, or what the speaker intends to do about it.

Caution

Sometimes, からは may be seen by itself. However, this pattern is relatively more formal, and will not usually be used in spoken language.

教師きょうしであるからは、生徒せいとの手本てほんとなるべし。

As long as you are a teacher, you must be an example to your students.

日本にほんに住すむからは、日本語にほんごを勉強べんきょうするべきだ。

As long as I am living in Japan, I must study Japanese.

二十歳はたちになるからには、ドライブしに行いこう！

Since I will be turning 20, let's go for a drive!

インタビューをするからには、本音ほんねを聞きき出だしたい。

Since we are doing an interview, I want to get their true opinion.

休業きゅうぎょうするからには、事前じぜんの説明せつめいをきちんとしてください。

As long as you are going to shutdown your business, please give a precise explanation in advance.

行事ぎょうじを任まかされたからには、絶対ぜったいに成功せいこうさせます。

Since I was entrusted with the event, I will definitely make it a success.

演劇えんげきをやるからには、まずは発声はっせい練習れんしゅうが大切たいせつです。

Since you will be performing, to start, speaking practice is important.

植木うえき職人しょくにんになるからには、辛つらい下積したづみ生活せいかつも覚悟かくごしなければいけない。

Since he will become a gardener, he must be prepared to live a hard working class life.

時間じかんを延長えんちょうするからには、それなりの料金りょうきんを支払しはらう必要ひつようがあります。

Since you are getting a time extension, it will be necessary to pay that fee.

温泉おんせんに行いくからには、美味おいしい料理りょうりを出だしてくれる宿やどに泊とまりたい。

As long as we are going to an onsen, I want to stay at an inn that will provide delicious food.

衆議院しゅうぎいんを解散かいさんするからには、ちゃんとした大義たいぎが必要ひつようだ。

Since the lower house is going to be dissolved, proper moral law is needed.

車くるまを自分じぶんで改造かいぞうするからには、それなりの技術ぎじゅつがないと後あとが大変たいへんだ。

Since you are going to remodel the car by yourself, if you don't have the skills, it will be no good down the road.

楽器がっきをやるからには、近所きんじょ迷惑めいわくにならないように配慮はいりょしてください。

As long as you are going to play an instrument, please give consideration as to not become a neighborhood nuisance.

日本にほんに行いくからには、できるだけ日本にほんの文化ぶんかや食たべ物ものを体験たいけんしたり、味あじわってみるべきだ。

As long as I am going to Japan, I should try to experience Japanese culture and food as much as possible.

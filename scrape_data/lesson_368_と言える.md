## About

About と言える

English

Japanese

と言いえる is a common expression that utilizes the potential form of the verb 言いう 'to say', to highlight that it is possible for something to be said. This phrase is often used to express a general idea about something that the speaker believes to be correct. と言いえる translates as 'it is fair to say (A)', or 'one could say (A)'.

As the case marking particle と is being used in its 引用いんよう (citation) use, と言いえる may follow any direct phrase/statement.

この儀式ぎしきは日本にほんの文化ぶんかの一ひとつと言いえる。

It is fair to say that this ceremony is part of Japan's culture.

あのビルは日本一にっぽんいち高たかいビルだと言いえる。

One could say that the building over there is the tallest building in Japan.

と言いえる is regularly seen paired with grammar patterns like だろう, or でしょう. This makes the overall statement more friendly, by considering the listener's thoughts about the issue being presented. と言いえるだろう can simply be translated as 'can we not say (A)?', or '(A) can be said, right?'.

このプランは成功せいこうしたと言いえるだろう。

We could say that this plan was a success, right?

タナカ選手せんしゅは国民的こくみんてきアスリートだと言いえるでしょう。

Can it not be said that Tanaka is an athlete loved by everyone in the country?

Caution

When the potential form of 言いう is linked with the auxiliary verb よう, it creates a much stronger phrase that is similar to 'let it be said that (A)'.

人ひとが話はなしているときに、携帯けいたいを見みるのは失礼しつれいだといえよう。

Let it be said that looking at your phone while someone is talking to you is rude. (Anyone would say this, so don't make me say it to you)

Fun Fact

The speaker is usually relatively confident about their statement when using と言いえる, but the usage of a form that expresses conjecture shows that they are open to the possibility of being wrong, thus softening the tone.

## Examples

Examples

Sentence
Translation

彼かれらは同時どうじにゴールインしたと言いえるだろう。

One could say that they got to the finish line at the same time.

船ふねは飛行機ひこうきほど速はやくないが、それでも船旅ふなたびを好このむ人ひとは多おおいと言いえるだろう。

Boats are not as fast as planes are, but it is still fair to say that a lot of people enjoy trips by boat.

発明家はつめいかは、「変人へんじん」という評価ひょうかを一般いっぱんの人ひとから下くだされてきたと言いえるだろう。

You can probably say that the inventors were labeled as 'eccentrics' by the common folk.

２０にじゅっ世紀せいきにはコンピュータによって生活せいかつしやすくなってきたと言いえるだろう。

It is fair to say that life is getting easier due to (the advent of) computers in the 20th century.

このマニフェストは自然しぜん環境かんきょうの現在げんざいの状態じょうたいについてのひとつの意見いけんだと言いえるだろう。

One could say that this manifesto is one of the opinions about the current state of the natural environment.

古ふるい中国語ちゅうごくごの発音はつおんが、現在げんざいの日本語にほんごに大おおきく影響えいきょうしていると言いえるだろう。

You can probably say that the influence of ancient Chinese pronunciation on modern Japanese is vast.

日本にほんは独自どくじの文化ぶんかで国際的こくさいてきに有名ゆうめいだと言いえるだろう。

It is fair to say that Japan is internationally famous for its unique culture.

スマホやタブレットのために子供こどもがいなくなった遊あそび場ばは時代じだいの象徴しょうちょうだと言いえるだろう。

You could say that, due to smartphones and tablets, empty playgrounds are a sign of our times.

彼女かのじょの長所ちょうしょは働はたらき者ものであることだと言いえるだろう。

One could probably say that her strong point is being a hard worker.

私わたしに言いわせれば、彼かれはポーランドのスティーブン・スピルバーグのような人ひとだったと言いえるでしょう。

If you ask me, I would probably say that he was something like a Polish Steven Spielberg.

話はなしている最中さいちゅうに割わり込こむのは、明あきらかに失礼しつれいだと言いえるだろう。

It is fair to say that interrupting someone when they are in the middle of talking is clearly rude.

前会計年度ぜんかいけいねんどに比くらべて、今年度こんねんどは成功せいこうだと言いえるでしょう。

Compared to the previous fiscal year, it is fair to say that this year is a success.

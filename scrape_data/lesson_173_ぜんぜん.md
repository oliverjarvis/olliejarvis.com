## About

About ぜんぜん

In Japanese, there are several adverbs that are traditionally used exclusively with negative expressions (containing ない). 全然ぜんぜん is one of them. When used at the beginning of a ない (or ません) containing phrase, 全然（ぜんぜん) has the meaning of 'completely not (A)', or 'not (A) at all'.

やばい、トイレットペーパーが全然ぜんぜんない。

Oh shoot, I don't have any toilet paper at all.

彼女かのじょは家事かじの手伝てつだいを全然ぜんぜんしなかったので別わかれました。

I broke up with her because she didn't help out at all with house chores.

In writing, it is equally as natural to use either 全然ぜんぜん as kanji, or hiragana only, so both forms may be used confidently.

ぜんぜん is more flexible (and friendly sounding) in casual conversation than 全まったく, a similar expression which has a formal (often negative) nuance.

今月こんげつもお金かねが全然ぜんぜん無ないからパンだけを食たべる。

I don't have any money this month either, so I am only eating bread.

昨日きのう後輩こうはいからもらったお土産みやげは全まったく美味おいしくありませんでしたね。

The souvenir we got from our junior member yesterday was not tasty at all, don't you think?

While both of these sentences have the meaning of 'not (A) at all', まったく could give the listener the impression that you are not pleased with the situation. This will depend quite a bit on tone of voice, though.

Caution

Despite originally being used only with negative expressions, it is becoming more and more common for ぜんぜん to appear with positive expressions as well. In these cases, it just has the meaning of 'totally (A)', or 'completely (A)'.

明日あした？全然ぜんぜんいいよ！

Tomorrow? That's completely fine!

Although this is technically not grammatically correct, it is becoming so frequently used that it is considered correct by most people (especially the younger generation).

Sentence
Translation

着物きものが全然ぜんぜん似合にあわない。

Kimonos don't look good on me at all.

全然ぜんぜん頑張がんばりたいと思おもわない。

I don't feel like trying at all.

私わたしは全然ぜんぜん英語えいごを話はなせないです。

I can't speak English at all.

あなたは全然ぜんぜん理解りかいが出来できていないね。

You don't understand at all do you?

今いま、機嫌きげんが悪わるくて、全然ぜんぜん嬉うれしくならない。

I'm in a bad mood right now and there's no way at all I'll become happy.

今いまの髪型かみがたは全然ぜんぜん好すきじゃないから、明日あした切きりに行いく。

I don't like my current hair style at all, so I'll go get my hair cut tomorrow.

事故じこにあったけど、私わたしは全然ぜんぜん大丈夫だいじょうぶ。

I was in an accident, but I am completely fine.

君きみが何なに言いっているか全然ぜんぜん分わからないんですけど。

I don't understand what you are saying at all.

明日あしたの競争きょうそうに全然ぜんぜん自信じしんがない。

I don't have any confidence about tomorrow's competition at all.

明日あしたの締しめ切きりには全然ぜんぜん間まに合あわなそう。

It doesn't look like I will make tomorrow's deadline at all. (Not even close)

この川かわの水みずは全然ぜんぜん綺麗きれいじゃない。

This river's water is not clean at all.

彼かれと話はなさない方ほうがいいよ。全然ぜんぜん気分きぶんが良よくないみたい。

You had better not talk to him. He is not in a good mood at all.

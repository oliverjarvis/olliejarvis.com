## About

About いかなる

English

Japanese

如何いかなる is a combination of 如何いか, and なる. なる being derived from the classical auxiliary verb なり 'to be'. 如何いか is never used as a word by itself, and will always be attached to something else when it has the reading of いか. However, its meaning is the same as the modern どう 'how'. Because of this, いかなる is interchangeable with どんな 'what kind of', with the exception that it is primarily used only in books and formal writing.

いかなる is classified as being attributive, meaning that it will appear directly before a noun, or modify a noun somewhere within the same clause.

いかなることがあっても、決けして手てを離はなしてはいけません。わかりましたか？

No matter what happens, you must not let go. Do you understand?

私わたしたちはいかなる時ときにも出動しゅつどうできるように、常つねに訓練くんれんしています。

We are constantly training to be ready to dispatch at any moment.

彼かれらはいかなる要求ようきゅうにも応おうじなければならない。

They must comply with any request, no matter what it is.

Just like どんな, when に is added to いかなる, a negative statement will usually follow. This translates as 'no matter how much (A)', or 'no matter what kind of (A)'. However, に will not directly follow いかなる like it does with どんな. Instead, it will appear later in the sentence before the verb.

いかなる状況じょうきょうに置おかれても、諦あきらめずに突つき進すすむことを忘れないでください。

Remember, no matter what situation you find yourself in, do not give up and keep pushing forward.

彼かれらは結婚けっこんしてから長ながいから、いかなる困難こんなんにあっても、二人ふたりで乗のり越こえられる。

They've been married long enough that no matter what challenges they face, they can overcome them together.

Occasionally, いかな may also be used, but this is far less common.

いかな状況じょうきょうにも耐たえ得うる人ひとになりたい。

I want to be a person who can withstand any situation.

Fun Fact

Both な and なる are derived from the classical auxiliary verb なり. Structures that stem from なり can be seen in many modern parts of the Japanese language. This also includes なら, the hypothetical form of だ, which shares the same meaning of 'to be' as なり. While な is the form that appears with most adjectival nouns, なる is still sometimes used in place of な when the speaker wants to be poetic, or give a sense of archaism.

Kappazon Group reserves the right to remove any product from the Kappazon online store, no matter the reason.

シヌラ電気動力株式会社でんきどうりょくかぶしきがいしゃは如何いかなる第三者だいさんしゃにも顧客こきゃく情報じょうほうを販売はんばい、配布はいふいたしません。

The Shinura Electric Power Company does not sell or disclose data to any third parties (no matter what).

如何いかなる方かたともログイン情報じょうほうまたはパスワードの共有きょうゆうはお控ひかえ願ねがいます。

We request that you refrain from sharing login or password information with anyone (no matter who).

パイロットは如何いかなる事態じたいになろうとも、適切てきせつに対処たいしょせねばならない。

A pilot has to react accordingly no matter what the situation might become.

ネットショップのサイト、返金へんきんについて：「一旦いったん支払しはらわれた金額きんがくは如何いかなる理由りゆうがあっても、返却へんきゃく致いたしません。」

Online shop's website, regarding refunds: 'Once paid, the amount will not be returned no matter what kind of reason there might be.'

ボスが如何いかなる犠牲ぎせいを払はらってでも売上うりあげを上あげると言いった瞬間しゅんかんに社員しゃいんは凍こおりついた。

The moment the boss said to raise sales no matter the sacrifice that needs to be made, the staff members froze.

如何いかなるピンチにも適切てきせつに対処たいしょできるなんて、彼女かのじょは多おおくの経験けいけんを積つんでいるんだね。

Something like being able to cope suitably no matter what kind of dilemma occurs, she must have a stack of experience.

警察官けいさつかんや消防隊しょうぼうたい、救急隊きゅうきゅうたいは如何いかなる時ときにも出動しゅつどうできるよう、常つねに準備じゅんびをしているそうだ。

As for people like police officers, fire fighters, or ambulance officers, I heard that they constantly make sure to be prepared to depart at any moment.

脱税だつぜいの罪つみを着きせられている某ぼう俳優はいゆうは、記者きしゃに対たいして如何いかなる質問しつもんにも答こたえない旨むねを伝つたえた。

As for that actor who was convicted of tax evasion, he conveyed that he aimed to not answer any kind of questions from the journalist. (no matter what kind of)

会社かいしゃの最高さいこう責任者せきにんしゃとして、私わたしは如何いかなる状況じょうきょうになっても逃にげ出ださずに責任せきにんを果はたすつもりです。

As the chief executive, I intend to fulfill my responsibilities without running away, no matter what situation may arise.

バンジージャンプを飛とぶためには「弊社へいしゃは如何いかなる責任せきにんも負おいません」と書かいてある契約書けいやくしょにサインする必要ひつようがあるようだ。

In order to go bungee jumping, it appears like you have to sign a contract that has 'our company does not bear any kind of responsibility' written on it. (no matter what kind of)

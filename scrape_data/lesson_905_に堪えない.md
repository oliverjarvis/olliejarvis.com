## About

About に堪えない

English

Japanese

に堪たえない is a construction that will translate as ‘to not be able to bear (A)’, or ‘to not be able to tolerate (A)’. It is a mix of the case-marking particle に, and the negative form of the る-Verb 堪たえる ‘to tolerate’, 堪たえない.

In this meaning, に堪たえない will be used following verbs. This is in contrast to the に堪たえない grammar point that follows nouns, and means ‘to be very (A)’, but is more literally ‘to be so (A) I can’t stand it’.

あの映画えいがに出でている役者やくしゃさんみんなが大根だいこん役者やくしゃだったので、見みるに堪たえなかったです。

Since all the actors that were in that movie were bad actors, I couldn’t bear to watch it.

田中たなかさんの話はなしはいつも人ひとの悪口わるぐちばかり言いっているので、聞きくに堪たえない。

I can’t tolerate listening to Tanaka-san because he is always talking ill about others.

この本ほんには過激かげきな表現ひょうげんがありすぎるので、読よむに堪たえない。

There are too many graphic expressions in this book that I can’t bear to read it.

Although 堪たえる will be used more often than other kanji that have the same reading and similar meanings, this is not always the case. Occasionally 耐たえる ‘to withstand’ will be used when the emphasis is on the ability to endure (A), while 絶たえる ‘to discontinue’, or ‘to peter out’ will be used when the emphasis is on not being able to suppress (A). For 絶たえる, this is especially used in the case of strong feelings.

仕事しごとの環境かんきょうがひどすぎて続つづけるに耐たえない。

The working conditions are so bad that I cannot bear to continue.

お客きゃくさんの所有物しょゆうぶつを壊こわしてお客きゃくさんをだますなんて怒いかりに絶たえない。

I am extremely outraged that they destroyed their customers’ property and lied to them.

Caution

The affirmative form of に堪たえる may also be used to express that something is ‘worth doing’. A closer interpretation of this would be similar to an English phrase like ‘to be passable for (A)’. Rather than meaning that something is particularly suitable, it just highlights that it also acceptably falls in the category of (A), despite not being intended for it, or perhaps not expected.

この漫画まんがは内容ないようがとてもよく、大人おとなでも読よむに堪たえる。

This manga is worth reading, even for an adult, because it is well written.

やっと公開こうかいするに堪たえる音楽おんがくができました！

I have finally made a song that is worth publishing!

## Examples

Examples

Sentence
Translation

くりやまさんの歌声うたごえは酷ひどすぎて聞きくに堪たえなかった。

Kuriyama-san's singing voice was so terrible that I couldn't bear to listen.

このホラー映画えいがには正視せいしに堪たえない、すごく残虐ざんぎゃくなシーンが多おおい。

In this horror movie, there are so many brutal scenes that I cannot bear to watch it.

最近さいきん、新聞しんぶんで悪わるいニュースばかりが続つづいているから読よむに堪たえない。

With all the bad news in the newspaper lately, I cannot bear to read it.

嘘うそをついてばかりいる政治家せいじかのインタビューはもう聞きくに堪たえない。

I can't tolerate listening to interviews with politicians who tell nothing but lies.

中ちゅう二に病びょうのころ撮とった写真しゃしんは恥はずかしすぎて見みるに堪たえないから消けした。

The photos I took when I was in my 'look at me' stage were so embarrassing that I couldn't bear to look at them, so I deleted them.

## About

About ながらに

English

Japanese

When combined with the case-marking particle に, ながら 'while' as a suffix will indicate that something remains in the state of (A), while also having or doing (B). This can be translated as 'while being (A), (B)', or '(A) as always (B)'.

ながらに will be seen following nouns, or verbs in their stem form. Additionally, して may be added to the end of に, without any change to the nuance.

自由権じゆうけんは人ひとが生うまれながらに持もっている権利けんりの一ひとつだ。

Freedom is one of the rights that people are born with.

家いえに居いながらにして仕事しごとができるから、職場しょくばまでわざわざ行いかなくても良よくなった。

I can work while being home, so I don't have to go all the way to my workplace anymore.

彼女かのじょは涙なみだながらに彼かれを見送みおくった。

She sent him off while crying.

子供こどもながらにして、なんで親おやがいつも喧嘩けんかをしていたかは理解りかいしていた。

Even as a child, I understood why my parents were always fighting.

ながらに is a structure that has many potential translations in English, but the key point to remember is that (B) is something that is being highlighted as happening or existing in same state as (A) in an unchanged state. This doesn't necessarily mean at the same time. For example, in the case of 生うまれながらに, it can be translated as '(B) has existed since birth', but what it really means is that 'while being in the state of (A), (B) is still there'.

Caution

ながらに tends to only be used with a limited set of words, so it is best not to use it with structures that you have not already seen at some point in the past.

Translation

生いきながら焼やかれるところだった。

She was almost burned alive. (while alive)

いつもながらあなたたちと話はなすのは楽たのしいですよ。

It is a pleasure to talk with you, as always.

コナンは生うまれながらのすぐれた名探偵めいたんていだそうだ。

They say that Conan is a great detective by nature. (by birth).

テレワークというのは家いえに居いながら働はたらくということだ。

Telework means to work while being at home.

コウタはカエデからの手紙てがみを手てに取とりつつ、涙なみだながらに喜よろこんだ。

While crying with joy, Kouta held the letter from Kaede in his hands. (in tears)

このカフェはその昔むかしながらの内装ないそうで、常連じょうれんさんから長ながい間あいだに渡わたって愛あいされています。

With upholstery as it was long ago, this cafe has been loved by faithful customers for a long time.

生うまれながらに孤独こどくだった彼女かのじょは、そのせいで今いまも人ひととの交流こうりゅうに苦手にがて意識いしきを持もっている。

Having been alone from birth, she was self conscious of the fact that she was bad at interactions with people due to that. (born with)

行いきつけの居酒屋いざかやの店主てんしゅは、いつもながら私わたしたち家族かぞくにサービスをしてくれました。

As always, the proprietor of my regular izakaya provided some freebies for my family.

どんな人ひとにも生うまれながらの使命しめいがあると言いわれているが、君きみはどんな使命しめいを背負せおっていると思おもう？

It is said that every person has a mission that they are born with, but what kind of task do you think you have been burdened with?

人ひとは私わたしが生うまれながらにしてフルートの才能さいのうを持もっていたと言いいますが、これは努力どりょくの結果けっかです。

People say that I was born with a talent for the flute, but this is the result of effort.

祖母そぼが涙なみだながらに広島ひろしまでの被曝ひばく体験たいけんを語かたる姿すがたを見みて、二度にどと核兵器かくへいきが使用しようされない事ことを祈いのらずにはいられなかった。

Seeing my grandmother talk about her experience of being bombed in Hiroshima while crying, I couldn't help but pray that atomic weapons are never used again.

料理りょうりレシピサイト：「昔むかしながらのオムライスの作つくり方かたを簡単かんたんで分わかりやすい動画どうがで紹介しょうかいしています。」

A site with cooking recipes: 'With this simple and easy-to-understand video, we will introduce a traditional omuraisu recipe.' (as it was long ago)

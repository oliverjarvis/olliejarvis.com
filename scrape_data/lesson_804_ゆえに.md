## About

About ゆえに

English

Japanese

When the noun 故ゆえ 'consequence' is paired with the case marking particle に, it will be used in formal situations to highlight reasons. This may be interpreted as 'because of (A)', 'due to (A)', or 'the reason is (A)'. However, 'consequently to (A)', or 'as a consequence of (A)' are also completely acceptable translations that may help to differentiate ゆえに from less formal structures.

As ゆえ itself is a noun, regular grammar rules such as being preceded by の will occasionally be observed when following another noun. Despite this, noun + の + 故ゆえ is actually quite uncommon in modern Japanese, and the の will usually just be omitted. In other cases, ゆえに will simply attach to the standard form of words.

お巡まわりさんたちが夜よるも眠ねむらずにパトロールしているが故ゆえに、夜よるでも安心あんしんして散歩さんぽできる。

Due to the police officers patrolling the area at night without sleep, it makes it safe for us to go on walks at night.

私わたしのおじいちゃんは目めが悪わるいが故ゆえに、運転免許証うんてんめんきょしょうを返納へんのうした。

My grandpa had to give up his driver's license because of his bad eyesight.

彼かれの接客せっきゃくは丁寧ていねいな故ゆえに、お客きゃくさんからの評判ひょうばんがいい。

His customer service is courteous and therefore well received by his customers.

日本にほんではまだ女性じょせいのゆえに差別さべつされることがある。

In Japan, women are still discriminated against because of their gender.

ゆえに may also appear at the beginning of new sentences. When this happens, it expresses 'as a result of (A)', where (A) is the entire previous sentence.

あの国くにでは高齢化こうれいかが深刻しんこくな問題もんだいになってきている。ゆえに、海外かいがいから若者わかものを呼よび込こみ始はじめた。

Aging is becoming a serious problem in that country. Due to that, they have started to attract young people from abroad.

The に will need to be replaced with の when ゆえ is being connected to another noun and therefore modifying it.

若わかさゆえの過あやまちであったとしても必かならず許ゆるさなければならないということでもない。

Just because something was done due to a young age, doesn't mean that it should be forgiven.

Caution

Just like 'as a consequence of' in English, ゆえに tends to be used far more frequently in regard to negative reasons than positive ones.

セリアック病びょうや小麦こむぎアレルギーを持もっている故ゆえに菓子かしパンのような甘あまいものすら食たべられない人ひとも存在そんざいする。

There are some people who cannot even eat sweets like 'kashipan' because they have celiac disease or a wheat allergy.

友達ともだちの美春みはるに彼氏かれしが出来できないのは、彼女かのじょの自分勝手じぶんかってさ故ゆえである。

The reason my friend Miharu cannot get a boyfriend is her self-centeredness.

僕ぼくは旧友きゅうゆうからからかわれる事ことが多おおいが、それは僕ぼくの人当ひとあたりの良よさ故ゆえだ。

There are many things that I was made fun of by my old friends about, but they were due to my attitude.

叔母おばは完璧かんぺき主義しゅぎで理想りそうが高たかすぎる故ゆえに、完璧かんぺきではない自分じぶんが嫌いやになり自殺じさつしてしまった。

As a consequence of my auntie's too-high ideals with perfectionism, she began to hate her imperfect self and unfortunately took her own life.

貧まずしさ故ゆえに窃盗せっとうを繰くり返かえしてしまう人ひとを見みる度たびに、この社会しゃかいの不公平ふこうへいさが嫌いやになります。

Every time I see people that repeatedly burgle due to their poorness, I begin to dislike this society's injustice.

私わたしはあなたを愛あいしているので、あなたが過去かこに若わかさ故ゆえの過あやまちを犯おかしてしまった事ことを受うけ入いれます。

Because I love you, I accept the things that were your indiscretions in the past as a consequence of your youth.

Unlike things like boats or cars, as a consequence of planes being able to traverse long distances in a short time, they are utilized by many people in the travel season. (due to)

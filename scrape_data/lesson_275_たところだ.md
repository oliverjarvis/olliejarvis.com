## About

About たところだ

In a similar way to how ところ (sometimes shortened to とこ) means 'about to (A)', or 'on the verge of (A)', when following the non-past form of a verb, when following the auxiliary verb た, ところ will mean 'just did (A)'.

To use this structure, make the past tense of any verb, and then add ところ.

今いま、先生せんせいに聞きいたところ。

I just asked the teacher.

仕事しごとは今いま終おわったところ。もうすぐ帰かえる。

I just finished work. So I'll head home soon.

While this expresses 'just did (A)', ていた may be used when you would like to express that you were 'just doing (A)'.

休やすみを楽たのしんでいたところで上司じょうしから電話でんわが来きた。

I got a call from my boss just as I was enjoying my day off.

Caution

When the kanji form of 所ところ is used, it is often referring to an actual physical location, rather than a situation/standpoint. However, this is something that native speakers will sometimes mistake. Therefore, seeing the kanji form and the hiragana form are both relatively common.

## Examples

Examples

00:03

Sentence
Translation

今いま食たべ終おわったところです。

I just (now) finished eating.

服ふくを脱ぬいでいたところに、母ははが部屋へやに入はいってきた。

My mom came into the room just as I was taking my clothes off.

新あたらしいおもちゃを渡わたしたところで壊こわれた。

Just after I gave the new toy (to someone), it broke.

友達ともだちはたまたま友達ともだちに会あう：「君きみに会あいたいと今いま思おもっていたところだわ！」

Friend meets a friend by accident: 'I was just thinking that I want to see you!'

改札かいさつに入はいったところで電車でんしゃの扉とびらが閉しまった。

Just as I (had) entered the ticket gate, the train doors closed.

いつも勉強べんきょうし始はじめたところで何なにかが邪魔じゃましてくる。

Whenever I have just started studying, something gets in the way.

牛乳ぎゅうにゅうを全部ぜんぶ飲のんだところで子供こどもが牛乳ぎゅうにゅうほしいって言いった。

Just when I had drunk all the milk, my kid said that he wanted some milk.

今いま時計とけいを見みたところだがもう何時なんじか忘わすれた。

Even though I just looked at the clock, I already forgot what time it was.

友達ともだちは電話でんわで話はなしている。みなみ：「何なにしてるの？」
つばさ：「ちょっと前まえに宿題しゅくだいを終おえたところなんだ。」

Friends are talking over the phone, Minami: 'What are you doing?'
Tsubasa: 'Just finished doing the homework.'

会社かいしゃでご飯はんを食たべたところ、同僚どうりょうにご飯はん行いこうと誘さそわれた。

Just when I had eaten my lunch at work, a co-worker invited me to go out to eat. (just ate)

アイスクリームを食たべたところでケーキを出だされた。

Just when I had eaten ice cream, cake was brought out. (just ate)

母親ははおやは子供こどもと話はなしている、ともこ：「きーちゃんはもう来きたの？」
母親：「今いま来きたところですよ。」

Mother and child are speaking, Tomoko: 'Has Kii-chan already come?'
Mother: 'She just came.'

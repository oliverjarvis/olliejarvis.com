## About

About のなかで

Just like the grammar point の中なかで～が一番いちばん～, this grammar point may be used when marking one thing from within a group, and then further describing it. The only difference is that の中なかで by itself simply marks something as being 'among (A)', or 'within (A)', rather than being the 'most (A)'.

ラーメンの中なかでは味噌みそラーメンが好すき。

Among ramen, I like the miso type.

このバンドの中なかでは誰だれが好すき？

Who do you like in this band?

の中なかで is also frequently used to describe things that are happening within a place, and will translate simply as 'in (A)', in these cases.

車くるまの中なかで夕食ゆうしょくを食たべました。

I ate dinner in my car.

この三みっつの中なかでは、赤あか色いろが好すき。

Among these three, I like the red one. (within)

フルーツの中なかで、イチゴとかパインが好すき。

Among all fruits, I like strawberries and pineapple. (within)

この中なかで一ひとつを選えらんでください。

Please choose one from among these. (within)

公園こうえんの中なかで子供こども達たちが遊あそんでいた。

The children were playing in the park. (Within)

全部ぜんぶの国くにの中なかで、一番いちばん行いってみたいのはどこ？

Among all countries, which one do you want to go to the most? (within)

グループの中なかで色々いろいろな意見いけんがありました。

There were various opinions among the group. (Within)

電車でんしゃの中なかで大おおきい声こえで喋しゃべらないでください。

Please do not speak loudly in the train. (Within)

ひらがなとカタカナと漢字かんじの中なかで、ひらがなしか書かけない。

Among hiragana, katakana, and kanji, I can only write hiragana. (within)

この四よっつの中なかで、三みっつも嫌きらいなの？

Among these four, you hate three? (within)

彼かれは雨あめの中なかで歌うたっていたから、服ふくが濡ぬれている。

He was singing in the rain, so his clothes are wet. (Within)

Communication among team members is important, yeah? (Within)

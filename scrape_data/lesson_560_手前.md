## About

About 手前

English

Japanese

The noun 手前てまえ 'in front of' has several different meanings in Japanese depending on whether it is being used with a verb in its standard form, or with a noun.

Regarding verbs, it expresses 'going forward from (A), (B)'. This is usually in relation to something that has happened either accidentally or completely, and may represent what will be done as a result of being in that situation.

崖がけから落おちる手前てまえで父親ちちおやに腕うでを引ひっ張ぱられた。

My father pulled me by my arm before I fell off the cliff.

勢いきおいで手てを上あげてしまった手前てまえ、答こたえずに手てを下さげるわけにはいかない。

From the standpoint of me raising my hand in the heat of the moment, there is no way of me backing out without answering the question.

先輩せんぱいにはいつもお世話せわになっている手前てまえ、彼女かのじょの頼たのみを断ことわるわけにはいかない。

Since my senpai always takes good care of me, there is no way that I can turn down her request.

This can also be interpreted as 'now that (A), (B)', and simply highlights that nothing can be done except to move on or 'forward' given the current circumstances.

When 手前てまえ appears with nouns followed by the case marking particle の, it often expresses that what is 'in front' of (A) is the reason for (B), or simply is 'in front of (A)'.

娘むすめの手前てまえ、運動うんどう音痴おんちな姿すがたを見みせるわけにはいかない。

I can't show my unathletic self in front of my daughter.

彼女かのじょの両親りょうしんの手前てまえ、発言はつげんには気きを付つけなければいけない。

I have to be careful of what I say when I am in front of my girlfriend's parents.

Despite the many translations, 手前てまえ always shows something that is 'facing' the noun or verb that appears with it. Due to this, thinking of this structure as similar to 'faced with (A), (B)' may be helpful, as English also expresses lack of option and finality in this way.

## Examples

Examples

Sentence
Translation

あなたの手前てまえにあるものを見みてください。

Please look at the thing that is in front of you.

オルガンをもっと手前てまえに配置はいちしてください。

Please position the organ even more in front.

死しんでしまう一歩いっぽ手前てまえで助たすかった。

I was helped just before I would have died. (on the brink)

私わたしの手前てまえの人ひとが、私わたしの友達ともだちです。

The person in front of me is my friend.

火山かざんの被害ひがいがうちの手前てまえまで及およんだ。

The damage from the volcano extended to right in front of my house.

こちらに過失かしつがあった手前てまえ、強つよいことは言いえません。

Our side made the mistake here, so we cannot complain. (we・this side)

空からっぽになる手前てまえでガソリンを入いれることができた。

I was able to get gas before it became completely empty.

手前てまえの看板かんばんを撤去てっきょしてください。

Please remove the sign in front.

境界きょうかい線せんの手前てまえで待まっていてください。

Please wait in front of the boundary line.

これまでよりも手前てまえで曲まがるカーブを投なげられるようになった。

I became able to throw a curveball that curves even more in front.

手前てまえで警備けいびしている彼かれを交代こうたいさせてください。

Please let me change places with the guy who is in front on defense.

改札かいさつの手前てまえでスイカを忘わすれてきたことに気きづいて良よかった。

I am glad that, in front of the ticket gates, I noticed that I had forgotten my Suica (card).

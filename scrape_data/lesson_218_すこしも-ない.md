## About

About すこしも～ない

Coming from the word 少すこし (a little bit), すこしも～ない is an expression which translates almost exactly to 'not even a little bit (A)' in English. Due to this, it indicates that there is 'no (A)', '(A) never happens', or 'not (A) at all', depending on whether a verb or an adjective is the word that is being negated. すこしも can come at the beginning of the sentence, or directly before the word/statement it is referring to.

今日きょうは人ひとが多おおいからすこしも離はなれないでね。

There are a lot of people today, so don't stray off. Not even a little bit.

何なにこのケーキ、すこしもおいしくない！

What is this cake, it's not even a little good!

彼女かのじょの彼氏かれしはすこしもイケメンではない。

Her boyfriend is not even a little handsome.

Fun Fact

This construction is another example of how the も particle expresses that the word before it is surprising in some way, or unexpected. This was briefly discussed in the Number + も grammar point.

今月こんげつもお金かねがすこしもない。

I don't have any money this month either.

Here we can see that も is highlighting 少すこし as being 'surprising'. This is where the 'not even!' meaning comes from.

Sentence
Translation

この景色けしきは少すこしも綺麗きれいではない。

This scenery is not pretty at all.

あの店みせのケーキは少すこしも美味おいしくない。

That store's cake is not delicious in the least.

少すこしも暑あつくないのに、汗あせをかいている。

It is not in the least bit hot, but I am sweating.

ガソリンの値段ねだんが少すこしも下さがらないね。

The price of gasoline has not dropped in the least.

今いまの話はなしが少すこしも理解りかい出来できなかった。

I did not understand the conversation in the least.

少すこしも怖こわくないの？今いま幽霊ゆうれいを見みたでしょう！

You are not scared at all? You just saw a ghost, right?

君きみは少すこしも太ふとっていないのに、ダイエット頑張がんばるの？

You are not overweight at all, but you still diet hard?

あなたは子供こどもの話はなしを少すこしも聞きいてあげてない。

You never listen to what your kid has to say. (Not in the least)

この工場こうじょうは少すこしも安全あんぜんじゃないけど、給料きゅうりょうがいいって。

This factory is not the least bit safe, but I hear the pay is good.

君きみは少すこしもお金かねがないのにロレックスを買かおうとしているの？

You do not have any money at all, but you are thinking about buying a Rolex?

私わたしは着物きものを着きてみたいけど、少すこしも似合にあわないかもしれない。

I want to try on a kimono, but it probably won't look good on me in the least.

最近さいきんお母かあさんと少すこしも連絡れんらくを取とっていないから今日きょう電話でんわをする。

I have not contacted my mom in the least bit recently, so I will call her today.

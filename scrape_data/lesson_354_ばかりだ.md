## About

About ばかりだ

English

Japanese

Occasionally, the adverbial particle ばかり is combined with verbs in their attributive form, to show that (A) is the only thing that is occuring. It may also imply that (A) is happening 'more and more', or that 'it keeps on (A)'. This phrase utilizes ばかり's standard meaning of 'only', and combines it with the auxiliary verb だ (or です).

ガソリンの値段ねだんは上あがっていくばかりだ。

The price of gas keeps rising.

最近さいきんは全然ぜんぜん体からだを鍛きたえていないから、筋肉きんにくがなくなっていくばかりだ。

Recently I haven't been working out, so I keep on losing muscle.

日本にほんの人口じんこうは減へるばかりです。

Japan's population continues to drop.

この辺へんは犯罪はんざいが多おおいので、治安ちあんが悪わるくなるばかりです。

Because there are so many crimes around here, the public safety of the area continues to get worse.

As can be seen with these sentences, this use of ばかり is paired almost exclusively with verbs that describe some form of change (higher/lower, stronger/weaker, better/worse, faster/slower, etc).

If the speaker would like to express further information in a (B) part of the sentence, だ will simply get changed to its conjunctive form で, before continuing.

この町まちの治安ちあんが悪わるくなるばかりで、しばらく良よくなりそうもない。

The public order of this town keeps on getting worse, and it doesn't look like it will get better anytime soon.

観光客かんこうきゃくが減へるばかりで、周まわりの店みせが潰つぶれていっている。

The amount of tourists keeps on dropping, and the stores around us are closing.

Caution

This use of ばかり is primarily seen with negative trends. However, this is not a grammatical rule, and positive trends may also sometimes appear with ばかりだ.

毎日まいにち夜よる遅おそくまで働はたらいているから、疲つかれがたまっていくばかりだ。

Because I work until late at night everyday, I keep getting more exhausted.

病院びょういんを変かえてから、体調たいちょうが良よくなっていくばかりだ。

Ever since I changed hospitals, my health keeps on getting better.

彼かれは傲慢ごうまんになるばかりだ。

He continues to get more arrogant. (only)

おじいさんの健康けんこうが衰おとろえるばかりだ。

The state of grandpa's health keeps on declining. (only)

最近さいきん、ビットコインの価値かちが下さがるばかりだ。

Recently, the price of bitcoin keeps on dropping. (only)

癌がんのため、体からだが弱よわっていくばかりです。

Because of the cancer, my body continues to get weaker. (only)

夏休なつやすみになってから、日本語にほんごを忘わすれていくばかりだ。

I have been continuously forgetting Japanese since summer vacation started. (only)

値段ねだんが上あがるばかりで、安やすくなる気配けはいがない。

The price only continues to go up and there is no indication of it ever going down. (only)

ほうっておけば悪わるくなるばかりだ。なんとかしないと。

If I continue on like this it will get even worse. I must do something... (only)

一生懸命いっしょうけんめい治療ちりょうしているのに、この患者かんじゃの体調たいちょうは悪わるくなるばかりだ。

Despite receiving dedicated therapy, this patient's condition continues to get worse. (only)

金融危機きんゆうききなのに、ビットコインの価値かちは上あがるばかりだ。

Despite the financial crisis, Bitcoin's value continues to rise. (only)

近ちかくにゴミ捨すて場ばがあるので、この辺あたりの土地とちの価値かちは減へるばかりだ。

Because the garbage dump is nearby, the value of land around here continues to decrease. (only)

日本にほんのような先進国せんしんこくでは、出生率しゅっしょうりつは低下ていかするばかりだ。

In developed countries similar to Japan the birth rate keeps on decreasing. (only)

彼かれは妻つまを亡なくしてから、うつ病びょうが重おもくなるばかりだ。

Since his wife's death, his depression continues to worsen. (only)

## About

About ようがない・ようもない

English

Japanese

様ようがない or 様ようもない is an expression used to highlight that there is no 'method' or 'manner' in which to do (A). It can be interpreted as 'there is no way to (A)', or 'it's impossible to (A)'.

This phrase will appear attached to the conjunctive form of verbs.

車くるまも自転車じてんしゃも壊こわれているので買かい物ものに行いきようがない。

Because my car and bicycle are broken, there is no way for me to go shopping.

パソコンが車くるまにひかれて粉々こなごなになったため直なおしようがない。

It's impossible to fix my computer because it was run over by a car and smashed to pieces.

電話でんわ番号ばんごうもメールアドレスも分わからないので、連絡れんらくのしようがありません。

I have no way to contact them because I don't know their phone number nor their email address.

様ようがない often focuses on that the action of (A) is not possible, despite that the speaker may actually want to do it.

Caution

Because 様よう will often appear in hiragana, it is important not to get it mixed up with よう, the auxiliary verb used to create the volitional form of verbs such as する.

## Examples

Examples

00:03

Sentence
Translation

何なんとも言いいようがない味あじだ。

There is no way to put this taste/flavor into words.

弁解べんかいのしようがない。

It is impossible to explain.

これはどうにもしようがない。

There is no way to do this.

彼かれは速はやすぎて追おい越こしようがない。

He is too fast, there is no way to overtake him.

そんなバレバレのサプライズ、驚おどろきようがない。

It is impossible to be surprised by such an obvious surprise.

そんな無理むりな要求ようきゅうをされても、応おうじようがない。

Even if such an impossible demand is made, it is impossible to comply.

確率かくりつが低ひくすぎて、投資とうしのしようがない。

The chances are too low, it is impossible to invest in it.

あまりにも雑ざつすぎて、カバーのしようもない。

Being exceedingly messy, it is impossible to cover it up.

あまりにも固かたい態度たいどで、親したしくなりようがない。

With an exceedingly hard attitude, it is impossible to become close.

そんな支離滅裂しりめつれつな質問しつもん、回答かいとうのしようもない。

It is impossible to answer such an incoherent question.

そんな架空かくうの話はなしをされても、反応はんのうのしようもない。

Even if such a fictitious story is told, it is impossible to react to it.

あんなにやる気きのない選手せんしゅなら、応援おうえんのしようもない。

It is impossible to cheer for an athlete if they have so little motivation.

## About

About 塗れ

English

Japanese

まみれ, seen primarily in hiragana only, is a grammar pattern that indicates being 'completely covered in (A)', 'smeared all over with (A)', or 'stained with (A)'. It will follow nouns, and may appear as まみれの when linked directly to a second noun that まみれ is modifying.

お母かあさん：「服ふく泥どろまみれじゃん！どうしたの？！」

Mother: 'You are covered in mud! What happened?!'

家いえに帰かえったら犬いぬが血ちまみれになっていたと思おもったら、どうやらいちごジャムを食たべて寝ねたみたい。

When I got home, I thought my dog was covered in blood, but it seems like he ate some strawberry jam and fell asleep.

あんな借金しゃっきん塗まみれの男おとことは付つき合あわない方ほうがいいよ！

I don't think you should date a guy like that, who is drowning in debt!

ストップ！そんな泥どろまみれの服ふくを洗濯機せんたくきに入いれないで！

Stop! Don't put clothes covered in mud in the washing machine!

In the vast majority of cases, まみれ will be used in regard to something that is negative, or can be perceived negatively even if it is not physical, like debt or desires. Examples of physically negative things include but are not limited to blood, mud, oil, etc.

Fun Fact

まみれ is the noun form of the る-Verb 塗まみれる, coming from the same kanji and with a similar meaning as 塗ぬる, 'to paint'. Just like 'to paint', まみれる and まみれ primarily describe something covering the outer surface of another object, rather than its contents.

The floor became covered in mud.

子供こどもは泥どろ塗まみれのまま遊あそんでいる。

頭あたまから汗あせ塗まみれになって、走はしっている選手せんしゅはすごいと思おもいます。

I think that players running with their heads covered in sweat are amazing.

仕事しごとで油あぶら塗まみれになったら、家いえに帰かえってからシャワーを浴あびると気持きもちがいいよね。

After getting soaked in oil at work, there's nothing better than having a shower after coming back home, right?

先生せんせいの田中たなかさんは氷こおりで滑すべって雪ゆきの塊かたまりで転ころび、雪ゆき塗まみれになってしまった。

Mr. Tanaka slipped on ice and fell on a lump of snow. He was completely covered in snow.

血ちまみれになって帰かえってきた妹いもうとを見みて、直すぐに警察けいさつへ１１０ひゃくとお番ばん通報つうほうをした。

When I saw my little sister come home smeared with blood, I immediately reported it to the police via 110.

ずっと放置ほうちしていた漢和かんわ辞典じてんが埃ほこりまみれになっていたので捨すてる事ことにしました。

Since my kanji dictionary that I have been constantly neglecting got absolutely covered in dust, I decided to throw it away.

風かぜの強つよい日ひに窓まどを開あけると部屋へやが砂すなまみれになってしまうのでやめた方ほうがいいですよ。

When you open your window on a day with strong wind, since the room will become covered in sand, you should hold off.

彼女かのじょ：「キモイ！机つくえがよだれ塗まみれだわ！」
彼氏かれし：「勉強中べんきょうちゅうに寝ねちゃったもので...」

Girlfriend: 'Gross! The desk is covered in drool!'
Boyfriend: 'I fell asleep while studying, so…'

所詮しょせん人間にんげんは欲よくまみれなのだから、それほど禁欲きんよくを推奨すいしょうしなくても良よいと思おもいます。

Since humans are all defiled with greed in the end, I think that not endorsing abstinence so much is fine. (smeared with)

テレビ業界ぎょうかいはパワハラやセクハラまみれだと聞きき、アナウンサーに憧あこがれる気持きもちが無なくなってしまった。

Hearing that the entertainment industry is stained with things like power abuse and sexual harassment, my fond feelings toward presenters disappeared.

友達ともだちに助言じょげんする：「借金しゃっきんまみれの現状げんじょうを変かえない限かぎり、誰だれも君きみと付つき合あってくれないと思おもうよ。」

Giving advice to a friend: 'As long as you don't change your current state of drowning in debt, I don't think anyone is going to date you.' (covered in)

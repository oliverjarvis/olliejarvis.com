## About

About 向け

English

Japanese

向むけ is a shortened form of the transitive verb 向むける 'to aim at', and is considered to be a 造語ぞうご (neologism). This means a word that was created for a specific purpose, and may not necessarily be used for anything outside of that purpose. The purpose of 向むけ is to attach to other nouns, in order to express that something is 'intended for (A)', 'aimed at (A)', or 'oriented toward (A)'.

向むけ may be seen followed by the case marking particle に (when followed by a phrase), or the case marking particle の, when linked with another noun.

この教科書きょうかしょは上級者じょうきゅうしゃ向むけに書かいてある。

This textbook is written oriented toward advanced people.

子供こども向むけに書かかれた本ほんを読よんで日本語にほんごを勉強べんきょうしたら？

Why don't you study Japanese by reading books written for children?

オフィス向むけのパソコンを１０じゅう台だい買かうとしたらいくらになりますか。

If I were to buy 10 computers intended for office use, how much would that cost?

これは大人おとな向むけの映画えいがだから、お前まえには見みせれないよ。

This movie is intended for adults so I can't show it to you.

In terms of products and similar items, although 向むけ highlights that something has been intended for (A), it doesn't necessarily mean that it is limited to (A).

私わたしが住すんでいる所ところは一人ひとり暮ぐらし向むけのアパートだけれども、３さん人にん家族かぞくでもなんの不便ふべん無なく住すめる。

The place I am living is intended for one person, but even as a three person family, we can live there with no inconveniences.

Caution

向むけ, coming from the transitive verb 向むける is used far more often to express that something has been specifically designed for (A). On the other hand, the suffix stemming from the intransitive verb 向むく, (向むき), expresses something that faces toward (A), but perhaps not intentionally.

そのおもちゃは猫ねこ向むけに作つくられている。

That toy is made for cats.

上級者じょうきゅうしゃ向むきの本ほんは難むずかしいです。

Books suitable for advanced learners are hard.

This app is intended for students, but anyone can use it.

This is a TV program aimed at foreigners, so I have no idea what is being said.

This movie is intended for adults. Please do not let children watch it.

This product was designed with small companies in mind.

Lately, exports aimed at Korean audiences seem to be flourishing.

First of all, I think it would be better if you studied with a textbook aimed at beginners.

This textbook is intended for people who are learning math, so it won't help those who are studying Japanese.

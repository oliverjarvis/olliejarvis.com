## About

About に当たる

English

Japanese

As mentioned in our 当あたり lesson, 当あたる often has a meaning that is similar to に対たいして 'to be in regard to'. Due to this, when combined with the case marking particle に, に当あたる will be translated as 'to correspond to', 'to amount to', 'to be appropriate for', or 'to be in regard to'. These may seem quite different, but we will examine the similarities shortly.

To use に当あたる, attach it to the end of an (A) noun that you would like to mark as being the 'point of reference'. (B) may come directly after に当あたる, or earlier in the sentence, so long as it is clearly separated from (A).

英語えいごの「what's up」は日本語にほんごの「元気げんき」に当あたる表現ひょうげんだ。

'What's up' in English corresponds to the expression 'genki' in Japanese.

その人ひとは私わたしの親戚しんせきに当あたる人ひとです。

That person is my relative.

先生せんせいはタナカ君くんのいとこに当あたる。

The teacher is Tanaka-kun's cousin.

今年ことしは仕事しごとを始はじめてから５年目ごねんめに当あたる。

This year amounts to the 5th year since I've started working. (It's been 5 years since I've started this job)

As for the link between に対たいして and に当あたる, it is as follows. In に対たいして, (A) and (B) exist simultaneously, but (B) opposes something about (A). In に当あたる, (A) and (B) exist simultaneously, but (B) matches something about (A). Due to this, unless the speaker is specifically trying to highlight something that is 'opposing', or 'matching', the base meaning of '(A) and (B) existing simultaneously' may be the standard translation.

ハクは納豆なっとうを食たべるのが好すきなのに対たいして、チヒロは納豆なっとうがあまり好すきじゃない。

As opposed to Haku who likes eating natto, Chihiro does not like eating it as much.

「Hello」は日本にほんで「こんにちは」に当あたる言葉ことばです。

'Hello' corresponds to the Japanese word 'konnichiwa'.

## Examples

Sentence
Translation

おじと呼よんでいるけど、実じつはいとこに当あたるんだ。

I call him 'uncle,' but in reality, he is my cousin.

江戸えどという都市としは現在げんざいの東京とうきょうに当あたる。

The city called 'Edo' corresponds to present-day Tokyo.

彼女かのじょの長男ちょうなんのリンゴパンはパムの父ちちに当あたる。

Her eldest son, Ringopan, also corresponds to the father of Pamu.

「地下鉄ちかてつ」に当あたる言葉ことばは、イギリスでは「underground」で、アメリカでは「subway」です。

The word corresponding to 'chikatetsu' is 'underground' in the United Kingdom, and 'subway' in the United States.

ドイツでは日本にほんの首相しゅしょうに当あたる役職やくしょくを「Bundeskanzler 」と呼よんでいる。

In Germany, the position corresponding to the Japanese prime minister is called 'Bundeskanzler'.

２００６にせんろくＦＩＦＡフィファワールドカップ・決勝けっしょうを見みていたのは約やく７億ななおく５０００ごせん万まん人にん。つまり、これは当時とうじの世界せかい人口じんこうの１０じゅう人にんに１いっ．てん１いち人にんに当あたる。

The amount of people who watched the 2006 FIFA World Cup Final was about 750 million. In other words, this corresponds to 1.1 out of 10 of the world's population at that time.

オレはきみのお母かあさんの弟おとうとなんだよ。つまり、きみにとってオジサンに当あたるんだよ。

I am the younger brother of your mother. In short, to you, I am your uncle.

ミオ：「ペソしか持もってないけど、アルゼンチン・ペソは日本にほん円えんで、いくらに当あたるかな？」
ユコ：「だいたい２にー．てん５ご円えんに当あたるでしょうね。」

Mio: 'I have only the peso, but I wonder how much the Argentinian peso corresponds to the Japanese yen?'
Yuko: 'It amounts to 2.5 yen.'

日本語にほんごの「すみません」に当あたる表現ひょうげんは英語えいごでは「I'm sorry」はもちろん「Thank you」や「Excuse me」でもある。

The expression corresponding to Japanese 'sumimasen' is not only 'I'm sorry' but also 'thank you' and 'excuse me' in English.

とある漫画家まんがかによるとポーランド語ごの「 ザクシャ・イン・ラブ 」はおおよそ日本語にほんごの「愛あいを永遠えいえんに」に当あたるらしい。

According to a certain manga artist, the Polish 'Zawsze in love' is roughly equivalent to the Japanese 'forever in love'.

英語えいごには仕事しごとを終おえて帰かえる時ときに言いう「お疲つかれ様さまでした。」に当あたる表現ひょうげんはない。

In English, there is no expression corresponding to 'otsukaresamadeshita,' which is said when leaving to go home after finishing work.

日本語にほんご能力のうりょく試験しけん中ちゅうには「四よっつの言葉ことばの選択肢せんたくしのうち、★の位置いちに当あたるものはどれですか」のような問題もんだいもある。

There are also questions on the JLPT like 'Among these four (word) choices, which one corresponds to the field with the ★?'.

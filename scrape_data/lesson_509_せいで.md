## About

About せいで

English

Japanese

Although written almost exclusively in hiragana, せい is a noun that may be better understood from its kanji, 所為せい, meaning 'doings'. せい is used to convey negative results that occurred due to the behavior or actions of another person/thing. Because of this, common translations of せい are 'fault', 'due to', or 'because of'.

As せい is a noun, it may be used after the attributive form of any other word. The case marking particle で will also regularly be seen after せい, especially when conveying the (B) result that occurred 'with' or 'by means of' those 'doings'.

昨日きのう夜よる遅おそくまで起おきていたせいで寝坊ねぼうした。

Because I stayed up late last night, I overslept today.

外そとが寒さむいせいで風邪かぜを引ひいた。

Because it is chilly outside, I caught a cold.

犬いぬの手術しゅじゅつの事ことが心配しんぱいなせいで今日きょうは寝ねれなさそう。

Due to me being worried about my dog's surgery, it doesn't seem like I am going to be able to sleep.

大雪おおゆきのせいで道みちが渋滞じゅうたいしてる。

Because of the heavy snow, the road is congested.

Fun Fact

The 'doings' that 所為せい represents is similar to the negative 'doings' that is used in English to place blame. This can be seen in phrases such as 'it was the weather's doings', or 'ah, so the missing cookies were your doings!'

遅刻ちこくしたのは渋滞じゅうたいのせいだ！

Me being late is the traffic's doing!

台風たいふうのせいでまた予定よていが変かわったよ。

Due to the typhoon's doings, my plans have changed again.

## Examples

Examples

Sentence
Translation

トムは暑あつさのせいで気きを失うしなった。

Tom passed out because of the heat.

誰だれのせいでもないと思おもいます。

I don't think it's because of anyone. (no one to blame)

湿気しっけのせいでカビがひどい。

The mold is out of control because of the humidity.

ストレスのせいで病気びょうきになってしまった。

I became ill due to stress.

物忘ものわすれがひどいのは、たぶん年としのせいです。

You are quite forgetful probably because of your age.

気分きぶんが悪わるくなったせいで、仕事しごとを休やすんでしまった。

Because I wasn't feeling well, I took the day off.

雨あめのせいでせっかくの遠足えんそくが中止ちゅうしになってしまった。

The long awaited trip was cancelled because of the rain.

トムがお皿さらを割わってしまったせいで、私わたしは怪我けがをしてしまった。

I got injured because Tom broke a plate.

寒さむいのは風かぜのせいでしょう。そんなに気温きおんは低ひくくないですよ。

I think you feel cold due to the wind. The temperature is not that low.

（彼女かのじょが）変へんに見みえるのはたぶん、髪型かみがたのせいですよ。

I think she looks strange because of her hairstyle.

人間にんげん関係かんけいがうまくいかないのは、たぶんあなたの性格せいかくのせいでしょうね。

I think your relationships are not going smoothly because of your personality.

日本語にほんご能力のうりょく試験しけんに失敗しっぱいしたのは、たぶん彼氏かれしのせいでしょう。

I think I failed the JLPT because of my boyfriend.

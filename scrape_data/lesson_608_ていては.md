## About

About ていては

English

Japanese

ていては is a structure often seen with verbs that will express that 'if one continues with (A), (B)'. In most cases, this will indicate a negative outcome. ていては is a combination of the て-form of a verb, 居いる 'to be' in its て-form, and は. The final ては is often considered its own conjunctive structure that may either exhibit hypothetical situations, or repetitive actions. In this case, it's a bit of a mix of both, depending on the verb it is attached to.

毎日まいにちお菓子かしばかりを食たべていては、いつまで経たっても痩やせませんよ。

If you keep only eating snacks, you'll never lose any weight.

毎晩まいばん遊あそんでいてはお金かねはたまりませんよ。

If you keep going out every night, you won't save up money.

仕事しごとの事ことばかりを気きにしていては、旅行りょこうは楽たのしめませんよ。

If you keep worrying about work, you won't be able to enjoy your trip.

Fun-fact

Apart from the inclusion of ている to the base verb in order to show continuation, the ては in ていては is the same ては that appears with other set patterns such as てはいけない, てはならない, and てはだめ when expressing something negative.

黙だまっていては、相手あいてに気持きもちが伝つたわらないんじゃない？

If you stay silent, how will the other person know how you feel?

来くるのが遅おそすぎます。先さきに始はじめていてはいけませんか？

I will be very late. Would you mind if you start without me?

力ちからを抜ぬいていては持もてない。もっと力ちからを入いれて！

If you keep relaxing, you won't be able to hold it. Put more effort into it!

暖房だんぼうをつけていては暑あつすぎる。もう春はるなんだから。

If you keep turning on the heat, it is too hot. It's already spring, you know.

そんな歩あるき方かたをしていては、ペンギンかと思おもわれますよ。

If you keep walking like that, people will think that you are a penguin.

太ふとっていてはメンバーに入はいれない。ダイエットをしてください。

If you keep gaining weight, you cannot become a member. Please lose some weight.

見みられていては具合ぐあいが悪わるい。ブラインドを閉しめてくれ。

If we keep being watched, I feel uncomfortable. Close the blinds for me, would you?

味噌汁みそしるは冷ひえていては飲のめないなぁ。温あたためてもらえませんか？

If the miso soup is cold, I cannot eat it. Could you heat it up for me?

光ひかっていては駄目だめだと言いわれた。もっと渋しぶいのが好このみらしい。

I was told that if it is brightly shining, it is no good. Apparently their tastes are more simple.

このまま毎日まいにちそんな風ふうにお金かねを使つかっていては、破産はさんしてしまいますよ。

If you keep spending money like this every day, you will go bankrupt.

問題もんだいを最後さいごまで避さけていては何なにも成なし遂とげられないでしょ。

If you keep avoiding problems till the end, you won't be able to accomplish anything.

理想りそう像ぞうの彼女かのじょを追おい求もとめていては、決けっして恋人こいびとと出会であえないよ。

If you keep chasing the idealized image of a girlfriend, you will never be able to find a lover.

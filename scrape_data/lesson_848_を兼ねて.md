## About

About を兼ねて

English

Japanese

を兼かねて is a grammar pattern that makes use of the standard meaning of the る-Verb 兼かねる 'to stretch across to', or 'to do concurrently'. The adverbial particle を will mark a noun which will be considered the secondary event or state, while 兼かねて will be followed by the primary event or state that will be done concurrently with (A). This is often translated as 'to (A) at the same time as (B)', 'to double as (A)', or 'to (A) also partly for the purpose of (B)'.

おばあちゃんはボケ防止ぼうしを兼かねて、毎日まいにちクロスワードパズルをしています。

My grandma does crossword puzzles also partly to prevent dementia.

日本語にほんごの勉強べんきょうを兼かねて、英語えいごの字幕じまくなしで日本にほんの映画えいがをみています。

I watch Japanese movies without subtitles also partly to learn Japanese.

ダイエットを兼かねて、毎朝まいあさランニングしています。

I run everyday also partly to lose weight.

を兼かねて often highlights (B) as the primary goal, where (A) is something that acts to support it, makes sense to be done together, or just gives the speaker a good excuse to do (B). In order to remember that (A) is actually the secondary goal in this structure, using the literal translation of 'while extending across to (A), (B)' may help.

In the case of multiple goals existing, the case-marking particle と will be used for listing of nouns.

運動うんどう不足ぶそくとストレス発散はっさんを兼かねて、キックボクシングジムに通かよっている。

I go to a kickboxing gym also partly to relieve stress and to get exercise.

体調たいちょう管理かんりと節約せつやくを兼かねて、寝ねる時ときはエアコンを使つかわないようにしている。

When I sleep, I try not to use the air conditioner also partly for my health and to save money.

Sentence
Translation

ダイエットを兼かねて、スポーツジムに通かよっています。

I go to the gym also (partly) to lose weight.

日本語にほんごの勉強べんきょうを兼かねて、日本にほんのゲームをしています。

I play Japanese games also (partly) to learn the language.

リスニングの練習れんしゅうを兼かねて、日本語にほんごの曲きょくを聞きくことにしています。

Doubling as listening practice, I decided to listen to Japanese songs.

散歩さんぽを兼かねて隣町となりまちまで歩あるいて買かい物ものに行いきました。

I walked to the neighboring town to do some shopping and (partly) for a stroll as well.

小遣こづかい稼かせぎを兼かねて趣味しゅみで同人誌どうじんしを描えがいている。

I draw doujinshi as a hobby and also (partly) for extra money.

昼食ちゅうしょくを兼かねて、多おおめの朝食ちょうしょくを１１じゅういち時じに食たべました。

Doubling as lunch, I had a larger breakfast at 11 o’clock.

取材しゅざいを兼かねて、ある温泉旅館おんせんりょかんへ旅行りょこうに行いくことにした。

Also partly to gather material for a story, I decided to go on a trip to an inn with a hot spring.

僕ぼくの毎朝まいあさの日課にっかは、情報収集じょうほうしゅうしゅうを兼かねてＳＮＳエスエヌエスをチェックすることである。

My every-morning routine is, doubling as information gathering, to check social media.

ウォーキングを兼かねて近所きんじょのスーパーまで買かい物ものに行いこうと思おもっている。

Doubling as walking, I am thinking about going to the local supermarket to do shopping.

ちよちゃんの誕生日たんじょうびが近ちかいので、お誕生日会たんじょうびかいを兼かねてパーティーを行おこないたい。

Chiyo-chan's birthday is approaching, so I want to organize a party that will double as a birthday meet-up as well.

文化祭ぶんかさいではクラスの出だし物ものの宣伝せんでんを兼かねて衣装いしょうを着きたままうろついている生徒せいとが多おおい。

Also doubling as a promotion for their class productions, there are lots of students wandering around the school culture festival in costumes.

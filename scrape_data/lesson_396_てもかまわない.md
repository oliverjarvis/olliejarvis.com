## About

About てもかまわない

English

Japanese

ても構かまわない is often used to express that something 'doesn't matter', or that the speaker 'doesn't mind'. This structure is a combination of ても (with verbs and い-Adjectives), or でも (with nouns and な-Adjectives) grouped with the negative conjugation of the う - Verb 構かまう, which means 'to brace'. Literally, this expression can be thought of as being similar to 'to not be uptight'. However, it is almost always simply used to convey that there is no issue with something.

ても構かまわない may be used with any type of word that appears in its ても or でも conjugation form.

君きみがお金かねを出だすなら、ピザでも寿司すしでも何なにを頼たのんでも構かまわないよ。

If you are going to pay, I don't mind whether you order pizza, sushi, or anything else.

運転手うんてんしゅさん、遅おそくても構かまいませんので、安全あんぜん運転うんてんでお願ねがいします。

Chauffeur, I don't care if it's slower, please drive safely.

仕事しごとはどんなに大変たいへんでも構かまいません、仕事しごとが出来できればうれしいです。

I don't care how busy work is, I am happy so long as I can do my job.

アルバイト募集中ぼしゅうちゅう。未経験者みけいけんしゃでも構かまいません。

Hiring part time workers. It doesn't matter if you are not experienced.

Fun Fact

構かまう is usually translated as 'to brace', but the kanji 構かま often has the nuance of 'to posture', or 'to pose' (into some type of strong/resistant shape). In the ても構かまわない grammar structure, this translates as (A) simply being something that will not cause the speaker to 'posture', 'position', or 'pose' themselves in a resistant way.

Sentence
Translation

無視むしされても構かまわない。

It doesn't matter (even) if I am ignored.

その写真しゃしんなら、見みても構かまいません。

If it is that picture, I don't mind if you look at it.

欲ほしい人ひとがいればあげても構かまわない。

If someone wants it, I don't mind (even if) giving it.

答こたえたくなければ、答こたえなくても構かまいません。

If you don't want to answer, it doesn't matter (even) if you don't answer.

暇ひまな時ときはゲームをやっても構かまわない。

It doesn't matter (even) if you play games when you have free time.

うまくいかなくても構かまわない。やってみることが大切たいせつだ。

It doesn't matter (even) if it won't go well. It's important to try.

濡ぬれても構かまいません。革靴かわぐつではないですから。

I don't mind (even if) they get wet. These are not leather shoes.

遅おそくなっても構かまわないので、ぜひ遊あそびに来きてください。

It doesn't matter (even) if it is late, so please come for a visit.

笑わらわれても構かまわない。僕ぼくはあきらめないぞ。

I don't mind if I am laughed at. I won't give up.

自分じぶんの家いえだから服ふくを着きなくても構かまいません。

It doesn't matter (even) if you don't wear clothes because it is your own house.

お金かねが無なくても構かまわない。だって、彼かれのことが大好だいすきだから。

It doesn't matter (even) if he doesn't have money, because I love him.

費用ひようはいくらかかっても構かまわない。とにかく買かってきてくれ。

It doesn't matter how much it costs. Just go buy it for me. (even if)

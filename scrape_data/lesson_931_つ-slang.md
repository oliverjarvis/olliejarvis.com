## About

About つ (Slang)

つ is a slang grammar pattern that originates from という, with the meaning of 'called (A)', 'that (A) that was said', or 'known as (A)'. Basically, it just quotes something that was previously said. つ may also be seen as っつ, or even つう.

Remember, という is just a combination of the quotation particle と, and 言いう, the う-Verb meaning 'to say'.

As と is the quotation particle, つ can follow almost any statement or word type.

つーかなんでお前まえ来きたの。

By the way, why did you come?

そんな下手へたな説明せつめいじゃわからんっつーの。

I'm saying there's no way anyone would understand such a bad explanation.

何なんつうか覚おぼえとらんけど、外国語がいこくごっぽい名前なまえだった気きがする。

I don't remember what they were called, but I have a feeling that it was a foreign-language kinda name.

Although つ is very strong slang, it is very common. You can often hear native speakers saying という with various degrees of abbreviation, but つ being the shortest possible.

The pause in the っつ version may indicate that the speaker is annoyed or running out of patience, but this is not always the case.

だから、俺おれは何なにも知しらないっつーの。

Because, I said don't know anything.

Caution

Just because 言いう disappears when said as つ, it doesn't mean that standard conjugation rules also disappear. If you want to highlight that something 'was said' in past tense, or 'is being said' in progressive form, structures like つった or つってん will be used.

おい、今いまなんつった？

Oi, what'd you say just now?

で、そいつはなんつってんの？

And, what's he been saying?

ている becoming てん is explained more closely in the ん slang lesson.

Sentence
Translation

彼かれは「もうすぐで来くる」っつった。

He said 'I'll come soon'.

あの人ひとは「山田やまだ」つう男おとこだ。

That person is a guy called Yamada.

「俺おれの話はなしを聞きけ」つってんだよ。

I said 'Listen to what I have to say!'

グランピングつうものをしてみたい。

I wanna try that thing called glamping.

「ここから立たち去され」つってんだよ。

They said get up and get the heck out of here!

ヤクザつう者ものは、どんな奴やつなんだい？

The people called Yakuza, what kind of guys are they?

ディズニーつうものは凄すごく人気にんきなんだね？

That place called Disney is super popular, right?

俺おれは泳およげないつってんのに、泳およがすな！

Despite saying I can't swim, don't get me to swim!

僕ぼくは元もとカノに「二度にどと連絡れんらくすんな」っつった。

I said 'don't ever contact me again' to my ex girlfriend.

先生せんせいつう者ものは、生徒せいとを平等びょうどうに扱あつかわなければならない。

Someone called a teacher must treat students equally.

あいつは「借金しゃっきんを返かえす」っつったのに、まだ返かえしてない。

Despite him saying 'I will return the money I borrowed', he still hasn't returned it.

私わたしと結婚けっこんするっつったのに、アイツは私わたしの元もとから消きえた。

Despite him saying he will marry me, that dude hit the road.

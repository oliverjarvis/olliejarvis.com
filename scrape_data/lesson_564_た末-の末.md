## About

About た末・の末

English

Japanese

Appearing with verbs in their past form, or grouped with nouns after の is 末すえ, a word meaning 'end tip'. に will follow 末すえ , marking the 'tip' as the point from which something will result.

While this grammar pattern is usually translated as 'after (A), (B)', or 'as a result of (A), (B)', the nuance is on various things having happened along the way, and then finally reaching the 'point' or 'conclusion' of (A).

色々いろいろと考かんがえた末すえに、お父とうさんの会社かいしゃを継つぐことに決きめた。

After much thought, I decided to take over my father's company.

迷まよいに迷まよった末すえに、彼女かのじょと別わかれることにした。

After much hesitation, I decided to break up with her.

５年ごねん間かんにわたる争あらそいの末すえ、アメリカ軍ぐんが撤退てったいを開始かいしした。

After five years of conflict, the U.S. military began to withdraw.

社長しゃちょうと長ながい議論ぎろんの末すえに、人事じんじの伊藤いとうさんをクビにすることに決きめた。

After a long discussion with the president, we decided to fire Ito-san from human resources.

末すえに may highlight either positive or negative results, but will suggest that some long-lasting effort or struggle eventually resulted in (B).

After (much) digging to reveal the onsen, I decided to build a ryokan.

これは、蚊かにさされた末すえの感染かんせん症しょうです。

This is an infectious disease that arises after being bitten by a mosquito.

いろいろ面接めんせつした末すえに、大だい企業きぎょうに雇やとわれたんだ。

After (doing) many interviews, I was hired by a big company.

あの人ひとは、散々さんざんおそわった末すえに仕事しごとをやめてしまった。

That person, after being thoroughly taught (continuously), unfortunately quit.

無理むりをして試合しあいにでた末すえに、大おお怪我けがをしてしまった。

After going (all out) into the match without listening to my body, I ended up getting severely injured.

彼女かのじょと長ながい議論ぎろんの末すえに、来年らいねんの６月ろくがつ、結婚けっこんすることに決きめた。

After a (long) discussion with my girlfriend, we have decided to get married next June.

頭あたまが痛いたかったせいで眠ねむれない夜よるをすごした末すえ、医者いしゃに診みてもらおうと決きめた。

After spending a (long) sleepless night due to a headache, I decided to see a doctor.

消費しょうひ税ぜいをあげた末すえに不況ふきょうになったら本末ほんまつ転倒てんとうだ。

After the (continuous) increase of the consumption tax, if there is a recession the government will have mistaken the insignificant with the essential.

憲法けんぽう改正かいせいの末すえに、戦争せんそうに参加さんかしたりしなければいいのだが…

After (much work on) the constitutional reform, one does not have to participate in the war, but...

衆議院しゅうぎいん解散かいさんの末すえの選挙せんきょだが、そもそもの解散かいさん理由りゆうが微妙びみょうだ。

As for the election concluding the dissolution of the lower house of parliament, no one really knows why it was broken up in the first place.

大学だいがく卒業そつぎょう後ごどうしようかとなやんだ末すえ、ベンチャー企業きぎょうを立たち上あげることにした。

After (continuously) worrying about what to do after graduation, I decided to start a business.

散々さんざん思考しこうを巡めぐらせた末すえに、ようやく銃じゅうを壁かべにかけて普通ふつうの生活せいかつを送おくりはじめた。

After (continuously) stewing on it, he was finally able to hang up his gun and begin an ordinary life.

## About

About からこそ

English

Japanese

Traditionally, the adverbial particle こそ is used to emphasize whatever noun (or noun phrase) it is attached to. However, when combined with the conjunction particle から, it focuses on emphasizing the 'reason' for something. This structure is very similar to phrases like 'precisely because (A)' in English.

からこそ is an expression that is primarily used after either verbs or nouns. In the case of nouns, the noun will need to be followed by the auxiliary verb だ, before からこそ is added.

俺おれは努力どりょくしたからこそ、試験しけんに合格ごうかくできたんだよ。頭あたまがいいからだけじゃないよ。

I was able to pass the test, precisely because I gave it my all. It's not only because I am smart.

先輩せんぱいに色々いろいろと教おしえて貰もらったからこそ、私わたしはここまでこれたのです。

I was able to get to where I am now because of all the things you, senpai, have taught me.

君きみだからこそできたんだよ。俺おれにはまだそんな凄すごい事ことは出来できない。

You were able to do it precisely because it was you. I can't do anything that amazing yet.

ここは綺麗きれいな川かわだからこそ、こんな美味おいしい魚さかなが釣つれるんですよ。

You can catch great fish here precisely because the river is so clean.

With からこそ, there may be several reasons for why something is the way it is, but こそ will show that there is a 'foremost' reason that should be considered before anything else.

タナカ君くんが休やすみなのに仕事しごとに来きてくれたからこそ、準備じゅんびが早はやく終おわったり、締しめ切きりまでに仕事しごとを終おわらせることができたんです。

We were able to finish with the prep work and finish work by the deadline, precisely because you, Tanaka-kun, came into work even though it was your day off.

あなたがいるからこそ、安心あんしんできるんです。

Precisely because you're here is why I can relax.

仲間なかまがいるからこそ、頑張がんばれる。

I can do my best precisely because I have friends.

難むずかしい状況じょうきょうだからこそ生うまれるアイデアもある。

There are also ideas that are born precisely because of difficult situations.

君きみが励はげましてくれたからこそ、ここまでやってこれたんだ。

I could make it this far precisely because you have given me so much support.

毎日まいにち努力どりょくをしたからこそ、一番いちばんになれた。

Precisely because I worked hard every day, I was able to become number one.

君きみの手伝てつだいがあったからこそ、大おおきな石いしを動うごかすことができた。

Precisely because you helped, we were able to move the large rock.

操作そうさが簡単かんたんだからこそ、お年寄としよりにも人気にんきがある。

That is even popular among the elderly precisely because it is easy to control.

お父とおさんが一生いっしょう懸命けんめい働はたらいているからこそ、何なに不自由ふじゆうなく暮くらせている。

We can live in comfort precisely because dad works hard.

昨日きのうまでの苦労くろうがあったからこその達成感たっせいかんだ。

It feels great precisely because I had been putting so much effort into it until yesterday. (emphasis)

彼女かのじょがいたからこそ、兎うさぎを捕つかまえることができた。

I was able to catch the rabbit precisely because she was there.

毎日まいにち運動うんどうをしているからこそ、健康けんこうでいられるのだ。

I can stay healthy precisely because I exercise every day.

あなたが案内あんないしてくれたからこそ、楽たのしい旅行りょこうができた。

Precisely because you were our guide, we were able to enjoy our trip.

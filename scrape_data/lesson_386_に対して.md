## About

About に対して

English

Japanese

に対たいする and に対たいして are two grammar patterns in Japanese that are regularly used to show when (B) is 'opposed to' or 'in contrast with' (A). 対たいする itself is a verb meaning 'to oppose'. Although this feeling of 'opposition' may be negative, in most circumstances, it just means 'toward' in the sense of targeting something.

に対たいする will be followed directly by a second noun, while に対たいして creates a pause in the sentence, before (B) will be explained in the second half of that sentence.

君きみに対たいする気持きもちは一生いっしょう変かわらない。

My feelings toward you will never change.

私わたしは店員てんいんに対たいする態度たいどが悪わるい人ひとが苦手にがてです。

I don't like people who have a bad attitude towards store employees. (People who target their bad attitude at store employees)

ああいう風ふうに、先輩せんぱいに対たいしてタメ口ぐちを使つかうのは良よくないと思おもうよ。

I don't think it is best to talk to a senpai using casual speech in that manner. (Talking casually toward a senior)

何なんでいつも私わたしに対たいしてそういう酷ひどいことを言いうの？

Why do you always say mean things like that to me? (With me as the target)

Fun Fact

Compared to に関かんして (another structure used to show how things 'relate' to each other), に対たいして focuses on (A) and (B) equally, explaining how those things interact, compare, or contrast each other.

このプロジェクトに関かんして質問しつもんがある方かたは私わたしに直接ちょくせつ電話でんわをしてください。

If you have any questions concerning this project, please give me a call directly. (Questions in relation to this project)

私わたしはサッカーが好すきなのに対たいして、彼かれはバドミントンが好すき。

In contrast to me liking soccer, he likes badminton. (Opposed to me who likes soccer)

Caution

As に対たいして strongly contrasts (A) and (B), this grammar structure will often be used to express differences between two things. This is very similar to 'whereas' in English.

マサミは難むずかしい本ほんを読よんだのに対たいして、カスミは簡単かんたんな本ほんを読よんだ。

Masami read a difficult book, whereas Kasumi read an easy book.

彼かれの仕事しごとは楽らくなのに対たいして、僕ぼくの仕事しごとはものすごく大変たいへん。

His job is carefree, whereas my job is very difficult.

He is kind towards everyone.

日本にほんの教育きょういく制度せいどに対たいしてどう思おもう？

What are your thoughts in regards to the Japanese school system?

誰だれに対たいしても親切しんせつな人ひとになりたい。

I want to be nice towards anyone and everyone.

父ちちが厳きびしすぎるのに対たいして、母ははは優やさしすぎる。

My father is too strict, in contrast, my mother is too nice.

はぐらかさないで質問しつもんに対たいして答こたえてください。

Regarding the question, please answer it, without dodging.

君きみは僕ぼくに対たいして冷つめたい態度たいどを取とるよね。

You are cold towards me, aren't you?

大人おとなに対たいしてそんな失礼しつれいな態度たいどを取とってはいけません。

You must not be so rude toward adults.

増税ぞうぜいに対たいして不満ふまんがある人ひとは多おおい。

There are many people who are dissatisfied regarding the tax increase.

あの人ひとは、政治家せいじかに対たいしていつも批判的ひはんてきだ。

That person is always negative towards politicians.

ルールを守まもらない人ひとに対たいしては厳きびしく取とり締しまってください。

Please be strict towards people who do not follow the rules.

イギリス人じんがビールを好すきなのに対たいして、フランス人じんは赤あかワインが好すきだ。

English people like drinking beer in contrast to French people who like red wine.

この部分ぶぶんに対たいして質問しつもんが多おおかった。ちょっと難むずかしかったのかしら...。

There were many questions regarding this part. I wonder if it was too difficult...

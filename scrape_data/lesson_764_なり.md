## About

About なり

English

Japanese

なり is an old-fashioned particle in Japanese that has several different uses. One of those uses is to convey the meaning of 'as soon as (A), (B)', or 'the moment of (A), (B)'. This structure will follow the dictionary form of verbs and is classified as a conjunction particle.

(B) is usually something that is surprising, and brought about by a strong sense of will or emotion. Additionally, なり is almost always used in relation to other people, rather than oneself.

息子むすこが家いえに上あがるなりトイレへと駆かけ込こんだ。

My son ran to the toilet as soon as he entered the house.

ママ友ともが私わたしの家いえに入はいるなり、冷蔵庫れいぞうこの中なかを物色ぶっしょくし始はじめた。

As soon as my mom-friend came into my house, she started to go through my refrigerator.

彼かれは机つくえに座すわるなりすぐスマホをいじり始はじめたから注意ちゅういした。

I warned him because he started playing on his phone as soon as he sat down.

Fun Fact

Despite appearing to be a conjugation of the う-Verb なる 'to become' at first glance, なり is actually one of the readings of 形なり, meaning 'shape', and refers to the overall situation of (A) in a similar way to ところ.

As soon as she heard the joke, she bursted out laughing.

うちの奥おくさんは毎日まいにち起おきるなりスマホで電子でんしメールを見みている。

As soon as my wife wakes up, she checks mail on her smartphone.

踊おどりを止やめるなり、彼女かのじょが急きゅうにキスしてくれたんだ。気持きもちいい。

As soon as I stopped dancing, she suddenly kissed me. I feel great.

実家じっかのお母かあさんからの電報でんぽうが来くるなり、山田やまださんは青あおざめた。

As soon as the telegram from his mother came, Yamada became pale.

故郷ふるさとに帰かえるなり灰燼かいじんに帰きした家いえを見みて、驚おどろき、悲かなしんだ。

The moment I returned to my home town, I saw my house reduced to ashes. I was shocked and saddened.

自宅じたくに訪問ほうもんしてきた男性だんせいが指名しめい手配てはい犯はんだと気きづくなり、妻つまは警察けいさつへ通報つうほうした。

As soon as my wife realized that the man who came to visit our house was a wanted criminal, she reported it to the police.

教授きょうじゅは授業じゅぎょう開始かいしを告つげるチャイムが鳴なるなり直すぐに授業じゅぎょうの本題ほんだいへ入はいりました。

As for the professor, the moment the chime that signifies the beginning of class rang, he immediately went straight into the main topic of the lesson.

以前いぜん会あった時ときに彼かれに失礼しつれいな事ことを言いったのは確たしかだが、彼かれに会あうなり罵ののしられるとは…。

When I met him previously, it is indeed true that I said something rude, but to think that he would hurl abuse at me the moment he saw me…

普段ふだんは深夜しんやにお風呂ふろに入はいる夫おっとが家いえに着つくなり急いそいでお風呂ふろに入はいったのを見みて妻つまは首くびを傾かしげた。

When seeing her husband, who usually has a shower in the middle of the night, enter the shower hurriedly upon arriving home, the wife became quite puzzled.

夕食ゆうしょくを食たべ終おわるなり自室じしつへ篭こもってしまった息子むすこを見みて、息子むすこがストレスを抱かかえている事ことに気きづきました。

When they saw their son shut himself in his room the moment he finished eating dinner, they realized that he was harboring some stress.

自他じたともに認みとめる雨女あめおんなである私わたしと旅行りょこうへ行いくと、どんな場所ばしょへ旅行りょこうに行いっても宿やどから出でるなり雨あめに降ふられる事ことになりますよ。

If you're going on a trip with me, who is generally accepted as a woman that takes the rain with her everywhere, no matter where we go, from when we settle in, til as soon as we depart, we'll get rained on.

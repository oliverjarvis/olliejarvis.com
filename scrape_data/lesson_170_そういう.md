## About

About そういう

Coming from the こそあど言葉ことば family of words, こう, そう, ああ, or どう may be used with the verb 言いう to form an expression which literally means 'in the way of (A)'. (A) being 'this' これ, 'that' それ, 'that over there' あれ, or 'what' どれ (in questions). As with all pre-noun adjectival words, this expression will be used directly before a noun.

こういう映画えいがは初はじめて見みる。

This is my first time watching a movie like this. (This 'kind' of movie)

そういう人ひとは嫌きらい。

I don't like people like that. (That 'kind' of people)

ああいう車くるまに乗のってみたい。

I want to try to drive a car like that one. (That 'kind' of car)

あなたはどういう音楽おんがくを聴ききますか？

What kind of music do you listen to?

Although こう, そう, ああ, and どう share a relationship with これ, それ, あれ, and どれ, they are their own individual words, which each have a meaning similar to 'as such'. These words are all adverb when used by themselves.

Caution

そう (the adverb), should not be confused with そう, the auxiliary verb. そう (the adverb) comes from the kanji 然そう, while the auxiliary verb does not have a kanji form.

## Examples

Examples

Sentence
Translation

そういう人ひともいるよ。

There are also people like that (you know).

どういう意味いみですか。

What do you mean? (Literally - What kind of meaning?)

ああいう消けしゴムが好すき。

I like erasers like that one over there.

こういう人形にんぎょうがほしい。

I want a doll like this.

こういう仕事しごとをしなくちゃいけない。

I have to do this kind of job.

そういうところに行いきたい。

I want to go to a place like that.

どういうことですか。

What do you mean? (in what way / how do you mean?)

ああいう人ひとになりたい。

I want to become that kind of person.

こういう車くるまでもいいですか。

Even if it is this kind of car, is it okay?

そういうプレゼントをあげます。

I will give a present like that.

そういう授業じゅぎょうは難むずかしいですね。

That kind of class is difficult.

どういう服ふくをお探さがしですか？

What kind of clothes are you searching for?

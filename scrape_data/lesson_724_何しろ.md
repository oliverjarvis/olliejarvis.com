## About

About 何しろ

English

Japanese

Although a combination of the substitute noun 何なに and the imperative form of する, しろ, 何なにしろ is often considered to be a singular adverb that carries the meaning of 'no wonder', or 'after all'. A more literal translation would be something like 'whatever the case, (A)', and just expresses that in any circumstance, only one conclusion can accurately be drawn in regard to (A).

何なにしろ will frequently appear at the beginning of a sentence, or at the start of the second clause.

何なにしろ彼かれはまだ始はじめたばかりなので大目おおめに見みてやってください。

After all, he is new, so please cut him some slack.

彼かれはできて当あたり前まえだよ。何なにしろ彼かれはプロなんだから。

It's no wonder he can do it. After all, he is a professional.

何なにしろ will regularly be seen partnered with statements including words like から or ので, in order to emphasize an explanation or reason as to why (A) is the way it is.

何なにしろ今日きょうは普段ふだんより暑あつかったからクタクタだよ。

After all, it was hotter than usual today, so I'm exhausted.

何なにしろこの商品しょうひんは不便ふべんすぎるので、どれだけ値下ねさげをしても売うれないでしょう。

After all, this product is too inconvenient and will not sell no matter how low the price goes.

Because it is so hot. It's no wonder that I have lost my appetite.

At any rate, the deadline is drawing near. Won't you hurry up?

何なにしろ基礎きそができていないから、彼かれに数学すうがくを教おしえるのは大変たいへんだ。

At any rate, since his math foundation is not up to par, it is difficult to teach him math.

あそこの会社かいしゃは何なにしろ規律きりつが厳きびしい。あれじゃあまるで軍隊ぐんたいだ。

At any rate, that company has strict rules. It is pretty much the equivalent of being in the military.

あの社長しゃちょうは何なにしろ強引ごういんだから、それで会社かいしゃを辞やめた人ひともたくさんいます。

At any rate, that CEO is overbearing, so therefore, the company has a lot of people quitting.

私わたしの父ちちは何なにしろ合理ごうり的てきで、無駄むだなことを一切いっさいしません。

At any rate, my father is rational and would never do anything pointless.

I want to treat you, but at any rate, my allowance is small. I don't have any money.

何なにしろ言葉ことば遣づかいがなってないから、面接めんせつ受うかるはずないよ。

At any rate, since his choice of words is unacceptable, there is a good chance (he) won't pass the interview.

彼かれは何なにしろ教養きょうようがないから、接客せっきゃくしていても微妙びみょうなことばかり言いってしまう。

After all, since he has no culture, he constantly says insensitive things while serving customers.

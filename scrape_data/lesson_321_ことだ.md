## About

About ことだ

English

Japanese

As a sentence ending particle, こと has two primary functions. These are as follows:

願望がんぼうや軽かるい命令めいれいを表あらわす - Highlighting a desire or weak command.

感嘆かんたんや疑問ぎもんを表あらわす - Highlighting astonishment or doubt.

We will focus on the first meaning in this grammar point, as the 'astonishment' nuance is not as commonly used in modern Japanese, and is primarily used only by women.

At the end of a sentence, こと will be paired with the dictionary (or ない) form of a verb, to highlight a particular action that either should, or should not occur. It is usually grouped with the auxiliary verb だ, although this is not always the case. This construction is most often translated as 'should/should not (A)', or 'ought to/not (A)'.

面倒めんどくさくても朝あさご飯はんを食たべることだ。

Even though it might be a pain, you should eat breakfast.

明日あしたの朝あさ８はち時じから試験しけんが始はじまる。何なにがあっても、時間じかん通どおりに来くることだ。みんな、分わかったか。

The exams will begin tomorrow at 8 am. No matter what happens, you should come on time. Understood?

どんなに疲つかれていても諦あきらめないことだ。

You should not give up, no matter how tired you are.

怪我けがをしたら、我慢がまんをしないことだ。すぐ責任者せきにんしゃに報告ほうこくするように。

If you get hurt, you shouldn't just suffer in silence. Immediately call your supervisor.

Caution

This use of こと implies a certain level of 'authority', and is therefore regularly used in relation to set rules and expectations (can very frequently be seen on signs in shops/parks, etc). Due to this, it should not be used when giving 'general' advice, especially to people of higher status.

先輩せんぱい、後輩こうはいはもっと大切たいせつにすることだ。

Senpai you should treat your kouhai with great care.

## Examples

Examples

00:04

Sentence
Translation

今回こんかいは、相手あいてを信しんじてみる事ことだ。

I think you ought to trust him/her this time.

生徒せいとは、先生せんせいに従したがう事ことだ。

Students should obey their teachers.

大事だいじなのは医者いしゃからもらった薬くすりを飲のむ事ことだ。

What is important, is that you should take the medicine the doctor gave you.

まずはやってみる事ことだ。改善点かいぜんてんが見みえてくるから。

You ought to try it first because you'll find out what needs to be improved.

覚おぼえたらすぐにしゃべってみる事ことだ。上達じょうたつするから。

As soon as you memorize something, you ought to say it out loud. If you do that, you will get better.

なんでも自分じぶん一人ひとりで決きめないで、社員しゃいんに相談そうだんしてみる事ことだ。

Don't decide everything by yourself, you ought to try to discuss it with the employees.
(someone giving advice on how to run a business)

成功せいこうしたいなら、何なにがあってもあきらめない事ことだ。

If you want to succeed, you should never give up, no matter what.

子供こどもたちに与あたえることのできる一番いちばんの贈おくり物ものは、いつもそばにいてあげる事ことだ。

The best gift you can give a child is to always be by their side. (should)

コツをつかみたいなら、とにかく何回なんかいもやってみる事ことだ。

If you want to get the hang of it (get better), for the time being, you should do it over and over again.

分わからないことがあれば、先生せんせいに聞きいてみる事ことだ。

If there is something you don't understand, you should ask the teacher.

僕ぼくはいつも物事ものごとを忘わすれてしまうらしい。もっとメモをする事ことだ。

It seems like I keep forgetting things. I should take memos more often.

明日あしたは大切たいせつな試合しあいがあるから、練習れんしゅうをする事ことだ。

Because there is an important game tomorrow, you should practice.

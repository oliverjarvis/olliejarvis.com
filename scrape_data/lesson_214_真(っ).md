## About

About 真(っ)

The prefix ま, coming from the kanji 真ま (true), is attached to words in Japanese to express that something is 'completely', 'exactly', 'genuinely' (A). Depending on the word that follows 真ま, several different conjugation patterns will be used. This conjugation depends on which column the first kana of the word after 真まcomes from. ま comes from the ま, み, む, め, も column, for example.

When linked with a ま column, or な column word, 真ま will become 真まん.

この猫ねこは真まん丸まるでかわいい。

This cat is completely circular and cute. (This cat is so fat that it is cute)

真まん中なかにある本ほんをとってください。

Please take the book in the middle.

When linked with a か column, さ column, or しゃ sound word, 真ま will become 真まっ.

体からだが真まっ赤かになるくらい お風呂ふろが熱あつかった。

The bath was so hot that my body went completely red.

サルが木きから真まっ逆さかさまに落おちた。

The monkey fell completely upside down from the tree.

道みちが雪ゆきで真まっ白しろになった。

The road turned completely white with snow.

When linked with a は column, 真ま will become 真まっ, and the first kana of the following word will change to a 'P' sound.

ちょっと、何なんで真まっ裸ぱだかなの？

Wait, why are you completely naked?

真まっ昼間ぴるまからこんな話はなしをするの？

You are going to talk about this in the middle of the day? (Are you seriously going to talk about something like this in the middle of the day?)

Caution

There are several exceptions to the standard changes that happen when using 真ま as a prefix. These are as follows.

When linked with 青あおい, 真ま will become 真まっ, and 青あおい will become 青さお.

空そらが真まっ青さおできれいだね。

The sky is completely blue and beautiful.

When linked with 赤あかい, 真ま will become 真まっ, and 赤あかい will become 赤か.

顔かおが真まっ赤かになってるよ、大丈夫だいじょうぶ？

Your face is completely red, are you okay?

It is still only 5 o'clock, but outside is completely dark.

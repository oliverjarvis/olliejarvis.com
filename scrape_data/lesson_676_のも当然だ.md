## About

About のも当然だ

English

Japanese

Like て当然とうぜんだ, the grammar point indicating that (A) is 'natural' or 'obvious', のは当然とうぜんだ works in a similar way, and highlights that it is 'no wonder that (A)', or 'it is only natural that (A)'.

This construction will be seen following verbs and い-Adjectives, or な-Adjectives and nouns that are followed by な.

会社かいしゃのお金かねを勝手かってに使つかったなら、クビになるのは当然とうぜんだ。

If you did use company money without permission, it's only natural that you get fired.

このタワーマンションは六本木ろっぽんぎにあるので、家賃やちんが高たかいのは当然とうぜんです。

This high-rise condo is located in Roppongi, it is only natural that the rent is expensive.

おばあちゃんは毎朝まいあさ１０じゅっキロも散歩さんぽしているから元気げんきなのも当然とうぜんだ。

My grandma walks 10 kilometers every morning, so it is no wonder she is so healthy.

この１いち年間ねんかん全然ぜんぜん勉強べんきょうをしてこなかったから落第らくだいも当然とうぜんだ。

I have not studied at all for the past year, so it's no wonder I flunked out.

As can be seen in several of the above examples, も may replace は, with only a slight change in nuance (も will place extra emphasis on the obviousness of the remark, according to the speaker).

Compared to て当然とうぜんだ, のは当然とうぜんだ is slightly stronger, and separates the previous statement from what the speaker considers 'natural'. This is equivalent to the difference between 'being happy is natural' (slightly weaker), and 'it's natural that one would be happy' (slightly stronger) in English.

## Examples

Sentence
Translation

税金ぜいきんを納おさめていないんだから、逮捕たいほされるのも当然とうぜんです。

Since (he) didn't pay (his) taxes, it is only natural that (he) would be arrested.

練習れんしゅうを怠おこたったんだから、ああいう結果けっかになるのも当然とうぜんだ。

Since you neglected practice, it is only natural that you would see those kinds of results.

そんなマッチョな体型たいけいでは、エプロンが似合にあわないのも当然とうぜんだ。

It is only natural that an apron would not look good on such a muscular body.

彼かれがあんな風ふうに振舞ふるまっていたなら、ほかの人ひとからの受うけが悪わるいのも当然とうぜんだ。

If he was behaving in that way, it is only natural that he is poorly received by other people now.

久ひさしぶりに彼かれに会あったのだから、彼女かのじょが嬉うれしそうなのは当然とうぜんだ。

Since she got to see him after being apart for a while, it is only natural that she seems happy.

あれだけ頑張がんばって絵えを描かいていたら、すぐに絵えの具ぐがなくなるのも当然とうぜんです。

If you put that much effort into painting, it is only natural that you would run out of paint straight away.

両親りょうしんが園芸えんげい家かなら、園芸えんげいのセンスがあるのも当然とうぜんですね。

If (your) parents are horticulturalists, it is only natural that they would have good taste when it comes to gardening.

台風たいふうが来きているのだから、遠足えんそくが延期えんきになるのも当然とうぜんだ。

Since a typhoon is approaching, it is only natural that the field trip would be postponed.

昔むかし車屋くるまやで働はたらいていたから、自分じぶんでオイル交換こうかんできるのも当然とうぜんだ。

I used to work as a car salesman, so it is only natural that I can change my own oil.

オートメーション化かが進すすめば進すすむほど、社員しゃいんの数かずが減へるのも当然とうぜんだ。

As automation technology advances, it is only natural that the number of employees will decrease.

本当ほんとうに惜おしい結果けっかでした。悔くやしくて涙なみだが止とまらないのも当然とうぜんですよ。

It was truly a regrettable outcome. It is only natural that (she) would be mortified to the point of being unable to stop crying.

その古ふるくて枯かれた木きがとうとう倒たおれたのも当然とうぜんだよ。そんな長ながい間あいだ立たっていたのはおどろくほどだよ。誰だれも怪我けがをしなくて嬉うれしい。

It is no wonder that old, withered tree has finally collapsed. It is surprising that it had been standing for such a long time as that. I am glad that no one was hurt.

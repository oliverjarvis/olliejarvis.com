## About

About 逆に

English

Japanese

逆ぎゃくに is a grammar pattern that indicates that something is 'contrary' to what has previously been said. This is a structure made up of the noun 逆ぎゃく 'reverse', 'opposite', or 'converse', and に. Due to the inclusion of に, this phrase behaves adverbially, and will usually appear between two phrases, to indicate that 'on the contrary to (A), (B)', or 'conversely to (A), (B)'.

手袋てぶくろをはめた方ほうが安全あんぜんだと思おもうかもしれませんが、手袋てぶくろが回転部かいてんぶに巻まき込こまれると逆ぎゃくに危険きけんなので、手袋てぶくろを外はずしてから作業さぎょうをしてください。

You may think it is safer to wear gloves, but on the contrary, it is dangerous because they could get caught in the rotating parts. So, please remove your gloves before working.

近道ちかみちだと思おもっていたのに、地図ちずアプリで調しらべてみたら逆ぎゃくに回まわり道みちだった事ことがわかった。

I thought that it was a shortcut, but on the contrary, I found out that it was actually a detour when I checked on a map app.

食たべやすいようにスパゲティを半分はんぶんにして茹ゆでたけど、逆ぎゃくに食たべにくくなった。

I broke the spaghetti in half to make it easier to eat, but on the contrary, it made it harder to eat.

In modern Japanese, 逆ぎゃくに is also frequently used at the beginning of a sentence, simply to indicate a rebuttal toward something that someone has just said, or as a slang version of 別べつに when asked questions about preferences, or similar things.

逆ぎゃくに、何歳なんさいに見みえますか？

On the contrary, how old do I look?

逆ぎゃくに、ラーメンは？

I don't care too much what we eat, how about ramen?

あの話はなしは事実じじつらしいが、逆ぎゃくに信しんじにくい。

I heard that that story is true but on the contrary, it is hard to believe.

親切しんせつで声こえをかけたのに、逆ぎゃくに苦情くじょうを言いわれた。

Even though I called out to him in a gentle voice, on the contrary he complained.

遅刻ちこくになると思おもって、急いそいだが逆ぎゃくに余裕よゆうでした。

I thought that I would be late and hurried, but on the contrary, I made it with plenty of time.

クレーム対応たいおうをしっかりしたら、逆ぎゃくに売上うりあげが伸のびました。

I dealt with the complaint firmly (assuming there would be a negative impact), on the contrary my sales increased.

寒さむいと思おもって厚着あつぎをして行いったら、逆ぎゃくに暑あつかった。

I thought it was cold and went wearing thick clothing. On the contrary, it was hot.

喉のどが渇かわいた状態じょうたいでコーラを飲のむと、逆ぎゃくに喉のどが渇かわく。

If you drink cola while you are thirsty, on the contrary, you will become more thirsty.

病気びょうきが治なおりそうだったが、薬くすりを飲のまずにいると逆ぎゃくにひどくなった。

The disease was on the verge of being healed, but I didn't take my medicine and on the contrary it got worse.

少すこし派手はでかなと思おもっていたのに、逆ぎゃくに「地味じみなネクタイだね」と言いわれた。

Even though I thought it was a little gawdy, on the contrary, someone said, 'Your necktie is quite reserved.'

こんな靴くつを履はいて行いって笑わらわれるかと思おもったら、逆ぎゃくに羨うらやましがられました。

I thought if I went wearing shoes like these I would be laughed at, on the contrary people were envious of me.

力ちから一杯いっぱい投なげるようにしたら、逆ぎゃくにストライクが入はいる確率かくりつが上あがった。

I started throwing it with all of my might (expecting it to be less accurate). On the contrary the probability of getting a strike increased.

重おもたいものを持もつと肩かた凝こりが酷ひどくなるかと思おもっていたけれど、鍛きたえられて逆ぎゃくに肩かた凝こりが治なおった。

I thought carrying heavy things would make my stiff shoulders worse, but working out on the contrary cured my stiff shoulders.

年上としうえの人ひとにタメ口ぐちで話はなしかけたら、その人ひとが逆ぎゃくに敬語けいごを使つかってきたから気きまずかった。

I talked casually with a person older than me. On the contrary, they used keigo (polite speech) with me and it was awkward.

## About

About から見ると

English

Japanese

から見みると is a common sentence pattern seen when something is being viewed either from the perspective of (A), or a judgment is being made based on looking at the situation of (A) objectively. This may be translated either as 'from the point of view of (A), (B)', or 'from looking at (A), one could say (B)'.

This grammar construction is comprised of the conjunction particle から 'from', the る-Verb 見みる 'to look', and any of the usual hypothetical statement markers such as と, たら, ば, or even the て-form. It will appear following nouns.

アニメに興味きょうみがない人ひとから見みると、どのアニメも同おなじに見みえる。

From the point of view of someone who is not interested in anime, all anime looks the same.

外そとからみたら、私またしのコレクションはゴミに見みえるでしょう。

From an outside point of view, my collection probably just looks like garbage.

私わたしから見みれば、彼女かのじょはあまり美人びじんじゃない。

From my point of view, she is not that much of a stunner.

このタイヤ跡あとの大おおきさから見みて、犯人はんにんはトラックを使つかったに違ちがいない。

By the looks of the size of these tire tracks, the perpetrator must have used a truck.

Caution

から見みると tends not to be used with things that cannot actually physically be seen or assessed, such as smells, sounds, etc.

あの人ひとの喋しゃべり方かたから見みると、あの人ひとは日本人にほんじんではないだろう。

From the point of view of how they speak, that person probably isn't Japanese. (Unnatural Japanese)

## Examples

Examples

00:04

Sentence
Translation

外そとから見みると、やりやすそうです。

From an outside point of view it looks easy.

老人ろうじん夫婦ふうふから見みると、若者わかものの恋愛れんあいは懐なつかしい。

From the point of view of elderly couples, young love is nostalgic.

普段ふだんから見みて、今年ことしは稀まれな気象きしょうが多おおかった。

From the view point of what is normal, there were a lot of rare weather phenomena this year.

脇役わきやくから見みると主役しゅやくは羨うらやましいが、主役しゅやくは主役しゅやくで大変たいへんらしい。

From the point of view of a supporting role, the lead part is enviable, however the lead role looks very tough.

小学生しょうがくせいから見みると中高生ちゅうこうせいは大人おとなに見みえるが、実際じっさいはそうでもない。

From the point of view of an elementary schooler, junior and senior high schoolers look like adults, but that is not really the case.

高級こうきゅうな食器しょっきだと思おもっていたのに、専門家せんもんかから見みると大たいしたことないようです。

Even though I thought it was expensive tableware, from the point of view of a specialist, it seems like it isn't that big of a deal.

素人しろうとから見みるとかなりうまい人ひとでも、プロの世界せかいでは全然ぜんぜん通用つうようしないらしい。

It seems that even people who are considerably skilled from a layperson's point of view would not get by in the pro world.

外国人がいこくじんから見みると日本語にほんごが上手じょうずな人ひとでも、日本人にほんじんからするとアクセントの違ちがいが気きになってしまう。

Even if a person seems proficient in Japanese from a foreigner's point of view, a Japanese person will notice a different accent.

一般いっぱん庶民しょみんから見みると芸能人げいのうじんは憧あこがれの職業しょくぎょうだが、結構けっこう大変たいへんな仕事しごとらしい。

From the point of view of the general public, having a job as an entertainer is longed for, however it apparently is rather tough work.

知しらない人ひとから見みるとただ暴あばれているようにしか見みえないが、あれは民族みんぞくの正式せいしきな踊おどりです。

From the point of view of a person who doesn't know, it looks like nothing more than someone flailing about, but that is a formal ethnic dance.

相撲すもうを知しらない人ひとから見みると力士りきしたちは太ふとりすぎだと思おもうかもしれないが、彼かれらはアスリートです。

From the point of view of people who don't know sumo, they probably think that the wrestlers are too fat, but they are athletes.

子供こどもはテレビゲームが大好だいすきだが、大人おとなから見みると何なにが楽たのしいのかよく分わからないゲームも多おおい。

Children love video games, but from the point of view of an adult, there are a lot of games that are hard to understand the appeal.

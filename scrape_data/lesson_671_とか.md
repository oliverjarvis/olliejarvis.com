## About

About とか

English

Japanese

Being a combination of the particles と and か, とか 'quotes' the previous statement as being something that has been heard, but is unconfirmed or uncertain. In English, this translates as 'I heard something like (A)', or '(A) or something was said'.

とか may appear after verbs, い-Adjectives, な-Adjectives, or nouns.

遠藤えんどうさんと近藤こんどうさんが結婚けっこんするとか。意外いがいじゃない？

I heard that Endo-san and Kondo-san are getting married or something. Isn't that surprising?

あの大手おおて家電かでんメーカーが開発かいはつした最新さいしんのスマホはたくさんのブロートウェアがインストールされているせいで動作どうさがものすごく重おもいとか。

I heard that the latest smartphone developed by that major appliance manufacturer performs extremely slow because of all the bloatware that comes installed, or something like that.

高橋たかはしくんの彼女かのじょが美人びじんだとか。あんなやつに美人びじんな彼女かのじょができたとは信しんじられないけどな。

I heard that Takahashi's girlfriend is beautiful or something. I can't believe that a guy like that could have a beautiful girlfriend, though.

あの人ひとは実じつはお金持かねもちだとか。

I heard that person is actually rich or something.

This meaning of 'or something' when using とか is fairly casual and will often be heard in everyday conversation.

Caution

This combination of と and か may appear the same as the とか used to indicate lists, but the former is simply a combination of the case-marking particle と, and the adverbial particle か, while the latter is a single listing particle. Despite this, both structures are originally combinations of と and か, and may be thought to have some overlap in meaning.

I heard that the music group called AKB48 is going to break up or something like that.

最近さいきんはカラーサングラスが流行はやっているとか。

I heard that color sunglasses are popular recently or something like that.

お母かあさんが朝あさごはんを食たべるのは大事だいじだとか言いっていた。

Mom was saying that eating breakfast is important or something like that.

先生せんせいがテストの問題もんだいは難むずかしくないとか言いってたけど本当ほんとうかな？

The teacher was saying that the test questions weren't hard or something like that, but I wonder if it's true.

鉄分てつぶんが不足ふそくすると無駄むだに落おち込こみやすくなったりするんだとか…。

It's said that if you have an iron deficiency, it's easy for you to get pointlessly depressed or something like that.

社長しゃちょうは年末ねんまつ年始ねんしに温泉おんせん旅行りょこうに行いってらっしゃるとか。

I heard that the company president is going on a hot springs trip over New Years or something like that.

この計画けいかくは絶対ぜったい成功せいこうするとか言いってたけどさ、そんな甘あまくないよね。

You were saying that this plan will definitely succeed or something like that, right? You're not really that naive are you?

今日きょうは雨あめが降ふるんだとか。だったら、傘かさを持もって行いかないわけにはいかない。

It's going to rain today or something like that. If that's the case, then there's no way I can go without an umbrella.

地球温暖化ちきゅうおんだんかに関かんする問題もんだいは解決かいけつしうるとか…。でも、現状げんじょうは悪わるくなる一方いっぽうである。

It's said that we're able to solve the problems in relation to global warming or something like that... But the real situation is that it just keeps getting worse.

めがねちゃん：「彼かれが昨日きのう、何なんて言いったか覚おぼえてる？」

Megane-chan: 'Do you remember what he said yesterday?'
Yankee-kun: 'He said, 'lets meet in front of school around 11' or something like that, but I am not really sure.'

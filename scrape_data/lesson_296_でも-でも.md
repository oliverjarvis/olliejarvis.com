## About

About 〜でも 〜でも

When the adverbial particle でも is used more than once in a sequence, it is an extension of one of its regular meanings, analogical reasoning. However, several 'analogies' are being presented.

This use of でも is regularly translated as 'whether it is (A), or (B)', or 'neither (A) nor (B)', depending on whether the rest of the phrase is positive, or negative.

サッカーでもバスケットボールでもいいからスポーツをやりたい。

Regardless of whether it is soccer or basketball, I want to play sports.

ケーキでもクッキーでも苺いちごが入はいっていたらどちらも食たべない。

Even if it is a cake or a cookie, I will not eat it if it has strawberries in it.

Literally, the translation of 〜でも〜でも is closer to the following:

Even if it's (A), even if it's (B), (C) is the result. (In a positive sentence)

Even if it's (A), even if it's (B), (C) is not the result. (In a negative sentence)

Sentence
Translation

これでも、それでも、何なんでもいいです！

Whether this, or that, whatever (is fine)!

サッカーでも、卓球たっきゅうでも、彼かれならスポーツは何なんでもできる。

Whether it's soccer or table tennis, he can play any sport.

電車でんしゃでも、バスでも行いける場所ばしょなので、便利べんりです。

You can get there whether by train or bus, so it's convenient.

彼かれがＭａｃマックでもＷｉｎｄｏｗｓウィンドウズでも使つかえるって。

(He said) that whether it's Mac or Windows, he can use them.

じゃんけんでも、サイコロでも何なんでもいいから、早はやく決きめてよ。

Whether it is with rock, paper, scissors or with dice, or whatever, hurry up and decide.

教科書きょうかしょでも、ネットでも日本語にほんごを学まなぶことが出来できるから習ならってみたらどう？

Whether it is with a textbook or online, you can learn Japanese so why not give it a shot?

６０ろくじゅっ点てんでも、１００ひゃく点てんでも、合格ごうかくしたらいいんだよ。

It doesn't matter whether you get a 60 or a 100, as long as you pass.

電車でんしゃでも、バスでも、飛行機ひこうきでも、タクシーでも使つかえる。

You can use either a train or a bus or a plane or a taxi.

ジュースでもコーラでも何なんでもいいから、早はやく飲のみましょう。

It doesn't matter whether it is juice or cola, let's hurry up and drink!

喉のどが渇かわいたので、水みずでも、お茶ちゃでも飲のみたいな。

I am thirsty, so whether it is water or tea I want to drink it.

英語えいごでも、日本語にほんごでも、中国語ちゅうごくごでも喋しゃべれる彼かれが凄すごいと思おもう。

Whether it is English or Japanese or even Chinese, he (who can speak those languages) is amazing.

円えんでも、ドルでも商品しょうひんを買かえるからこのコンビニが便利べんりだ！

Whether it is with yen or dollars, you can buy products at this convenience store so it is convenient!

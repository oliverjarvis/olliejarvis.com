## About

About という点から考えると

English

Japanese

と言いう点てんから考かんがえると is a set phrase which indicates that something is being discussed from the 'point of view of (A)'. This may be translated as 'from the viewpoint of (A)', 'speaking in terms of (A)', or 'when viewed from the angle of (A)'. The noun 点てん 'point' can be thought of as directly indicating the aspect of (A) that is being focused on. Literally, the meaning is 'if considered from the aspect of (A), (B)'.

This expression often follows either verbs or nouns, However, when following nouns, の may occasionally replace という, especially in cases where it is a simple noun, rather than an entire quotation that just happens to finish with a noun.

日本にほんのデジタル化かが遅おくれているという点てんから考かんがえると、デジタル人材じんざいの育成いくせいやデジタルインフラの整備せいびに力ちからを入いれなければ日本にほんのデジタル化かはどんどん遅おくれていくだろう。

If we think in terms of Japan's digitalization lagging behind, if Japan doesn't focus on improving their digital workforce and infrastructure, it's digitalization will fall further and further behind.

子供こどもの教育きょういくという点てんから考かんがえると、楽たのしく勉強べんきょうをさせる事ことが重要じゅうようです。

From the standpoint of educating children, it is important to make learning fun.

健康けんこうの点てんから考かんがえると休やすむ時ときはちゃんと休やすまなければ身体からだを壊こわす可能性かのうせいが高たかまります。

From the standpoint of health, if you don't rest when you need to rest, the possibility of physical damage increases.

Fun-fact

Although a combination of the quotation particle と and the う-Verb 言いう 'to say', という is mostly written in hiragana and considered to be a set phrase which simply indicates that the contents of the phrase preceding it requires explaining in some way.

Even if we assume it will work, if one thinks in terms of production costs it is still inefficient.

エンドユーザーの便利べんりさという点てんから考かんがえるとそれはむしろ不便ふべんである。

If we think in terms of end user convenience, that might actually be inconvenient.

仕事しごとの効率こうりつの点てんから考かんがえると１いち時間じかんに５ご分ふんの休憩きゅうけいを取とる方ほうがいい。

If we think in terms of work efficiency, it is better to take a 5 minute break every hour.

ビジネスという点てんから考かんがえると需要じゅようが高たかいということは高たかい利益りえきの可能性かのうせいがあるということだ。

If we think in terms of business, high demand means a high probability of profit.

健康けんこうの点てんから考かんがえると砂糖さとうの多おおい食たべ物ものを食しょくすればするほど肥満ひまんの可能性かのうせいが高たかくなる。

If we think in terms of health, the more sugary food you consume, the higher the chance of obesity.

さらに、子供こども向むけの製品せいひんの開発かいはつという点てんから考かんがえると違ちがったアプローチが必要ひつようです。

Moreover, if one thinks in terms of the development of a product for children, a different approach is needed.

クライアントの点てんから考かんがえると値段ねだんが安やすい事ことは、ファストフードのレストランが人気にんきになった主おもな理由りゆうの一ひとつである。

If one thinks from the client's standpoint, then one of the main reasons why fast food restaurants have become popular is that they are cheap.

ハイテク企業きぎょうという点てんから考かんがえると、利益りえき創出そうしゅつのためのイノベーションが何なによりも重要じゅうようだ。

If we think in terms of a high-tech company, innovation in order to generate profit is more important than anything.

日本にほんの教師きょうしは１週間いっしゅうかんに働はたらく時間じかんの点てんから考かんがえると部活動ぶかつどうなどにより世界せかいで一番いちばん忙いそがしい。

If we think in terms of hours worked per week, Japanese teachers are the busiest in the world due to club activities and the like.

お客様きゃくさまの個人こじん情報じょうほうを安全あんぜんに扱あつかうという点てんから考かんがえるとデータが暗号化あんごうかされなければ、システムは完璧かんぺきに安全あんぜんではない。

If we think in terms of safely managing guests' personal data, unless data is encrypted, the system won't be completely safe.

If one thinks in terms of the three factors required at the time of buying a new smartphone, 'battery life', 'screen resolution', and 'price', then as expected the models of this brand are the best.

It is said that nowadays, word processors can automatically fix all errors in documents. From such a perspective it seems that there is no need to learn proper spelling or grammar. However…

## About

About に比べて

English

Japanese

に比くらべて is a set expression in Japanese, used when comparing one thing to another. It is often simply translated as '(A) compared to (B)', or 'when comparing (A) to (B)'. 比くらべる is a る - Verb that means 'to compare', or 'to contrast'.

に比くらべて will be attached directly after a noun, before the (B) part of the sentence will explain the comparison that is being made.

東京とうきょうの冬ふゆは北海道ほっかいどうの冬ふゆに比くらべて全然ぜんぜん寒さむくない。

Tokyo's winter is not cold at all compared to Hokkaido.

去年きょねんに比くらべて今年ことしの冬ふゆは暖あたたかい。

Compared to last year, this year's winter is warm.

Occasionally, the case marking particle と will be used instead of に. に and と are largely interchangeable in this expression, with に focusing slightly more on the changes/differences between similar features of (A) and (B). Alternatively と often mutually contrasts (A) and (B), discussing their individual/unique characteristics.

大阪おおさかの人口じんこうは東京とうきょうの人口じんこうに比くらべて小ちいさい。

Osaka's population is smaller compared to Tokyo.

彼かれも真面目まじめだけど、君きみは彼かれに比くらべるともっと真面目まじめだ。

He is serious too, but you are more serious compared to him.

君きみは彼かれと比くらべて真面目まじめだ。

Compared to him, you are serious. (You are serious, while he has other qualities)

日本にほんはアメリカと比くらべて小ちいさい。

Compared to America, Japan is small. (Japan is small, compared to America, which is big, among other things)

Caution

The way that 比くらべる is conjugated is not overly important to the sentence, so long as the meaning of 'if', or 'when' is conveyed. Due to this, に比くらべたら, に比くらべれば, and に比くらべると are all possible combinations.

他ほかの地域ちいきに比くらべたら、この地域ちいきは住すみやすい。

Compared to other areas, this area is very easy to live in.

去年きょねんに比くらべれば今年ことしは寒さむいけど、昨日きのうに比くらべたら今日きょうは暖あたたかい。

If compared to last year, it is colder this year. However if we compare it to yesterday, it is warm today.

新型しんがたのパソコンに比くらべるとこのパソコンは遅おそく感かんじるけど、そんなに遅おそくない。

If we compare this computer to a new model, it feels slow, but it's not that slow.

## Examples

Examples

Sentence
Translation

野球やきゅうに比くらべたら、サッカーの方ほうが好すきだ。

When compared with baseball, I like soccer more.

一学期いちがっきに比くらべて成績せいせきがよくなった。

My grades have improved compared to first semester.

昨日きのうに比くらべたら今日きょうは暖あたたかい。

Compared to yesterday, today is warmer.

去年きょねんに比くらべて今年ことしは雪ゆきが多おおい。

There is more snow this year compared to last year.

昔むかしに比くらべると、今いまの人ひとは長生ながいきだ。

People live longer nowadays compared to those in the past.

先輩せんぱいに比くらべたら、僕ぼくはまだまだ日本語にほんごが下手へただ。

Compared to my superior, I am still poor at Japanese.

以前いぜんの家いえに比くらべたら、今いまの家いえは快適かいてきだ。

Compared to our last house, the house we have now is more liveable.

マニュアル車しゃに比くらべたら、オートマの方ほうが運転うんてんが楽らくだ。

Compared to manuals, automatic cars are easier to drive.

アメリカに比くらべたら、日本にほんの方ほうが安全あんぜんだと思おもう。

I think that when compared to America, Japan is the safer of the two.

他ほかの痛いたさに比くらべて、頭痛ずつうは一番いちばん辛つらい。

When compared to other types of pain, headaches are the worst.

夏なつに比くらべて、ずいぶん涼すずしくなったが、まだまだ暑あつい。

Compared to summer it is quite cool, but it's still hot.

高級こうきゅう肉にくに比くらべると少すこし味あじは落おちるが、この肉にくも十分じゅうぶん美味おいしい。

When you compare this meat with expensive meat, it is not as good. However, it is still quite delicious.

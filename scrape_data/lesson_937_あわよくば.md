## About

About あわよくば

あわよくば is a combination of the old-fashioned い-Adjective あわよい 'convenient', 'timely', and the conjunction particle ば 'if'. It can be translated as 'if the time is right', 'if possible', or 'if luck is on my side'. Despite its roots, あわよくば is treated as a single word in modern Japanese, and is used in the same way as a typical adverb.

あわよくば will come before a phrase expressing a desirable outcome.

あまり勉強べんきょうをしてないが、あわよくば合格ごうかくするだろう。

I haven't studied much, but I'm sure I'll pass if I'm lucky.

あわよくば推おしの子とデートしたいと思おっている。

If possible, I would like to date the person I'm a fan of.

あわよくば今週こんしゅうは家いえでゆっくりしたい。

If luck is on my side, I want to relax at home this week.

Fun Fact

Notice that ば attaches to the く form of the い-Adjective あわよい rather than けれ, as would usually be required with modern conjugation rules. This is due to the conjugation rules for ば in relation to い-Adjectives being different in both classical and modern Japanese.

競馬けいばで勝かって、あわよくば大金持おおがねもちになるぞ。

If you win at the horse races, if you're lucky you'll become really rich!

議員ぎいん：「あわよくばトップで当選とうせんしたい。」

Member of congress: 'If possible, I want to get elected to the top spot.'

あわよくばミスコンで優勝ゆうしょうしたいと思おもっている。

If luck is on my side, I am thinking that I would like to be the grand champion in the beauty pageant.

この詐欺さぎが成功せいこうしたらあわよくば裕福ゆうふくになれる。

If we succeed with this scam, all going well we'll be able to become wealthy.

会社かいしゃの業績ぎょうせきが上あがった。あわよくば僕ぼくの給料きゅうりょうも上あがるかも。

Our company performance improved. If I'm lucky my salary will improve too.

この裁判さいばんに勝かったら、あわよくば大金たいきんが転ころがり込こむかもしれません。

If I win this trial, all going well a big payout of cash will come in.

人事じんじ異動いどうがあるらしく、あわよくば私わたしも昇格しょうかくするかもしれない。

It seems that there is going to be a personnel reshuffle, so if I am lucky I might get promoted.

有名ゆうめいなクラブに行いくことになったから、あわよくば芸能人げいのうじんに会あえるかもしれない。

Since I'm going to be going to a famous club, all going well I might meet a famous person.

彼かれは彼女かのじょに振ふられたばかりだから、あわよくば私わたしと付つき合あってくれるかもしれない。

Since he just got dumped by her, he might date me if I am lucky.

あわよくば欲ほしかったブランド物もののお財布さいふが手てに入はいると思おもったが、売うり切きれていた。

I thought that I'd be lucky to get my hands on a famous brand of wallet that I wanted, but it was sold out.

彼女かのじょを花火はなび大会たいかいに誘さそって、あわよくば付つき合あえたらいいなぁ。

I invited her to the fireworks festival, if possible it would be nice if she went out with me.

引ひき分わけ、いや、あわよくばこの試合しあいに勝かちたいと思おもっている。

A draw, nah, if possible I think that I would like to win this match.

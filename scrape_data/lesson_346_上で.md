## About

About 上で

English

Japanese

上うえで is an expression in Japanese that is used after the past form of verbs, or nouns followed by の. It shows that something will happen 'after (A)', or 'upon (A)'. This grammar point is used in a similar way to its literal meaning, which is 'with the above/previous (A), (B)'. 上うえ as 'above', and で as 'with', in で's use as a case marking particle.

この企画きかくは社長しゃちょうと相談そうだんした上うえで、キャンセルすることに決きめました。

Upon consulting the CEO of the company, we have decided to cancel this project.

色いろんな車くるまを運転うんてんした上うえで、どの車くるまを買かうか決きめたいと思おもいます。

Upon driving various cars, I would like to choose which car to purchase.

以下いかの内容ないようを確認かくにんの上うえで、サインをしてください。

Upon confirming the details below, please sign your name.

以下いかのリンクからログインの上うえで、内容ないよう変更へんこうをしてください。

Upon logging in from the link below, please change your information.

上うえで is a structure that is primarily used in formal settings. Due to this, it will not be used in casual conversations very often. It also strongly hints at (A) being a requirement for (B), or (A) being something that would logically be expected before (B).

こちらの商品しょうひんは医者いしゃに相談そうだんした上うえで、使用しようしてください。

Please use this product upon consulting with a doctor.

Caution

Due to 上うえで being used to show logical progressions from (A) to (B), (A) cannot be a verb that is passive. Passivity in Japanese often implies that someone/thing has a lack of control in relation to the actions that happen to them/it. As a result of this, (A) not being able to be controlled would result in (B) sounding like it could not be planned for.

医者いしゃに教おしえられた上うえで、使用しようしてください。

Please use this upon being told to by a doctor. (Unnatural)

This is different to the way that passivity works in English (simply showing that something received an action upon it).

Sentence
Translation

家いえの中なかを見みた上うえで、買かおうと思おもっている。

I am thinking that I will buy the house after I have seen the inside. (upon)

説明書せつめいしょを読よんだ上うえで、ご使用しようください。

Please use it after reading the instructions. (upon)

変更へんこうした上うえで、間違まちがいがあるかどうか確認かくにんするべきだ。

After making changes, you should check whether or not there are any mistakes. (upon)

妻つまと相談そうだんした上うえで、手術しゅじゅつを受うけることを決きめたいと思おもう。

I would like to make the decision about undergoing surgery, after I discuss it with my wife. (upon)

慎重しんちょうな調査ちょうさの上うえで、被告ひこくは有罪ゆうざいと決定けっていしました。

Upon careful investigation, the defendant has been found guilty. (after)

十分じゅうぶん付つき合あった上うえで、彼女かのじょと結婚けっこんしたいかどうか決きめます。

After going steady for a sufficient amount of time, I will decide whether or not I want to marry her. (upon)

アンケート用紙ようしに記入きにゅうした上うえで、来店らいてんしていただけると助たすかります。

It would help us a lot if you would fill out the questionnaire before (upon) coming into our shop/restaurant. (after)

３さん年間ねんかん、日本語にほんごを勉強べんきょうした上うえで、日本にほんに仕事しごとを見みつけに行いくことを決きめた。

After learning Japanese for three years, I decided to go to Japan to look for a job. (upon)

ちゃんと調査ちょうさした上うえで、報告書ほうこくしょを提出ていしゅつしてください。

I want you to submit your report after you have done the proper amount of research. (upon)

メンバー全員ぜんいんに確認かくにんした上うえで、結果けっかを教おしえてほしい。

I want you to let me know the results after confirming with all members. (upon)

住民じゅうみんと協議きょうぎの上うえで、橋はしを開通かいつうするかどうか結論けつろんを出だしたいと思おもいます。

We would like to come to a conclusion on whether or not to open the bridge to traffic upon consulting with the inhabitants. (after)

わが社しゃは研究結果けんきゅうけっかを詳細しょうさいに分析ぶんせきした上うえで、その化学物質かがくぶっしつを製造せいぞうすることに決きめた。

After detailed analysis of study results, our company decided to produce that chemical substance. (upon)

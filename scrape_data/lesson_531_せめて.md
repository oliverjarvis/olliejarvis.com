## About

About せめて

English

Japanese

Coming from the verb 責せめる, meaning to have the 'onus to do (A)', せめて is an adverb that indicates that doing (A) is the minimum expected in order to achieve some desired outcome. It is often translated simply as 'at least' in English.

せめて usually alters the meaning of an entire phrase, and will appear at the beginning of sentences.

ご飯はんは残のこしても良いいから、せめて刺身さしみだけは全部ぜんぶたべて。

You can leave the rice, but at least finish the sashimi please.

せめて国語こくごの宿題しゅくだいはやっておきなさい。

At least finish your Japanese homework in advance.

Stemming from 責せめる, this structure usually implies that there is some sort of onus or responsibility to do (A), rather than (A) simply being a minimum amount. Due to this, it is often used with grammar points that express desire or opinions such as たい, ほしい, てほしい, べき, and others.

せめてこの曲きょくだけでも弾ひけるようになりたい。

I want to be able to play this song, at least.

えっ... たったの５ご千円せんえんだけ？せめて１いち万まんは欲ほしいよ。

What…only five-thousand yen? I want at least ten-thousand yen.

別べつに毎日まいにち洗濯せんたくをしろっては言いわないけど、せめて晩御飯ばんごはんだけは毎日まいにち作つくってほしい。

I'm not telling you to do laundry everyday, but I want you to at least make dinner every night.

１いち日にちにせめて１いちリットルの水みずを飲のむべきだ。

You should drink at least 1 liter of water a day.

Mother: 'At least eat your sides or something.'

Would you at least let me change? I am soaked.

いい案あんではなくてもいいので、せめてアイデアだけでも出だしてください。

Since it is okay even if it isn't a good idea, please at least submit an idea.

楽器がっきを寄贈きぞうさせてください。せめてもの恩返おんがえしです。

Please let me give you an instrument. It is at least a repayment of the favor.

買かわなくてもいいので、せめて感想かんそうを教おしえてください！

Since it is okay even if you don't buy it, please at least tell me your impression!

せめて観測かんそく結果けっかを教おしえてください。この通とおりです！

Please at least tell me the results of the observation. Like this!

せめて角度かくどだけでも教おしえてもらえれば、その形かたちを再現さいげんできるのに。

If you would at least tell me the angle, I would be able to reproduce that shape...

泊とまらなくてもいいので、せめて温泉おんせんだけでも入はいっていってください。

Since it is okay even if you don't stay overnight, please at least go to the onsen.

マラソンで勝かつのは無理むりだけど、せめて、完走かんそうしたい...

I realize it is not possible to win the marathon, but I want to finish it at least...

かつてのような強つよさが取とり戻もどせなくても、せめて半分はんぶんくらい取とり戻もどせれば...。

Even if I cannot take back my former strength, if I can at least get back half of it...

ライトノベルの主人公しゅじんこう：「君きみは居候いそうろうだから、せめて自分じぶんの料理りょうりは自分じぶんでするべきだよ。」

The protagonist of a light novel: 'You are a freeloader, so at least you should cook your own food!'

## About

About ～やがる

やがる is a colloquial usage of the auxiliary verb 上あがる 'to go up' or 'to be complete' that can be attached to the conjunctive form of verbs and will often be translated as 'to have the nerve to (A)', or 'to have the gall to do (A)'. It is primarily used by men in response to actions performed by others that bring about feelings of scorn or disdain.

あの黒くろの車くるまはしょっちゅう俺おれの家いえの前まえに駐車ちゅうしゃしやがるから迷惑めいわくしてる。

That black car has the nerve to park in front of my house all the time and it's a nuisance.

何なにを勝手かってなことをしやがる！頼たのんでもないことを勝手かってにやるな。

What the heck obnoxious things are you doing! Don't freely do stuff that I didn't even ask you to do.

Alternatively, やがる may also be attached to the て-form of verbs, which is simply an abbreviation of ている + あがる as an auxiliary verb once again. The meaning however will be the same.

おいおい、また集金しゅうきんきてやがるじゃねーかよ。

Oi oi, as if you're telling me they'd have the nerve to come here collecting money again.

あんなに注意ちゅういしたのに、また犬いぬにちょっかいを出だしてやがる。

Despite warning him that many times, he still has the nerve to bother the dog again.

That guy had the nerve to lie to me!

あの居酒屋いざかやはぼったくりをしやがる。

That izakaya is a freaking rip off.

あの野郎やろう、俺おれにデタラメを教おしえやがった！

That guy had the nerve to tell me such nonsense!

あのガキ、俺おれに偉えらそうな態度たいどをとりやがる！

That brat, to have the nerve to take that arrogant attitude with me!

あの女おんな、俺おれを騙だましてお金かねを奪うばいやがった！

That woman, she had the nerve to deceive me and freaking robbed me of my money!

チンタラ歩あるくな！さっさと歩あるきやがれ！

Don't walk sluggishly! Freaking get a move on and walk!

くそっ、あの社員しゃいん、また仕事しごとをサボってやがる！

Dammit, that employee, he has the nerve to flake on work again!

アイツはクソだ。弱よわい者ものいじめをしてやがる。

He is trash. He has the gall to pick on weak people.

全然ぜんぜん意味いみが分わからない。何なにを言いってやがる…。

I have absolutely no idea what you mean. What are you trying to freaking say?

あいつ、万引まんびきをしてやがる！警察けいさつを呼よばないと！

That guy is freaking shoplifting. I gotta call the police.

あの生徒せいとはふざけた事ことを言いいやがる！懲こらしめるぞ！

That student has the nerve to say idiotic things! I am going to discipline them!

コーチ：「あいつ、また格下かくしたの相手あいてに負まかされやがって！」

Coach: 'That guy, he freaking lost again against a lower ranked opponent than him!'

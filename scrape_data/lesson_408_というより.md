## About

About というより

English

Japanese

When combining the quotation use of the case marking particle と with the う - Verb 言いう 'to say', and the case marking particle より 'comparing', というより presents a phrase that is being compared through further evaluation/reconsideration. This may be thought of as similar to 'rather than saying (A), (B)' in English.

というより may be used after any type of word. However, due to phrase that comes before it 'determining' something that will then be compared, nouns and な-Adjectives will require だ (despite being omitted in casual speech). だ may also be omitted if the sentence finishes with a だ that is describing the same thing in a different light.

あの人ひとは仕事しごとをしているというより、仕事しごとをさせられている感かんじがする。

Rather than saying that he is working, it seems like he is being made to work.

日本にほんの天井てんじょうが低ひくいというより、あなたの身長しんちょうが高たかいだけだと思おもう。

Rather than Japanese ceilings being low, I think you are just tall.

僕ぼくは暇ひまだというより、なにをすればいいか分わからないだけだ。

Rather than having nothing to do, I just don't know what I should be doing.

ここは公園こうえんというより、小ちいさい広場ひろばみたいな物ものだ。

Rather than calling this a park, it is something more like a small field. (だ used at the end rather than following 'park', as 'field' is the actual state that the speaker considers true)

Caution

It should be remembered that より (in all of its uses) will highlight something that is 'less'. This means that the (B) part of というより sentences will always describe something that is 'more', 'closer', 'more accurate', etc.

マサミさんは綺麗きれいというより、かわいい。

Masami is more cute than beautiful.

その話はなしがつまらないというより、興味きょうみがないだけ。

It's not that the story is boring, it's just more that I have no interest in it.

## Examples

Examples

00:03

Sentence
Translation

今日きょうは暖あたたかいと言いうより暑あつい。

It is hot rather than warm today.

あの人ひとはかわいいと言いうより綺麗きれい。

That person is beautiful rather than cute.

彼かれは聡明そうめいと言いうよりずる賢がしこい人ひとです。

Rather than saying that he is wise, he is cunning.

彼かれは技術者ぎじゅつしゃと言いうより芸術家げいじゅつかだ。

He is more of an artist than an engineer.

これは悲かなしさと言いうより後悔こうかいに近ちかい。

Rather than saying that this is sorrow, it is closer to regret.

それは理由りゆうと言いうより言いい訳わけですよね？

Rather than calling it a reason, it's an excuse, right?

これはサッカーと言いうよりフットサルのコートみたいだ。

This appears to be more of a futsal court than a soccer pitch.

「嫌きらい」と言いうより「好すきではない」と言いった方ほうが正確せいかくだ。

Rather than saying 'I hate it', it is more accurate to say 'I don't like it'.

これは草くさと言いうより、竹たけみたいだ。

Rather than saying that this is grass/weeds, it looks like bamboo.

来月らいげつと言いうより来年らいねんと言いった方ほうがいいかな。

Rather than saying next month, maybe it is better to say next year.

オニズカさんは先生せんせいと言いうより友達ともだちだと思おもっています。

I think of you (Onizuka-san) as more of a friend than a teacher.

毎日まいにちと言いうより、一週間いっしゅうかんに五回ごかい運動うんどうをすると言いった方ほうが正ただしい。

Rather than saying every day, I would say it's more correct to say that I exercise 5 days a week.

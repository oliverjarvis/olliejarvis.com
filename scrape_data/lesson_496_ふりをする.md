## About

About ふりをする

English

Japanese

ふりをする is an expression in Japanese that is used to show that someone is 'pretending to be (A)', 'putting on airs of (A)', or 'acting as if (A)'. This construction comes from a combination of the noun 振ふり 'a shake', the case marking particle を, and する. It can be thought of as meaning 'to give off the impression of (A)', or 'to shake off the impression of (A)'.

As ふり itself is a noun, this expression may be used with the attributive form of any other word.

この蛇へびは人ひと等などの大おおきな動物どうぶつが近ちかづくと死しんだ振ふりをするらしい。

I heard that this snake pretends to be dead when humans and other similar big animals approach it.

僕ぼくは子供こどもの頃ころ、よく寝ねている振ふりをした。

When I was a child, I would often pretend to sleep.

欲ほしいのはこれではなかったけど、せっかく買かってくれたから嬉うれしい振ふりをする。

This is not what I wanted, but since they went out of their way and bought it for me, I will pretend that I am happy.

俺おれの前まえではそんな、元気げんきな振ふりをしなくていいよ。

You don't have to pretend that you are happy in front of me.

カケル君くんはカエルが好すきだからいつもカエルの振ふりをして遊あそんでいる。

Because Kakeru-kun likes frogs, he always pretends to be a frog when he plays.

Fun Fact

The noun 振ふり has several different nuances in Japanese, with only one of them being used in this particular expression. That meaning is 'to show a certain behavior pattern on the outside'. Due to this, ふり doesn't specifically mean 'to pretend', just that what is happening on the outside may be different to what is happening within a person (either mentally or physically).

泣ないた振ふりをしたって無駄むだですよ。

When you pretended to cry, it was useless.

私わたしの犬いぬは寝ねた振ふりをすることがある。

There are times when my dog pretends to sleep.

子供こどもは、注意ちゅういされた時ときに、聞きこえない振ふりをした。

The child pretended not to hear while she was being scolded.

病気びょうきの振ふりをして会社かいしゃを休やすんだ。

Pretending to be sick, I took the day off work.

見みて見みない振ふりをするのは良よくない。

It's not good to turn a blind eye to something. (pretend not to see)

嬉うれしくない振ふりをしていたが、実じつはかなり嬉うれしかった。

I was pretending that I wasn't happy but, actually, I was quite happy.

彼女かのじょは若わかい振ふりをしているが、結構けっこう歳としをとっているように見みえる。

She is pretending to be young, but she appears to be quite old.

分わかっている振ふりをしていたが、あの説明せつめいは全然ぜんぜん理解りかいできなかった。

I was pretending I understood, but I couldn’t understand that explanation at all.

先生せんせいの前まえでだけ真面目まじめな振ふりをする人ひとは嫌きらいだ。

I dislike people who pretend to be good students only in front of the teacher.

どんなことについても知しっている振ふりをする人ひとは信頼しんらいができない。

I don't trust people who act as if they know everything.

騙だまされた振ふりをして、振ふり込こめ詐欺さぎ犯はんの逮捕たいほに貢献こうけんしました。

She contributed to the arrests of the bank transfer scammers by pretending to be tricked (by them).

熊くまに遭遇そうぐうした時とき、死しんだ振ふりをしてもあまり意味いみがないと聞きいた。

I have heard that when you encounter a bear, even if you pretended to be dead, it won't make much difference.

## About

About Verb[て] + みせる

English

Japanese

て見みせる, primarily written in hiragana, is an emphatic structure that indicates that the speaker is going to do (A) in a way that it is clear to the listener. This can be translated as 'I will definitely (A)', or 'I swear I will (A)', but the literal translation of 'I will show you that I'll do (A)' also makes the point clear.

To use てみせる, simply alter the verb preceding it into its て-form.

お前まえのために、次つぎの試合しあいでは勝かってみせる！

I will definitely win the next competition for you!

来週らいしゅうのテストで合格ごうかくしてみせる！

I will definitely pass the test next week!

足あしが痛いたいけど、完走かんそうしてみせる！

My legs hurt, but I am definitely going to finish the race!

Due to みせる requiring a target for who the speaker is showing, this grammar pattern will only be used when there is either directly someone that will be shown (A), or when the speaker is talking about showing someone (A) that does not happen to be there at the moment either physically or temporally. It will not be used when simply strongly stating that you will do (A), unless who sees that (A) is important. In a sentence like this, where the person being shown is not obvious, it will come across as 'I'll show everyone'.

As て見みせる literally just means to 'show (A)', it will often be used without any special emphasis. Whether it is being used emphatically or not will usually be obvious from the overall context of the sentence.

## Examples

Examples

00:05

Sentence
Translation

「何なにがあっても、必かならずファンの期待きたいに応こたえて見みせます！」

'No matter what happens, I will definitely live up to my fans' expectations!'

ピアニスト：「この曲きょくを明日あしたまでに弾ひきこなしてみせる！」

Pianist: ‘I will definitely be able to play this song well by tomorrow!’

「今年ことしこそ、必かならず運転うんてん免許めんきょを取とって見みせるよ。」

'I will definitely get a drivers license this year!'

「どんな手てを使つかってでも、絶対ぜったい、成功せいこうして見みせる！」

'I will definitely win, no matter what it takes!'

妻つまを喜よろこばせるような美味おいしい料理りょうりを作つくってみせる！

I will make delicious food that my wife will be thankful for, just watch!

日本語にほんごを完璧かんぺきにマスターして、日本人にほんじんの友達ともだちをビックリさせて見みせる！

I will perfectly master Japanese and surprise my Japanese friends for sure!

面接めんせつで良いい印象いんしょうを与あたえて、必かならずこの仕事しごとをゲットして見みせる！

I will give a good impression in the meeting and get this job whatever it takes!

猫ねこカフェに行いったら、おもちゃで沢山たくさんの猫ねこを操あやつってみせる！

When I go to a cat café, I will play with lots of cats with a toy for sure!

子供こども：「将来しょうらいは有名ゆうめいな俳優はいゆうになって、ママとパパに良いい生活せいかつをさせてみせる！」

Child: ‘In the future, I swear that I will become a famous actor and give my mom and dad a good life!’

小説しょうせつ：「必かならずこの手てで魔王まおうを倒たおして見みせるという勇者ゆうしゃエミリアの言葉ことばに、わたしたちは賭かけることにした。」

Novel: 'We decided to bet on Emilia the hero's words, that she would definitely defeat the demon king with her very own hands.'

負まけた後のち、相手あいてを挑発ちょうはつする：「次つぎこそ、必かならず勝かって見みせるぞ！あなたには負まけない！」
相手あいて：「よし、かかってこい！」

After losing, challenging the opponent: 'I will definitely win next time! I won't lose!'
Opponent: 'OK! Bring it on!'

アニメ、 友達ともだち１いち：「先輩せんぱいに私わたしの気持きもちを絶対ぜったい気きづかせて見みせるわ！」
頼たよれる友達ともだち２に：「がんばれ！」

An anime, Friend 1: 'I will definitely make senpai realize my feelings!'
Supportive friend 2: 'Go for it!'

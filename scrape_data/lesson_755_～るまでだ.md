## About

About ～るまでだ

English

Japanese

までだ, and までのことだ are structures that are usually translated as 'merely (B)', or 'simply (B)'. However, they are almost always used in somewhat hypothetical sentences, and translate much closer to 'one can only but (B)'. This nuance indicates a sense of resignation, or understanding on the part of the speaker that they personally have no options beyond (B).

This grammar pattern will be used following verbs in their dictionary form, and will often come at the end of a sentence that uses なら in the first clause. In this way, it will mean 'if (A), then (B) is all that can be done'. (A) will almost always be a negative situation.

誰だれにも教おしえてもらえないなら、ネットで調しらべてみるまでだ。

If no one is going to teach me, I will just search it online.

もし失敗しっぱいしたのなら、もう一回いっかい挑戦ちょうせんするまでだ。失敗しっぱいしても諦あきらめないことが大切たいせつだ。

If you made a mistake, you just have to try again. It is important to not give up even if you fail.

誰だれも迎むかえにきてくれないのなら、電車でんしゃで帰かえるまでのことだ。

If no one is going to come pick me up, I will simply go home by train.

この会社かいしゃに入社にゅうしゃできなかったら、別べつの会社かいしゃを探さがすまでのことです。

If I can't join this company, I will simply look for a different one.

Examining the standard meaning of the particle まで as signifying an extent, it can be seen that this meaning is kept in までだ, with the speaker expressing that (B) is the extent of what is possible given the negative circumstances brought about by (A).

Sentence
Translation

「問題もんだいが起おきても、解決かいけつするまでだよ。」

'If a problem arises, I will just solve it.'

もしＪＬＰＴジェーエルピーティーに落おちたら、もう一度いちど試ためしてみるまでのことだ。

If I fail the JLPT, I will simply try it once again.

電車でんしゃに乗のり遅おくれたら、次つぎのを待まつまでのことだ。

If I miss the train, I will simply wait for the next one.

夫婦ふうふの話はなし：「やめなくちゃならないというなら、やめるまでのことだ。」

A conversation between a couple: 'If you say I have to quit, then I will simply quit.'

祖国そこくで就職しゅうしょくできなかったら、外国がいこくへ移住いじゅうすることになるまでだ。

If I can't find work in my motherland, I will simply move abroad.

ファンタジー小説しょうせつ：「峠とうげを越こえるのが不可能ふかのうならば、モリアの坑道こうどうを通とおり抜ぬけるまでのことだよ。」

Fantasy novel: 'If passing through the mountain pass is impossible, we will simply go through the mines of Moria!' (just)

飲のみ会かいのせいで終電しゅうでんを逃のがしてしまったのなら、タクシーを拾ひろって帰かえるまでだ。

If I miss the last train due to the drinking party, I'll simply just grab a taxi and go home.

一度いちど試験しけんに失敗しっぱいしてもまた受うけるまでのことなので、そこまで思おもい詰つめる必要ひつようはありません。

Even if you fail the examination once, since you just need to take it again, there's no need to torment yourself about it.

元彼もとかれが「君きみと別わかれなくちゃいけないなら死しぬまでだ。」と言いった時ときはゾッとしました。

I was horrified when my ex-boyfriend said 'if we must break up, I will just die'.

確たしかにその会社かいしゃと提携ていけいを結むすびたいのは事実じじつだが、不ふ公平こうへいな条件じょうけんを提示ていじされたら断ことわるまでだ。

Certainly, while it is true that I want to form a bond with that company, if any imbalanced conditions are suggested, I will simply refuse.

心こころの健康けんこうほど大事だいじなものは無ないのだから、そんなに今いまの仕事しごとをやりたくないなら辞やめるまでです。

Since even mental health is not something that is valued, if you really do not want to do your current work that much, simply resign.

性格せいかくや食しょくの好このみが合あわないとなれば別わかれるまでだ。結婚けっこんを考かんがえないうちはそうやって場数ばかずを踏ふむつもりだ。

If our personalities or tastes in food are not compatible, we can simply break up. Doing that while we're still not thinking about marriage is something that I intend to gain experience from.

## About

About も～ば～も

English

Japanese

も～ば～も is a construction in Japanese which presents aspects of something to which both (A) and (B) are true. も will be connected to a noun, before the hypothetical marking particle ば will appear following a verb, い-Adjective, な-Adjective, or noun. In the case of nouns and な-Adjectives, なら or ならば will be used.

In this way, も～ば～も just highlights that 'If (A) is true, then so is (B)'. This usually just emphasizes that (A) and (B) are things that go together hand-in-hand, with one trait or characteristic always existing if the other does. Standard translations include 'both (A) and (B) are (C)', or 'the trait of (C), is in (A) and (B) as well'.

お父とうさんはイタリア語ごも出来できれば、スペイン語ごも話はなせる。

My father can speak Italian and Spanish.

日本にほんの冬ふゆは湿度しつども低ひくければ、温度おんども低ひくいから外そとで火ひを使つかう時ときは気きをつけないといけない。

Winter in Japan is cold and dry, so you have to be careful when using fire outside.

このギターの音おとも好すきなら柄がらも好すきなので、一生いっしょう手放てばなしたくありません。

I like the sound and the design of this guitar, so I never want to let it go.

マルチ商法しょうほうの話はなしを聞きくたびに、騙だます方ほうも騙だます方ほうなら、騙だまされる方ほうも騙だまされる方ほうだといつも思おもう。

Whenever I hear about pyramid schemes, I always think that if the scammers are scammers, then the scammed are scammed. (I always think that the scammers and the scammed are both at fault.)

Caution

With も～ば～も, both traits will be things that share some sort of mutual connection. This means that both (A) and (B) will either be positive, neutral, or negative, but rarely a mixture of either one. Despite this, (A) and (B) can occasionally be contrastive, such as with 'to like', and 'to hate'.

あの居酒屋いざかやのスタッフの態度たいどもよければ、値段ねだんも高たかいから人気にんきがある。

The attitude of the staff at that izakaya is good and it is expensive, which is why they are so popular. (Unnatural Japanese)

## Examples

Examples

Sentence
Translation

彼女かのじょは地位ちいもなければお金かねもない。

She has neither status nor money.

あの人ひとは、実力じつりょくもあれば、努力どりょくもする。

That person has capabilities and puts forth effort.

彼かれは撮影さつえいもできれば、ナレーションもできる。

He can do photography and also narration.

「うまくいっている」という実感じっかんもなければ、兆候ちょうこうもない。

There is not any feeling of 'it is going well,' nor is there any indication.

総理大臣そうりだいじんは、経済けいざいも見直みなおせれば、人権じんけん問題もんだいも解決かいけつできます。

The Prime Minister can reform the economy and resolve human rights issues.

あの曲芸師きょくげいしは、茶碗ちゃわんも回まわせれば、座布団ざぶとんも回まわせる。

That acrobat can spin a tea cup and spin a cushion.

あの子こは、算数さんすうもできなければ国語こくごもできないから、将来しょうらいが不安ふあんです。

That child cannot do math, and cannot do Japanese, so I am anxious about their future.

彼かれは、スポーツも上手じょうずにできれば、仕事しごとも効率こうりつよくできる。

He can play sports well and does his job efficiently.

あのバスは、指定席していせきもなければ予約よやくもできないから、行いってみないと乗のれるか分わからない。

Since that bus does not have assigned seating and you cannot reserve a seat, if you do not go and check, you won't know if you can ride or not.

あの候補者こうほしゃは、地盤じばんもなければ知名度ちめいどもないから、たぶん受うからないと思おもいます。

Since that candidate does not have a base and does not have popularity, I think perhaps he will not win.

人ひとは皆みんな、短所たんしょもあれば弱よわさもある。だからこそ、助たすけ合あって生いきるのです。

Everyone has disadvantages and weaknesses. For that reason, live to help each other.

彼かれは、習字しゅうじもできれば、ピアノだって弾ひけるし、スポーツも万能ばんのうだ。

He can write well, as well as play the piano, and he is versatile in sports.

## About

About うちに

English

Japanese

When うち, (sometimes seen using the kanji 内うち, or 裡うち) is paired with the case marking particle に, it expresses a period/space 'within' which something else will/should happen/exist. It quite often (but not always) carries the nuance of (A) being the 'best', or the 'optimal' time/space within which (B) could occur/exist. In English, うちに can simply be translated as 'while', or 'during'.

うちに may be paired with the dictionary or ている form of verbs, the plain form of い-Adjectives, nouns followed by の, or な-Adjectives that are followed by な.

彼かれと毎日まいにち会あううちに、だんだんと彼かれのことが好すきになってきた。

As I meet with him every day, I have gradually come to like him. (As I spend more time with him every day, I have grown to like him)

熱あついうちに食たべて！

Eat it while it's hot!

お爺じいちゃんがまだ元気げんきなうちに家族かぞく皆みんなで旅行りょこうに行いこう！

Let's all go on a family trip while grandpa is still healthy!

今いまのうちに明日あしたの準備じゅんびをしておこう。

Let's get ready for tomorrow while we have time. (Before it is too late)

Unlike 間あいだに, うちに expresses something that can happen/exist anywhere 'within' the span of (A), and is therefore non-specific as to where (B) is located.

運転うんてんしている間あいだに眠ねむくなったので、コンビニで休憩きゅうけいをした。

I got sleepy while driving, so I took a break at a convenience store. (While in the process of driving)

運転うんてんしているうちに眠ねむくなったので、コンビニで休憩きゅうけいをした。

I got sleepy while driving, so I took a break at a convenience store. (At one point while driving)

Fun Fact

The kanji 裡うち refers literally to the inner walls/lining of something, and anything that is enclosed within that space. This can be thought of as where the 'non-specificity' of うち comes from, in regard to where within that space something is.

Sentence
Translation

近ちかい内うちに連絡れんらくします。

I'll be in touch before long.

鉄てつは熱あつい内うちに打うて。

Strike while the iron is hot.

授業じゅぎょうを受うけている内うちに、宿題しゅくだいを完成かんせいさせた。

I was able to finish my homework while taking a class.

赤あかちゃんは食たべている内うちに寝ねてしまった。

The baby fell asleep while eating.

日本にほんにいる内うちに東京とうきょうに行いってみたい。

While I am in Japan, I want to see Tokyo.

朝顔あさがおはみるみる内うちに成長せいちょうし、大おおきくなった。

The Morning Glory grew taller and got bigger within / during a short time.

一晩ひとばんの内うちに性格せいかくを変かえることはできない。

It is impossible to change your personality over the course of / during one night.

明あかるい内うちに家いえに帰かえろう。あの道みちは暗くらいから。

Let's go home while it's still light out. Since that street is dark.

若わかい内うちにお酒さけを飲のんだりタバコを吸すったりしてはいけません。

You must not drink and smoke while you are young.

僕ぼくが仕事しごとをしている内うちに、君きみが夕食ゆうしょくを作つくってくれるから助たすかる。

It is quite helpful that you make dinner while I do my work.

やっている内うちにどんどん上達じょうたつするから、あきらめてはいけない。

You must not give up because you will continue to get better while doing it.

一生いっしょうの内うちに一度いちどは聖書せいしょを読よんでみたいと思おもっている。

I would like to try to read the Bible at least once in / during my lifetime.

## About

About にもとづいて

English

Japanese

In order to say that one thing is 'based on' another in Japanese, the expression に基もとづいて will be used. This is a combination of the case marking particle に, the う - Verb, 基もとづく 'to originate from', and the conjunction particle て. This expression regularly highlights some kind of judgement/conclusion that the speaker has made, using (A) as their base.

To use に基もとづいて, put the noun (or noun phrase) that you would like to identify as a 'basis' before に基もとづいて, and then express your judgment/conclusion in part (B) of the sentence.

クライアントの指示しじに基もとづいて、編集へんしゅうをしておきました。

I have edited this based on the client's instructions. (Edited in advance, assuming that would be the client's wishes)

以前いぜん成功せいこうした計画けいかくに基もとづいて、新あたらしい計画けいかくを立たてようと思おもっています。

I am thinking of making a new plan based on the previous successful plan.

Alternatively, the past form of 基もとづく may be used when connected directly to a second noun. (A) に基もとづいた (B) directly expresses that (B) is based on (A).

私わたしは実話じつわに基もとづいた映画えいがが好すきです。

I like movies based on true stories.

来週らいしゅうまでに、アンケートに基もとづいたグラフを作成さくせいしてください。

By next week, please make a graph based on these surveys.

Fun Fact

The kanji 基もと is fairly accurately translated as 'foundation', and is regularly used to highlight things that 'stem' from something. Due to this, に基もとづいて may also be used to identify things that are 'produced from (A)', 'created from (A)', or 'invented based on (A)'.

あの国くにの法律ほうりつは宗教しゅうきょうに基もとづいて作つくられている。

That country's law is founded on religion.

先月せんげつ貰もらった資料しりょうに基もとづいて、このレポートを書かきました。

I wrote this paper based on the documents you gave me last month.

この小説しょうせつは歴史れきし的てき実話じつわに基もとづいて書かかれている。

This novel is written based on true historical events.

I lend money based on trust.

法律ほうりつに基もとづいて、判断はんだんさせていただきます。

I will make my judgment based on the law.

ちゃんと計画けいかくに基もとづいて、動うごいてください。

Please proceed in accordance with the plan.

あなたの意見いけんに基もとづいて、変更へんこうを加くわえました。

I implemented a modification based on your opinion.

間違まちがった前提ぜんていに基もとづいているから、当然とうぜん結果けっかも間違まちがっている。

The results are incorrect because (the experiment) is being based on incorrect assumptions.

長年ながねんの観察かんさつに基もとづいて、この結論けつろんに至いたった。

I came to this conclusion based on many years of observation.

実話じつわに基もとづいたドラマには説得力せっとくりょくがある。

Dramas based on true stories are very convincing.

教授きょうじゅの理論りろんに基もとづいて、研究けんきゅうを続つづけてみます。

I will try to continue the research according to the professor's theory.

I made an appraisal based on the results of the analysis.

I will make future plans based on my experiences.

An essay based on my personal opinion.

A movie based on actual historical events.

## About

About までもない

English

Japanese

までもない is a grammar pattern that consists of the particle まで 'until', the adverbial particle も, and ない 'there is not'. This structure will primarily be seen with a translation similar to 'there is no need to (A)', or 'it is not necessary to (A)'. However, a more literal translation of 'it is not so far as to (A)' is also completely acceptable, and still carries the correct nuance.

までもない will be used following verbs in their dictionary form, and may or may not have a part (B) explaining why (A) is not necessary.

痛いたいけど、指ゆびは全部ぜんぶちゃんと動うごくから病院びょういんに行いくまでもないと思おもう。

Although it hurts, all my fingers move properly so there is no need to go to the hospital.

息子むすこを見みて、何なにか悪わるいことをしたと分わかった。聞きくまでもなかった。

I could tell that my son did something bad by just looking at him. It was not necessary for me to ask anything.

あの人ひとならなんとかしてくれるよ。心配しんぱいするまでもないよ。

Knowing that person, they would probably do something about it. There is no need to worry.

As can be assumed by the use of まで, the primary nuance of までもない is that while there may be some sort of slight issue or problem, the speaker does not consider it as being something that has reached a point that is worth being concerned about. It may also indicate that because something is obvious, it does not need to be confirmed, etc.

Caution

When coming between an (A) and a (B) phrase, the conjunctive form までもなく will be used, but this is less common.

言いうまでもなく、不法ふほう投棄とうきで近所きんじょの人ひとが迷惑めいわくしています。

It goes without saying that illegal disposal of garbage is causing troubles to the people in this neighborhood.

## Examples

Examples

Sentence
Translation

「今いまさら訂正ていせいするまでもなく、明あきらかに間違まちがっている。」

'There is no need to correct it, it is clearly wrong.'

先生せんせいは、すでに全すべて解説かいせつしたので聞きくまでもない。

There is no need to ask questions because the teacher has already explained everything.

これはただの打撲だぼくで骨ほねにヒビが入はいっているわけではないので病院びょういんに行いくまでもない。

Since this was just a body-blow, and it is not the case that there is a fracture to the bone, there is no need to go to the hospital.

あのね......そんごくうはクリリンなんかとは比くらべるまでもなく、それどころかベジータよりもずっと強つよいんだから。

Hey, there is no need to compare Goku to someone like Krillin, because he is far stronger than Vegeta.

この試験しけんの難むずかしさから考かんがえると、試験しけんの結果けっか発表はっぴょうは見みるまでもなく明あきらかだ。

Judging from this exam's difficulty, the results are clear without the need to see them.

ゆきちゃん：「さきちゃん、あれはひどかったよ。」
さきちゃん：「あんな奴やつなら、優やさしくするまでもない。」

Yuki: 'Saki, that was cruel.'
Saki: 'If it's a guy like that, there is no need to be friendly.'

私わたしの家いえはここから徒歩とほ五分ごふん圏内けんないなので、わざわざ私わたしを家いえに送おくるまでもないよ。

As for my house, since it is within the range of a 5 minute walk by foot from here, there is really no need to go out of your way to escort me home.

その問題もんだいなら私わたし一人ひとりで解決かいけつできるから、わざわざ他ほかの部署ぶしょから助すけっ人とを呼よぶまでもない。

If it is just that issue, since I can resolve it by myself, it is not necessary to go out of the way to call for a helper from another department.

全身ぜんしんに返かえり血ちを浴あび、犯行はんこうに使つかわれた凶器きょうきを持もった彼かれが殺人犯さつじんはんである事ことは確たしかめるまでもなく明あきらかです。

Being that he was completely covered in the blood of his victim, and had the murder weapon, there is no need to confirm that he is the killer. It is plain as day!

彼かれの事ことなら心配しんぱいするまでもないよ。だって誰だれよりも賢かしこくて体力たいりょくも精神力せいしんりょくもあるんだから。

If it is him, then you don't need to worry! You know, because he is more clever, more resilient, and has a stronger will than anybody else!

母親ははおやが社長しゃちょうで父親ちちおやが医者いしゃである生徒せいとの家庭かていに金銭きんせん的てきな余裕よゆうがある事ことは言いうまでもない。

It is needless to say that a student who has a managing director as a mother and a doctor as a father has a financially abundant household.

コーラの瓶びんを開あけようとしているとき、Ａエー：「待まっててよ。栓抜せんぬきをすぐ持もってくるよ。」
テーブルの端はしっこで瓶びんを開あける、Ｂビー：「わざわざ持もってくるまでもないよ。」

When trying to open a bottle of coke, A: 'Wait a sec! I'll quickly bring a bottle opener.'
*Opens the bottle with the edge of the table* B: 'No need to bother bringing it!' ('You don't need to go to the trouble of getting it!')

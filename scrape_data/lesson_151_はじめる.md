## About

About はじめる

To express that actions are beginning or ending in Japanese, the verbs 始はじめる (to commence), or 終おわる (to end) will need to be used. In this lesson, we will focus on actions that are 'beginning'.

To say that (A) is starting/beginning, we will need to attach はじめる to the ます stem of almost any verb. Whether you use the hiragana form, or the kanji form does not matter, as there will be no change in nuance.

来月らいげつからお金かねをため始はじめます。

I will start saving money from next month.

歌うたを歌うたい始はじめる。

To start singing a song.

Due to はじめる being a verb itself, the tense can be changed, to show that something will start, is starting, or has started.

明日あしたから日本語にほんごを習ならい始はじめる。

I will start learning Japanese from tomorrow. (Will start)

雨あめが降ふり始はじめてきた。

It is starting to rain. (Is starting)

高校生こうこうせいの時ときにお金かねを貯ため始はじめた。

I started saving up money when I was in high school. (Has started)

Caution

It can be easy to accidentally use 始はじまる, the intransitive form of this verb. However, with this expression, the transitive form will always be used, due to the verb that はじめる is attached to being the thing that 'is started', by 'something' else.

It started to snow this morning.

明日あしたから、日本語にほんごを習ならいはじめます。

I will start learning Japanese from tomorrow.

練習れんしゅうしはじめてください。

Please begin to (do) practice.

９く時じになってから、働はたらきはじめた。

I started working from nine.

彼かれが話はなし始はじめてから１いち時間じかん経たった。

It has been an hour since he started talking.

海うみに行いってすぐ泳およぎはじめた。

I went to the ocean and started swimming at once.

また騒さわぎはじめた。本当ほんとうにあいつらうるさいね。

They started being boisterous again. They are really noisy, aren't they?

日本にほんに行いってから、文法ぶんぽうを勉強べんきょうしはじめた。

I started studying grammar after going to Japan.

日本語にほんごを勉強べんきょうしはじめてすぐ、日本語にほんごの難むずかしさが分わかった。

Right after I started studying Japanese, I realized the difficulty of the language.

大学だいがくを卒業そつぎょうしてから、仕事しごとに行いきはじめた。

I started going to work after graduation from college.

この商品しょうひんを売うりはじめてから、どんどんお客きゃくさんが増ふえています。

Since we started selling this product, customers have been on the rise.

まずひらがなとカタカナを勉強べんきょうして、その後あと漢字かんじを勉強べんきょうしはじめた方ほうがいいよ。

First study hiragana and katakana. After that it would be better if you began to study kanji.

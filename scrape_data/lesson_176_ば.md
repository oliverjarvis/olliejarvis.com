## About

About ば

There are several different ways to express 'if' in Japanese. One of these is through using the conjunction particle ば. ば presents hypothetical situations (possibilities), and pairs with verbs using an う to え sound-change conjugation method on the terminal (last) kana. Let's examine this with a few verbs first.

仕事しごとを辞やめれば自由じゆう時間じかんが増ふえるけど、お金かねが無なくなる。

If I quit work, I will have more free time, but I will have no money.

雨あめが止やめば、今日きょうも釣つりに行いくつもりです。

If it stops raining, I intend to go fishing today also.

そこの信号しんごうで右みぎに曲まがれば病院びょういんに着つきます。

If you turn right at the light just there, you will get to the hospital.

店みせに電話でんわをすれば、すぐに予約よやくができるよ。

If you call the store, you will be able to make a reservation immediately.

あいつがくればパーティーが楽たのしくなると思おもう。

If he comes to the party, I think it will be more fun.

As we can see with these verbs, the final kana will change to an え sound, from its original う (る to れ, う to え, く to け, す to せ, etc.) before adding ば. It is important to practice these, to not get them mixed up with verbs that use れる, and られる. Unlike with these verbs, the base form of する and くる will not change when using ば.

To conjugate い-Adjectives with ば, the い will be replaced with けれ, one of the standard い-Adjective conjugation forms. After this, just add ば.

頭あたまが痛いたければ、薬くすりを飲のんで寝ねた方ほうがいい。

If your head hurts, it would be better if you take some medicine and then sleep.

ラーメン屋やのチャーハンが不味まずければ、そこのラーメンも不味まずいだろう。

If a ramen shop's fried rice tastes bad, then the ramen probably tastes bad too, right?

For な-Adjectives, and nouns, なら (another standard conjugation form of these words) will be added, before attaching ば.

刺身さしみが嫌きらいならば 、寿司すしも嫌きらいでしょう。

If you do not like sashimi, then you probably don't like sushi either, right?

バイクならば、バイク専用せんようの駐輪場ちゅうりんじょうに止とめてください。

If it's a motorcycle, please park it in the designated bike parking lot.

In the negative form (using ない), both the auxiliary verb ない, and the い-Adjective ない will conjugate in the same way. Simply replace い with けれ, and then add ば.

私わたしが作つくったクッキーを食たべたくなければ、捨すててもいいよ。

If you don't want to eat the cookie that I made, you can throw it away.

カレーが辛からくなければ、いっぱい食たべれる。

If the curry isn't spicy, I can eat a lot of it.

トラックの運転手うんてんしゅでなければ、この仕事しごとの辛つらさが分わからない。

If you aren't a truck driver, then you wouldn't understand the hardships of this job.

Fun Fact

The なら in ならば is often used by itself to mean 'if', and is an abbreviation of the classical auxiliary verb なり 'to be'. In its hypothesis form, なり will become ならば. However, なり is very irregularly used by itself in modern Japanese, with なら, and ならば being far more common.

Caution

So that you do not mix up the conjugation rules for ば, and passive verbs, remember that the last kana of the verb will change to an あ sound, before adding れる with passive verbs. However, this change is to an え sound with ば.

運動うんどうをすれば痩やせるよ。

If you exercise, you will lose weight.

夜よる遅おそくに電話でんわをされると迷惑めいわくです。

It is very inconsiderate of you to call this late at night.

ヘルメットを被かぶらなければ、バイクに乗のれない。

If (you) do not wear a helmet, then (you) cannot ride a motorbike.

明日あした雨あめが降ふれば、映画えいがを見みに行いくつもり。

If it rains tomorrow, then I will go watch a movie.

毎日まいにちサッカーの練習れんしゅうに行いけば、プロのサッカー選手せんしゅになれる。

If you go to soccer practice every day, then you can become a professional soccer player.

今週こんしゅうの金曜日きんようびまでに出来できなければ、来週末らいしゅうまつに終おわらせなくてはいけない。

If (you) cannot do it by this Friday, then (you) have to finish it next weekend.

展覧会てんらんかいに行いけば、色々いろいろなことを学まなべる。

If (you) go to an exhibit, then (you) can learn a variety of things.

天気てんき予報よほうを見みれば、明日あしたの天気てんきが分わかるだろう。

If (you) look at the weather forecast, then (you) will know tomorrow's weather (yeah?).

地図ちずをよく見みれば迷子まいごにならないから、よく見みてね。

Since you won't get lost if you look at the map well, please look properly.

そのコーヒーを好すきでなければ、この飲のみ物ものも絶対ぜったい好すきじゃないでしょう。

If you do not like that coffee, then you most certainly will not like this drink.

この本ほんを読よめば、日本にほんの文化ぶんかが分わかる。

If you read this book, then you will understand Japan's culture.

丁寧ていねいに話はなせば、面接めんせつがうまく行いくと思おもうよ。

If you speak politely, then I think that your interview will go well.

ＬＥＤエルイーディーを使つかえば、電気代でんきだいが大分だいぶ減へります。

If you use LEDs, then your electric bill will go down greatly.

毎日まいにち運動うんどうをして、プロテインを飲のめば、筋肉きんにくが増ふえると思おもう。

If you exercise everyday and if you drink protein shakes, then you will gain muscle.

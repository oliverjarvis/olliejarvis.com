## About

About がち

English

Japanese

がち is a suffix in Japanese that will be used after nouns, or the conjunctive form of verbs. It conveys that someone is 'apt to (A)', 'tends to (A)', or 'is prone to (A)'. Although not always negative, がち is most frequently used to express negative tendencies.

ここに来くるといつもパフェを頼たのみがちだけど、今日きょうはパンケーキを頼たのむ。

When I come here I always tend to order parfait, but today I am getting a pancake.

うちの子こは病気びょうきがちなので、週しゅうに二に、三さん日にちぐらいは学校がっこうを休やすみます。

My child is prone to getting sick, so they usually miss two or three days of school each week.

サボりがちな人ひとはだいたい成績せいせきが悪わるい。

People who tend to skip school usually have bad grades.

Fun Fact

がち as a grammar structure comes from the word 勝がち, meaning 'a win'. 勝がち in itself is used to express something that appears 'repeatedly', or 'again and again'. Due to this, がち may be thought of as similar to 'to win out' in English, meaning that 'although there are many possibilities, (A) winning out is the most likely'. This 'winning out' may be due to preference, or sheer good/bad luck.

暇ひまなときはテレビを見みがちだけど、忙いそがしい時ときには全然ぜんぜん見みない。

Watching TV wins out when I am bored, but when I am busy I don't watch it at all.

俺おれが回まわすと当あたりがちだから、俺おれにやらせてみろ。

Hitting the jackpot wins out when I spin it, so why don't you let me do it.

釣つれないことはありがちだから、落おち込こまなくてもいいよ。そういう日ひもあるさ。

Times that you cannot catch anything often win out, so don't be so upset. It happens.

That is a mistake beginners tend to make.

彼かれはいつも遠慮えんりょがちだ。

He always tends to hold back.

My boy is apt to get sick.

あの人ひとはすぐ、痩やせることを諦あきらめがちだ。

That person is prone to quickly give up on losing weight.

私わたしは昔むかしから遅刻ちこくしがちだ。

I have always been prone to being late.

車くるまばかり乗のっていると、運動うんどう不足ぶそくになりがちだ。

If you are always riding around in a car, you tend to not get enough exercise.

サボりがちな生徒せいとを家いえまで迎むかえに行いって、学校がっこうへ連つれてきた。

I went to the house of the student who tends to skip school, and brought him back.

私わたしは元気げんきだと思おもわれがちですが、実じつは体からだが弱よわいんです。

I am prone to be considered energetic, but I actually have a delicate constitution.

好すきなことを先さきにして、すべきことを後回あとまわしにしがちで良よくないな。

It's not good that I am prone to do things I want first and postpone important things I should be doing for later.

彼かれは最近さいきん学校がっこうを休やすみがちなので心配しんぱいです。

I am worried because recently he has developed a tendency to skip class.

恥はずかしがり屋やだからなのか、彼女かのじょはいつも伏ふし目めがちです。

She always tends to drop her eyes, maybe it is because she is shy.

学生がくせいたちは忘わすれがちだが、この問題もんだいは意外いがいとテストに出でる。

Students are apt to forget, but this problem will actually appear on the test.

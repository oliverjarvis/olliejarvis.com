## About

About おそらく

English

Japanese

恐おそらく is an adverb which originally stems from the conjugation of the yodan verb, 恐おそる 'to fear'. In most cases, 恐おそらく is simply used as a formal way to say 'probably', or 'perhaps', and is usually partnered with other structures of conjecture such as だろう, かもしれない, etc.

As 恐おそらく behaves adverbially, it will most often appear at the beginning of sentences before a statement of conjecture is made.

妻つまは今夜こんや会社かいしゃの人ひとたちと飲のみ会かいに行いくと言いっていたので、おそらく１１じゅういち時じごろぐらいまでは帰かえってこないと思おもいます。

My wife said she was going to a drinking party tonight with some people from work, so she probably won't be home until around 11:00 or so.

あいつの不自然ふしぜんな行動こうどうからして、おそらくあいつが犯人はんにんだろう！

Judging by that guy's unnatural behavior, he's probably the culprit!

おそらく明日あしたは雨あめなので、明日あした予定よていしていたピクニックは中止ちゅうししましょう。

Perhaps it may rain tomorrow, so let's cancel the picnic we had planned for tomorrow.

Although 恐おそらく can, and often does, carry the meaning of 'I fear that (A)', it isn't necessarily always used in relation to negative things. In the case of this particular grammar pattern, the main nuance that 'I fear that (A)' conveys is modesty on behalf of the speaker in that they fear their assumption may be right, given the available information.

Fun-fact

Yodan, or 四段活用よだんかつよう verbs are a type of classical-Japanese verb that have primarily been replaced by godan (う-Verbs) in modern day Japanese.

## Examples

Examples

Sentence
Translation

恐おそらく彼かれはここにいないだろう。

He most likely isn't here.

恐おそらく明日あした雨あめが降ふる。

Perhaps it will rain tomorrow. (I fear that...)

彼かれだったら、恐おそらくこんなことできっこないでしょう。

If it's him, he most likely won't be able to do it. (I fear that...)

「くそ、恐おそらくあなたの結婚式けっこんしきには行いけないじゃないか。」

'Dang it, I think I probably won't be able to go to your wedding.' (I fear that...)

道路どうろに沿そって行いけば恐おそらくガソリンスタンドが見みつかるでしょう。

If you go along the road, you will most likely see the gas station.

私わたしの兄あには恐おそらく１１じゅういち月がつ頃ごろアメリカから帰かえってくるでしょう。

My older brother will probably return home from the US around November.

凶器きょうきは怪我けがから察さっするに恐おそらくナイフのようなものでしょう。

The murder weapon was, judging from the injuries, most likely something resembling to a knife.

彼かれは何なにか言いいたそうに私わたしをみた。恐おそらく何なにか重要じゅうようなことでしょう。

It seemed to me that he was about to say something. Most likely something important. (I fear that...)

問題もんだいの原因げんいんを特定とくていするために最もっともすべきことは、恐おそらくその機械きかいの分解ぶんかいと分析ぶんせきだろう。

Probably, the most effective action to identify the cause of the problem would be the decomposition and analysis of the machine.

妊婦にんぷ：「赤あかちゃんの性別せいべつはなんですか？」医者いしゃ：「恐おそらく女おんなの子こです。でもまだわかりません。」

Pregnant woman: 'Is it a baby boy or girl?' Doctor: 'Probably a baby girl. However, I am not sure yet.'

名探偵めいたんてい：「この人ひとの背せの高たかさを鑑かんがみると恐おそらく彼女かのじょが殺人犯さつじんはんだろう！」

Great detective: 'Taking this person's height into account, she is most likely our killer.'

代表だいひょう者しゃが「大統領だいとうりょうは恐おそらく再選さいせんをねらうまい」と言いっていた。

The representative was saying that 'the president will most likely not aim for re-election'.

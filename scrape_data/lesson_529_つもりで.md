## About

About つもりで

English

Japanese

つもりで, being a combination of the noun つもり 'intention', and the case marking particleで 'with', this structure is used in several different ways, depending on what comes before it. When combined with verbs, な-Adjectives or nouns in their plain forms, it implies that (B) is being done with the intention of (A).

As つもり is a noun, の will be required before it when grouped with other nouns, while な will be required when linked to な-Adjectives.

何なにも買かわないつもりで新あたらしく出来できたショッピングモールへ行いったが、色々いろいろ買かってしまった。

I went to the newly built shopping mall without any intention of buying anything, but I ended up purchasing various things.

新鮮しんせんな魚さかなを選えらんだつもりで買かったのに、パックから出だしたらものすごいにおいがした。

I chose this believing that it would be fresh fish, but when I took it out of the package, it smelled incredibly bad.

冗談じょうだんのつもりで言いっただけなのに、相手あいてを傷付きずつけてしまった。

Despite saying it with the intention of making a joke, I ended up hurting their feelings.

However, when paired with verbs in the past form, つもりで implies that the speaker is 'acting as though (A)', 'pretending that (A)', or 'is convinced that (A)'.

今度こんどは、主人公しゅじんこうになったつもりで読よんでみてください。

Next, try reading it as if you were the main character.

パーティーでダンサーになったつもりで、みんなの前まえで踊おどった。

At a party, I danced in front of everybody, as if I was a dancer.

This meaning simply comes from the intention of something being contrary to what is actually true.

Examples

Sentence
Translation

ご馳走ちそうするつもりで行いったのに、おごってもらうことになった。

Despite going with the intention of treating everyone, they treated me.

本ほんにするつもりで執筆しっぴつしていたが、これはボツだな。

I wrote with the intention of publishing a book, but this will surely be rejected.

お土産みやげのつもりで買かったが、食たべてしまった。

I bought it with the intention of giving it as a gift, but I ended up eating it.

車掌しゃしょうになったつもりで、毎日まいにち電車でんしゃに乗のっている。

I ride the train every day acting like/pretending I have become a conductor.

日本にほんに行いったつもりで、日本語にほんごを勉強べんきょうしています。

I am studying Japanese as if I went to Japan.

今月末こんげつまつまでに完成かんせいさせるつもりで、毎日まいにち努力どりょくをした。

With the intention of finishing by the end of the month, I worked on it every day.

こっそり返かえすつもりでペンを借かりたのに、返かえすところを見みられてしまった。

Despite borrowing the pen with the intention of returning it secretly, just as I was returning it, I was seen.

趣味しゅみのつもりで始はじめた歌うたでしたが、コンサートを開ひらくまでに成長せいちょうできました。

I started singing with the intention of it being a hobby, but it's grown to the point where I have concerts.

カレーを作つくるつもりで買かい物ものをしたのに、結局けっきょくシチューを作つくった。

Despite going shopping with the intention of making curry, I ended up making a stew.

祭日さいじつにやるつもりで宿題しゅくだいを残のこしておいたのですが、全然ぜんぜんできませんでした。

I set aside my homework with the intention of doing it over the holiday, but I didn't do any of it.

再来週さらいしゅう旅行りょこうに行いくつもりで準備じゅんびしていたのに、仕事しごとの都合つごうで行いけなくなってしまった。

Despite getting ready with the intention of going on a trip the week after next, due to circumstances at work, I unfortunately cannot go.

受験じゅけんするつもりで勉強べんきょうすると、集中しゅうちゅう力りょくが湧わいてくる。

If you study with the intention of taking an exam, the ability to concentrate will come to you.

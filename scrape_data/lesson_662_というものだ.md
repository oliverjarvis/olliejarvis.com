## About

About というものだ

English

Japanese

というものだ is a fairly strong expression in Japanese that indicates that the speaker has the impression that (A) is the way it is, based on their objective judgment. This is usually translated as 'my impression of (A) is that'. However, the more literal translation is closer to '(A) is just a thing that'. This emphasizes that the speaker has high certainty that what they are saying (based on their experience) is the true nature of (A) in most cases.

というものだ is constructed of という, the noun 物もの 'thing', and だ. It will be seen at the end of phrases that the speaker or writer is making a judgment about.

何なにがあってもお互たがい助たすけ合あうのが友達ともだちというものだ。

Helping each other no matter what is just what friends do.

先輩せんぱいが着つく１０分じゅっぷん前まえに着つく。それがマナーというものだ。

Arriving 10 minutes before your senpai arrives. That is just manners.

どんなに疲つかれていても諦あきらめない。それがアスリートというものだ。

Not giving up no matter how tired you are. That is my impression of an athlete.

As this grammar pattern is quite direct, it is often used when criticizing something. However, this is not always the case, so will need to be judged based on context.

Caution

In casual conversation, という may be shortened simply to って, without any change in meaning.

何なにがあっても泣なかない。それが男おとこってものだ！

Not crying no matter what. That is my impression of a man!

## Examples

Examples

Sentence
Translation

その言いい分ぶんはわがままというものだ。

My impression is that your complaint is selfish.

あなたの好すき嫌きらいは食くわず嫌ぎらいというものだ。

My impression is that your likes and dislikes are prejudiced (dislike without trying).

嘘うそはつかない。それがジャーナリストというものだ。

Not telling lies. That is my impression of what a journalist is.

楽たのしくないと意味いみがない。それがサークルというものだ。

If it isn't fun, it is pointless. That is my impression of club sports.

彼かれはいつも結果けっかを出だす。あれが実力じつりょくというものだ。

He always gets results. That is my impression of competency.

すぐには忘わすれられないのが失恋しつれんというものだ。

Being unable to instantly forget about it is my impression of a broken heart.

誰だれよりも汗あせをかく。それが主役しゅやくというものだ。

To sweat more than everyone else. That is my impression of what being a leading role is.

時ときには残酷ざんこくな結果けっかになることもある。それが勝負しょうぶというものだ。

Sometimes encountering a terrible outcome. That is my impression of a contest (victory or defeat).

彼かれが知しらない真実しんじつを話はなすことが、親切しんせつというものだ。

My impression is that it is kind to talk to him about truths he does not know.

毎日まいにち少すこしずつしか進すすまない。それが撮影さつえいというものだ。

I can only progress a little each day. That is my impression of what photography is.

どんな困難こんなんにも立たち向むかおうとするのが職人しょくにんというものだ。

My impression of a craftsman is someone who stands and faces any hardship.

嫌いやな仕事しごともあるが、給料きゅうりょうは安定あんていしているのがサラリーマンというものだ。

My impression of being a salaryman is that you have a job that you hate, but your salary is steady.

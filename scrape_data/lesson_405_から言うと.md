## About

About から言うと

English

Japanese

からいうと (appearing with or without kanji) is a phrase in Japanese that is used to express a particular viewpoint or standpoint from which some type of evaluation, conclusion, or opinion can be drawn. The と that appears here is one of the assumption uses of this particle, meaning that から言いえば may also be used.

からいうと will always follow a noun (or noun phrase), before the (B) part of that same sentence will describe some kind of evaluation/opinion/etc. This structure closely reflects phrases like 'speaking in terms of (A), (B)', or 'what can be drawn from (A) is that (B)'.

私わたしの経験けいけんから言いうと、このやり方かたが一番いちばん効率こうりつがいい。

Speaking from my experience, this way of doing it is the most efficient.

私わたしの立場たちばから言いえば、彼かれは絶対ぜったいいつか成功せいこうする。

Speaking from my position, he will make it big one day.

Occasonally, the use of particles such as と or ば may be skipped entirely, with から言いって being used instead.

この結果けっかから言いって、この計画けいかくはあまりよくありませんでした。

Speaking from these results, this plan was not that great.

Caution

'Name + からいうと' may not be used with this expression, as a person is not a 'situation'. However, it is perfectly ok to use something like 'name の 立場たちばからいうと', as this highlights a specific situation that the said person is in.

タクミから言いうと、後輩こうはいのタケシ君くんは次つぎの試合しあいで勝かてそうなの？

Speaking as Takumi, do you think your kouhai, Takeshi-kun can win in the next competition? (Unnatural)

タクミの立場たちばからいうと後輩こうはいのタケシ君くんは次つぎの試合しあいで勝かてそうなの？

Takumi, speaking from your position, do you think your kouhai, Takeshi-kun is going to win at the next competition? (Natural)

サトミの視点してんからいうと、あの人ひとたちは採用さいようしない方ほうがいいという事ことですか？

Satomi, from your point of view, are you saying that we should not recruit those people? (Natural)

Caution

Due to からいうと regularly highlighting some sort of 'overall situation', it will not be used with momentary/visual things. In these cases, while not strictly incorrect, it may sound quite unnatural to a native speaker.

家いえのレイアウトから言いうと、この家いえは３０さんじゅう年前ねんまえに建たてられた家いえだと思おもう。

Speaking from the layout of this house, I think it was built 30 years ago. (Not incorrect, but a little unnatural)

A more appropriate choice here would be something like から見みると, as this can be used to make purely visual assessments.

法律ほうりつから言いうと、それは違反いはんです。

Speaking in terms of the law, that is a crime.

結論けつろんから言いうと、失敗しっぱいです。

Speaking in terms of results, it is a failure.

値段ねだんから言いうと、このコーヒーは高たかすぎるよ。

Speaking in terms of price, this coffee is too expensive.

物価ぶっかから言いうと、この地域ちいきは住すみやすいですね。

Speaking in terms of prices in general, this region is easy to live in.

景色けしきから言いうと、あの山やまは最高さいこうだよ。

Speaking in terms of scenery, that mountain is the best.

場所ばしょから言いうと、あの家いえは悪わるくないと思おもうよ。

Speaking in terms of location, I think that that house is not too bad.

私わたしの経験けいけんから言いうと、ゼミでは先生せんせいと仲良なかよくなるべきだ。

From my experience's point of view, you ought to get along with teachers at seminars.

成績せいせきから言いうと入社にゅうしゃは厳きびしいが、その人柄ひとがらでなんとかなるかもしれない。

Speaking in terms of his grades, getting a job at a company will be hard, but his personality might land him a position somewhere.

使つかいやすさから言いうと、こっちのパソコンの方ほうがいいと思おもいます。

Speaking in terms of ease of use, I think this computer is better.

実力じつりょくから言いうと、間違まちがいなくメダルは取とれると思おもう。

Speaking in terms of real ability, there is no doubt that he is capable of getting a medal.

消費者しょうひしゃの立場たちばから言いうと、値上ねあげは好このましくないと言いうことになる。

From a consumer standpoint perspective, price increases are not going to be favorable.

飲のみやすさから言いうとこのピザ屋やさんのワインより、昨日きのう飲のんだワインの方ほうが美味おいしい。

Speaking in terms of palatableness, the wine we had last night is better than the wine at this pizzeria.

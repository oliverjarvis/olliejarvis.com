## About

About なるべく

Combining 成なる (to become), and 可べく (to be permissible), なるべく is an expression that literally means 'as each opportunity becomes permissible/presents itself'. This is often simply translated as 'as much as possible', or 'whenever possible'.

なるべく (usually written in hiragana) is regularly used before an entire phrase, to express that (A) should happen as much as possible.

外そとに出でかけるときはなるべくドアのカギをかけてください。

When you go out, lock your doors as often as possible.

仕事しごとで疲つかれていても、なるべく夕食ゆうしょくは食たべてください。

Even if you are tired from work, please eat dinner whenever possible.

Due to なるべく expressing the 'possibility', or 'having the opportunity' to do something, it will sound considerably softer/more polite than できるだけ. できるだけ is a similar expression which more directly states that something will be done 'to the full extent possible'.

明日あしたはできるだけ早はやく来きてね。

Tomorrow, please make sure to come as early as possible.

明日あしたはなるべく早はやく来きてね。

Tomorrow, please come as early as possible.

Sentence
Translation

I will go as fast as possible.

なるべく早はやめに連絡れんらくしてください。

Please contact me as soon as possible.

なるべく正直しょうじきに話はなしてください。

Please speak as honestly as possible.

なるべく早はやめに犯人はんにんを捕つかまえるつもりです。

We intend to catch the criminal as soon as possible.

なるべく沢山たくさんの人気にんきな場所ばしょへ行いきたい。

I want to go to as many famous places as possible.

なるべくわかりやすく説明せつめいしてください。

Please explain so that it is as easy to understand as possible.

インフルエンザにかかった時ときは、なるべくゆっくり休やすむほうがいい。

When you catch the flu, you should rest as much as possible.

日本にほんに行いく前まえになるべくお金かねを貯ためた方ほうがいい。

Before going to Japan you should save as much money as possible.

机つくえの上うえをなるべく綺麗きれいにしたほうが仕事しごとがしやすい。

It is easier to work if the top of your desk is as clean as possible.

運転うんてんしている時ときはなるべく注意ちゅういしてください。

While driving, please be as careful as possible.

死しぬ前まえになるべく多おおくの国くにへ行いきたい。

I want to go to as many countries as possible before I die.

お客きゃくさんが来くるので、なるべく綺麗きれいにしてください。

We are having guests, so please be sure to make (the house) as tidy as possible.

## About

About ずにはすまない

English

Japanese

ずにはすまない is used to indicate that one ‘has no choice but to (A)’, or ‘won’t get away without doing (A)’. It is a formal expression that is a combination of the negative classical auxiliary verb, ず, the case-marking particle に, the adverbial particle は, and the standard negated form of the う-Verb 済すむ ‘to come to pass’, or ‘to finish’. Due to this, the more literal translation is ‘it will not come to pass that (A) is not done’, with the more natural English interpretations conveying the same feeling.

As we can assume by this expression beginning with ず, it will exclusively follow verbs.

あんなに迷惑めいわくをかけてしまったんだから、謝あやまらずにはすまないだろう。

I caused so much trouble, so I won’t get away without apologizing.

取引先とりひきさきのお偉えらいさんに飲のみに誘さそわれたから、行いかずにはすまない。

A high-ranking business partner invited me out for a drink, so I have no choice but to go.

会社かいしゃの先輩せんぱいからの電話でんわは出でずにはすまない。

I have no choice but to answer phone calls from my senpai from work.

Just like other instances where ず is used with する, ぜず will be the correct conjugation pattern.

やばい、田中君たなかくんのゲーム機きを壊こわしてしまった。これは、弁償べんしょうせずにはすまないな。

Oh shoot, I broke Tanaka-kun’s gaming console. I won't get away without paying him back.

The standard (ない) negative form of a verb may also be followed by ではすまない. This has the same meaning as ずにはすまない.

父ちちが大切たいせつにしていた釣竿つりざおを折おってしまった。謝あやまらないではすまない。

I accidentally broke a fishing rod my father took great care of. I won’t get away without apologizing.

税金ぜいきんは払はらわないではすまない。

You can’t get away without paying taxes.

Caution

This construction will tend to sound unnatural in cases where the speaker only considers (A) to be something that is necessary based on their personal feelings. (A) is usually something that would be the expected course of action according to anyone.

Sentence
Translation

重要じゅうような会議かいぎだから、間まに合あわないではすまない。

It's an important meeting, so I won't get away without making it on time.

彼かれは相談そうだんもなく重要じゅうような道具どうぐを廃棄はいきした。首くびにならずにはすまないだろう。

He has scrapped an important tool without consultation. He won't get off without being fired.

私わたしは子犬こいぬを助たすけるためとはいえ、無断むだんで車くるまの窓まどを破やぶった。罰ばつを受うけずにはすまないだろう。

I broke the window of the car without permission, even though I was trying to save a puppy. I won't get away unpunished.

この深刻しんこくな問題もんだいは難むずかしいので、上司じょうしに話はなさずにはすまないだろう。

I have no choice but to talk to my boss about it, since it is a serious problem.

大人おとななのだから、知しらずではすまないだろう。

Since you are an adult, you won't get by without knowing it.

本当ほんとうは僕ぼくがミスをしたのに、彼かれがかばってくれたんだ。彼かれには謝あやまらずにはすまない。

Even though I made a mistake, he covered for me. I won't get away without apologizing.

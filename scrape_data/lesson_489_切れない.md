## About

About 切れない

English

Japanese

In the negative-potential form, 切きれない will attach to the conjunctive form of other verbs, to express that a particular action is 'unable to be completed'. Let's look at some examples.

大盛おおもり頼たのむの？いくらあなたでもここの大盛おおもりは食たべきれないと思おもうよ。

Are you going to order the large one? I don't think you can possibly finish the portion they serve here.

あの山やまは一日いちにちでは登のぼり切きれない。

You can't possibly finish climbing this mountain in one day.

Unlike 切きる, which usually attaches to verbs to show that something was done to the point of running out, 切きれない often implies the exact opposite. This means that the (A) verb cannot be completed specifically because there is too much/many of something in order to finish it.

宇宙うちゅうには数かぞえ切きれないほどの星ほしがある。

There are too many stars in space to count. (Can't finish counting)

この肉にくは厚あつすぎて歯はでは噛かみ切きれない。

This meat is so thick that it is not possible to chew with your teeth.

切きれない may also imply that something cannot be done 'to the point of satisfaction'. In this case, the feeling of dissatisfaction is what the speaker considers too much to overcome.

私わたしのこの気持きもちは言葉ことばでは表あらわしきれない。

I cannot possibly explain this feeling of mine with words. (Cannot explain satisfactorily)

楽たのしみ過すぎて待まちきれない。

I am so excited that I can't wait. (Cannot wait satisfactorily)

空そらの星ほしの数かずが数かぞえ切きれない。

You can't completely count the number of stars in the sky.

お皿さらが多おおすぎて、洗あらい切きれない。

There are too many dishes. I will be unable to finish washing them.

新曲しんきょくの発売日はつばいびが待まち切きれない。

I absolutely cannot wait for the release of their new song.

辛からかったから、食たべ切きれなかった。

Because it was spicy, I was unable to finish eating the food.

この溢あふれる思おもいを抑おさえ切きれない。

I am unable to completely control my emotions from overflowing.

安やすかったから、持もち切きれないほど買かってしまいました。

Because it was cheap, I bought more than I could carry.

掃除機そうじきで吸すってもほこりを取とり切きれない。

Even if you use a vacuum cleaner, you will be unable to completely get all of the dust.

こんなにたくさん本ほんを借かりてきても読よみ切きれないんじゃないの？

Even if you borrowed all of these books you cannot possibly finish reading them all.

この耐たえ切きれない悲かなしみを一緒いっしょに乗のり越こえよう！

Let's overcome this completely unbearable sorrow together!

どんなに愛あいしているかを言葉ことばで伝つたえ切きれない。結婚けっこんしてくれる？

Words cannot completely convey how much I love you. Will you marry me?

貨物かもつ、多おおすぎて載のせ切きれない。一部いちぶを置おいていくしかないな。

There is too much cargo and I cannot load it completely. There is no choice but to leave part of it.

俺おれのためにこんなに世話せわをしてくれて、この気持きもちは言葉ことばで表あらわし切きれない。

You did so much for me, I am not able to express my gratitude completely with words.

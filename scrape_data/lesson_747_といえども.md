## About

About といえども

English

Japanese

といえども is a combination of the case-marking particle と, the hypothetical form of 言いう, 言いえ, and the conjunction particle ども, which is used in much the same way as けれども, or ても. Due to this, despite the more literal translation of 'even if one were to say (A)', it is usually translated simply as 'even if (A)', or 'although (A)'.

といえども will be seen following nouns, い-Adjectives, or verbs in any of their standard form.

いくらお金かねに困こまっているといえども、闇金やみきんには絶対ぜったい手てを出ださないよ。

Even if I was really troubled about money, there is no way I would turn to a loan shark.

前まえは一回いっかいで成功せいこうしたといえども、毎回まいかい一回いっかいで成功せいこうできる訳わけではない。

Even though we were able to succeed before on our first try, that doesn't mean that we can succeed in one try every time.

彼女かのじょがどれだけ優やさしいといえども、そんなことばかりやってたら別わかれられるぞ。

Even though she is super nice, if you keep doing such things she is going to break up with you.

たとえ犯罪者はんざいしゃといえども、動物どうぶつみたいに扱あつかってはいけない。

Even if they are criminals, you must not treat them like animals.

といえども may also follow な-Adjectives, but だ will precede といえども in these cases.

運転うんてんが上手じょうずだといえども、シートベルトは必かならず着用ちゃくようしなくてはならない。

Even if you are good at driving, you must wear a seatbelt.

いくら有名ゆうめいだといえども、なんでも好すき勝手かってやっていいと言いうことではない。

Even if you are famous, it doesn't mean that you can just do whatever you wish.

Despite the nuance being slightly different, といえども can be thought of as a very old-fashioned way of saying といっても. Due to といえども being old-fashioned, it is often used for emphasis and will frequently be paired with expressions like いくら, いかに, どんなに, etc.

Fun Fact

Although very rarely used, sometimes いえども is seen as 雖いえども. In these cases the kanji 雖いえど, which means 'however', is simply paired with も. This creates a slight difference between 'however even (A)', and the more modern 'even if one were to say (A)'. Despite this, the meanings are used interchangeably in modern times.

## Examples

Examples

Sentence
Translation

ヒジカタが恐おそるべしと雖いえども、スギモトの敵てきではない。

Even though Hijikata is incredible, he is no match for Sugimoto.

手術しゅじゅつ中ちゅうは一瞬いっしゅんと雖いえど気きが抜ぬけない。

During an operation you cannot lose focus, not even for a second.

たとえ囚人しゅうじんと雖いえどもときどき刑務所けいむしょを出でることができる。

Even a prisoner can leave prison from time to time.

雨天うてんと雖いえども、ゴミ拾ひろいのボランティアをしないわけにはいかない。

Even if it is rainy weather, there is no way that I can not volunteer for the litter clean up.

王様おうさまと雖いえども、法律ほうりつを犯おかすことは出来できません。

Although he is the king, he cannot break the law.

弁護べんごのためと雖いえども、暴漢ぼうかんを撃うつべきではないという意見いけんもある。

There are also opinions that one should not shoot a hoodlum, even in self-defense.

対戦たいせん相手あいてが子供こどもと雖いえども、気きを抜ぬくことは出来できない。

Even if my match opponent is a child, I cannot let my guard down.

人間にんげんはいくら手練てだれと雖いえども生いきている限かぎり間違まちがいをすることがある。

Although humans are skilled, as long as they live they will make mistakes.

私わたしたちは双子ふたごと雖いえども、見みた目めも性格せいかくも全まったく違ちがいます。

Even though we are twins, both our looks and our personalities are completely different.

いくらイケメンだと雖いえども、それだけで付つき合あおうとは思おもえない。

Even if he is that much of a stud, I don’t think that I could date him just on that.

不況ふきょう下かで損失そんしつが出でていると雖いえども、我わが社しゃは遅滞ちたい無なく給与きゅうよを支払しはらう。

Even when taking losses during a recession our company pays salaries without delay.

親おやを説得せっとくするためといえども、そのためにネットから嘘うその情報じょうほうを引ひっ張ぱってくるのは卑怯ひきょうだろう。

Even if it is in order to persuade your parents, hauling in false information from the internet for that purpose is pathetic, isn't it?

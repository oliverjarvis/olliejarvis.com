## About

About どうせ

English

Japanese

どうせ is an adverb used in Japanese to express that some particular state or condition of (A) will result in (B). Usually, there is no way to stop (B) from happening, and it is considered a natural consequence of (A). In English, this translates closely with phrases like 'at any rate', or 'in any case'.

Being an adverb, どうせ most often appears at the beginning of sentences, before going on to describe the inevitable situation.

どうせまたパチンコに行いくんでしょ。

In any case, you are going to the pachinko parlor again, aren't you?

どうせ私わたしのことなんて好すきじゃないんでしょ。

In any case, you probably don't even like me.

どうせ often carries the nuance of frustration or resignation that (A) cannot be changed. In these cases, it is often paired with other grammar structures such as なら or だから. This shows that if (A) cannot be changed, then (B) is a good idea. This usage translates similarly to 'if (A), you might as well (B)'.

どうせ新あたらしいスマホを買かうならもうちょっといい奴やつ買かいなよ。

If you're going to buy a new smartphone, you might as well buy something a little better.

どうせまた負まけるんだから頑張がんばっても時間じかんと体力たいりょくの無駄むだだよ。

We're going to lose again anyway, so trying our best would be a waste of time and energy.

Fun-fact

This grammar structure comes from a combination of どう 'how', and the imperative form of する, せよ. In this way, it is just emphasizing that no matter 'how (A) is done', the result cannot be changed.

どうせダイアモンドなんて買かってもらえない。

I have no doubt I won't be able to get them to buy me diamonds.

どうせ「ウドの大木たいぼくだ」と悪口わるぐちを言いわれるのがオチだ。

I have no doubt that it will just end with me being called 'big and useless'.

どうせ通訳つうやくはつけてもらえない。自力じりきで頑張がんばるしかない。

In any case, I can't have it interpreted. I have no choice but to try my best.

私わたしがどう謝あやまっても、どうせ彼かれは許ゆるすつもりはないだろう。

No matter how much I apologize, I have no doubt that he won't forgive me.

どうせご飯はんを炊たかないといけないのだから、あなたの分ぶんも炊たきますよ。

In any case, since I have to cook rice, I will cook your share too.

どうせもう蓄たくわえがないのだから、最後さいごのチャンスに賭かけてみよう。

In any case, since there is no reserve left, I will try to bet it on my last chance.

またどうせ散ちらかすんでしょ。せっかく片付かたづけたのに。

You will no doubt make a mess again, won't you? Even though I went through the trouble of straightening everything up.

どうせ付つき合あわないといけないのだから、楽たのしんだ方ほうがいい。

In any case, since I must accompany you, it would be better to enjoy it.

どうせ車くるまを買かうのなら、ヒーターシートが付ついているのを買かおう！

If we are buying a car anyway, let's buy one with heated seats.

君きみの話はなしは聞ききたくない。どうせだますつもりなんでしょ。

I don't want to listen to what you have to say. I have no doubt that you plan to deceive me, right?

この試験しけんの難むずかしさから考かんがえると、どうせ失敗しっぱいするに決きまっている。

Judging from the difficulty of the exam, I have no doubt that my failure has been decided.

どうせ短期たんきのバイトだと思おもって適当てきとうにやっているんじゃないですか？

At any rate, you are thinking that this is a short-term, part time job and are halfheartedly working, aren't you?

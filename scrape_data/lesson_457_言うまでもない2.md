## About

About 言うまでもない ②

English

Japanese

Unlike は言いうまでもない, which usually comes after other phrases, 言いうまでもない tends to come at the beginning. It is a structure that combines the う - Verb 言いう, two (2) adverbial particles まで and も, and the い-Adjective ない. This is regularly translated as 'it goes without saying that (A)', or 'it is needless to say (A)', where (A) is whatever comes later in the sentence.

言いうまでもない is often followed directly by ことだが, が, けど, and similar expressions, to add the nuance of 'but'.

言いうまでもないことだが、毎日まいにち漢字かんじの勉強べんきょうをすれば、どんどん漢字かんじを覚おぼえることができる。

It goes without saying that if you practice kanji everyday, you will steadily memorize them.

電化でんか製品せいひんを使つかっている間あいだ、言いうまでもないが、濡ぬれた手てや水みずの周まわりで使つかうのは危険きけんだ。

While you are using electronics, it is needless to say that it is dangerous to use them with wet hands or around water.

言いうまでもないけど、君きみに会あえて良よかった。

It goes without saying, but I am glad that I met you.

Fun Fact

言いうまでもない is a polite way to express that something is 'obvious', without unintentionally sounding arrogant.

言いうまでもないことだが、お酒さけを飲のんで運転うんてんをしてはいけない。

Obviously, you should not drink and drive.

Sadao-san's wife, Emi-san can speak Korean, not to mention English.

It goes without saying that if you drink and go swimming, then you might drown.

言いうまでもないことだが、家いえのドアを開あけっぱなしにしてしまうのは泥棒どろぼうを家いえへ招まねくこととおなじだ。

It goes without saying that leaving your house doors open is equivalent to inviting in thieves.

言いうまでもないだろうが、借かりたものは持もち主ぬしに返かえすべきだ。

It probably goes without saying that anything you borrow should be returned to its rightful owner.

東京とうきょうオリンピックのあいだ、言いうまでもないが電車でんしゃやバスなどはすごく混こむだろう。

It goes without saying that during the Tokyo Olympics, trains, buses, etc will be extremely crowded.

それが人ひとによるのは言いうまでもないですが、広ひろい視野しやからいうと共通きょうつうの特徴とくちょうもあります。

It goes without saying that it depends on the person, but if you look from a wider perspective, there are also some common features.

言いうまでもないのだが、より良よい商品しょうひんはより高価こうかである。

It goes without saying that better goods are more expensive.

## About

About とおり

English

Japanese

Often, the noun とおり carries the meaning of 'in that way', or 'just like that'. In its kanji form, とおり will be written as 通とおり, and literally means 'street', or 'way'. However, it is regularly used to indicate the level of 'sameness' one thing has with another. This 'sameness' meaning appears when とおり is used as a suffix.

とおり (or どおり) may be used with verbs or nouns. In the case of verbs, it is common to see it being used after both the plain form, and the simple-past form.

お客様きゃくさまのおっしゃる通とおりです。

Honored guest, the thing you say is precisely correct. (That's exactly right)

彼かれは僕ぼくの思おもった通とおりの時間じかんに帰かえってきた。

Just as I expected, he came back when I thought he would come back.

Both どおり and のとおり may be used directly after nouns. However, とおり will only be used when the case marking particle の comes between the noun and とおり.

計画けいかくどおりに進すすめて行いきます。

We will continue as the plan suggests.

ご覧らんの通とおり、私わたしの手てには何なにもありません。

As you can see, I have nothing in my hands.

While both of these structures may be used interchangeably, どおり is more common, especially in speech.

Fun Fact
Constructions like 言いった通とおり and その通とおり are used very frequently, simply as a polite way to agree with what has been said by another. In these cases, it translates similarly to 'that's exactly right', or 'that's it!'.

リカの言いった通とおりだった！あそこのパン屋やさんはすごくおいしかった！

It was exactly as you said, Rika! The bakery over there was very delicious!

本当ほんとうにその通とおりです！何なんでいつも私わたしたちがやらないといけないんですか。

That's honestly exactly it! Why do we have to do it all the time?

It was just as I thought.

あなたのおっしゃる通とおりです。

It will be precisely/just as you say.

合格者ごうかくしゃは次つぎの通とおりです。

Those who passed are as follows... (Precisely)

お察さっしの通とおり、彼かれは病気びょうきです。

Exactly/Just as you may guess, he is quite ill.

物事ものごとはいつも計画けいかく通どおりにはいかないものだなぁ。

Things will not always go precisely/exactly as planned.

結果けっかはすべて予想よそうしていた通とおりだった。

The results were just as we expected.

今回こんかいは僕ぼくの言いう通とおりにしてくれないか？

Can't you just do precisely/just as I say this time?

契約けいやくどおり、二年間にねんかんは会社かいしゃを替かえられない。分わかるか？

Precisely/Just as the contract states, you can't change the company for two years. Got it?

原因げんいんは先日せんじつ報告ほうこくした通とおりです。

The cause is exactly/just as I reported the other day.

説明書せつめいしょの通とおりにやらないと、故障こしょうの原因げんいんになりますよ。

If you don't follow the instructions precisely/exactly, that might lead to it breaking down.

今いま、卒業そつぎょう論文ろんぶんが順調じゅんちょうです。予定よていどおりに完成かんせいさせられそうです。

Right now my graduation thesis is coming along nicely. I can finish it exactly/just as planned.

まったくその通とおりだ。すぐにやらないからこんなことになるんだ。こう言いえば満足まんぞく？？

That's precisely/exactly it. It ended up like this because I didn't do it immediately. Satisfied now?

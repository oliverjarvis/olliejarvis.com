## About

About ～(の)姿

English

Japanese

When the noun 姿すがた 'figure' is used as a suffix, it describes the outward appearance of someone. This may be in relation to what the person is wearing, their overall shape, body type, or style. 姿すがた is often translated as '(A) looking', 'wearing (A)', 'clad in (A)', or 'performing (A)'.

姿すがた may be used after verbs in their attributive form, or after nouns followed by the case marking particle の.

昨日きのう初はじめてお父とうさんが働はたらいている姿すがたを見みた。

Yesterday was the first time I saw my father (his figure) working.

息子むすこが悲かなしんでいる姿すがたを見みると、私わたしまで悲かなしくなってくる。

When I see my son in a sad state, it makes even me sad.

さっき男おとこの人ひとの姿すがたが見みえたけど、気きのせいかな。

Earlier, I saw a man (his figure), but maybe I was seeing things.

デパートで息子むすこの姿すがたが見みえなくなって、少すこしパニックになった。

I lost sight of my son's presence at the department store, and it made me panic a little.

姿すがた is a word that focuses primarily on the way that someone appears to the observer. Therefore, it is also often used to describe the observer's impression of who they are looking at, in regard to their presence, or lack thereof.

娘むすめの歩あるく姿すがたが夫おっとに似にている。

The way my daughter walks looks just like my husband.

犬いぬの寝ねている姿すがたはとてもかわいい。

The way my dog looks when he is sleeping is so cute.

Caution

After nouns, の will sometimes be omitted, but this is not always the case. In general, の may be dropped after kanji compounds, after words that express outfits or clothing, but not after simple words like 人ひと, 時とき, 元もと, etc.

やっとお前まえの花嫁はなよめ姿すがたが見みれて嬉うれしいよ。

I am so happy that I can finally see you as a bride.

シンデレラは１２じゅうに時じになった瞬間しゅんかんに元姿もとすがたに戻もどった。

As soon as it reached 12 oclock, Cinderella turned back into her original appearance. (Unnatural Japanese)

## Examples

Examples

Sentence
Translation

この地域ちいきには人ひとの姿すがたが見みえない。

There is no human presence in this area. (figure)

あなたの動うごく姿すがたが好すきです。

I like the way you look when you move. (moving figure)

友達ともだちとの話はなし：「あの人ひとは顔かおも綺麗きれいだし、立たち姿すがたも美うつくしい。」

Chat between friends: 'That person has a pretty face, and also the way they stand is attractive.' (standing figure)

衣料品店いりょうひんてん、クライアント：「自分じぶんの姿すがたを鏡かがみで見みてみるよ。」

Clothing store, client: 'I will look at my appearance in the mirror!' (figure)

２０１２にせんじゅうに年ねんに最後さいごに会あってから、あの男おとこの姿すがたを見みていない。

I haven't seen him (his presence) since the last time I met him in 2012. (figure・appearance)

友達ともだちとの話はなし：「彼かれは頭あたまがいいだけでなく、立たってる姿すがたもイケてる。」

Chat between friends: 'Not only is he smart, but the way he stands is also cool.' (standing figure)

新聞記事しんぶんきじ：「映画会社えいががいしゃのパーティーには、華はなやかな着物きもの姿すがたの女優じょゆうたちが出席しゅっせきしていた。」

Newspaper article: 'Actresses dressed in brilliant kimonos made an appearance at the movie company party.' (appearance・looks)

友達ともだちとの話はなし：「彼女かのじょの笑わらう姿すがたを見みかけて、私わたしも嬉うれしくなった。」

Chat between friends: 'I happened to see the way she laughs, and I also became happy.' (laughing figure)

母親ははおやが息子むすこに話はなす：「イズク君くん、あなたが初はじめて歩あるいた姿すがたを覚おぼえてるわよ。可愛かわいすぎたよ。」

Mother speaking to son: 'Izuku-kun, I remember the way you looked, walking for the first time. It was just too cute.' (walking figure)

論文ろんぶん：「コンピュータ技術ぎじゅつでアイスマンの生いきていた時ときの姿すがたが復元ふくげんされた。」

Essay/thesis: 'The way the iceman looked when he was alive has been restored using computer technology.' (figure/appearance)

新聞記事しんぶんきじ：「マスク姿すがたの容疑者ようぎしゃ、警察官けいさつかんの姿すがたを見みて逃にげる」

Newspaper article: 'The mask-wearing suspect escapes at the sight of a policeman' (figure of a masked man)

ノベル：「高齢者こうれいしゃを手伝てつだうボランティアの姿すがたを見みて、泣なくほど感動かんどうした。」

Novel: 'I was touched to the point of crying after catching the sight of a volunteer helping elderly.' (figure of a volunteer)

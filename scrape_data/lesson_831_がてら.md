## About

About がてら

English

Japanese

がてら is a neologism that is used in the same way as an adverbial particle in modern Japanese, and will connect to the conjunctive form of verbs, and to nouns. It is similar to ながら in that it will be translated as 'while (A), (B)', 'during (A), (B)', or 'on the occasion of (A)'.

When (A) is a noun, it will almost always be a noun that signifies some sort of physical movement.

弟おとうとを駅えきから迎むかえがてらに、今夜こんやの夕飯ゆうはんの買かい物ものをしに行いく。

I will go grocery shopping for tonight's dinner on my way to pick up my brother as well.

友達ともだちの家いえに行いきがてら、近ちかくの神社じんじゃにお参まいりに行いった。

While going to my friend's house, I went to pray at the shrine nearby as well.

ワンちゃんの散歩さんぽがてらに、ペットショップにいってくる。

While taking my dog on a walk, I am going to go to the pet store as well.

運動うんどうがてらに会社かいしゃまで自転車じてんしゃで行いくことにした。

On the occasion of exercising, I decided to ride the bicycle to work.

Unlike ながら, がてら focuses more on (A) being a good opportunity which can be utilized or taken advantage of in order to do (B).

Fun Fact

がてら is a vocal change of がてり, which originally comes from an abbreviation of the conjunctive form of 糅かつ, かて 'to mix together', and あり. So かてあり was abbreviated to かてり, which then went through sound changes to がてり and がてら. However, the 'being mixed together' meaning is still kept in the modern day nuance of this grammar point.

## Examples

Examples

00:04

Sentence
Translation

「帰かえりがてら手紙てがみ出だしてくるね。」

I'll post the letter on my way home as well.

「イノシシの散歩さんぽがてら、ちょっとコンビニまで行いってきます。」

'While taking a walk with my pet wild boar, I will go to the convenience store as well.'

「お花見はなみがてら、公園こうえんを散歩さんぽしよう。」

'Let's take a walk in the park while viewing the cherry blossoms as well.'

回顧録かいころく：「ヒースロー空港くうこうへの移動いどうがてら、観光かんこうスポットを訪おとずれた。」

Memoir: 'I visited some tourist attractions on my way to Heathrow Airport as well.'

友達ともだちとの話はなし：「じゃあ買かい物ものがてら、紅葉こうようを見みに行いかないか？」

Chat between friends: 'So, why don't we view the autumn leaves while shopping as well?'

広告こうこく：「観光かんこうの後のちには休憩きゅうけいがてら立たち寄よってみてはいかがでしょうか。」

Restaurant advertisement: 'How about you drop by while taking a break after sightseeing (as well)?'

帰省きせいがてら懐なつかしい喫茶店きっさてんやゲームセンター、よく行いっていた海岸かいがんへ足あしを運はこぶつもりだ。

I plan to make my way to a nostalgic coffee shop and game center along the way home, and the beach that I used to often go to as well.

運動うんどうがてらバス停ていを二ふたつ手前てまえで降おりて、残のこりの距離きょりを早歩はやあるきで歩あるく事ことにしました。

On the occasion of exercising, I decided to hop off the bus two stops before my place and quickly walk the remaining distance.

気分きぶん転換てんかんのためにお散歩さんぽがてら、近所きんじょの神社じんじゃに立たち寄よる事ことにした。

For a change of pace, while taking a stroll, I decided to stop at a neighborhood shrine as well.

鬱うつになってしまったので、療養りょうようがてら仕事しごとを長期的ちょうきてきに休やすむ決断けつだんをしました。

Since I became depressed, while I was recuperating I decided to take a long-term break from work as well.

不倫ふりん相手あいてからの「仕事しごとがてら、また家うちに泊とまりにきたら？」というメールを夫おっとに見みられてしまった。

The message from the person that I was cheating with that said 'while you're at work, why don't you come stay at my place as well?' was unfortunately seen by my husband.

勉強べんきょうがてら音楽おんがくを聴きいていたら、いつの間まにか勉強べんきょうは一切いっさいせずに音楽おんがくだけ聴きいていた事ことに気きづきました。

When I was listening to music on the occasion of doing some study, I suddenly realized that I had not done any study at all, but was just listening to music.

## About

About どころか

English

Japanese

どころか is a combination of the noun ところ 'place', and the sentence ending particle か. The change from ところ to どころ is a consistent sound change that is seen regularly in sentences that use 所ところ negatively.

どころか may be translated as 'far from', 'let alone' or 'anything but'. It strongly negates any statement before it, while also emphasizing what comes after it.

どころか may be paired with the attributive form of any other word. However, this is not a strict rule, as it can also be seen with the 語幹ごかん stem form of な-Adjectives, and directly attached to nouns.

このカレーは甘あまいどころか、辛からかった。

Far from being sweet, this curry was spicy.

北海道ほっかいどうは寒さむいどころか、暑あつかった。

Far from being cold, Hokkaido was hot.

昨日きのうの仕事しごとは楽らくなどころか、めちゃくちゃ大変たいへんだったよ。

Far from being easy, work yesterday was hectic.

彼女かのじょは酎ちゅうハイどころかビールも飲のまない。

Let alone Chuhai, she doesn't even drink beer.

Sentences that use どころか must always have a part (B) that emphasizes some type of far more important information (in the speaker's opinion).

Fun Fact
As か simply transforms どころ into either a question, or a statement of doubt, the literal translation of どころか is similar to 'it's not even remotely (A)' as a statement, or 'do you honestly think it's (A)!?', when asking a direct question.

そんなにゆっくりしているどころか。

This is not the time to be even remotely that relaxed!

俺おれがやるどころか？そんなことをやるのは弟おとうとしかいないだろ！

Do you honestly think I would do such a thing? My brother is the only person who would do that.

Far from being humble, he is arrogant.

Far from taking a break, he gave everything he had.

Even though I tried to be nice to her/him, far from being thanked, I was scolded.

英語えいごどころか日本語にほんごもちゃんと喋しゃべれていない気きがする。

Far from speaking English, I feel that I can't even properly speak in Japanese.

Far from you helping me, it is rather me who ended up helping you!

彼かれはヨーロッパまで行いくどころか、玄関げんかんから一歩いっぽも出でていない。

Far from going to Europe, he hasn't even taken a step outside.

Eating in excess even if it's healthy food is not good for you. Far from that, it will make you ill!

Despite studying so much, far from getting better, my grades got worse.

Far from being helpful, he is disturbing the work.

体調たいちょうが悪わるいのに、休やすむどころかいつもよりたくさん仕事しごとをしているから心配しんぱいです。

I am worried about them, because even though they are in poor physical health, far from resting, they are working even more.

## About

About つつある

English

Japanese

When the particle つつ 'while doing' is combined with ある 'to be', it indicates that someone or something is in the ongoing process of (A). (A) will be a verb in its ます-stem form. This formal grammar structure primarily highlights that (A) is slowly but surely continuing or changing. This 'change' is often in regard to an increase, decrease, expansion, contraction, or something similar.

This pattern can be translated simply as 'to be (A)ing'.

毎年まいとし、年金ねんきんの受給額じゅきゅうがくが減へりつつあるので、生活せいかつに困こまっている高齢者こうれいしゃが増ふえてきている。

Every year, more and more elderly people are having trouble making ends meet because their pension benefits are decreasing.

日本にほんでは物価ぶっかが上あがりつつある。

Prices in general are rising in Japan.

最近さいきんはインターネットを使つかって買かい物ものができるので、わざわざお店みせに行いって買かい物ものをする人ひとが減へりつつある。

These days, people can shop using the Internet, so fewer people are going out of their way to go to stores to shop.

Caution

Compared to ている, つつある tends not to be used in relation to noticeably physical motions, but rather highlights actions that indicate an ongoing state of change (such as the blooming of a flower), rather than simply 'continuing'. This is especially true for verbs that have an obvious end or completion point.

彼かれはりんごを食たべている。

He is eating an apple. (Natural Japanese)

彼かれはりんごを食たべつつある。

He is eating an apple. (Unnatural Japanese)

## Examples

Examples

Sentence
Translation

おかげさまで、病気びょうきは治なおりつつあります。

Fortunately, my disease is healing.

契約けいやくしつつあったのに決裂けつれつした。惜おしいことをした。

Despite having been in the process of signing a contract, the discussions broke down. I did something regrettable.

大だい流行りゅうこうしたインフルエンザの猛威もういも、今いまは落おち着つきつつある。

The fury of the widespread influenza is now calming down.

アフリカの野生やせい動物どうぶつの数かずが減へりつつあるのは残念ざんねんだ。

It is a pity that the number of wild animals in Africa is decreasing.

在庫ざいこ過剰かじょうになりつつあるから、値下ねさげしてでも売うってください。

Since inventory continues to overflow, please sell them even if you have to lower the price.

反対はんたい派はの抗議こうぎ活動かつどうが加熱かねつしつつあるから、なんとか抑おさえないと…

Since the opposing faction's protest action is heating up, we have to do something to hold them back...

後半こうはんものすごい売うり上あげを伸のばしていて、前期ぜんきの損失そんしつをカバーしつつある。

Sales in the second half are great, they are covering losses from the first half.

７０ななじゅう年代ねんだい中ちゅう、石油せきゆの価格かかくが急きゅうに上あがりつつあったので、オランダでは自転車じてんしゃの使用しようが広ひろまった。

In the 70's, the price of oil was rising rapidly so riding bicycles became a common practice in the Netherlands.

Ａエーの事業じぎょうとＢビーの事業じぎょうの仕事しごと内容ないようが重かさなりつつあるから、統合とうごうしようと思おもう。

Since the subject matter of business A and business B continue to overlap, I am thinking about merging them.

地球ちきゅう温暖おんだん化かのために砂漠さばくに変かわりつつある国くにがある。また、海うみに沈しずみつつある島国しまぐにもある。

There are countries that are changing into deserts due to global warming. In addition, there are also island countries that are sinking into the ocean.

近年きんねん、経済けいざい的てきにはＡエー国こくとＢビー国こくの協力きょうりょく関係かんけいは強化きょうかされつつあるが、軍事ぐんじ的てきには国境こっきょう付近ふきんにおいて小規模しょうきぼ紛争ふんそうが絶たえない。

In recent years, the economical relationship between country A and country B is strengthening. However, when it comes to the military, small-scale conflicts close to the border do not cease.

ちゃんと水みずをあげなかったから、植木うえきが枯かれつつある。

Since I didn't properly water it, the potted plant is withering.

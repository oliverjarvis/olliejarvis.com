## About

About に気をつける

English

Japanese

Another of the large number of collocations in Japanese based on 気き 'attention', or 'focus', is 気きを付つける. This is just a combination of the noun 気き, を, and the る-Verb 付つける 'to adhere', or 'to attach'. 気きを付つける primarily follows (A) に. This marks (A) as something that the speaker is 'adhering' their attention to, and translates as 'to pay attention to (A)', 'to be careful of (A)', or 'to watch out for (A)'.

This grammar pattern is used after nouns or noun-like phrases, indicating the thing that is being warned about, or that the speaker themselves is being cautious of.

最近さいきん、電車でんしゃに痴漢ちかんが沢山たくさんいるらしいから怪あやしい人ひとに気きをつけて帰かえってくるんだよ。

I hear there are a lot of perverts on the trains these days, so watch out for suspicious people on your way back home.

車くるまで歩道ほどうを横切よこぎる時ときには歩行者ほこうしゃに気きを付つけましょう。

Be careful of pedestrians when you drive through a sidewalk.

Most often, 気きを付つける will be used to warn about possible errors that could be made, mistakes, or simply the loss of focus. Due to this, it frequently follows negative verbs as ないよう, highlighting that something should be done in a 'manner' that doesn't result in (A).

このギターは俺おれの姉ねえちゃんのやつだから壊こわさないように気きを付つけてね。

This guitar belongs to my sister, so please be careful not to break it.

今月こんげつはお金かねを無駄むだ遣づかいしないように気きを付つけよう。

This month, I will be careful not to waste money.

## Examples

Examples

00:04

Sentence
Translation

頭あたまをぶつけないようにシャッターに気きをつけよう。

Let's be careful not to bump our head on the shutter.

電柱でんちゅうに気きをつけて車庫しゃこ入いれをしてください。

Please watch out for the telephone poles when parking.

氷こおりは滑すべりやすいから、足あしもとに気きをつけて。

The ice is slippery, so please watch your step.

妊娠にんしん中ちゅうの女性じょせいは、健康けんこうに気きをつけるべきだ。

Pregnant women should be careful of their health.

階段かいだんは急きゅうだから、落おちないように気きをつけなさい。

Since the stairs are steep, take care not to fall.

通とおりを横切よこぎる時ときには、車くるまに気きをつけて。

When you are crossing the street, watch out for cars.

言葉ことばに気きをつけよ！先生せんせいとしてふさわしくない行動こうどうだ！

Watch your language! Your behavior is unbecoming of a teacher!

あの家いえに住すむなら、湿気しっけに気きをつけた方ほうがいいです。

If you are going to live in that house, it would be better to be careful of humidity.

土地とちを買かうなら地盤じばんに気きをつけて選えらぼう。

If we are going to buy land, let's make our decision while paying attention to the ground (where we will build).

締切しめきりに気きをつけて作業さぎょうしていたのに間まに合あわなかった。

Despite working while being careful of the deadline, I didn't make it in time. (carefully)

あのパソコンは、弱点じゃくてんに気きをつけて使つかえばすごい威力いりょくを発揮はっきする。

If you use that PC while taking into account its shortcomings, it is capable of amazing power.

母はは：「パソコンの隣となりで食事しょくじをする時ときは、水みずを零こぼさないように気きをつけて。」

Mother: 'When you are eating next to the computer, take care not to spill water on it.'

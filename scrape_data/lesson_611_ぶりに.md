## About

About ぶりに

English

Japanese

When used as a suffix, the voiced form of 振ふり 'style', 振ぶり will be used to indicate either the way something appears, or some specific lapse of time since the last time something happened. In this lesson, we will focus on the 'lapse of time' meaning. This can be translated as 'for the first time in (A)', or 'haven't done (B) for (A) time'.

As ぶり (appearing most often in hiragana) is a suffix, it will be used after nouns. When followed by に, the part (B) portion of the sentence will give more information about what wasn't done for the period of (A). Additionally, ぶり may be followed by の when attached directly to a second noun that is being modified by ぶり.

一いち年ねんぶりに五ごキロも走はしったから明日あしたは絶対ぜったいに筋肉痛きんにくつうだ。

I am definitely going to be sore tomorrow because I ran a 5K for the first time in a year.

３年さんねんぶりに風呂ふろに入はいる。

I will bathe for the first time in 3 years.

４よ年ねんぶりの寿司すしだ！やっぱり日本にほんの寿司すしはうまいな！

This is my first time having sushi in 4 years! Man, Japanese sushi is the best!

高橋たかはし選手せんしゅが１０年じゅうねんぶりの優勝ゆうしょう！

Takahashi won for the first time in 10 years!

Caution

Although it is not incorrect, it may be considered unnatural to use ぶり in situations where the interval of time is not significant, such as with minutes, seconds, and hours.

５１ごじゅういっ分ぷんぶりに家いえに帰かえる。

I will go home for the first time in 51 minutes. (Unnatural Japanese)

Sentence
Translation

歯医者はいしゃに行いくのは二年にねん振ぶりだ。

Going to the dentist is something I haven't done in 2 years.

十年じゅうねん振ぶりに、高校こうこうの友達ともだちに会あった。

I met a high school friend who I hadn't seen for 10 years.

２に週間しゅうかん振ぶりの寿司すしを食たべた。

I ate sushi for the first time in two weeks.

三さん年ねん振ぶりに日本食にほんしょくを味あじわえて幸しあわせです。

For the first time in three years, I am happy that I could taste Japanese food.

十日とおか振ぶりの快晴かいせいで、とてもさわやかだ。

For the first time in 10 days, the weather is nice and it is very refreshing.

１００ひゃく年ねん振ぶりに火山かざんが噴火ふんかしました。

The volcano erupted for the first time in 100 years.

叔父おじさんに会あうのは半年はんとし振ぶりですね。

It will be the first time in six months that you get to see your uncle, right?

大谷おおたにが３さん打席だせき振ぶりにヒットを打うった。

Ohtani got a hit for the first time after not getting one during the last three at bats.

数日すうじつ振ぶりに原稿げんこうを書かき始はじめた。

I began writing my manuscript after not having written for several days. (for the first time in...)

演劇えんげきを見みたのは何なん年ねん振ぶりだろう。

It has probably been several years since I saw a play. (for the first time in...)

こんなに綺麗きれいな景色けしきは３さん年ねん前まえの秋あき振ぶりだ。

This is the first time since fall three years ago that I've seen a view this beautiful.

二に大会たいかい振ぶりに同おなじ組合くみあわせでの決勝けっしょう戦せんだ。

For the first time in two tournaments, it is a finals game with the same match up (of teams/athletes).

しばらく振ぶりに見みた運河うんがは、とても美うつくしかった。

Seeing the canal for the first time in quite a while, was exceedingly beautiful.

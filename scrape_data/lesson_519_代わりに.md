## About

About 代わりに

English

Japanese

代かわり 'a substitute' is a noun that is used to convey when (B) is acting as a replacement or substitute for (A). The case marking particle に will come after 代かわり, before a (B) statement will highlight who the replacement is, and possibly what they are doing.

As 代かわり is a noun, it may be used after the attributive form of any word.

俺おれが社長しゃちょうに電話でんわする代かわりに、お前まえはお客きゃくさんに電話でんわをしておいて。

In exchange for me calling the boss, can you call the client in advance?

あのプールは古ふるい代かわりに安やすい。

That pool is cheap, to substitute for it being old.

仕事しごとは大変たいへんな代かわりに楽たのしい。

Work is fun, in exchange for it being difficult.

私わたしの代かわりにやってください。

Can you do it in my place?

Occasionally, 替かわり 'to exchange' may be used instead of 代かわり 'to substitute'. However, within the context of this particular grammar structure, there is little to no change in nuance.

Fun Fact

代かわりに is often used at the beginning of sentences, simply to express a more preferable option for something. In these cases, it comes across as 'rather than that, (A)', or 'instead, (A)'.

代かわりに、もっといい方法ほうほうを教おしえてあげるよ。

Rather than that, I'll teach you a better way!

代かわりにこれを持もって行いって。

Instead of that, can you go take this?

Sentence
Translation

バターの代かわりにマーガリンを使つかっています。

I am using margarine instead of butter.

コーヒーの代かわりに紅茶こうちゃでは駄目だめですか？

Is tea in place of coffee not good enough?

玉たまねぎの代かわりに長ながねぎを使つかおう。

Let's use green onions in place of ordinary onions.

電話でんわの代かわりに、メールを送おくった。

Instead of calling, I sent him an email.

私わたしの代かわりにこの問題もんだいを解といてください。

Please solve this question instead of me.

筆記ひっきテストの代かわりに面接めんせつ試験しけんがある。

I have an oral examination instead of a written test.

君きみの代かわりに僕ぼくがやってみるけど、うまくできる自信じしんがない。

I'll try doing it instead of you, but I'm not confident I can do it well.

彼かれが遅刻ちこくをしてしまったので、彼かれの代かわりに発表はっぴょうした。

Since he was late, I presented instead of him.

先生せんせいの代かわりに勉強べんきょうを教おしえてみたけど、うまく教おしえられなかった。

I tried teaching instead of my teacher, but I was unable to teach well.

この靴くつなら長靴ながぐつの代かわりになるね。ゴ*テックスだから雨あめでも大丈夫だいじょうぶそうだ。

You can wear these shoes in place of boots. Because they are go*-tex, you can even use them in rain.

バナナが嫌きらいで食たべれないからビタミンが取とれない。バナナの代かわりに何なにを食たべたらいい？

I don't like bananas so I don't eat them and can't get those vitamins.
What should I eat in place of a banana?

先生せんせいが風邪かぜをひいて休やすんだので、代かわりに校長こうちょう先生せんせいが授業じゅぎょうをした。

Because the teacher caught a cold and took the day off, the principal taught the class in his place.

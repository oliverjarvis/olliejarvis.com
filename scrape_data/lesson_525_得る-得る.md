## About

About 得る・得る

English

Japanese

得える is a る-Verb that has the meaning of 'to gain', or 'to acquire'. When attached to the conjunctive form of verbs, it takes on the nuance of 'for (A) to be possible', or 'to be able to (A)'.

順調じゅんちょうにいけば、二年後にねんごに会社かいしゃを立たち上あげることもあり得える。

It's possible that I'll set up my own company if everything goes well after two years.

いくら気きをつけていても、交通こうつう事故じこは起おこり得えるものだ。

No matter how careful you are, it's possible for a car accident to occur.

Compared to できる, which means that the potential to do something exists, 得える means something closer to 'there is a possibility that (A) can be done', or 'the possibility to do (A) has been gained'.

Caution

得える may be seen as either える or うる. The primary difference between the two being that うる is a relatively old form that is often seen in literature, and spoken far less frequently than える. Despite this, there is a tendency for うる to be the preferred spoken choice when the kana that appears before it is え, such as 教おしえる becoming 教おしえうる, rather than 教おしええる.

そういう事ことはセクハラにもなりえるので、そういう事ことはやらない方ほうがいい。

Things like that can be considered as sexual harassment, so you shouldn't do that.

考かんがえうる事ことは、すべてこの企画書きかくしょに書かいておきました！

I wrote everything I can think of on this proposal!

Fun Fact

得える is far more formal/bookish than できる. However, there are several set phrases that are very common in daily speech that use this particular grammar point, such as あり得える 'possible', and あり得えない 'impossible'.

うん、それもあり得えるね...

Yeah, that is also a possibility...

電気代でんきだいがまた値上ねあがりするみたいだよ。マジあり得えない。

It looks like the electricity bill is going to go up again. There's no way.

## Examples

Examples

Sentence
Translation

それはあり得える。

That can be possible!

その対策たいさくは考かんがえ得うる。

It is possible to consider that countermeasure.

災害さいがいはいつだって起おこり得える。

Disaster can occur at anytime. (there is a possibility that it can occur)

対応たいおう次第しだいでストライキは回避かいひし得える。

A strike can be avoided depending on how it is dealt with.

耕たがやし得うる土地とちを持もっている方かたは田植たうえができます。

People who own arable land can plant rice. (can be farmed)

消防士しょうぼうしである彼かれは、信頼しんらいし得うる人ひとです。

Being a firefighter, he is a person you can trust. (there is a possibility it can be done)

彼かれなら、もっと大おおきなことを成なし得うるだけの力ちからがある。

In his case, he has the power to be able to accomplish even greater things. (there is a possibility that it can be accomplished)

この仕事しごとで知しり得えた情報じょうほうは、誰だれにも話はなしてはいけません。

I can't talk to anyone about the information I was able to obtain through this job.

生しょうじ得うるあらゆる事態じたいを想定そうていするのは不可能ふかのうです。

It is not possible to predict every circumstance that can arise. (there is a possibility that it can arise)

その言いい方かたは恐喝きょうかつにもなり得えるから、やめた方ほうがいい。

It would be better if you stopped, since saying it in that way can become a threat/blackmail.

我わが社しゃにでき得えるだけの努力どりょくはいたします。

I will put in as much effort as our company is capable of. (there is a possibility it can be done)

彼かれは群衆ぐんしゅうの目めにも耐たえ得うる精神せいしん力りょくの持もち主ぬしだ。

He possesses the force of will to be able to endure the piercing eyes of the crowd. (there is a possibility that it can be endured)

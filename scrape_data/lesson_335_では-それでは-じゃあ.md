## About

About では・それでは・じゃあ

English

Japanese

では and じゃあ are both abbreviations of それでは, and used as conjunctions in Japanese. This structure may be considered as a combination of それ, the case marking particle で, and the binding (adverbial) particle は, but is commonly treated as a single word. それでは, では and じゃ is the order of use from most formal to least formal.

それでは has two primary functions. It can be used to continue in the same topic, showing that '(B) is a result of (A)'. Or it may be used to change the conversation topic completely, showing that '(A) is at a good point to become (B)'. In each of these cases, it can be translated as 'and with that' in English. 'If so', 'well then', and 'in that case' are also common translations.

それでは、あなたは行いきたくないという事ことですか？

In that case, you mean that you don't want to go?

では、こちらの商品しょうひんはどうでしょう？

Well then, how about this product over here?

用意よういできた？じゃあ、５ご分ふんで出でよう。

Are you ready? In that case let's leave in 5 minutes.

The translation of 'in that case' is most commonly used when one person is making an assumption/drawing a conclusion about something that someone else has just said.

それでは、あなたがやったってことでいいですね？

In that case, would it be correct to say that you did it?

Fun Fact

それでは, では, and じゃあ are also frequently used as a polite way to say 'goodbye'. In these situations, it comes across in a similar way to 'well…. I best be off' in English.

では、私わたしはこれで失礼しつれいします。

Well, this will be when I'm going to excuse myself.

じゃあ、ここで失礼しつれいします。

Well then, I will take my leave.

では、こうしたらどうでしょう。

Well, how about trying it this way?

では、次つぎの問題もんだいです。

Well then, on to the next question!

じゃあ…本ほんを取とってくるから。ちょっと待まってて。

Well... I'll go grab my book. Hang on a sec.

では、また来週らいしゅうの火曜日かようびに会あいましょう。

Well, let's meet again next Tuesday.

それでは次つぎ会あう時ときまでに考かんがえておいてください。

In that case, please think about it before we meet again.

では、必要ひつような書類しょるいに記入きにゅうして、明後日あさってまでに提出ていしゅつしてください。

In that case, please fill out the necessary documents and submit them by the day after tomorrow.

それでは、今日きょうの授業じゅぎょうはここまでです。また明日あした。

Well then, that is all for today's class. See you tomorrow.

それでは発表はっぴょうします。優勝ゆうしょうは田中たなかさんです！

Well then, I will announce the winner now. It is Tanaka-san!

じゃあしょうがない。明日あしたでいいから、忘わすれないで持もってきてね。

Well, it can't be helped. Tomorrow is fine, so don't forget to bring it okay?

それでは、合格ごうかくした人ひとはこちらに並ならんでください。

Well then, those who passed, line up here please.

では、明日あしたの予定よていをお伝つたえしますので、メモしてください。

Well then, I'm going to tell you the plans for tomorrow, so please write it down.
